抢先体验游戏

立刻获取体验权限然后开始游戏，并随着游戏的发展参与其中。

注意：该抢先体验的游戏内容尚不完整且也许会发生变化。如果您目前不是特别想玩这款游戏，那么您应该等待一段时间来观察游戏的开发进度。了解更多

开发者的话：

为何要采用抢先体验这种模式？

“I think that a game can't be considered finished without the help of the community, thats why it will be in Early Access for some times.
I am the only one who is developing this, and it's very hard to complete a game almost without funds.
So I am here to ask for your help and most important to see if this game can be of your interest :)”

这款游戏的抢先体验状态大约持续多久？

“A month, more or less.
That's enough time to add all the new features I'm working on, but most important it's the time for you to give me your opinions.”

计划中的完整版本和抢先体验版本到底有多少不同？

“There will be new contents for sure, that's why we are here talking about Early Access versions.
New Items and new types of dungeons are already been planned for the future.
However constant updates will always be here, even after the Full version releases.
I am still focusing on make the game different and more random every time you play it!”

抢先体验版本的现状如何？

“The experience you'll get from this Early Access version will be very similar to the Final Release.
There are already tons of items and equipments you can have, the main structures, the enemies, etc.. are already been done.
The game is already fully playable and enjoyable!”

在抢先体验期间和结束之后，游戏价格会有所不同吗？

“The price will not change when we leave early access.”

在开发过程中，你们是如何计划让玩家社区参与进来的？

“A game live only thanks to his community.
I will read all the comments and accept any suggestion to make this game better and enjoyable for everyone.”

关于这款游戏

Someone has sneaked in your house during the night and placed a bomb in your basement! Find your way through procedurally generated dungeons and try to survive as long as possible, disarming bombs and fighting your enemies. Find dozens of powerups and items to complete the levels and go deeper in these endless caves.

系统需求

    最低配置:
    • 操作系统: xp/7/8/8.1/10
    • 处理器: Dual Core
    • DirectX 版本: 11
    • 存储空间: 需要 260 MB 可用空间
    • 声卡: yes
