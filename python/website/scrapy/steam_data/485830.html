Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“I want Star Merc to be an amazing, expansive and immersive 2d RPG. I have always aimed from the beginning to create a scifi game with multiple choices involving diverse alien factions in a fictional universe. I consider myself a scifi nut and Star Merc is the type of game that i know can benefit from community feedback and ideas, i want the game to appeal to any other scifi nuts and that means including any great ideas that are suggested such as storylines, locations even races and monsters. Im a one guy team creating Star Merc and the help from the community through development will also help with any major issues and faults within the game, I am dedicated to making the game the best i can and if i can cater to every space fans needs then I will do it to the best of my ability and the feedback i have received for the game so far has been great.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“I work on the development of Star Merc everyday so I cant imagine it will be in early access for too long the latest would be winter 2016, any new additions to the final release will be done threw updates and any additions to storyline or lore for the universe from the community will be added in every update when in early access.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“The scope of the game will be the same as it was from the beginning, lots of planets, locations, races and galactic problems needing solutions. Everything that the game will have is the same as the early access version except it will be expanded upon through updates.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The current version of Star Merc available is playable with all of the features added at a basic level, i am adding new content to the game everyday and the lore of the Galaxy is constantly being expanded upon as i develop the game.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“I plan to heavily expand the game so the price may increase due to this, however i will always first and foremost listen to the community and their opinions, this is my first game to release on a major platform and i don't want to let anyone down who has paid money for the game I made.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“Space games are like space itself, the possibilities are infinite and I know myself that i can only come up with so many ideas while trying to polish other areas of the game. I want the community to get involved in every aspect of the game with new ideas or additions that they feel is relevant to the universe. Any ideas from the community are welcome and if they fit the game universe and feel and are possible then I will do my best to implement them. I love getting feedback about the game wether its good or bad because all feedback is taken on board.”

About This Game

Star Merc is an open world rpg where player choices heavily affect outcomes.

You assume the role of a manufactured space mercenary who is instructed on basic mission details and sent on his way expected to complete the objectives his creators have set. However, it is the players who decide what orders to follow and who from, the game provides many different choices that heavily affect situations, outcomes and relations within the game. Players also have the freedom of traveling to different planets and locations searching for loot, missions or shady business.

Some of the features include

- The freedom to travel when and where depending on the choices you make

- Many different weapons and items at your disposal

- Unlock special suit abilities as you level up

- Different Alien races to befriend or destroy

- Encounters with hostile and friendly space monsters

- Many different environments, planets and locations

- Important choices that drastically change circumstances and your surroundings

Early Access Review
Posted: 24 June
I only just downloaded this because I love science fiction, RPGs, and exploration in video games. So far this game looks very promising, however the difficulty level is crazy-high for battles. Enemies can destroy you with two hits. Aside from the difficulty level, the size of the planets is rather small too. Some things that I really like about this game so far are the vague story that hints at a greater mystery and plot and the impressive level of choice. In the very beginning of the game you are told to go do something for your creator, however you can deny him and go explore the universe. I thought that was a pretty nice touch. Aside from the gameplay and level design, the music is a mixed bag. I like the ambient music in the game because it adds a lot to the dark atmposphere, however it loops too quickly and becomes irritating. I would like to keep playing this game and see what it has to offer, but I keep finding myself selectively battling because my resources are running low and battles are unfair. Overall I am happy with my purchase of this game so far. My main recommendations are to focus more on memorable music with longer tracks that are distinguishable in the game and not generic. Then lower the difficulty to the point that the battles are not unfair and the player can actually grind without using a ton of health potions. If those issues are fixed then I would focus on making the planets larger and more explorable. That is obviously easier said then done, so the music and difficulty lowering should be top priority. This truly is a unique and ambitious game. I just hope it keeps improving and that people will take notice and buy it when it is finally finished.
