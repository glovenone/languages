About This Game




Looking For A Job?

The Corporation Needs You!

Apply at Suit City HQ today and put the future of the world in your hands!

The Corporation IS you! The Corporation keeps our beloved society in order and in justice.
The Corporation supplies the task and the task supplies cash! And remember citizen, more
reward for the less questions you ask... Our perfect society relies on us to keep the cogs in
the machine lubed up and quiet. The cogs keep the machine running and functional.
We need cogs like you to support the city in the most noble of ways. Take action!
Take a stand! Take this application in your hand and apply now!

Necessary Credentials:
  • Strong Fists
  • Chiseled Jaw-line
  • Fantastic Tie
  • A Very Girthy Briefcase
  • Fighting Experience
  • Lack of Heart
  • Love for the Corporation!

Please submit your application along with your $0.99 application fee to start today!



Features
  • Over 99 Different Types of Enemies
  • Find Hidden Treasures Within Each District
  • Enjoy a Kickin' Soundtrack Composed by Gbone and the Biz
  • 86% Interactive Environments
  • Lots and Lots of Sandwiches
  • Multiple Hours of Game Play
  • 35 Achievements
  • Steam Trading Cards

All of this and more for less than 1 dollar!

BUY THE GAME! IT'S JUST A DOLLAR (And even less in a sale)

Anyway, let's get serious. This game is a typical RPG, nothing that special. The graphics are pretty unique, unless it looks lazy to you. The game doesn't look very pleasing, but it's worth more than you pay.

Some things to note is that it has (Both Bad and Good):

-References to various things (Video Games, Social Networks, War, Business, Politics, etc.)
-Some funny moments (but nothing to laugh out loud)
-Interesting Story Plot (although it seems this game is just for the laughs, the whole setting of this game is very dark. I mean, everybody has to work or else they pretty much die. Also, in the game, they are various things that can actually disturb you.)
-Boring Type of Battle System (At first, the battle system is enjoyable. But once you progress in the game, the battle system starts to get bland and you get annoyed.)
-Secret Achievements (I know most games have this, but this game has some secrets that are very hard to find. Most of this game's achievements are part of progress, but some are hidden throughout the game.)
-Difficulty (This game looks hard at first. But if you go in the right direction, and do everything the game says, battles and the game overall is just too easy. The difficulty wasn't there when I played the game.)


But don't get this wrong though. This game is very worth your time. Anyway, get this game! Your money is well spent because this game is very fun and interesting. Do I need to put a rating?
Fine, 8/10

(The game is pretty short/Took me about 5 hours to finish the game. Don't expect too much on a $1 indie game that used RPG Maker.)

Summary:
Suits: A Business RPG is an RPGMaker game that has set price to a euro. As there are many bad RPGMaker games and bad cheap games, that doesn't sounds much appealing, right? This game is actually a big surprise!

By the terms of gameplay, it's still the good old system we know from, for example, Final Fantasy. But what makes this game great is the ubiquitous jokes and critics that aren't just about business sphere - They have made jokes of almost everything! Undertale-like puns? Check. Gaming critique? Check. Culture references? Check. Political humour? Check. Controversial humour? Check!

This game is here for a price of a cheap coffee, offering few hours full of fun and entertainment. You won't regret buying this if you don't mind the gameplay and crazy graphics.

Pros:
- Jokes in every corner.
- Unique graphics style. It's lazy style but it perfectly fits the comedy that the game offers.
- Very low price.
- Steam achievements.

Cons:
- JRPG grinding. Some places feel a little like a Zubat cave. But it's not that bad here as in other (serious) games of the genre. I was still missing something like Repel.

An RPG involving you as a businessman, your mission in this black and white, dreary world is to slowly go into corruption.

I can't believed that I've paid just 47p for this amazing game, I would've paid £4-5 for this and would've been more than happy to!

If you can set aside the art (which I love, it gives the game a depressing feel to it), and you love RPGs then seriously just go for it! The music itself, well there's barely any but that's a good advantage for this game! It's meant to make you think about this world and how horrible it is, knowing that it's all about the money and screw anyone who thinks differently (even just a tad!).

The gameplay is just spot on; you're able to navigate around multiple worlds from the hub when unlocked and each area has its own look. The battle sequence is the usual turn by turn RPG style, but that's certainly not a bad thing at all! In fact, I'm glad that the battles are like this, as it makes me nostalgic.

The writing, my GOD the WRITING! If you see this, TALK TO EVERY PERSON AND EXPLORE THE WORLD!!! The humor in this is glorious, taking its own environment and making little jokes about it (for example, a person very early in the game is called Loanrenzo)! not only this, but it references so many memes that are currently popular, such as Tai from Youtube (fuel units), Donald Trump (small loan of a million dollars), the clocks reading 4:20 and many more! Many a chuckle was had while playing this lovely gem of a game.

I'm still not finished with this game yet, as I've only played for about an hour at this moment of time but my goodness it's amazing, I mean, why are you still reading this review? BUY IT!
