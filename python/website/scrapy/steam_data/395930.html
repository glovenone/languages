About This Game


Yarrrrrr, friends!
Scarlett is back in an all-new Farm Frenzy adventure that puts the fun back in farming! Guide the red-haired beauty as she sets aside her vacation plans to help the animals of an abandoned island learn to provide for themselves. With a few frenzied clicks of your mouse, grow crops, feed animals, and gather produce as you race against the clock to complete your goals. In time, you'll work your way up from caring for a few chickens to manufacturing goods and engaging in commerce with neighboring islands.

Manage your time and resources well, and you'll earn a multitude of gold medals and unlock awesome achievements. As the story of the wild and wacky animals of the island and the troublemakers among their ranks unfolds, you'll be treated to colorful island visuals, vibrant animation, and the authentic sounds of a farming paradise! Don't miss the fun and the challenge of this exciting, no-holds barred chapter in the smash hit Farm Frenzy saga!

Features:

  • Touchs screen friendly
  • Point-and-click simplicity
  • Challenging and fun levels
  • Hilarious, eye-popping animation
  • Multiple medals and achievements

Farm Frenzy: Heave Ho is the sequel to Farm Frenzy: Hurricane Season and is a casual game from Buka Entertainment/Alawar. The game bears the Buka trademark cute childrens story book graphics. As is characteristic of nearly all games from this publisher the game is family friendly and well suited for younger children, but will appeal to casual game fans of all ages.

The graphics are full HD and are both cute and well animated. The artstyle is colourful and pleasing to look at. The game has been given a pirate themed make-over this time with all the animals wearing pirate themed attire or sporting pirate themed tattoo's.

The sound is contextual with pleasant samples. Music is sequenced sound loops in a pseudo country style arangement which is pleasing to listen to. There appears to be a single in-game track repeated during game play stages which gets a bit repetitive. The repetition is broken to an extent by different tracks for the menu and pre-game instructions.

The player is tasked with purchasing chickens, collecting eggs, processing the eggs to make powder eggs, processing the powder eggs to make pasta and also accumulating funds. Storage facilities can be upgraded as can manufacturing facilities. Dogs can be hired to help capture ferrets and bears which can be sold at the market. Cats can be hired to collect goods and place them in storage. As storage becomes full goods need to be transported to the market for sale. There are additional facilities and more stages to complete than in earlier games in the series.

The style of play is arcade action with puzzle solving and resource management aspects. There are rewards for completing level completion criteria within certain time constraints.

The game is fun to play and it can be quite challenging completing stages within the required time.

The game size is small which should be a relief to those with limited storage capacity on their compter or laptop.

To summarise, a fun family friendly game with really cute graphics and good re-play value for those who like casual games.

7.9 / 10

Farm Frenzy: Heave Ho:
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Relaxing on a small boat, Scarlett is enjoying the smooth and gentle rocking that occurs between each swell. When a storm hits suddenly, her boat capsizes and she finds herself stranded on a small island occupied by pirate animals…. What is going on here?

As the friendly animals come to Scarlett’s rescue (well, that is what she thought) she quickly finds out that they are in need of a cracker of a farmer!

Farm Frenzy: Heave Ho is the sequel to Farm Frenzy: Hurricane Season, and if you enjoyed the previous one you will definitely have a good time playing this game.

Essentially, you will have to complete all tasks per level within a specific time to achieve the golden chest. Upon your success you will receive a token which you can use in the slot machine to get well-needed booster which you will be able to use on the next level. Of course you will also gather money which will can be used to purchase upgrades.

The pirate theme does give this game a plus and the cartoon style graphics are always fun. One thing I noticed is the levels are considerably more challenging than the previous title. In terms of the audio, not much change; I would personally prefer something a little bit exotic, Flamenco perhaps?

In-game achievements but no Steam achievements, however you will still be getting trading cards.

Positives:
~~~~~~~

- Fun and challenging
- Nice cartoon graphics
- Value for money
- Trading cards

Negatives:
~~~~~~~~~

- No Steam achievements
- Slightly repetitive

A fun management/strategy game for all ages. Great addition to have in your games library!

8/10

Farm Frenzy: Heave Ho is next in the series of the very popular time management game series, and it maintains the same standard of quality of its predecessors. If anything, it may be a little too similar to the previous entry with few gameplay changes, although if you enjoyed Hurricane Season, this one will certainly appeal to you.

The game is your standard genre fair; each level gives you a set of objectives in the area of collecting a number of items and creating food dishes. You're only given so much time and money to purchase livestock and tend to your land, so figuring out a strategy on what to buy and sell while keeping critters out of the barnyard and picking up produce before it disappears is the gist of the gameplay. Things can get hectic, especially if you're trying for gold, but can be rather laid back if you're not worried about challenging yourself.

http://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=506817570
As you complete levels, you'll earn stars (in-game currency) that can be used to upgrade your farm equipment which will then churn out more product or give more storage space. You can also level up your trusty companions to better help fend off pesky intruders, and even buy tokens to use on the slot machine, making an overabundance of currency actually useful. Completing in-game achievements also awards slot machine tokens which can be used to win more tokens, stars, and power-ups useable in the game levels. The power-ups aren’t required, but are beneficial if you’re aiming for gold trophies.

All of the artwork has a cartoony aesthetic to it that compliments the casual game play elements quite well. This is clearly the same artist as the previous game; all the characters look the same save for wearing pirate-ey apparel, but it’s pleasant on the eyes and fits the overall humor of the game.

Once again, the absence of Steam Achievements is a sore spot. At least the in-game achievements award tokens which are much more valuable now since they are no longer awarded from gold trophies. But full Steam integration is a must for casual games, as it adds so much value to the overall package. Trading cards are indeed nice, but not enough.

That said, Heave Ho is another great casual title, especially if you're looking for lighter fair to relax with in-between more serious gaming sessions. Despite my misgivings with the lack of Steam integration, it’s still a solid time management game that will appeal to those who enjoy the genre.

Bottom Line: Recommended for those looking for a relaxing casual title with lots of polish and character.
