11 June

LAST Major Escape This Gameplay Update!

We have finally released our final update for Escape This.
The update has removed all old maps and if any old maps are still in your game folder and you attempt to load them it's safe to assume they wont load as intended. But with the removal of all old maps we have add 21 maps out of the intended 50 maps. We are still working on the Hard and Extremely Hard maps.
We have also changed the camera and the key to change its view. The key is now 'C'. We have also made the game harder again by giving the player 100 health. We feel the game needs to be a challenge so we lowed the player health from 350.

Patch Notes:
Added: New maps.
Changed: Camera(Now activate switch with the 'C' key)
Changed: Player Health.
Removed: Old maps.

This will be the last major game play update we do for the game how ever we will still fix bugs and finish off the other maps.

We hope you all enjoy our new multi-layer maps.

About This Game

Escape This

is a casual puzzle game where the main objective is for the player to complete the given stage.
Each stage is selected at random.

All maps can be edited and you are also able to create your own unique maps and share them with the rest of the community.

Can you escape?

Escape This is an upcoming Steam release in which the player controls a cube from a 3D birdseye view and navigates the cube from the dedicated spawn at the start of a map to the green cube at the finish of the map, marking the completion of the level.

Escape This consists of various levels, ranging from 'Basic' to 'Very Hard', in which you will undoubtedly determine to complete as it engages the player with a very unique 'I'll-finish-it-this-try' sensation which we have rarely experienced in similar games.

Similar to Bloxorz(Miniclip), Escape This seems to have established a firm foundation within such a quick development period, evidently proving its inevitability that it will strive to become a treasured masterpiece on Steam.

Cons:

-Unfriendly interface.
-VERY slow gameplay.
-Few options.
-Looping soundtrack.

Summary:
This is just a low budget game however the developer continues to update the game and fixing bugs, that's good but it's not enough.
Are you coming for the achievements? Do you have one dollar and don't mind to spend it? Do you have free time? You only need 5 minutes. If all your answers were "yes" buy this game, otherwise no.
