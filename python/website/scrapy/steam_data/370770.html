About This Game

Holodrive is a super fast-paced 2D multiplayer shooter about exploding robos with crazy weapons and collecting outfits! When Holocorp created Dummy its purpose was to be a companion robot, your fabulous best friend. No one could predict these robots would turn out to be obsessed with exploding each other! Now they compete in special arenas and are equipped with a vast arsenal of guns, an unhealthy dose of unpredictable power-ups and wearing the most diverse accessories available. Nothing can go wrong!

Holodrive is going to be a Free to Play title in the future. You can read more about it down below.

Destroy other players in fast-paced combat

No robot stays materialized long in the arena. Dummys love exploding each other only to be materialized again and continue their spree. Use the map to your advantage and surprise your enemies by teleporting right into the combat or hiding behind anti-bullet barriers!

Equip and collect a vast arsenal of weapons

Holocorp has designed the Dummy to be able to carry up to 7 weapons at the same time. Pick up weapons and ammo as you platform through the levels or grab weapons from enemies you kill. Get different weapons by opening packs, craft them or equip your free weapon thatâs available on rotation every 3 days!

Mobility, Versatility, Unpredictability

Every Dummy is equipped with a jetpack, your robot is made to move fast through the arena, gaining momentum in ramps and using power ups for efficient traversal. Also by using random Power ups, Dummys are able to turn the tide of battle and surprise their opponents with effects like Disarm, Invincibility or Invisibility!

Become the Most Fabulous

Customizing your robot was Holocorpâs original intent. Hat, head, torso, arms, legs, skin and jetpack are fully customizable! Open Holopacks and complete your album with sets like the Shinobi and become a ninja; or mix it with pieces of the Buccaneerâs Loot to become the best pirate-ninja ever!

Youâll be able to collect any item by just playing the game! Open Holopacks to get a random item or dematerialize your copies and craft them into stuff you donât have. The best way to put it is I have a love/hate relationship with this game.
Mechanics wise, it's extremely similar to a flash game I used to play called Raze- in essence, it's a 8 man murderfest. You have your standard (Team) Deathmatch, and also a unique mode called "collectors," where you need to kill enemies, collect their power and deposit it at a bank. And this is where the love/hate comes in.

Collectors can be extremely fun when the teams are equal- but usually, they aren't. A lot of the players have a mentality where if they join a game they can't die. Ever. You'll see a person come in, get shot a few times, and then leave. This applies to any gamemode, but Collectors is where it hurts the most. You'll need people on the defensive, taking energy from the enemy's core, but also people on the defense making sure people don't steal any from yours. So whenever this balance is broken, the game becomes unfair. During a 4v4, getting an ultra kill is extremely satisfying- but if it's some 3v2 where our team is kicking â¥â¥â¥, it just kinda loses its fun for both me and the enemies. My teamates might like it, who knows? In the Deathmatch modes, people leaving is a bit less of an issue unless it turns into a 1v3 or something.

All this, I can deal with- I'll get a little mad, sure, but that's only a few games. But there's one issue, a glaring one that's an absolute pain and is in every match. All the players are de-synced.
You can get hit by bullets meters away from you, you can get killed by people not even on the same elevation as you, and that's whenever I really question why this is a thing. I can get lag and some teleporting or rubberbanding, but this desync is absolutely insane and really ruins the game whenever I manage to swan dive through an enemy's bullets flawlessly, but apparently one hit me and I'm god damn dead. This is especially annoying with the 1-Hit Kill rocket launchers, which you can see the rockets perfectly fine, but even if you're miles above it, you're dead because of it. This way of dealing with lag in a PvP game like this is a way to make you mad due to stray bullets counting as hits.

I know I spoke really negatively about the game, but whenever the players have an equal skill level and the lag isn't too noticeable, the game is EXTREMELY fun. It can degrade to absolute madness at times that's left me in stitches, though some of the unlockable weapons are a bit unbalanced. For example, the Minigun can rip things to shreds in seconds, and the shuriken gun thing's shuriken mines last a little too long. Other than that, the weapons are pretty nicely balanaced and seem to encourage getting the jump and flanking enemies as most of them can kill pretty quickly. Though variety in the starting weapons would be nice, or at least make it viable enough so you don't die as soon as you spawn.

All in all, this game has a lot of good, but hampered by a small but significant amount of bad. It's also pretty cheap. Though, it hasn't garnered much of a following yet, and I'm fearful that the community might die out within its early months.
