About




Mini's Magic World is an action platformer with a diverse cast of characters and levels to explore. Fight bosses, find hidden treasures, follow the rhythm of music, chase or be chased. Follow the adventures of a young student of magic on a journey to the Tower of Magic to save the world and defeat an evil wizard.

You can play it with up to 3 friends in a co-op mode that works with gamepads and even on a single keyboard. Cooperate or fight for the best score.

Each level is carefully designed to provide you with fun and challenge. Some levels are simple, some complex, some horizontal and some vertical, some are time based, while others require following the rhythm of music, in some you fly on a magic carpet - you never get bored because each provides a unique experience.

The game currently features 7 music levels, each with it's own track. The gameplay in those levels is synchronized as long as you don't stay behind, so be quick and be prepared for a challenge.

Mini’s magic world is a 2D platformer with cute graphics. The world of this game looks like Super Mario 4 and Rayman. The game is pretty long with plenty of levels and can keep you entertained for hours on end.

Graphics:

The game features very cute, almost flash-like graphics. The game ran smooth for me and I had no problems with the FPS whatsoever. The game’s art style is nice too but nothing too fancy but the nice colorful palette is pleasing to the eyes. Some levels feature Limbo like graphics as you can only see the silhouette of the terrain and character and the background is nice and colorful. Can be seen in the below image:

http://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=687279845

Sound:

While the soundtracks are cute they get repetitive. The voice acting somewhat is also has an emphasis on being cute and it really is nice hearing the little girl say “fireball” or “Ouch” every time you upgrade or get injured your spell.

Gameplay:

The gameplay of this game is not the most original but it is quite fun and you won’t be bored with this game if you had nothing to do. The game features a lot of level, even though they’re not long they do have their own unique designs. There’s a lot of different types of enemies e.g. snails, bees, bats, frogs etc. Every 10 levels you get to fight a boss e.g. first boss was a giant frog. You have a spell which is short range and you must be near enemies to kill them with your spell attack. You can also upgrade your spell attack to fireball attack which can be shot and is long range. Some spells give you invulnerability for a short time You can kill enemies by stomping them just like good old Mario. When you stomp or shoot them with your spell they go in this bubble (think bubble bobble) and you can shoot or jump on them once again to earn more coins. You can collect coins, stamps etc. which are scattered everywhere in the levels. Mini also has a wall jump ability which can be used to climb some areas.

Some of the levels offer different variety as well, like the magic carpet levels, in these levels you ride a magic carpet and you can shoot bees and other enemies coming towards you and the screen keeps auto scrolling. The carpet can be a bit difficult to handle though. Some levels feature un-killable enemies which will follow you throughout the level (sort of like the angry sun in Super Mario Bros 3). There can be teleporter levels as well. When you collect enough coins to earn 3 stars you unlock challenge mode which is the same level just harder.

You start off without any extra hearts and you can find hearts hidden in blocks which can be with either head-butting them or using spells on them. The game is also playable with 3 people locally. You can bind keys so it’s pretty easy to co-op locally with friends if you have controllers. Hit detection is a bit so-so in this game, as you must hit the enemies in the exact spot or you can miss them. The game gets difficult as the level progress but not too difficult. Hidden spots are also pretty common in this game. Short gameplay footage can be found below:

http://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=687285175

Graphics: 8/10
Sound: 7/10
Gameplay: 7/10
Overall: 7/10

Conclusion:

A fun platformer which is easy to pick up and play. Kids will especially enjoy this title as it has very cutesy, fun looking graphics and sound. If you have nothing to do, this game will definitely keep you entertained for at least 6-8 hours as it has many levels and lots of stuff to do.
