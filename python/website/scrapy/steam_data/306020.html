19 May

Update 3.2 is now available - BOSS BLOONS!

Hello and welcome to another content update for BTD 5 - it's an exciting one!

New Content

Boss bloons have been added to the game! That's right, the mighty boss bloons from Bloons Monkey City are invading BTD 5. Look for the button the track select screen for your chance to challenge these behemoths and win some fantastic rewards. New bosses will appear periodically, so be sure to check back regularly!

NOTE: The boss button will only show when there's a boss event active.

Bug Fixes
  • Monkey Ace now correctly counts bloon pops.
  • You can now place five towers in the Full House special mission.
  • Fixed a bug that caused some activated abilities to not target camo bloons.
  • x/3 Spike Factory spikes now have the correct pop cap.

That's all for this update! We hope you guys enjoy the new bosses :)

About This Game

Five-star tower defense with unrivaled depth and replayability.

The Bloons are back in full HD glory and this time they mean business! Build awesome towers, choose your favorite upgrades, hire cool new Special Agents, and pop every last invading Bloon in the best ever version of the most popular tower defense series in history.

Featuring an all New Tower and Special Agent, glorious HD graphics, original Tracks and Special Missions, a whole new tier of Specialty Building upgrades, and a never-before-seen Monkey Lab to upgrade your towers, Bloons TD 5 delivers hours of fun and challenging play to fans and new players alike.

“Nations need to brace themselves for a complete loss of productivity”
- Jayisgames, 4.7/5.0

More Awesomer New Features:
- Cool new towers – Heli Pilot, Bloonchipper, Engineer
- Exclusive new Special Agent – Radadactyl
- All new Monkey Lab – researches unique abilities and tower upgrades
- Specialty Building Tier 4 Upgrades
- Exclusive tracks

Hours and Hours of Awesome Gameplay:
- 21 powerful towers with Activated Abilities and 2 upgrade paths
- 10 Special Agents
- 40+ Tracks (plus their Reverse versions!)
- 10 Special Missions
- 250+ Random Missions
- New Bloon enemies - tougher Camos, Regrower Bloons, and the fearsome ZOMG
- 50+ Steam Achievements
- Steam Cloud Support
- 3 different game modes
- Freeplay mode after mastering a track
- 3 difficulty settings and family-friendly theme so anyone can play

And that's just the beginning - heaps of planned updates will keep Bloons TD 5 HD fresh, fun, and challenging for many months to come. Now it's time to pop some Bloons!

Remember to save to the Steam Cloud after each victory to save your progress.

I have a little bit of time on this game and I think I'm ready to write my first review ever.
I'm going to compare this game to the In-Browser version for you to determine if it is worth the $5 it is on sale.

Pro's of Steam Game
1. Very Fluid and there will be little to no lag ever. For me, lag usually started around round 60 In-Browser, in this version, it does not start occuring to maybe the 100+ levels, and that is with plenty of towers out and darts flying everywhere.
2. More Turrets to add some more variety. These include Engineer, Helicopter, Shredder. The helicopter is my personal favorite and these new turrets add some new dynamics to work with.
3. There are maps after maps after maps. I mean just maps galore. It will be awhile before you finish all of them on all difficulties.
4. Monkey Cash just flows in while playing the Steam version. It seems as though you always have cash handy which allows you to actually buy the agents without thinking that you are wasting money and that you would actually have to spend real money to do microtransactions.

Now there is a negative that occurs in this game though, which all surrounds the Co-op.
1. There is no chat. Like none. You cannot communicate with your teamates on Co-op and it is really hard to do hard levels without this, which will make you just have to play them with a friend you have and just hop in a Discord call or something.
2. There are glitches that occur with Co-op all the time. You can usually play one game through with a friend, but lets say that you fail and want to retry, 9/10 times, it will glitch up and your friend will never join the next match. So you have to leave and make a new lobby and re-invite. Not horrible, it just gets really annoying after a while. Also, if you go into a quickmatch, expect 50% of the time, you will get disconnected from your team mate.
3. Invisible Bloons. While playing with my friend, I have noticed that sometimes i will be able to see some bloons and my parter won't. This has cost us countless matches because he legitimately can't see the bloons that I can.
4. Banana glitch. In Co-op, if your friend puts down a banana farm, the bananas will pop up on your screen as well. They have no impact to you and they don't give anyone money, but they will still pop up on your screen. The only way to have a temporary fix is to have your friend put down the banana farmer agent. He will keep the bananas off your screen.

Overall I really like this game. Co-op can be a little bit of a hassle and annoying; however, if you can get a game going with a friend, it can be very enjoyable. I would rate this game a 8/10 with the 2 off being Co-op.
