About This Game

The year is 196X and the Communists have won the space race. They've set up a research colony on Venus free from the prying eyes of the Americans. However, the Americans were not far behind with their own space program. They send you, Sgt. John Dagger, in a one-man vessel to Venus. Your mission: uncover the Soviet Super-Weapon being created in the Venusian Laboratory.

Includes an all-new Hard difficulty mode with faster enemies and altered encounters.

Features

  • Fight your way through a variety of enemy types, including Soviet soldiers and Venusian lifeforms.
  • Acquire Soviet weapons technology and use it against them.
  • Betray the Ninja Empire.
  • Travel across multiple environments, from Soviet security stations to dark caves to the lush jungles of Venus.
  • Behold the glory of the lush world of Venus rendered in space-age pixel art.

Nice little retro action game with top-down view.

I really liked the title soundtrack, but level music is nice too.

Not too difficult to beat at easy, but if you're badass you could try Hard Mode :). I would say it takes about 2-3 hours to beat the game. There is some difficulty curve, in later levels you get mini bosses that you have beaten before as a regular boss, so at some point it will take several tries to beat certain parts of the game. The most difficult would be the bossfights. You will need the mouse for aiming where you shooting at.

There is possible to upgrade your weapon from the question mark crates: you can get rocket, mini machine gun and kinda boomerang upgrades for your weapon which makes easier to progress and kill enemies. Checkpoints are available at regular distances, so you're not penalized in any way for dying. Indeed you will lose your current weapon upgrade if you die.

I would definitely recommend at least trying this game, since I got it from a greenlight bundle there's nothing I can complain of :). I really enjoyed it.
