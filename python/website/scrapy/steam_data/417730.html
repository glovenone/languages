Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“Shakedown has gone a long way in development and now it's time to pass the final stage with your help. Steam Early Access is the best way to develop Shakedown Racing with your feedback and to get the best out of the game's concept. Shakedown works and plays well as it is, but I'd like better level design, campaign progression and more game modes. I consider it incomplete in that regard. With Early Access you can help shape the game's direction. That's the way we can be all more satisfied from the game - me that I made a complete package and you that you own and helped build a game of a complete value!”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“Currently the game is in a complete shape in terms of bugs fixed and content. It has grown a lot during the EA stage thanks to your feedback. Yet more polish, more options and more content can be added to the game for free and this is why I'll extend the full release date until more people can contribute to this project - developers and players.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“Full version will be further developed based on the users feedback. I'm considering more Arenas and redesigned Single Player Campaign. Also one of the most desired features by me and the players who have seen the game is adding Multiplayer - PvP and Team Based. It's really important and exiting to see your take on those and more features.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“Game is ready for complete playing and features four different locations, with two varieties for the tracks, twelve levels in the Stadium Arena all playable with nine cars from different classes. The visual concept is also complete, with possible adjustments to the cars details. All those would be subject of polish and development during Early Access stage.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“Yes, the price of the game will increase after Early Access. If you're considering it, I suggest you get it now as the rest will come for free for you.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“Player feedback will play integral role in the game's development during Early Access. I'll be directly and personally daily involved with each suggestion here on the community hub or on the game's Facebook and Twitter pages. I'll keep a summarized list, available to everybody with all the eligible suggestions for the game. On each update I'll keep a release form with included elements and fixed issues, which will be open for discussion as well. So you're welcome to join! :)”

About This Game


Welcome to Early Access of Shakedown Racing One!
This product is not finished! Get it if you're excited to play in the current state.



Colorful track racing on three locations with nine cars to choose from.

Cars
Tuner, muscle, SUV, exotic type of cars each with its own character - rear, front and 4x4 cars. They will bite you, especially on jumps and corners.

Tracks
Twisty tracks with many obstacles and challenges. It's fun, but prepare to be tested.

Arenas
New game mode. The Stadium Arena may be close to a popular e-sport game, but it actually originates from a mode initially introduced in Ultimate Truck Simulator called "Balls and Gates". Balls, Gates, Colors in a sport event.

Graphics
I usually steer to more realistic visuals, yet I've really come to love how this one turned out in the game. Mills, castles, ships, metal balls and pumpkins.

Have fun!
Head on to the discussion board for comments and suggestions.

NOTE for Controllers: Menus are NOT supported, only cars. You can see the mappings here:
https://www.flickr.com/photos/47251476@N05/26527195353/sizes/
If you still want to change them you can go to the input tab on the game's launcher.

Early Access Review
Posted: 18 November, 2015
Really great physics in this game. It is top notch compared to other indie racing games. Also the pastel-colours create a very nice prominent art style which sets this game apart from others. Another great thing is how there are numerous different modes of racing, so it is not just the same thing over and over. It's also ruthlessly hard, as the description will indicate, your skills are required. Make a mistake and you pay the price.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=C-lJHdwVA9I
Now it is still in early access, so that greyed-out multiplayer button in the main menu would be absolutely amazing to experience. I hope they can add that to the game at some point in the near future so we can try this out. This game has a promising future!

In my short experience, I was not able to find a game mode where you race against AI cars. Just against the clock. I would love to hear that this is already in the game somewhere, because I feel that this is required of a good racer. But as-is, there is some fun to be had here doing the challenges.

Gripes: controller support is broken. I had to use keyboard and mouse, which was upsetting. The arrow keys on the keyboard and so without analog controls it is a huge setback. Since the game is only a few days old, we'll cut it that slack. But I think that would be the first thing this game needs fixed.

Also, there were some frame drops in some areas. Very capable system getting a rock solid 60 FPS and it would suddenly die down to 15 FPS during a turn. These areas were few and far between so it was not a rough ride just a thing to note.
