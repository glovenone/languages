Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“Because we want to work with you to provide the most realistic championship experience for you to play alongside the televised series. This is a snooker product for the World Snooker Championship fans, utilising the original WSC Real snooker physics, and we want to build a product for you, one for Snooker lovers around the world to enjoy.

Snooker Nation is an add on to Pool Nation Series, a snooker title for snooker fans. Part of the early access plan is to aim to link the two Title's; Snooker Nation and Pool Nation FX.

A snooker game, with championship rules, made for snooker fans, play on easy or hard depending on your level.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“We're hoping to be in early access for no longer than a year. We will follow the communities lead on when we feel its ready for a full release”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“It depends on where the community see it going...

However there are a few main features we would like to add going forward:
  • Rests - to take long shots down the table making play more realistic
  • Only able to take shots you could do in the real world
  • Full commentary

    We are going to fully follow the communities lead we want to provide the most realistic championship experience outside of the crucible.
”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“
  • There is a full offline snooker championship career in a championship venue, played on championship tables.
  • Versus mode for local multiplayer playable offline
  • Online play to play snooker matches of various frames globally
  • A selection of customisable snooker cues and decals.
”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“We have no set plans for the price going forward, it depends where it goes in terms of development ideas from the community, we may gradually raise the price as we ship new content and features, but this is not guaranteed.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We want to schedule set updates, that we can work together towards. Each update will be planned in advance and we will take it one update at a time based on community feedback :)”

About This Game

Welcome to the Snooker Nation Championship, the most important event of your digital Snooker Career. Challenge the very best Snooker players in the world to take home that all important win and lift the famous Snooker Nation flaming trophy.

The offline tournament spans 6 rounds, competing in the qualifiers through to the grand final held in the Snooker Nation Championship venue, played on traditional Championship specification tables.

Or take a break from the crowds by challenging players online and climb the global leaderboards. Collect a variety of cues and compete in online snooker leagues.

Snooker Nation Championship, is utilising the WSC Real championship physics engine, providing the very best digital snooker experience on Steam.

Snooker Nation Championship is digital snooker at is best.

*Snooker Nation Championship is not affiliated with the televised World Snooker Championship series

Early Access Review
Posted: 3 June
You can tell this game is still in early access. A fair few things need polishing, like the referee's voice etc. But the referee calling out to crowd to be quiet is a nice touch, even when the crowd makes no real audible noise at all...The main area that you immediately notice needs attention is the audio. But, as stated, the game is in EA so that's perfectly understandable.

As for the mechanics, the game takes probably about half an hour to get the hang of. If you already know the rules of snooker very well and watch the sport regularly it will be even quicker probably. The design of the game is good and the graphics quality are great. The controls are actually really well thought out, and the user interface is handy - particularly the aim assist, which goes red if you're aiming at a ball which isn't on, for example. Definitely a game to unwind to with a beer after work, or something light hearted to play if you're a bit stressed out.

It's surprisingly addictive, and rewarding. When you get that tricky shot on the cushion just right, and the side on the cue ball perfect, the ball pots, the white comes away from the cushion perfect for the black - it's just satisfying for any snooker fan. If you don't know snooker very well, but enjoy playing other table sports, you may also find this game really interesting. Definitely well worth the few pounds paid for.
