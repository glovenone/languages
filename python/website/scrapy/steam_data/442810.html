About This Game

UFO Online: Invasion is a turn-based tactical combat MMORPG set in the post-apocalyptic world

In February 2024 a huge alien spaceship attacked Earth. Myriad capsules containing xenovirus landed and began spreading their contents across the world. Right after that alien dropships began the descent. The main alien objective was to gather genetic material generated by natural evolution process. Then they planned to totally eradicate life on our planet and re-seed it with primitive life forms, as they have done many times before. But they have failed.

It was too late for their plans. Human evolution has been much more efficient than they expected, and they faced fierce resistance. In this war every man was fighting not for resources but for the right of humanity to exist.

After this war, our world has changed beyond recognition. Alien mothership suddenly left Earth's orbit, but some alien life-forms and some of their infrastructure remains on Earth.

So the War Is Not Over.

Simultaneous turns tactical combat

Players have 60 seconds to choose their actions during "Planning phase" and watch their actions resolve during "Simulation phase". This system eliminates countless hours of waiting for your opponents to complete their turns, thus allowing massive tactical PvP battles with dozens of participants.

Large Open World

Full of hidden treasures, quests, missions and dangers

Player-driven Industry

Gather resources, craft weapons and equipment, use them or sell for profit.

Player-driven Economy

Buy low - sell high. Destroy your competitors in a trade wars

Flexible Skill System

Choose from more than 100 skills in 11 masteries to create unique character

Rich Social Interactions

Create and improve your clan or join existing one. Wage bloody wars with enemy clans and alliances to control bases

Conquerable Bases

Fight for control of the bases, defend them, improve them and use to craft most advanced equipment

Play Your Own Style In Ufo Online!

I haven't played too long. But enough to know that the reviewers below are being a little harsh.

It's not a terrible game by any means. There's no pay to win, though there is a cash shop.

Progression is fairly slow, at least it seems that way so far. You'll mostly be murdering mass amounts of slavic dogs and getting better tracksuits to squat in. I have yet to see any Vodka, though(Update: Acquired Vodka).

There's a lot to learn to the game, which is why progression being slow isn't too big of an issue. Lot of inventory management, but the gameplay itself when it gets down to the combat is smooth and comprehensive.

The overworld map movement is kind of weird, but works given the playstyle. While I haven't participated in it yet, there seems to be some form of PvP system where you can enter someone else's PvE battle and ♥♥♥♥ them up. Though I could be wrong, and it might be PvP only. I honestly can't tell, I only know that you can jump into random's games and help them, but there is also an option to PvP however I've always been told I can't do that(Because of some mechanic that probably involves clans??) Update: So PvP works weird. There are safe areas, which are towns and buildings that you can't be attacked at perior. Then there are "Secure" areas where you can be attacked by creatures but not players. Then there are "wastelands" which are unsecure areas and you can be attacked by players in those tiles. There are also PvP arena matches in the main starting city, but those usually have level requirements to enter so that is mostly end-game stuff. I'm sure there are Clan PvP mechanics, too but as I haven't gotten there I can't comment on it.

As for that one guy complaining about not being able to level up your stats: He's an impatient ♥♥♥♥ who didn't wait long enough. You buy skills from the market, or get them from quests, and use those to level up your stats directly. If he had paid attention to the tutorial he would know this.

As well, at level 4-5 when you get your weapons skill up to level 4, you go into an arena and get to try out classes from the looks of it. Seems to be Machine gunner, sniper, and tank class. Though I only have Weapons at level 3 so far. So I will maybe revise my review once I get there. Update: So it turns out there aren't really 'classes' per se. There are armor types that buff specific weapons(Tank, Assault, Tactical for Tanking, LMGs and ARs, and Sniper Rifles/SMGs respectively). There is a way to reset your stats, but it only popped up once the first time and I think because I got a quest from the guy who does it, I can't talk to him again until I complete it.

I only barely touched on the crafting system, but it seems fairly complex. Will also update when I get something on that end.

Anyway, if you want a Multiplayer RPG with turn-based combat, this is a good game to try.

tl;dr Turn-based Slav dog murder simulator 2016.
