About This Game

VRC Pro takes you deep into the world of cutting edge, modern RC cars. You can race up to 15 different RC cars on real existing mini race tracks all around the world; all fully customizable & tunable. With top speeds of around 120 km/h (75 Mph) and insane acceleration on ultra tight tracks; get ready to experience a whole new level of racing!

GAMEPLAY & REALISM:


Build your RC racing career in an online E-sport community with over 125.000 racers, from beginners to advanced. Join online championships and Internet Multiplayer racing with your electric or nitro powered racing machine. Or if you're a casual racer, have fun JUMPING, BASHING, FLIPPING and CRASHING!

Available cars:
✓ Nitro buggies
✓ Electric buggies
✓ Short course trucks
✓ Rally cross cars (coming soon!)
✓ Touring cars
✓ Drifter cars
✓ Pan cars
✓ NASCAR trucks
✓ more cars are being added all the time...

VRC PRO is by far the most realistic RC racing simulator available today, with over 14 years of non-stop physics and vehicle dynamics development. This sim is supported by over 40 industry leading RC brands and organizations. Real existing mini race tracks have been modeled in precise detail.

E-SPORT RACING


Experience the world of high-end RC racing motorsport! VRC Pro features a serious, competitive E-sport game environment with over 125.000 registered racers, including top class racers from the real RC racing scene. Join online championships at your own level and make your way up to the top. All vehicles are cutting edge modern RC racing machines -fully customizable and tunable- exactly like in real world racing.

E-sport features:
- Online championships (on-going race series, any day, anytime)
- Internet multiplayer racing (up to 10 cars, anytime)
- Time trials (best day, week and month records)
- Training with opponent cars

GAME FEATURES:


- 15 cars (onroad & offroad, 1:12 to 1:8 scale)
- 22 tracks (indoor & outdoor)
- online race events
- internet multiplayer
- time trials
- ghost cars & practice opponents
- achievements
- car tuning & customization
- build your car from scratch
- rostrum view, 1st person view and behind-car view
- keyboard, gamepad and joystick support

EXPERIENCE THE FLOW:


Get ready to experience the unique flow of RC racing. With RC racing, you are both spectator and racer at the same time. Yes, this is challenging, but once mastered it is one of the most rythmic, rewarding and addictive flow experiences you will ever have!

Welcome to the World of high-end RC racing!

Keyboard, gamepad and joysticks are supported.

System Requirements

    Minimum:
    • OS: Windows XP SP 3, .Net framework 3.5
    • Processor: Hyperthreaded Intel CPU, AMD Athlon 64 CPU
    • Memory: 1 GB RAM
    • Graphics: 256 MB Pixel Shader 2.0 graphics card (nVidia GeForce FX 5600 or ATI Radeon X600)
    • DirectX: Version 9.0c
    • Storage: 2 GB available space
    • Sound Card: Soundblaster compatible soundcard
    Recommended:
    • OS: Windows 7 or Windows 8, .Net framework 3.5
    • Processor: Multi-core CPU (Intel Core i5 or i7, AMD Phenom II QUAD X4)
    • Memory: 4 GB RAM
    • Graphics: 1GB dedicated RAM, Pixel Shader 3.0 graphics card (AMD Radeon HD 5870, nVidia GTX 560)
    • DirectX: Version 9.0c
    • Network: Broadband Internet connection
    • Storage: 3 GB available space
    • Sound Card: Soundblaster compatible soundcard
    • Additional Notes: VRC USB adapter or controller, for hosting multiplayer sessions high upstream connection is recommended

Posted: 15 April, 2015
VRC Pro is an RC simulation game from developer Virtual Racing Industries that is currently available on Steam as well as on the VRC Pro website. VRC Pro gives you access to the world of modern RC cars and racing and provides a method of training for amateurs and veterans of RC racing alike. While it may be intimidating at first VRC Pro is an excellent way to get in to the world of RC motorsport.

A word of warning, don't expect this to be an RC racing game because that's not what it is, this is a simulation game and a realistic one at that. Also I'd just like to say I've never really raced RC cars in real life, I used to own some RC cars which I used to drive around but that's about as far as my experience with RC cars goes.

+Great graphics
+Realistic physics
+All of the included cars (15) and all of the included tracks (22) are real
+A good variety of different types of car and different types of track
+A huge amount of customization options for the cars included as well as the ability to create your own from scratch
+Has gamepad support as well as support for real RC controllers (adapter required)
+The controls at first seem complex and difficult to use but once you get the hang of them they're great
+Great performance
+A fixed camera view as well as an up close 'following camera'
+There is a practice mode for races, helps to familiarise you with a track before you take part in the main race
+Practice cars (AI) are very good
+Several different game modes both offline and online
+Good amount of people online and I believe it can be played with people who own the non-Steam version also
+Provides a good way to enjoy RC racing without having to spend a lot of money on a real RC car
+The developer is active on the Steam forums

+/-You pretty much have to play this with a controller, personally I'm fine with that but I know a lot of people get annoyed if they can't play with KB&M

-You can't pause the game even when playing offline
-Some of the tracks seen on the Steam store page aren't included in the Steam version of the game
-Not all of the tracks have the option for you to practice or race against AI, kind of annoying if you're like me and only want to play the game offline
-It's not very begginer friendly and it only has a very simple tutorial

Verdict:
8

A great way to learn how to race RC cars with realistic physics, great graphics, a fair few cars to drive and a number of tracks to drive them on. If you have any interest in racing RC cars then this is definitely a game you should pick up.

***This review was written using a key provided by the Developer for review purposes***

I am very impressed by a game that had been out/in development for so long and the work put into it shows.

Pros:
-Very realistic
-Real cars and tracks
-Online races/esport type matches as well as series (haven't touched these yet as my skills are lacking so far but look fun)
-Very in-depth custiomization (body, parts, aerodynamics, ride height, shocks, balance, steering, etc...)
-Punishes you for bad habits
-Can use a real rc controller (need to buy an adapter sold separately)
-Has been out/in development for ~14 years, it shows

Cons:
-Slightly misleading info on steam. The videos/pics show some tracks not yet availible in steam version and felt slightly slighted by the fact I thought I would be getting some of the ones in the videos/pics. After reading their forums and the steam forums it seems they are aware of some of these things and are fixing them. The other tracks from what I gathered will be released as a "deluxe" dlc. I am dissapointed that $40 did not get get me the "whole package" or what I was expecting. HOWEVER this is a SIM and a polished one at that and I completely agree with their asking price (for now).
-Will be fixing steam/non steam compatabilty or previous buyer issues. (more forum info)
-Wish more tracks were included
-Is an older game, ~14 years, other than graphics it really dosen't show, and the graphics are not bad by any means.

Conclusion:
-Definitely recommend this game to anyone interested in a sim rc experience or anyone who has had an rc car.
-DO YOUR RESEARCH on what you will get with steam version/non steam license. Steam version contains more than enough to really enjoy this game I just personally wish there were more tracks included. Everything seems like it will make it's way over to steam and the playerbase will only grow from that.
-Asking price is fair.
-Use a gamepad at the very least if not a real rc controller (xbox 360 controller worked fine for me)
