It's a kart racing game steeped in Garfield lore, offering immense kart customizations. Levels are a bit bland, items during races could use a bit more variety. Honestly, I was expecting complete garbage, but apart from a few minor bugs, the game machanics are really only as bad as Mario Kart, and for kart racing games, that's saying a lot.
Only big problem: There's no multiplayer. For these sorts of games nowadays, online is the standard, but this doesn't even have local. Even the game application is called "GarfieldKartNoMulti", so it was clearly someone's conscious decision NOT to include it.
I prefer to reminisce about the old twitter account. At least that was multiplayer.
