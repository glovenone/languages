About This Game

We have been waiting for you commander. The Dzerhgan forces are amassing an offensive force to strike our newest colony on Jernae 2. Gather your crew as there is no time to waste! We need these alien scum to realize they should look elsewhere if they want easy pickens. Starship: Nova Strike is a side scrolling action game that will draw you into its universe and captivate you for hours. Can you save an entire colony from destruction and send the alien invaders fleeing to their homeland?

This was the first of two reviews I did on Livestream yesterday, nearly a week from the time it was supposed to be released.

In the twenty-four hours since that stream went live, both this game and Krog Wars went live on Steam, and now I can do an official review. I've spent a few minutes with the Steam version, and I've found this one somewhat improved from the version we used on the Stream.

They corrected exactly one bug, that being the one where the Game Over screen requires that you shut down the game and restart it before you can play again. This had the expected side effect of correcting the High Score saves, which isn't a bad thing. So, they get that much credit.

For those of you who have played Hovering Heli Death, this game will look somewhat familiar-- That's because it is. It's a direct asset swap with the other game, including the project name in the title bar in Windowed Mode. The game itself is horribly optimized, with Fastest graphics being almost unplayably fast, and the Best setting being almost the opposite.

A video record of the stream in its entirety can be found here: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=h_CCsNv9NUM

Again, this game's pre-release review has been shifted to the "General Discussions" forum, which is NOT visible if you don't already own the game. This IS a form of censorship, and I have zero issues calling it out.

Fortunately, the consumer can look at regular reviews prior to buying the game, so I paid out money again so that you can have the warning you deserve. This particular game should not have been made, and although they did fix one issue, it's a far cry from producing a good game.
