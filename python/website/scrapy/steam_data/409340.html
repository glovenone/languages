Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“I think the project can be developed and improved with a lot of commitment, we are two and we can tell that we are developing a good product with the help of the beta testers and with the feedback of the players who will write their point of view on the official page. in fact we are developing many ideas like maps, weapons, style of play, modes. we want then make our product unique but at the same time enjoyable for all the players.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“I think that the fps that we are developing now expresses a minimum of its potentiality. within end 2016/begin 2017 we want to plan the base contents, after that updates will continue.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“The basis of the game are already done, but there is still a lot to do to make the game better. However we can ensure you that new contents will be added every month:

-New weapons.
-Better maps.
-Some PowerUps
-Workshop

If you have ideas don't hesitate to write them in the HUB or at the mail of reference.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“Perraw, at the moment is at the begin of the developing progress. Many bases of the game are already defined but the majority of the contents are only a hint and they can probably change quite drastically.
That said the game at the moment has great performances, in the current state however it's probable that some guns are unbalanced, network is unstable precisely because we are testing a new generation of network still in developement.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“Price will remain unchanged, it will be the same in the final version.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We are trying to collect advices or ideas to add to the project and during developement i bear in mind criticism and approvals from the community.”

About This Game

Perraw - FPS the Clone War

Why Clone War? The Clone War is to create the same character's movement, change weapons, fire etc. (except movements) this is the first mode that we offer, other modes will be added with future updates!



In 2035 the land becomes uninhabitable, so the human species on the planet migrates HUXLA 15, where the territory is divided into two parts.
After some time, the resources become scarce, so was born the war Kulras, fought by the regiments, Alpha and Scorpion.
With the continuation of the technology, they are developed powerful AIs, which then are used in war.
Unfortunately due to a malfunction of the central system, the robots have lost control starting to kill everything that moved, thus extinguishing the human race.
But the war continues, between this machine ...



FPS gameplay
Use Usual Weapons,And Unusual ones like:
The Blobber
The ShockThunder
FireBlast
And Others..
Destroy your friends in the Multiplayer Warfare.
MORE COMING SOON....
