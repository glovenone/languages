About This Game























Caveman World: Mountains of Unga Boonga is a retro inspired 2.5D platformer adventure game set in fantasy prehistoric time. You play as a Caveman, exploring various levels and overcoming physical obstacles. Levels become increasingly more difficult as you encounter new enemies, obstacles, traps and more!

Posted: 8 July
Caveman World : Mountain of Unga Boonga is not only a preposterously enjoyable video game, but also an intelligent and sharp-tongued satire of contemporary caveman timeline. It represents a refinement of everything that the cavemen brought to the table thousands of years ago. It’s technically more accomplished in every conceivable way, but it’s also tremendously ambitious in its own right. No other world in video games comes close to this in size or scope, and there is sharp intelligence behind its sense of humour and gift for mayhem. It tells a compelling, unpredictable, and provocative story without ever letting it get in the way of your own self-directed adventures through the caveman history.

It is one of the very best video games ever made.

Graphics options are plentiful and powerful, with everything from standard quality and view-distance adjustments to a field-of-view slider (though it’s more limited than discriminating FOV players might want - modders have already remedied that). Controls are customizable, and you can play your own music library (just put it in the background. It’s hard to complain much about that.

For the most part, the menus are well done and feel good to use with the mouse and keyboard. That’s best exemplified with the moving system, which feels like you’d expect navigating a jungle or desert with a keyboard to feel. The one place this breaks down a little is in the score menus; it’s a little tougher to navigate the menu because the game has such great graphics so it might be a little laggy on some computers
Have fun gamers!
-Alex
