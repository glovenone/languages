31 May

Design-a-Troop Project!

Hi Everyone!

Thanks to a great community suggestion over the weekend, we've decided to do something a little unusual for one of our upcoming troops... we're going to let YOU design it!

Here's what you need to know:

One of our forum regulars, Jainus, has very kindly agreed to manage the whole process for us (it was his idea after all), which is great for everyone, because it leaves us more time to work on the game!

Jainus has kicked off the thread, which is in the Community Content area of the web forums here: http://community.gemsofwar.com/t/design-a-troop-project-part-1-poll-1-is-live/7718


More details:
We will be keeping a watch on the whole process, for both balance & technical & copyright issues. We DO reserve the right to change some stuff, but it will be mostly because our tech might not allow certain traits/spells to be created (our system isn't infinitely flexible, unfortunately).

The troop will be Legendary, so you'll be creating not just a troop and a spell, but also a Legendary Trait!

When the initial design is done, it will go out to art, and you'll get to see the various art iterations as they come in.

And finally we'll make the troop available to everybody when it's done in a Glory-Reward pack, along with some matching traitstones.

The whole process might take a while (art itself can be slow, and changes to tech can be even slower!) but it'll be a nice insight into the design process for everyone.

So head on over to the forums and start voting!

Best,

-GoW Team

25 May

2.0 Patch Notes

Hi All,

The wait is over, Patch 2.0 arrives! Get all the details on what's new and what's changed below!

PVP Rework
* The PVP Menu now has 6 new menus, Ranked, Casual, Stats, Defense, Battle Log and Leaderboard.

Ranked:
* Allows you choose between 3 opponents to earn points for rankings in the weekly event.
* Ranked is played at normal difficulty with the Combo-breaker turned off.
* The old Rank system is still around as the new Tier system (we've just renamed it, since "Rank" now applies to your position on the PvP Ladder).
* Tier Rewards can be collected once when you’ve reached a new tier level.
* Rank Rewards given at the end of an event are based on what rank you were when the event ended.

Casual:
* Allows you choose between 3 opponents for a fun battle that does not affect PvP points or ranking.
* Casual is played at your currently-selected difficulty.
* Casual battles have a slightly reduced reward compared to Ranked battles.

Stats:
* Shows various stats for your PvP Career including:
* Current Tier and rewards for each Tier.
* Current Rank and rewards for with week’s event.
* Current Wins and Losses for Invades and Defenses.
* Favorite Troop for invading.
* Most Invaded Kingdom.

Defense:
* The Defense menu works the same as before, but now displays the current progress for successful defense rewards.

Battle Log:
* The Battle Log displays all of your recent battles (invades and defenses), whether you won or lost, and how long ago the battle was.

Leaderboards:
* Leaderboards show both your current rank as well as the top 100 players in the current weekly event.

Summoning Improvements
* Summoning is no longer limited to a cap of level 15 for troops.
* Summoned troops are now limited by what level you have raised that troop to.
* Summoned troops now gain any team bonuses earned at the start of battle (from Troop type or Kingdom bonuses).
* Summoned troops come in with any trait you have unlocked on the troop.
* Summoning changes do not apply to Arena.

Hero Profiles
* We’ve added a new social feature to the game: Hero Profiles.
* Hero Profiles allow you to view various information about another player in the game.
* You are able to use the Hero Profile to invite players to your guild or play in a friendly battle.
* Friendly battles do not give out rewards.
* You can access the new Hero Profile menu by tapping on a player’s name in chat or viewing it in the PVP Menus (either from the Leaderboard or Battle Log menus).

New Kingdom
* Glacial Peaks has appeared on the map to unlock.
* New troops (including 2 new legendary troops) have been added to chests.
* Glacial Peaks introduces a new spell mechanic: Mana Burn
~ Mana Burn deals damage to an enemy based on the enemy’s current mana.
~ Mana Burn does not drain Mana from the enemy.

VIP Chests
* VIP Chests have had some improvements:
~ If you draw an epic card, you will gain 2 copies of that reward.
~ If you draw a traitstone you gain 2 copies of that traitstone.
~ VIP Chests now also rewards 2000 gold for every chest you open.

Mythic Troops
* Mythic troops are super rare troops that can drop from Glory, Gem, and VIP chests.
* Mythic troops are the only troops with triple Mana colors.
* Like Legendaries they have a unique 3rd Trait.
* New Mythic Troop- War- has been added to chests.

Treasure Hunt
* Red Chests no longer reward Gold
* Vaults no longer reward Gold and Souls

General
* Claim All button has been added to the Mail menu, allows you to claim all rewards from mail at once.
* Victory and Defeat Menus has had a visual rework, and now display rewards from battles similar to Treasure Hunt mini game.
* Increased security to reduce amount of hacking with the new PvP changes.
* Mechanist Class’ legendary trait, Clockwork, has been added to the class.
* You can now opt out of Global Chat but still be part of Guild Chat.

Troop Balance
Infernal King
* Immortal trait chance has been reduced from 30% to 25%

Banshee
* Mana cost has been reduced from 14 to 12

Troop Refunds
* Infernal King is available for refund for 1 week.

Major Bug Fixes
* Game no longer crashes when casting Soothsayer’s spell when there are no purple gems on the board.
* Frenzy should no longer activate multiple times from damage.
* Game should no longer end when a Troop is summoned from final troop’s Trait (such as Daemonic Pact).
* On Hit Traits (Such a Great Maw’s Hunger) should not trigger on multiple troops with large skull cascades.
* Hero Class Troop types should now be working correctly. Hero should now gain benefits/weaknesses from troop type spells/ bonuses.
* Watcher’s Mana drain on status effect now applies to Frozen, Disease and Death Mark.
* AOE Damage will now pop Barrier.
* We believe we have addressed the main cause of Dust Devil crashing after casting his spell.
* Fixed Mist Stalker’s spell so he should be dealing poison as intended.
* Hero Class Magic bonuses should no longer apply in the Arena.

Known Issues
* Gem Mastery bonus from Hero Classes is not included/ displayed on the Gem Mastery Overview screen in the Hero menu.
* AI troops with Blessed and Curse are not activating correctly.

About This Game

Welcome to Gems of Wars, the newest Puzzle-RPG hybrid from the creators of Puzzle Quest, the original Match 3 RPG!

KEY FEATURES
· COLLECT and STRATEGIZE – Equip your hero with weapons and armor and lead a team of fantastical monsters into battle. Think strategically, upgrading your units and utilizing their unique abilities to defeat powerful bosses.
· TEAM UP – Join a guild, chat with friends, and work together to unlock powerful buffs.
· COMPETE – Participate in weekly events and PvP tournaments to win exclusive prizes.
· JOURNEY – Fight your way through a vast array of quests, each with a distinctive story and game-changing rewards.
· PLAY ANYWHERE – Link your account and play across devices and platforms.

There is no PvP! The "PvP" mode is fake.

I'm wondering why nobody has mentioned this before. Everyone is discussing whether or not this is a pay-to-win game, but nobody said this before, which in my opinion is the biggest let-down:

The PvP mode is fake! This game claims to have a PvP mode and offers ranked PvP games, but all you do is just play versus the 'cards' of another player, controlled by AI. Same with friends: You can play against the deck of your Steam friends, controlled by AI. That's it - you can not really play against anyone else -- so there is no real PvP mode.


I started playing this game, started to like the game's concept and was looking forward to play some PvP and also get a friend to play it, so we can duel every now and then. But as it turns out, they misuse the term 'PvP' for playing versus AI. There is no PvP mode.

This review was written after the big 2.0 patch which reworks how "PvP" works. Guess what, the reworked PvP system is still only fake PvP.

I really like Puzzle Quest and this indeed is a new game in the same match-RPG genre. I wouldn't call it bad, though I still don't really recommend it.

The overall formula is not changed a great deal by being made free to play, but there ARE changes and they DO make a difference. They went from single hero with spells to a party with one hero and a bunch of collectable/upgradable creatures to create a reason for random drops to be a thing. Between that and a typical overbearing free to play game tutorial, I found myself wishing I were playing Puzzle Quest 2 again.

I would say that if you want to play this, you may as well go see if there's a mobile version, because it was clearly designed for touchscreen and I think it would be nicer to play on a tablet. I wanted to like this game. Really I did. I mean, it's basically Puzzle Quest, and I LOVE puzzle quest. For a free game, there is a lot of content to it, from quests, to items, challenges, troops for your party, and so on. I should love this game.

Then I ran out of coins, and could no longer play the game.

To elaborate, at the start of the game, you're given a few hundred coins. These aren't used to buy better equipment or level up your soldiers, (as far as I am aware, I may just not have gotten that far yet) they're the currency that lets you play the game at all.
Yes, your kingdom generates a certain number of coins per hour I believe (up to 500 per day max to start) and quests earn you a meager amount, (most I've gotten so far was 9) but every single challenge and quest takes about 50 coins to play.

The cash shop I don't mind. It's a free game, and they need to make money to stay in business. I understand that part.
But I will never understand game mechanics that actually prevent you from playing the game. Any desire I might have for potentially spending money on the shinies is killed by the fact that I just plain can't play the game until a set period of time passes.
