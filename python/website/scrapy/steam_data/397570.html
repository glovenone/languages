23 March

Update 1.3 - The Edge of Night

New Mode

"Switch", this mode is unlocked by earning 100k points on Nightmare difficulty, if you already have the bomb weapon unlocked you'll already have it enabled after the update.

During this mode at the start of every boss fight the direction that enemies spawn in will switch. You'll find yourself having to hug closer to the middle of the screen and switch up how you view the game in the process. It is a bit of a tougher mode, with a difficulty curve halfway between Nightmare and Normal, with the switching adding something new to the mix.

Solo Leaderboard: http://steamcommunity.com/stats/397570/leaderboards/1200264
Co-op Leaderboard: http://steamcommunity.com/stats/397570/leaderboards/1200267

New Track

Bobby Rose put together an additional track for the main gameplay track set : https://soundcloud.com/bobby-rose-388454214/the-edge-of-night

Balance & Tweaks

Players now start the game fully charged with their unlocked weapon ability.

Time Weapon Cooldown 100 -> 125
Bomb Weapon Cooldown 100 -> 150
Shield Weapon Cooldown 200 -> 225

Base Snake boss segment health: 1200 -> 2500
Snake boss difficulty health scaling has been increased by 33%
Fixed an issue where the snake boss was dropping extra power ups.

Casual mode difficulty scaling has been increased: 25% -> 50%

Changed: Chaser boss minions spawn closer to the edge of the screen, we'll be watching this closely in the near future if we brought them in too close or what additional changes we'd like to this boss encounter.
Fixed: Chaser boss was spawning an extra minion after his wave spawn was already complete
Fixed: In co-op dead players were able to pick up power ups

Changed: In Co-op mode player bullets are their player color rather then all white
Changed: Picking up an extra life in multiplayer allows a dead player, who died when there were no available lives to spawn in again.
Fixed an issue where a new player who started attempting to spawn when there were lives but choose a weapon when no available lives were available would crash the game.

Added additional SFX layers, there is an issue with the laser boss firing off too many SFX which we'll fix up very soon.

More coming soon!

About This Game

Inspired by “shmups" and shadows, Shmadow is a modern twin-stick side-scroller that nods to retro arcade gameplay. Use your light to blast through hidden enemies, unlock new powers, and rack up a high score before the darkness takes over. Upon destruction, bosses drop power-ups that correspond to their defenses. These power-ups, used in combination with your customized starting ability, will help you fight the next threat that comes your way. Destroying bosses, dodging bullets, and the element of darkness create an action-packed experience that will put your speed, skill, and strategic thinking to the test!

Features


  • Infinite gameplay: Fight your way through endless streams of deadlier enemies
  • 4 player local co-op: Recruit friends to help you beat back the darkness
  • 12 special weapons and power-ups
  • Leaderboards: crush your friends’ scores and beat players from around the globe
  • Original adaptive soundtrack
  • Full controller support: enjoy the game with controller of your choice
  • Three difficulty modes: play in casual, normal, or if you dare, nightmare mode

Shmadow is a fun little twin-stick shmup game with tough bosses and power-ups to help you through the game. One of the special things about this game is that it features 4 player LOCAL co-op(Xbox and Playstation controller fully supported and required for co-op), something that is not often done in video games these days. You can play this game with your buddys on the coach while drinking root beer and eating Oreo's.

But on to the actual substance of the game.
You are a ball of light navigating through the dark with the help of your bullets to light the way and destroy all that goes against you (which is pretty much everything on the screen except for the background). You can get power ups after defeating bosses you encounter. These bosses can be very brutal, from slowing down and speeding up time, to bombarding you with lazers and drowning you with bullets. All while trying to avoid or destroy black blobs that damages/kills you opon touch.

There are three difficulties: Casual, Normal and Nightmare. These names don't lie. Casual is fairly easy with some practice, Normal is what you would expect and Nightmare is for those who enjoy getting pwned (I know I still say pwn, I'm stuck in 2007). The game also features a leaderboard for each difficulty.

And the music in this game is fairly decent. Goes well with the game.

For the core price of 2$, I personally think this game is worth a checkout.

I've played this game on Mac and PC and it's pretty smooth on both

Shmadow is very simple but quite original at the same time. The game is a side scrolling shoot em up where you are a light mote using your light to fight against the darkness. The game has fog of war, and your mote and your bullets reveal an aura around themselves. As you are damaged your mote gets smaller, the healthier you are the bigger your field of vision.

The game is very challenging, at no point playing this game did I feel like "well with this power up it's game over"
Power ups do not last long so you are never over powered. The bosses can be quite troublesome, and the fact that you can't see obsticles until the last moment make the game very punishing. That said, it can be played casually and is very enjoyable, a good little time waster. The fact that the game is very straight forward (I played it the first time without even reading the controls, and I figured everything out as I went) but still has enough content and challenges to keep the most hardcore shmups players engaged.

Pros:
- Smooth, responsive
- Different
- nice simple look
- pleasant background music
- works on Mac and PC
- Great price, has cards, achievements, leaderboards
- no Issues with controler, and was responsive on my macbook keyboard too

Cons:
- Difficult (this may not be a con to some people)
- Limited soundtrack
- Somewhat limited menu

7.5/10, a must try for any shmups fan
