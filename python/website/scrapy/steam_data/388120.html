About This Game

Spakoyno is a Russian visual novel set in the late USSR. This is a story of an average Russian guy named Kirill. The characters are left at the borderline conflict of old and new society. High crime rates, corruption and a change of morals… Can he survive the new society?

The brother of the protagonist has gone into criminal underground and the father spends all his time at work and can't protect his sons. Militsiya doesn't do anything about local criminal groups. Kirill is left alone… Everyone is against him: Kirill doesn't favor neither patriots, nor bandits or soviet conservatives. He wishes for peace in our world, so he decides to struggle.

I really wanted to like this game. After having played Russian Horror Story which I quite enjoyed made by the same developers, I had hopes this could be equally as good if not better. The subject matter and period of turbulent transitional history are a story that isn't greatly told to Western audiences. This could have been a great way to allow people to explore these events. With much potential comes much regret for buying this game unfortunately.

The stories that unfold are very short, uninteresting, contains uninteresting characters, and barely has much dialogue at all. The art style is appealing to me at least. The few pieces of music featured are ok and somewhat catchy. The biggest most glaring complaint comes with the fact that the game is missing entire images, which leave you staring into totally blank scenes, displaying only the text box and a "missing img error" displayed.

http://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=625344827

This comes after very many delays with the release. Not sure how this passed off as a completed game at all. I couldn't even tell I had finished one of the stories, since it displayed no end credits or mention whatsoever. Instead just recycling me back to the start leaving me confused.

Could not recommend this at all. All around this feels like a bit of a lazy effort. Doesn't contain any of the charm as with their previous game. Leaves me with a rather bitter taste after having bought it on release now. If you must have it wait for a bare minimal sale.
