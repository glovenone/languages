About This Game

The planet is being attacked and it will take a bodaciously bad-ass cursor pilot to blast the scum to whence it came! Dodge, dip, duck, click and point your way to glory in the most gnarly retro-arcade death trap that you are likely to ever play! Point Perfect will put that "I'm a real PC gamer" attitude to the ultimate test and might make you cry a little in the process. But fear not young pilot, because with great challenge comes even greater glory and nerdy bragging rights! For a select few it will even mean graduating from the prestigious Point Perfect University of Aeronautics. Which of course includes a printable diploma that you can hang on your wall to make Mom proud!

Features


  • Procedurally generated levels means no game is quite the same!
  • Over 20 bosses that can evolve new attacks and patterns for you to adapt.
  • 24 trophies to unlock as well as a secret game mode.
  • Get trolled! Point Perfect will test you to the limit and make fun of you if you fail!
  • Steam achievements
  • Trading cards

System Requirements

    Minimum:
    • OS: Windows xp/ Vista/ 7
    • Processor: 1.4 ghz or faster
    • Memory: 1 GB RAM
    • Graphics: Direct X 8 compatible graphics card
    • DirectX: Version 9.0c
    • Additional Notes: Two button mouse required. With scroll wheel.

Hey everyone and this is my review about the game!

This is a pixelated Fast Pace reflection game.


Interface/Menu/Settings

You are able to play this game in fullscreen mode (Press F)
There is NO MANUAL OR TUTORIAL OR HINTS IN-GAME.
You are able to change the volume of the Music and Sound with a slidebar.
Main menu music is okay.

About the game.

You are playing with a mouse cursor and you have to dodge and avoid any contact. If you touch an enemy/wall/projectile or whatever, you will die and lose a life. It requires alot of fast reflexing and focus. There is no manual or tutorial but on the same time you only need a mouse cursor. I didnt knew you were able to drag with the cursor to make block to kill enemies. I thought the only thing what u have to do is avoid. The game has "achievements and unlockments". You are able to choose from some "abilities" before you are goign to start. 1 more HP, get 1 point each second. and Drag power level 1.


Game is super Repetitive.
Doesn't have alot of variety.
Only Mouse cursor required.
Alot of Trial and error.
There is no Pause button, If you press ESC you will get kicked to Main menu with a nice results of losing all CURRENT progress.
If you have died, you will lose all of your power. (unless ability)

Other things.

Game has some References to other games and things.
Developer trying to be funny with "text".
I have the feeling hitbox isn't always 100% correct.
The more power you have the bigger your drag will be.

-------------------

I Give this game a 3/10
