Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“Dead Acres is in Early Access to gather feedback from players and evolve the game based on that feedback.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“It depends on what features we add. We are looking to add VR support and (possibly) online co-op play. Other features will depend on player feedback. We are looking to finish the game within 3-6 months.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“The Early Access build has 2 levels which offer of 50 nights of play. The final version will have 8 levels and 200 nights of gameplay. Other features and improvements will depend on data and feedback gathered from players. We are definitely look to add VR support for Oculus Rift and Vive. If is within our time budget, online co-op may be added as well.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The current state of the game is pre-alpha. Only 1 level of 8 is playable at the moment, but level 1 and all core systems are fully functional. We are shooting to complete at least 1 level per month (or more). Some UI and sound bugs are still present.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“We are planning to price the game lower for Early Access as a reward to those that put up with the rough early build and provide us with essential feedback to improve the overall product. Once the game is complete, the price will go up a little bit.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We are hoping that players will be very vocal with their feedback as this is the only way we can fix problems and improve the game. Right now, we have an email address they can use to contact us directly. We will also eventually set something up on Steam forums and Reddit to gather feedback as well.”

24 March

Next 2 Levels - Stone Cold and Camp Fubar

Here's a quick summary of what's up next with Stone Cold and Camp Fubar:

Stone Cold - Jack knows where you can get some heavier artillery. Unfortunately, on the way there your RV runs out of gas in the middle of a radioactive desert wasteland, leaving you both at the mercy of attacking mutant sand worms and nuclear zombies. To make matters worse, all gas in the area has been seized by a large bandit gang.

Camp Fubar - The spot Jack spoke of is actually an old Army depot. The heavy weaponry is definitely there, but well-guarded by an overzealous Army general with a fierce army (and a dark secret). He won't give up his cool toys without a fight and the place is crawling with zombie soldiers too. Only the strong survive!

These levels will introduce new enemies, new bosses, enemy vehicles (both ground and air), player controlled turrets (limited ammo), new crops, heavier weaponry, and new defense items for your farm.

About This Game

Dead Acres is a survival farming game where you must grow crops and defend your home and animals from the zombie apocalypse. Challenge yourself in 8 different world locations, each with different types of enemies. Your ability to farm and defend yourself are the keys to your survival!

What makes this title even worse is that we have another over saturated genre includedâ¦zombies. Every night you have to defend your simple plot of land against zombies. There is no tutorial in the game, meaning you have to work absolutely everything out for yourself, even though there isnât much to learn, it would have been nice to know how to farm your crops from the start instead of me destroying them by accident from using the wrong tools.
