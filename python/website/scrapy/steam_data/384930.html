I often wonder why games that are obviously terrible still somehow get greenlit. Pilot Crusader is another one of those games that a classic formula but doesn't do anything new with it.

Pilot Crusader got the inspiration from games like Space Invaders. Every round you destroy every alien ship without getting hit (that means Gameover). Every 3 rounds you have to battle a mothership and that's it basically.

I wouldn't say the game looks terrible graphic-wise, some of the space ships do look nice but there are only a few different enemies to battle, so things get boring pretty quickly. There are also 3 ships you can choose from, which have a different kind of attack. Somehow the second space ship made my game lag whenever I was shooting....

Overall this game could be fun for 5 minutes if it was free but it's not and I consider this one of the worst Space Shooters out there.

Can you turn a simple game like Space Invaders into a mess? Yeap, you can. Afterall we are living in a wonderful times in which you can turn every classic into a mess.

Starting the game

Once you start the game you are great by 1280x720 resolution(at least on 1920x1080 monitor) with no option to change... hell there arent many options to change in the game. Do you want Music/Sound Effects/Explosions/Shake Screen on or off. Thats about it . Okay.. maybe the gameplay will be worth it.

Gameplay

So you pick a ship(1 of the 3 avaible ships) and go fight alien ships( I saw 4 kinds of regular ships + 4 kinds mothership). So you fly around firing your cannon/s and blow ships, once they blow up another wave comes greated by "robotic" voice. With every wave , enemy count increases to the point where you get killed with 1 shot and need to start all over. There is no end level so not much to fight for unless to kill 5 minutes which could be spent in more productive way in other games.
Every now and then you will encounter mothership levels which can be hilarious at times. Either ship not moving or heading to ram you with higher speed than your own ship(I wished I had motherships engines). But there is also progression with mothership waves, the higher the way, the more guns it got!!! Hardcore!!!
Now lets stop talking about enemies and see what tools you got to fight against them. 3 ships with questionable design.
Ship N.1 got 1 gun but on the design you can clear see 4. But in the game you shoot a single rocket so why are there 4 visually added guns when only 1 is working? For show off? Oh and should add that its the fastest ship you have(4 out of 5 points; prolly motherships are 5 out of 5)
Ship N.2 got .... wait for it .... 2 guns(when you can see 4 on the ship design) but the issue with it is that those are "flak" guns and good for close combat only which is hell of a fun when you can die from 1 shot . Should add that it cause fps drops like mad once you start shooting. Also .. speed 3 out of 5 points
Ship N.3 the biggest, the strongest... the crappiest. You have 3 guns that you fire in spread so wide than you will rarely hit more than 2 targets at once. And lets not forget speed 2 out of 5!!!
Common thing about all ships is the special weapon : Nuke!!! Which charges up as you hit targets and when used it may or may not hit the ships.
And all this glorious battle happens over a single background image.

Is it worth it?

Nope, not a chance, even when its damn cheap you better save money from not buying 2-3 crappy games and get a good one.

Why my playtime is so low?

Because there is nothing to see else in the game, 5 minutes in you will unlock the 3 ships, play with them to the point where everything is the same and you are done. There are no achievments to earn, cards to idle or story to follow.

Overall

No... just no.

Was waiting to review until I unlocked the second ship. So here is an overal review of the game:

PRO's:

PRICE (Well worth it)
The difficulty is well balanced and substantially gets harder after I unlock the second ship.
Love the explosions and variation of the explosion sounds
The background music is enjoyable, but I also love the mute option! haha
Always love when games put in the classic screenshake!
Enjoyed the multiple styles of ships ! I have only played with the first two but the second one is awesome!


CON's:

Lack of configuration options
Would love to see a global leaderboard and not just local


OVERALL

The game was a good fun fast paced shooter based in space with a mix of the classic and newer technologies with good explosions and sfx. Would love to see some more configuration options and more ships! Overall a great buy for a only $0.99!

SUGGESTION

Buy if you like small fun shootemups that will keep you distracted for a couple hours for under $1.00 on sale!
