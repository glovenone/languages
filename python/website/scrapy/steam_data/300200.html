Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“Because we need more time to finish the game, but we still want to know if the players like the way it's going. We would love your feedback, good or bad.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“Until we finish the game. That'll be about mid 2015. We'll try to make big monthly updates.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“The Full version will have:
- Full acess to all floors in the Hospital.
- All achievements available.
- Full language support (for the languages that we have).
- Side-quests (that are not available in the current E.A.)
- Full storyline.
- Support for additional content”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“Currently, we're kind of Demo Version. But there'are still things to improve.
- Underground and 1st Floor available.
- Jeff AI is a little bugged.
- Menu not fully functional.
- Animations still to improve.
- Only a few Achievements.
- No side-quests.
- Some little bugs.
- No SAVE!
- PJ's need a voice!”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“Yes.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“The community can help us by giving us feedback, by playing the game!
From that, we can make improvents in the game, add new content, or modify the existent ones.
We want to make the game you all want.”

Urban Legends Update Delay Explain.

Hello everyone, sorry for not responding soon.

Sorry for not responding soon. Steam did not notificate our team about the new topics, etc.

Urban Legends are in the final stages of development.
We did not want to share in Early Access because we felt that was holding your development. So we aimed for the full release.

Why the delay?
We made a huge decision when we changed the engine of Urban Legends (UDK to Unreal 4). This takes time. We did not foreseen a LOT of problems that came with the transition.

We're not going to say a date (because we are really bad at saying it) , but is 110% sure it's going to launch this year.

Sorry for the all the inconvenience.

About This Game

What would you do if you were looking for revenge and ended up involved in something unexpected, far beyond what you imagined?
That is the story of P.J., a journalist that finds himself trapped in an abandoned hospital, searching for answers about his past. Desperate and afraid, he is now face to face with an assassin, Jeff the Killer. A game that brings a new meaning to the word thriller. Prepare for sleepless nights and risk yourself in the world of Urban Legends.

Features:
  • Engaging story;
  • Feel a new kind of terror;
  • Hiding is your only weapon;
  • Jeff the Killer and others Urban Legends;
  • Survive!

System Requirements

    Minimum:
    • OS: Windows XP/Vista/7
    • Processor: 2.0Ghz
    • Memory: 2 GB RAM
    • Graphics: Nvidia GeForce 200 or Better
    • DirectX: Version 9.0
    • Storage: 1 GB available space
