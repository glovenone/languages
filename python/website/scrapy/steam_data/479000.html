Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“We think we have a fun concept for a VR game, We want feedback to be able to develop and release the best experience as possible.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“As long as we need and as long as enough people want us to devellop the game further.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“If enough people like it :
More Maps, More weapons, More Zombies!!!”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“Only one map for now.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“The price will increase gradualy with the evolution of the developpement.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We will listen to your feedback and proposals concerning the developpement of the game”

About This Game

The Virtual Reality has transformed everyone into evil zombies, IrreVRsible makes you fight for your survival against hordes of VR zombies using guns and katanas. Will you survive the IrreVRsible?

Unlock weapons by performing katana kills and headshots.

Defend your rank against other players on the online scoreboard.

Who will be the best survivor of the IrreVRsible?
