About This Game

Battle for the Sun is an action-packed, first-person shooter documenting the realities of war seen through the eyes of a special forces soldier in a world under alien attack. We find ourselves abruptly thrown into an adventure as a talented soldier carrying out his duty in this epic war.

STORY

4 years ago, army alien experiments conducted by the National Army Space Research (NASR) in Area 52 provoked an unexpected disaster. An alien, captured by NASR soldiers for experimentation and research, found a way to escape and plead for help from its home planet. Alien attacks that first began as revenge over time morphed into a systematic occupation that became a threat to human life. Our hero, Dylan; in order to save his family, country, and planet decides to take part in this war. His superior skills were not unnoticed by General Briggs so, after grueling training, Dylan joins the special forces. He goes on to become one of the most important men carrying out strategic missions. While enmeshed in the horror of war Dylan's camaraderie, loyalty and virtue will be tested.

You're a special forces soldier who seems more interested in his secret crush for his General rather than fighting against the aliens invading Earth: once you've stopped drooling over him you'll be going through several missions where you'll be trying to turn the tide of the war.
Battle for the Sun is a very basic and dull FPS with a gameplay that can be summed up as sprint through bare levels/ shoot a few aliens while getting in cover whenever needed/ look for the next waypoint/ rinse and repeat.

PROS:

-cutscenes are unintentionally funny in a "so bad/ cheesy it's good" way

CONS:

-bad graphics, low res textures, blurry environments, lights clip through objects and models look lifeless

-embarassing animations, walking characters seem to be sliding around and their feet will sometimes clip through each other: enemies killed while prone will also sometimes stand up before doing their death animation

-terrible sound, weapons sound extremely weak, voice acting is bad and suffers from awful recording quality

-barebones story and what little can be found is cliched and badly written , most missions even lack an explanation on why you're doing what you're doing

-actual gameplay is poor, weapons are overpowered and extremely precise (even shotguns kill from half a level away), grenades and rpgs have an huge area of effect, aliens are only effective due to homing shots, large areas between alien encounters are just boring to navigate

-basic AI can even have problems navigating stairs or reacting to enemies on different heights

-dull, repetitive level design

-beeped out swearing is as silly as it sounds

-tacked on "skill system" has little to no effect on actual gameplay

-extremely short, took me less than an hour and a half

http://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=661342658

Battle for the Sun is at least good for a laugh if you got it for free like me, the cutscenes and dialogues alone are so bad and silly- as if they were written by a young overexcited teen- to easily make you laugh. Seriously, just look them up online. Sadly that's all there's to it as once you're done laughing at how terrible everything is you're still left with an hour long game to play through, if you're so inclined.

Gunplay is bad, shotgun trumps everything basically as it can headshot and one shot kill aliens from miles away: on the other hand aliens are extremely weak so the devs decided to give them homing shots that follow the player and can also curve through corners if you dont sidestep fast enough. But don't be worried, if you die you'll restart at the last checkpoint and everything you've killed stays dead so there's basically no challenge whatsoever or penalties for dying. Moving on sounds are bad, voice actors seem to have worked using a potato for a recorder, the skill system does absolutely nothing and the lack of explanation for your objectives makes every mission an uninteresting chore...why am I gathering boxes in a cave? why am I destroying that purple sphere? no one knows.

Even though it can be good for a chuckle if you happen to own it there's no real reason for you to buy this game as there's nothing fun to be found here: even if you can stomach most of the Cons I've listed the game is simply boring and dull.
