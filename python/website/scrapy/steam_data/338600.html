About This Software

PowerDVD is an all-purpose entertainment station, providing the ability to enjoy virtually any type of digital media. Not only is PowerDVD an unrivaled Blu-ray, Blu-ray 3D, and DVD player, it also provides superior playback performance for movie and video files, including MKV, MP4, AVCHD, and XAVC-S. Additionally, PowerDVD plays audio files in the most popular lossless formats, and supports all major image formats. Users can even enjoy their Camera RAW images directly without having to convert them first.

Features:

  • Amazingly Lifelike Home Theater
    New TrueTheaterÂ® Color and TrueTheaterÂ® Sound technologies optimize sound and visuals, delivering a premium movie experience.

  • Advanced Audio Capabilities
    WASAPI Exclusive Mode eliminates quality degradation by configuring audio devices to match source formats, including Dolby TrueHD, DTS-HD, ALAC, FLAC, MP3, WMA, and more.

  • Extended Range of Supported Formats
    Now you can play Blu-ray and DVD ISO files directly, without having to mount the files using a virtual drive tool.

  • Improved Player Personalization Options
    Media panel tabs can be hidden in order to streamline the interface, and custom background images can be used as program wallpaper.

  • Ultimate Slow Motion Video
    An intelligent playback algorithm analyzes high speed video, and implements selective decoding and rendering to ensure smooth playback with zero stutter.

  • Enhanced 4K Playback
    An enhanced playback engine, and selective use of Overlay Mode for 4K monitors, provides smoother playback of 4K movies.

  • Battery Life Optimization
    PowerDVD and its companion app Power Media Player implement many performance optimizations to reduce power consumption and extend the useful battery life for laptop computers and mobile devices.

  • Improved Performance for Online Media
    PowerDVD adds functionality that YouTube and Vimeo lack, while optimizing online content through TrueTheaterÂ® technology.

  • Faster than Ever Startup
    Exclusive Instant Play technology makes PowerDVD one of the quickest-loading media players in the world.

  • Companion Mobile Apps
    Remote turns an iOS or Android device into a handy remote control for PowerDVD.
