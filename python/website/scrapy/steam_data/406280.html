Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“DesertLand 2115 Early Access is to provide you with the opportunity to play the game now, and also help provide important feedback to the development team to make the game the best that is possible. DesertLand 2115 is expected to have issues and missing features during the early access phase, so if your are not comfortable playing a game that is being actively developed, then we highly recommend you wait to purchase the game until a later date.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“It is expected to be in early access for approximately six months.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“For the full version we plan to add more weapons, enterable underground tunnels more rich world in objects, day night cycle.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The game client appears to be stable at this point ,players can see initial iterations of player versus player, looting for resources like water, energy, weapons and ammo. Players should anticipate an evolving feature set, bugs, incomplete content, missing features, and potentially game breaking issues.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“The game price is not expected to decrease, but may increase as it gets closer to release.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We believe that player feedback should play a major role in the development of our games and invite players to join and discuss on the Steam page, http://gamejolt.com/games/desertland-2115/46878 or http://www.indiedb.com/games/desertland-2115”
