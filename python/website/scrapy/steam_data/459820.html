Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“Our goal is to make a fun and flirty game that everyone can enjoy, and we think the best way to do that is to get as much feedback as we can from our players, so we can make tweaks. Sometimes the difference between a So-So game and a great game is a couple of killer suggestions!”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“Not long - it depends a lot on our players. The game is actually very close to being finished, but we're intentionally leaving room to tweak and make changes. If the response is positive, we'll be out of Early Access in a jiffy!”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“The full version will have more ladies, and we're planning on adding new stuff regularly. We have lots more ideas for growing and expanding the game in the near future.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The game is, like, 90% done. Like 93% done. If our Early Access fans are happy with the build that'll jump up to 99% pretty darn quick.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“The game is free! It has a Diamond currency for In-App-Purchases, but players are able to earn a bunch by playing the game. We thought the game worked a lot better in this format than as a paid install.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“That's why we're here! We want as much feedback and thoughts on the game. We're planning on holding polls for new features, and engaging with fans on what they want to see next. Give it a try!”

24 June

Update now live! V.0.40

Heya!
Hope you all are having a great summer so far. We've been busy working on some bug fixes and minor updates, which are:

New Features
  • Added school girl uniforms!
  • Applied a color tint to jobs that have reached maximum level.
  • Now the number of time blocks purchased show on the store page.
  • Avatar customizations now persist across prestige.
  • Added disclaimer to gifts page regarding when outfits can be purchased.
  • Continued our work to reduce file size - eliminating another 15+MB while still adding new content.

Bug Fixes
  • Fixed a bug where players could give gifts/dates to girls that hadn't reached the appropriate level.
  • Fixed a bug that was showing the date count while the date was progressing.
  • Try saving purchased multiplier as soon as possible on a prestige to fix issue with that multiplier being lost.
  • Hid the 'again button' when dating and you don't have enough money to go on another date.
  • Minor formatting to the prestige and store page.
  • Improved color difference between Quill and Elle's profile pictures (for easier readability).
  • Temporarily disabled navigation of the UI with arrow keys until we can fully understand what sort of exploits exist with this system.
  • Fixed a bug where the 'Go Again' button wouldn't appear if you earned enough money to go on a date while the date overlay was enabled.
  • Some code was added to help with calculating progress during long periods of offline progress. This especially helps with our WebGL builds.

Thanks for your patience while we continue to work on adding new stuff. Keep on finding bugs and posting them to the Forum :D You da best!!

~Sad Panda

Posted: 7 July
This game is better than most clicker games. Why? Because clicking retains relevance for a very long time. In almost every click game there comes a time when walking away or closing the application is the best method of progression. In this, it's not, because pressing the "chat with girl" button gives an instant boost that scales with the stat they like. Oh, also it's got dating sim things involved.

See, the game says it's trying to be funny, but most of the humor is bland puns or obvious name changes. What's actually interesting are the girls themselves and the archtype they represent. While a normal clicker game makes you wonder what the next big boost to your click power is, this one makes you wonder who the next character you get to interact with is. Now that interaction is just clicking a few things and getting the job they like, but there is a small story going on with each one that's interesting in its own right.

The down side is the long wait times on certain things. Right now one girl wants a 200,000 dollar gift and I'm making just a few thousand every minute. You can relax these by paying real life money for diamonds that you can spend in game for boosts, like shortening every timer in the game by x16. If that's a deal breaker for you, that's fine. I won't argue that.

Now, I'll be honest. I don't usually write reviews for these things. I think I may have written one for Sakura Clicker last year, maybe. But I'm writing this for one reason, and one reason only. ... I am a giant My Little Pony freak, and these devs put in a character that is just Rainbow Dash. Rainbow hair, athlete, blue jacket. These devs have got my number, and I am afraid I can do nothing but recommend the game after that.

Pros: Novel idea for a clicker game, vastly less boring than other clickers, actually keeps you invested in playing instead of encouraging you to leave it on over night.

Cons: Wait times that can only be shortened by earning diamons one at a time through achievements or buying them in bulk with real money, which is only a problem because the game is interesting (in another clicker it wouldn't be enticing at all), using a save slot costs diamonds after the first one? I don't know how that works, honestly, but it's worrying.

Posted: 18 June
Introduction
Crush Crush is a clicker/idle game with a cute anime aesthetic. If you enjoy courting beautiful women then this game is probably right up your alley. Despite using a generic clicker game model, there is also a bit of dating sim action as you have to raise your stats, work jobs and go on dates with your ladies as well give them gifts. The game offers a wide variety of waifus and more to come when the game leaves the Early Access stage.

Story
In this game you play a nameless generic character, each route gives you some backstory on how you met the girls. Usually you end up crashing into them or making them upset in some way and you then have to atone for your actions. The girls all have different personalities and you can talk to them or click on them to get more dialogue. The story is very simple, though it does offer a good bit of comedy and even some meme humor.

Gameplay
The game is mostly an idler, your main objective is to raise a girl’s affection level and progress in your relationship with her. You can also click on the girls to raise her affection levels or chat with her. The dating simulation aspect comes from raising your stats which are then used to get jobs to earn money. Your stats also matter when it comes to the girls as her affection level won’t raise via idling unless you have a certain stat, a good example is Quill. You unlock her early on but cannot raise her affection via idling unless you have the Luck stat which you don’t unlock until later. Money comes into play in letting you buy your lady friend gifts or take her out on dates. To get to the next relationship level with a girl you’re often required to meet a few different criteria, like giving her certain gifts or taking her on certain dates or even have a a stat raised to an expressed level.

Graphics/Audio
Raising the girls’ relationship levels offers new artwork of the girls-to-be scene and all the artwork is easy on the eyes. Many of the girls are very busty and a lot of the artwork depicts them in a sexy way. If you enjoy anime then the artstyle will work for you. All of the user interfaces in the game are bright and colorful, and everything is easy to read. There is no voice acting in the game and the background music in the game is all the same. There are some sound effects in the game but nothing extraordinary.


Pros
+ Very addictive and fun.
+ Good graphics and audio.
+ Beautiful women to court with great anime aesthetics and fulfilling to your waifu needs.
+ Steam achievements and trading cards.
+ It’s free to play.

Cons
- Can take quite a bit of time to progress with a lady.
- Some requirements to woo a lady are very over the top.
- Microtransactions.

Final Thoughts
As an anime fan and a lover of beautiful ladies I really enjoyed this game. It’s a fun time waster and can be left idling for quite a while. I imagine this game is geared more towards male gamers due to the sexy nature of the women but I didn’t mind at all. The game developers are actively listening to the community and improving things in the game which shows promise for when the game leaves the Early Access stage.


Rating: 8/10
