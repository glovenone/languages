What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“We want to form a community of passionate players who demand the best and suggest the best. So Steam Early Access seems like the perfect option. Super Arcade Football has a lot of scope; from different ways to play, different game modes, online tournaments and a detailed career mode to name a few, but we look forward to the steam community to name the rest.
We want to make a great football game and a great arcade game with the input from passionate players.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“We are aiming to be in Early Access for about 10-12 months (assuming everything goes swimmingly). We speculate the features that may take up most of the development time are the career mode and online features, as they are quite large beasts to tackle and demand a lot of iteration. However, all versions in Early Access and the final release will focus on intense fun.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“Other than a good dollop of polish...features. Features spawned from the amazing suggestions of Steam users as well as our own ideas. Below we have listed what's in the game currently, but we have big plans for the upcoming career mode and the online multiplayer side. Thereâll also be many many more teams, tournaments, modifiers and game types.

Weâll also have obliterated all the bugs that are probably nesting in the current versionâ¦ hopefully.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“A good singleplayer and local multiplayer experience. You can play quick games (changing the game types like indoor mode, slippery ice mode etc) either singleplayer or any combination of up to 4 human players (for example, 1vs3, 2vs2 etc)

You can also compete in preset tournaments such as the World Cup and the Euro Cup, or create your own custom tournaments. All can be played singleplayer or with friends locally.

At the moment there are 43 teams available (all national teams). Each with different stats and difficulty levels, we aim to include all national teams and a large majority of club teams by the final release.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“We plan to gradually raise the price as we ship new content and features”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We will be posting regular/weekly updates on the progress of the game as well as persistently asking the community how to make the best football game ever.
