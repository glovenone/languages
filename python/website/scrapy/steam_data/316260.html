About This Game

Disney Universe is an off-the-wall non-stop action-adventure video game where Disney worlds and characters mix up for the first time. Players will be able to suit up in iconic character costumes and face challenges in legendary Disney worlds. Battle through each world and experience a different style of gameplay, with various movie-inspired storylines being explored throughout the game. Players can defeat enemies, collect coins and power-ups, unlock new costumes and explore with friends in a mix-up of worlds inspired by numerous Disney and Disneyâ¢Pixar films.

Ok, So I of all the new Disney games that were recently released on Steam, I have to say that Disney Universe and Epic Mickey 2 are my favorite so far with Disney Universe on top.

You start off picking from a very generous choice of costumes for your character, from Mickey Mouse, Donald Duck, Tron, A Bug's Life, the Princess from Aladdin, Angelica from Pirates of the Caribbean and a bunch more. And you can find a ton more costumes throughout the game to add to your costume menu and use.

This game feels almost like your playing a LEGO video game but the amount of fun you will have here is intense.
The graphics detail is awesome and the sound fx and background music really gets you into the game. It's really good.
The cut scenes are really well done with a lot of humor and makes you look forward to seeing the next cut scene. Lot's of fun!
The gameplay is what definitely makes it for me. You run around beating up mischievous and evil little minions that are so bad they even do bad things to themselves. LOL. As you clobber them left and right, they drop mickey tokens that you will collect for unlocking the costumes you find throughout the game.

There are some moments that just cut right into the action when an arcade machine drops into the center offering you some kind of crazy challenge. Here is an example: I was fighting a bunch of minions with my sword having a hell of a time BAM BAM COMBO COMBO and all of a sudden an arcade machine drops from the air in front of me and I run up to it and activate it and it gives me a challenge to kill all the minions with a laser gun, arcade style. O.O
Oh hell Yeah! PEW PEW PEW. I was blasting them left and right! HAHAHA!!! xD

Oh and MOUNTS! Yes, you will find mounts everywhere from a cannon you can ride around and blast everything and ride animals like a sheep with a power slam or a pig with a toxic fart and a lot of other animals and things to mount and ride and find out what their skills are.

POWER-UPS! You will find power-ups all over. Some turning you into a tornado wiping everything out in your path or turning you into a jackhammer to drill into the floor and gather Mickey tokens or turning you into a snowman to zap and turn everyone into ice blocks. There are a lot of power-ups.
Also, you can level up your main weapon and every time you do it transforms into something else. I had a paintbrush and then it transformed into a magic wand and then a sword and when I got the Jack Sparrow costume, I soon after got a power-up that changed my sword into a parrot. LOL.

There are also a lot of other fun things you will find like throwable objects, bombs and water balloons and other fun stuff, and there are things that will turn you into a zombie. O.o

I experienced all this within the first 30 minutes of gameplay and I am still finding more and more fun power-ups, challenges, secrets, mounts and costumes and who knows what else will come.

This game makes great use with the 360 controller as well as Keyboard and Mouse and is a lot more fun if you have a friend come over for local co-op. It would have been so much more fun if it had online play but with the amount of fun I have with Disney Universe, I can't complain. :D

So should you get this game? Heck yea! This game is a lot of fun! xD
