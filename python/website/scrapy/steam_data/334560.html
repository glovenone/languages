About This Game

You are a genetically engineered killing machine with time on your hands. Having fulfilled your original purpose of saving the human race, you now get your kicks taking down all challengers in the Galactic Hero's Tournament.

Hone your skills and level-up your Hero in single player, or skip straight to the main event and challenge your friends in one of the hilarious and frantic online skirmish modes.

KEY FEATURES

  • Designed for multiplayer from the ground up. All maps support six players including bots as desired. Up to four human players can play locally without split screen. What's more, multiple local players can join online matches together, enabling online couch vs couch gaming.

  • Game modes include co-op, free-for-all, and team based options. Hunt for gnomes, survive alien onslaughts and fight to the death in any of the six stylish 2.5D arenas.

  • Choose your weapons at the start of each round and unlock them by collecting loot. Rounds begin with Melee combat and progress through: ranged, heavy, explosive and special item categories. Strategically choose your load-out to defeat your foes!

  • Level-up your Hero and unlock over 30 new weapons including: Grenade Launcher, Sticky Bomb and Chicken. Earn over 30 heroic achievements to unlock stat buffing hats!

  • Controls support gamepads and mouse/keyboard with bindable keys and buttons.

Square Heroes is a multiplayer focused arena shooter from Australian Developer Gnomic Studios. In Square Heroes you create your owngenetically engineered killing machine that's only goal in life is to kill anything that moves, fight off aliens or even collect gnomes because gnomes are cool.

+Nice cartoony graphics
+Characters, both player and computer are well designed and somewhat remind me of BattleBlock's design
+Quite a few different maps, each in a different environment
+Quite a few different game modes ranging from Team Deathmatch to Gnome Hunt
+Lots of weapons to unlock as you level up
+Lots of character headwear to unlock as you complete achievements, each one has a different buff and debuff that goes with it
+Great AI with different difficulty settings
+Takes a fair few kills to unlock your weapons once in a match which means it's difficult for someone to get miles ahead of everyone else
+Super simple controls
+Bucket loads of fun when playing with friends
+No idea how they achieved this but even when players have a super high ping the gameplay is still super smooth and there's very little lag
+Nice music and sound effects
+Has great local co-op and you can still play online even with four local players
+Has a Achievement Hunter reference, as an AH fan this makes me very happy
+Awesome devs who are super friendly, very active in the forums and fix issues with the game very quickly

+/-The devs have focused on getting the multiplayer right and have neglected the singleplayer portion of the game, if you're looking for MP only then that's great but if you want some good SP too you might be disappointed

-You can't create private matches, kind of annoying if you're trying to play with five friends and a random player joins
-Apart from changing your character's colour and their headwear there's not much in the way of character customisation

Verdict:
8.5

A super fun multiplayer experience with nice cartoony graphics, well designed characters, lots of unlockables and great devs to back it all up. If you're looking for a fun multiplayer experience with friends I strongly suggest you picking this awesome game up!

At first I was a bit skeptic, but then I joined some friends and played a match with them, and then I played another match with them, and another, and another. Without any further delay, I present to you my review of Square Heroes.

Gameplay
The player gets to customise a character that will later be used as a personal weapon of mass destruction, or as a punching bag. Not only will that character fight with pride and honor, killing foes and moving up the ranks, it will also gain new weapons and abilities to allow the player to be even better at whacking said foes. Both graphics and soundtrack/sound effects are merely a drop in the ocean of awesomeness that Square Heroes is. I do, however, strongly advice playing it with friends. If you don't have any, I'm sure you can find some on google!
The more you play, the better you'll become, even if itâs only due to better weapons/abilities!

Pros
  • Easy to pick up;
  • Controls are as simple as they come;

  • Cartoonish graphics, which make smacking your friends a memorable thing;
  • The soundtrack is good and so are the sound effects (whack whack!);
  • Good variety of maps;
  • Very fun multiplayer.

Cons
  • Single player mode lacks some excitement;
  • Could use some more options to customise the character.

Verdict
I'd give it a 8/10 Really fun game to play with friends, you'll surely enjoy whacking their virtual pawns.

This game was reviewed using a code sent by the developer.

Square Heroes reminds me of a mash up between Awesomenauts and Worms. This fast paced side scrolling twin stick shooter grabs your attention right from the start. The cute art style is very well presented, and the music is oddly catchy.

Gameplay wise, you are a square with a jetpack, and you have to complete tasks depending on which game mode you are playing. These vary from team death match right the way up to Capture the gnome. My favourite has to be "Capture the Gnome"; it is essentially domination type game where you have to hold the gnome (flag) for a couple of seconds in order to capture it. This can lead to some pretty crazy scenes at the gnome point, more often than not; it results in someone blowing 3 or 4 other people up in order to save anyone at all capturing the gnome.

Even though the game has just come out, I was very easily able to find a game online and jump straight in. Even though I ended up missing the first five minutes of the match, this seems to be a very see-saw type game as you can soon catch up with everyone else.

Yet again, this is another game with added local co-op and is an excellent game to play when you just want to kick back for 15 minutes or so. I highly recommend playing Square Heroes with your friends on voice as it is an absolute blast.
