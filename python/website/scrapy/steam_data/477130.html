About This Game

Surge is on of the first real-time VR music video created for the new generation VR headsets and gives an amazing insight in the possibilities of VR as a medium for music videos. The seamless fusion between music and visual effects come to life in an overwhelming experience which is truly out of this world. You have to see it to believe it. Surge was created by 3D animator and musician Arjan van Meerten.

This is an amazing VR experience. It is short but it takes an important first step in making a music video actually work in VR. Now I have seen other music videos in VR like the Run the Jewels video which is part of Wevr Transport. Even though that video was also great it had a fixed camera and it felt more like watching a 360 video(which is basically what it was) and less like I was actually in that space. Surge is different in that it is made in the Unreal Engine and has positional tracking and the whole package which means you feel like you are actually there. Couple that with amazing set pieces which go along perfectly with the music and cram it into a short music video and you have Surge. Its free so I definitively recommend you try it out.
