30 June

MAJOR UPDATE V2.10 (Robot Puppy, Home, Whiteboard and more)

Version 2.10 is Live. Get it now! (If you have older version you can't play with players who has this new version, make sure you have this update)

Features and fixes;

* 'Companion' is added to menu. We have a robot puppy for everyone :) You can call him anytime with selecting companion icon. Click again to send him back. He is fun and interactive. We will advance his AI and give you some crazy options for your companion. Also later on we will have a lot of different companion options.

* 'Home' Location is added. This is our landing location from now on! You can still go any other location from menu. We will have some important changes on Home location later on.

* 'Whiteboard' Location is added. You can draw anything and save/embed your drawings to this location. This is like an open whiteboard for all users to share their creation. We will periodically clean up some existing drawings or open all new whiteboard.

* Palette options for brush. Now when you chose brush on other controller you will see a palette with some options. You can change brush type, save and load drawings. If you click save, everything you draw on that scene will be saved, which you can load anytime you want. Save option is location specific for now.

* You can change color and size of the brush with touchpad on controller.

* Improvements on location user count information.

* Improvements on brush.

* Some hidden secrets.


Next update, coming soon;

* 'Life' features for drawings
* New brush features
* Gender change
* User profile and related features
* Mobile support

I recommend this with caution: This metaverse thing is not for kids. There are predators on here that are looking to talk sex amongst other things so ,please parents, do not let your kids into this game. Lock it out !!! That being said I didn't really talk to anyone in this last round of visiting but the first time I went I was not please with the dialog that was spoken to me. This time around I checked out some of the lobbies. There was a club, a vacant hospital ward that was creepy, are cartoony city where you can literally shoot zombies from a gun. The same in the ward. And much to my delight there was a forest with some low poly dinosaurs but a good variety. In there you ride on a hover craft. Just try to go in one direction. If you try playing around with it you may get sick. There is voice support on here but you can mute that if you are just visiting or meeting specific people. Overall I would say this is a good meeting place for Steam friends before a MP or COOP match as you wait for other persons to get online OR if you are into actually meeting new people on here you can but at your own risk or liking if you are the preverted/risky type. I wish I didn't have to say all that but my conscience has to speak out. Get it and enjoy what the Metaverse has to offer. Ciao.

Pretty cool updates, optimizations and changes. This is great for people who love the Metaverse world.
