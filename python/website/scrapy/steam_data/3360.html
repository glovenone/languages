This game of Big Money doesn't look like a thinking mans game but it is. You have to get 3 or more of the same color close enough together to have all 3 touching so you can make them disappear but when you make those 3 disappear it makes the other colors surrounding come together so you can collect upon those colors to create great combos of as many colors or tokens as you muster up together. It has different game modes to entertain those that want a challenge and for those that just like to get rid of things in their lives and see progress in little portions. It is a great game for those people that like to think their way through complicated situations of decision making. It is also a great game for casual gamers looking to burn a couple minutes and money while playing.
