The video is finally here: https://youtu.be/fwTIULAVwrA

To say it fair and square:
  • Square Brawl has a great amount of content and variety for its price.
  • The gameplay is simple yet great fun, especially when you gather around some people to play with.
  • The particle effects look great and the music, while repetitive at times, fits the game pretty well.
  • The game runs at a solid 60 fps and has some nice options for things like fisheye, screen shake and Antialiasing.
  • Things like recoil on ranged weapons add a nice layer of complexity to the game.

However, there are also some negatives to be found:
  • (Probably just personal preference) No option to pause the game
  • No online Multiplayer
  • Really minor propblems with AI(getting stuck in corners at times)
  • Spawning at small maps was broken in the prerelease version(sometimes you spawn directly above pits with no way to recover) If that is the case in the release version, I don't really know.

All in all a fun game, that would be a definite buy if it had online multiplayer.
This way I can only fully recommend it if you get some people to play it with.

In this review, I am going to compare Square Brawl to Nidhogg. If you dont know what Nidhogg is, then look it up.

First off, like Nidhogg, Square Brawl (SB) has graphics that are very... square. Unlike Nidhogg, however, SB takes advantage of its art style, and somehow makes it look beautiful. That, combined with the screen shaking and other interesting effects, make the game very pleasing to the eye.

Also like Nidhogg, SB is an indie game. That means that only a few people are working on it, and it has a very limited budget. However, unlike Nidhogg, SB actually has a decent amount of content. In fact, it has way more content on release day than Nidhogg has now, almost 2 years after it was released.

So, all things considered, SB should cost at least 2-3x the amount of Nidhogg, right? Wrong. SB costs 1/3 of Nidhogg's price, and in my opinion, is way more enjoyable. If I had one gripe with this game, it would be that there needs to be more variation in weapons. I think that the best way to solve this issue would be to create more weapons, and keeping the current ones as well. I think perks would be intersting too.

Overall, I rate this game an 8.5/10
