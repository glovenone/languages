Posted: 27 June
! That ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ Trying To Steal Our Gold ! is a strategic platformer that requires pinpoint accuracy and precise movements in order to comlete a level. One could say that this is called a "Rage Platformer" such as the likes of other games such as Unfair Platformer. You play as a theif, who I have dubbed "Bob", and have a goal to steal all of the gold in the mine from the green creatures you see in the preview of the game, Leprechauns. The game scales in difficulty as it progresses. New "enemies" appear every few levels to provide a challenge and the precision aspect makes the game repetitive.

There are really only 3 types of enemies. The first is what I call a "virus ball"..... cause it looks like that thing from YuGiOh Kappa. The second is a homing ninja star. And finally we have the glorious leprechauns.

The main issue with this game is just the repetitiveness and blandness of the game. There are only 17 levels and you are only given a limited amount of dynamite in the level to work with. Yes, this does provide some strategy to the game, but overall it just takes away from the core gameplay. Often, the game would require you to do a trickshot with the dynamite and it would take multiple tries. Unless, you get everything perfect in a level, you could end up being one or two dynamite short and have to start the entire level over again.

There is little plot to this game as well (IKR PLOT, never would've guessed with this game). Basically there are like 2 cutscenes. One to introduce the leprichauns and why they are angry at you and the second for the end game. Not much to it.

Overall, the controls are actually not that bad. The first level explains them fairly well. It takes some time to get used to but it is easy once you are able to get it down. As of right now, I would not recommend getting this game. The amount of content the game has to offer, the rage it can provide, and the sheer blandness of the story/gameplay makes it not worth it.

I would recommend it, however if the game is under $1.00. This seems like a game that should be marketed at a $0.99 or lower price. That seems more than reasonable. The inition $3 or even the summer sale $1.19 is not worth it at all. So come back to this game when its price is lower.

Gameplay: 3/10 - Interesting concept but tons of repetition
Plot: 2/10 - Lackluster plot that gets introduced halfway through
Visuals: 1/10 - I thought my character was Bob the Builder - No joke

Overall: 2/10 - Wait for a sale.

Posted: 1 May
Pretty basic platformer / puzzler game, dime a dozen really. Best part for me was the nice, unobtrusive music, although it's a bit repetitive. Goal of the game is to steal gold (which looks like apples, like so many other has pointed out) and clear path with dynamite. Since you have only limited amount of dynamite and have to return to starting position after collecting all the gold, you can't blast everything in level. With some planning you'll be able to clear enough blocks to reach all the gold, while leaving still a path back to starting position.

Graphics is pretty usual, 3d with a little bit lighting thrown in the misc. Reminded me a lot of early 2000 platformers. Controls aren't as solid as they could be, but good enough that you can navigate around the levels without too many problems. Lack of time limit makes the game perfect for relaxed playstyle and I suspect it's easy enough to start the game and play couple of levels now and then. Pace is slow and new elements aren't introduced too quickly.

Not my favourite game, and I got bored really soon. A little bit polish and it could be decent.
