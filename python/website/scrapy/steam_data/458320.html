About This Series

The best players are not made overnight. Being the best of the best in CS:GO takes tremendous dedication and skill. This package contains a collection of interviews that will take you around the world and inside the lives of the CS:GO elite, offering an in depth look at the stories behind the players that are shaping the scene today.
