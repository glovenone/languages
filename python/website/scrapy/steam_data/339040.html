About This Game

Based on Walt Disney’s feature 3D animation film Chicken Little, Chicken Little the action-packed video game catapults you into the hair-raising adventures of the movie and beyond. Live the adventures of Chicken Little and his friends, including Fish out of Water, Abby Mallard and Runt of the Litter. It takes more than cool moves to complete each mission. Rocket jet packs, slingshots and catapults get you out of sticky situations like alien invasions, spaceship piloting and antigravity challenges. Saving the day takes an arsenal of gadgets, friends and hero power!

First off, I'm saying I reccomend this game because the game itself is fun, and brought me back to my child days where I played this nonstop on the PS2. However, the negative things about this game is that there are a few issues with it that are quite annoying.

Negatives:
-No Fullscreen mode, game is perma-bound to windowed.
-Only 4 pre-set resolutions with the max being 1280x1024.
-No Steam overlay.
-No graphics options.
-Mouse cursor stays in-game no matter what. I strongly reccomend using a controller to play if you want to avoid this.

Positives:
+Extremely fun game, especially for those that played it as a kid, like I did.
+Fully rebindable keyboard keys, (You can even use the mouse, which was uncommon for games as old as this.), and fully rebindable controller buttons.
+Nice sound design, all voices, sounds, and music are clear and crisp.
+Nice level design, very intricate and full of secrets!

All in all, I do reccomend this game. It gives you that nice, nostalgic feel. For this price, it's a really good deal, and you are sure to get hours of fun from it.
