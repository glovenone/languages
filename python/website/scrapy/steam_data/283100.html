9.11.2015

So I am playing this game now since more than a year. Which means I found some kind of motivation in it to stick with it.

For someone with low tier equipment, it will take a while.

- Still some base things are missing: No friendlists, the chat is kind off a mess, no trading. But those things will be changed.

The Pros

+ The devs stick with it and there is always someone answering and helping if there are any issues. So their support is superb.

+ The game is constantly improving. Some changes sometimes are too much, when it comes to balancing. So your best armor isn't that good a major update later, which can be annoying, but over all, the updates improved the game.

+ All the things I have mentioned below are now available. Skills (more coming), Crafting (best armor/weapons is only available through crafting), Quests (there are quests for every planet, they are kind of repetitive though, but they'll add more). Beyond that, you can level your character.

+ The whole game got more depth. Resistances and damagetypes are now playing a role. Farming and refining smart is important. Even basebuilding got more depth. There is alot of decorations available.

+ The game provides now some motivation and groupplay. You can go farm bionicores to augment your equipment by killing liars. As a soloplayer you can farm wandering families.


Alot has changed and I am looking forward to the future of this game.

What I expect in the future:
More groupplay stuff (events?), Enclaves (Guilds), more monsters, more skills, character customizations and vehicles.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

I also knew that there are only coremechanics available and most of the features are in developement. That's why it is tagged as early access.
If you expect a finished and flawless game, you probably shouldn't look for early access games.
If you like to see games grow in developement, this game could be for you.

What you get, if you buy these packages:
