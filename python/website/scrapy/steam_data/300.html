About This Game

Day of Defeat offers intense online action gameplay set in Europe during WWII. Assume the role of infantry, sniper or machine-gunner classes, and more. DoD:S features enhanced graphics and sounds design to leverage the power of Source, Valve's new engine technology.

Have never played the original Day of Defeat so I can't compare it to this.

I received Day of Defeat: Source through the Source Multiplayer bundle that came with Counter-Strike: Source, Day of Defeat: Source and Half-Life: Deathmatch back in early 2011.

DoD:S is a really fun game I would recommend to anyone who likes WWII games and also likes First Person Shooters that require teamwork.

In DoD:S you either play as the Allied Forces (U.S. Army) or the Axis (Wermacht) and each side has the same classes but with different weapons and equipment.

Only downside is that because the game is old there is a lack of active servers but the game is still fun.
