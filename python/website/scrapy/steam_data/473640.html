About This Game

Wyatt Derp is a brand new action game with inspiration from old school games like gun smoke and blood brothers. You play Wyatt Derp a ruthless lawman out for justice! Journey through the wild west blasting through towns and shooting holes into every criminal around. Collect gold from the enemies you fill full of lead to purchase upgrades, shotgun shells, dynamite, and more! Enjoy fast paced action in a awesome western themed shootem up that will keep you playing for hours.

Some Of The Great Features You Will Enjoy:
Upgrade shop that will improve a variety of aspects for Wyatt Derp
Dodge bullets by anticipating shots or tumbling
Awesome western environments
Tons of badguys
Bonus Rounds to collect extra rewards
Different special attacks like shot gun blasts and dynamite
And More!
