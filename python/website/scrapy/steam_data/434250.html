About This Game

Suit up, boys! The battleground is open and danger has never been more real. Enemies lurk at every turn, their trigger fingers just itching for the opportunity to take you out. Once the mask drops, be ready to pull the trigger.
Masked Shooters 2 puts you on the front line of a dangerous gunfight that can only end in victory or defeat; there will be no white flags to wave. As a covert ops soldier, you have access to advanced weapons that will blow your enemies away, if you know how to use them. From a pistol and sniper rifle to a grenade launcher and RPG, your arsenal is packed for a maximum kill rate, but it won’t be easy. Enemies have their own weaponry and your ammo is limited, so make every bullet count.

From detailed graphics to life-like sound, this first person shooter game has everything you need if you enjoy adrenaline pumping, fast-paced gameplay. Choose to join a team or show off your sniping skills in free for all mode, where it’s you against the world. You can play offline against pitiless AI soldiers or join a room online to play against others in real time for a true test of your skill. There’s only one answer in Masked Shooters: it’s time to lock and load.

~~~~~ Features ~~~~~

• Action packed gameplay
• Realistic shooting mechanics
• Multiple weapons: Sniper, pistol, knife, grenade launcher, RPG, machine gun, ak47, grenades
• Play offline or online
• Brutal AI enemies for challenging offline play

I don't see the point.

The developer (who i assume is just one person, it certainly seems that way) greenlit and is now selling a fps multiplayer shooter in 2016 with nothing that wasn't being done in 2005. You get two identical maps, which are very generic, and one brown map that is supposed to resemble a Roman fighting arena with teleport pods because...? Total of 3 maps, and they all are crap.

Multiplayer will be dead soon, if it isn't already. At this point, it'll be just another amateur hour dead MP only game on the steam store. Of hundreds already.

All the weapons here are in every other game, only they control and behave worse than usual. The grenades are hilariously awful.

The sniper rifle is a one shot, one kill so that's basically the only weapon worth using. When enemies die, their rag doll has a massive fit 50 percent of the time, where it twitches or flies all over the place for a few seconds. Weird, nonsense physics. Players can abuse the jump to make themselves harder to hit, but it really isn't that effective. But you can expect to see a lot of people just jumping around with rocket launchers, if you do encounter a match. People aren't matched based on their location, its a free for all with whatever lobby you wish to join.

This is generic as generic first person multiplayer shooters go.

When asked why the game has no volume options, sliders, or mute for certain types, the developer responded with this.
"I didn't add one, you can open mixer volume from a windows tray icon (lower right of your desktop) while you have the game opened (minimized), and there lower the sound for masked shooters 2"

Lazy.

Thanks greenlight. This developer is already trying to get more lazy half arsed crap greenlit and probably will because the same people who greenlit this and ultimately forgot all about it, will do the same thing. They never actually buy these games.

Don't get me started on why this is an apparent sequel to a game nobody heard of, or cares about. Its not like you can play the first one, anyway. It was probably an itch.io failed project.
