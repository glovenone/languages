About This Game

The widely popular Pro Evolution Soccer 2016 now is available as a Free to Play edition!
Play the online mode myClub, offline Exhibition Match and Training mode as free to play content.

*To play other modes on Pro Evolution Soccer 2016, you are required to purchase Pro Evolution Soccer 2016 separately.
*Online matching against Pro Evolution Soccer 2016 is available in this title.
*Save data cannot be transferred from other titles.
