Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“Force of Elements is in beta, and we need your feedback to make it better!”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“Approximately six months, depending on feedback from the community.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“The full version will contain:

  • Twelve heroes
  • An in-depth crafting system.
  • Expanded hero levelling.
  • Flexible character customization.
  • Randomized Loot.
  • Monster battles.
  • Timed seasons based around the primal elements.
  • Live tournaments.
”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The current version contains:
  • Six playable heroes (with two more following shortly after release).
  • A ranked progression system.
  • Friend challenges.
  • Hero trials.
  • Achievements.
  • Quests.
”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“The game will be priced according to user feedback. The price may or may not change, depending on whether or not the community feels they are getting good value for the content provided.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We need community feedback! The plan is to use player input to tune and adjust game balance and mechanics.”

About This Game

Force of Elements is the game of head-to-head combat where your field of battle is a match 3 gem board. Choose a unique hero, swap gems to unlock elemental mana and annihilate your opponent in fast-paced battles that will leave you hungry for one more round.

Less candy, More CRUSH.

Force of Elements is designed from the ground up for competitive gaming with diverse character-based abilities that make interacting with the gem board a tactical experience unlike any match 3 game you’ve played before. Anyone can learn to play in minutes. Mastering your favorite heroes and outwitting opponents in real-time battles will keep you playing and streaming for years to come.

Key Features:

  • Two ways to play; rapid-fire TIMED mode and tactical TURNS mode
  • Customize your play style with powerful AURAS
  • NEW HEROES released regularly
  • RANKED play and TOURNAMENTS - COMING SOON

Unleash the POWER of the ELEMENTS!

In the realm of Acharos, power derives from the five elements. Each Hero has an affinity with their chosen element, but skillful players can tap into all five by creating power gems.

  • Fire: Incinerate your enemies with primordial energy. Fire is the element of chaos and destruction.
  • Air: Masters of Air can manipulate time itself, extending their turns for bigger combos. Knowledge and control are attributes of Air.
  • Water: The most protean element, Water is all about transformation. Water heroes manipulate the gem board to their advantage.
  • Earth: Call on the forces of nature to outlast your opponent. Earth abilities emphasize strength and renewal.
  • Void: The essence of emptiness, Void is a dangerous element to embrace. Void abilities allow heroes to curse, cripple and drain their opponents…often at a price.

System Requirements

    Minimum:
    • OS: Windows 7/8/10
    • Processor: 2 GHz
    • Memory: 1 GB RAM
    • Network: Broadband Internet connection
    • Storage: 150 MB available space

DISCLAIMER: There is one very important aspect of this game you need to be aware of right off the bat. It's buggy as hell. There's been at least a dozen matches that I've played where everything just stops working and you have to alt+F4 out of the game (conceding doesn't even work). This is EXTREMELY annoying and I really hope they fix this soon. That said, let's continue with the review.

This game is cool if you like Puzzle Quest and want a competitive mode that's at least somewhat active.

The F2P is mostly fair, although the system of upgrading auras is sketchy and forces you to stick to a particular character if you want to have a real edge in combat. In the case of this game, I wouldn't recommend spending any money on the gem packs unless you really want a specific character. So a F2P where playing for free is actually viable! That is really cool.

There is a ranking system, but it doesn't mean much. You can get to Diamond in a day, and that's where you'll meet the players who play regularly. But of course as anyone who has played Puzzle Quest knows, there isn't much of a skill aspect to the game, it's almost entirely luck and who can match gems the fastest. So don't expect this to be streamed on Twitch anytime soon or get MLG status. That said it's a fun little game and does have some interesting mechanics.

Early Access Review
Posted: 30 December, 2015
So far, I have to say that I am incredibly impressed.

I waited anxiously for the game to release, and I was happy to see all the positive reviews. I'm also happy to add my own extremely positive review to the game.

I initially started playing as Ki, due in part to the skin and the fact that she was the first character I got to play as. Started messing around with the 5 turn games, and got my ♥♥♥ handed to me by everyone else. I thought that it surely had to be the character. Unlocked 1 or 2 other characters, and tried them as well. Still got my ♥♥♥ handed to me. Then, I decided to try the Trials. Went back to Ki.

First 4 fights were easy, and then the last 1 took me 3 or 4 tries. And that's when the magic happened. Instead of actively trying to make random matches, I started playing to the strengths of Ki's abilities. And lo and behold: it worked!

Unlocked a few auras, messed around a bit, got 1 I liked up to level 2, and practiced a bit more in Trial mode and that 5 turn mode. What a blast! Moved on to Ranked matches, and managed to get up to Silver Rank 1, with a win streak of about 7 or 8 matches, I think.

With the initial 500 Gem allotment, and the fact that the game is liberal in giving you these Gems for victories, and even a few for defeats, unlocking the Auras and Skins doesn't require godlike skill, just some perseverance. And perseverance is always made easier if the game is fun, which it sure as hell is! I've seen no bugs or crashes, and the artwork in the game is beautiful on all characters.

A special mention to the community so far, as well. Every opponent I've played against so far, has been gracious in defeat, and humble in victory.

The game has a solid base for what it is, and can easily be transformed into a massively engrossing affair. An absolute steal at the current price, and worth it if you enjoy the Match-3 gameplay style. Wicked Fun: you really live up to your name with this one so far! Keep it up!
