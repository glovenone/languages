About This Game

Get behind the wheel of a super powerful jet vehicle and blast off!
Try your abilities in an incredibly difficult and extreme simulator.

We challenge you to tame your jet monster!
Only the true aces will be able to reach the finish line, others will be disintegrated! Remember, this is neither an arcade or realistic driving game, this is an extreme simulator!

Early Access Review
Posted: 6 July
Product received for free
I received this game for free and was actually pretty excited to play it, but was phenomenally disappointed.

As I was playing this game, I noticed that the vehicles were next to impossible to control and would fly off the track if you went at even the slightest of turns (you know those bottle rockets you would make as a kid out of old soda bottles that would jet off wildly into the air? It felt like trying to pilot one of those). I thought it might just be an isolated problem that just I was having because I didn't have any upgrades, but as I would upgrade my jet I noticed that it still was completely uncontrollable. I then looked to the Internet to see that it wasn't just my problem, it seems that just how the game was designed. Absolutely horrendous and makes the game near impossible to play.

If having a driving/racing game where you CAN'T EVEN STEER THE VEHICLE wasn't a deal breaker enough:

The maps are very poor, bland and look nearly identical to one another. Notice how all of the in-game screen shots are in the desert. You know why? Because seemingly all of the maps take place in the desert. Which map do you choose: racetrack in the desert 1, racetrack in the desert 2, or racetrack in the desert 3 (this time with a jump)? Boring. The developers put in basic turns and things combined with the occasional jump/bridge and such in a failed attempt to make the tracks interesting, but it doesn't matter because actually trying to use these things will make you crash.

Combine all of that with a horrible soundtrack, bad controls, bizarre interface, near-nonexistent jet customization, mediocre at best graphics and the fact that the developer has seemingly abandoned this project in its Early Access phase and you get a very lackluster experience. I would not recommend that you pick this title up, even on sale. Take it at face value: not even the developer really even seems to care about it. That's saying something.

Early Access Review
Posted: 16 July, 2015
Jet Racing Extreme is a racing game where you get behind the wheel of a jet powered car and race around one of (currently) four tracks. There's not much else to say about the game itself so I'll crack on with the rest of my review.

+A fair amount of customisation is available for your car, you can change the paint colour which is pretty basic but you can also upgrade the components of your car as well as fine tune them to create the best vehicle possible
+The car's damage models are good and the car breaks apart nicely upon impacts, smashing into things is a big part of the game so this is rather important
+Decent sound effects, the car noises are reasonably realistic in particular

-Keyboard controls are, as expected of a racing game, bad and despite the dev claiming the game is best played with a gamepad the gamepad controls are quite possibly worse with terribly thought out bindings and no way to change the bindings for the gamepad (keyboard bindings can be changed)
-There are currently only four maps available, two of which are just a short section of one of the other maps
-The camera occasionally swaps angle randomly and most of the camera angles in the game are completely useless for a fast paced racing game - Seemingly this has now been fixed
-The driving and general physics aren't even slightly realistic despite the claims on the store page and in the forums
-None of the UI is explained at all making it almost completely pointless
-The point system doesn't really work properly, I sometimes got given stunt points for crashing into objects and spinning uncontrollably which doesn't really make sense
-A few grammatical and spelling errors, obviously not everyone speaks perfect English but it can't be too hard to get someone to proof-read
-Despite not being a mobile port (the devs are planning to port it to mobile however) it does very much feel like a game that belongs on a smartphone or tablet rather than a PC or laptop
-The mouse cursor always stays visible, one of my major pet peeves in games
-You can't reverse so if you crash into a wall and can't turn out of it you're forced to restart
-You can't pause the game! Another of my major pet peeves, seriously it's 2015 and we can't pause our games?
-As you can see from the below comment section, the dev really has no idea how to communicate with the Steam community, telling me, among other things, that I'm wrong because I disagree with him

Verdict:
Avoid it like the Plague (2/10)

Jet Racing Extreme is a poor mobile port that fails at what it sets out to achieve, with poor controls, sub-par graphics and a fair few other issues this game is neither worth your time nor money.

No drama, Just Reviews.
