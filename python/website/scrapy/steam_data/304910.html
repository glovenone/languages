About This Game

THIS IS THE ULTIMATE WWII AIR COMBAT GAME!



You’ll participate in visceral dogfights, protect cities and fleets, bomb enemy structures and even fly through tunnels. Everything is set up in accurately recreated real life locations like Pearl Harbor, Coast of Dover, Midway, Germany and France.

Immersive Gameplay
Take off and land on landing strips and carriers. You will fly in fully recreated cities with realistic weather, accurate atmospheric scattering with aerial perspective and highly detailed planes with interactive cockpits.

Huge historic Fleet
Fly highly detailed WWII top aces planes. Watch the progressive damage affecting planes and finally being shredded into pieces.

Huge Collection Of Cross-platform Online Modes
Invite your friends to play online! Choose between cooperative and competitive modes: Survival, Last Man Standing, Free Flight, Free for All, Team Match, Capture the Flag and Defend the base. Play with your friends regardless of the platform (available on iOS, Android, Mac, Windows, Linux).

Features
  • 3 singleplayer campaigns with a total of 17 missions, recreating historical events from WW2.
  • 5 tutorials explaining how to play the game
  • online internet multiplayer and local LAN multiplayer
  • 29 planes each with 3 different stat versions (meaning 87 planes, all included in the game, no DLC)
  • 8 different play modes, accessible either in multiplayer or singleplayer (against AI bots)
  • endless mode (survival) against the AI bots, alone or with your friends
  • exploration mode with no enemies
  • customizable plane weapons (10 weapons plus acrobatic smoke)
  • customizable plane paint job
  • support for gamepads and joysticks (simple or HOTAS setup)
  • fully customizable controls

My eyes might be bad, but this game is still fun. When I was in school, one of my favorite games for the PS2 was Secret Weapons over Normandy. This game feels similar with a bit more contol, which is a good thing. There are lots of planes to unlock, ranging from early WWII fighters to jets that came in toward the end of the war. Different campaigns have you flying planes from both Allied and Axis sides. Combat is fast and fun. The music is pretty decent.

If you're a person who needs every single aspect of flight rendered in perfect scientific detail, this game might not be for you, but if you're like me and you love WWII planes, this game is a great investment for only 10 USD. If you get it when it's on sale, even better!

Cons to be aware of:
No voice acting. All mission prompts are via text on screen. My vision sucks so I missed some.
Several planes, but it feels like more could have been added. 99 cent DLCs with 5-8 planes would be nice.
Music can get a bit repetitive, but you don't notice if you're fighting.
I can't figure out how to lower landing gear.

Sky Gamblers: Storm Raiders
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

First of all, I must say that I am not a fan of flight simulation at all. And there is only one reason for it, too hard to learn and play.

Saying this, I was quiet intrigued with this game just because it looks fun. Let me tell you I was spot on. You do not have to remember how to start an engine and all the rest. All you have to do is to push up the throttle and you get to the fight within a minute and shoot planes. “RIGHT NOW AT 3 O’CLOCK!”

All fighter planes from the era are well represented in this game. Graphically, this game could have been better but you can’t have everything. To be honest they are decent.

For the controls side of it, I think using the mouse and keyboard is about as weird as it sounds. It will take you a little time to master both setups anyway.

You can play as a single player and have 3 campaigns at your disposal (Pacific, Africa and Battle of Britain). You also have a survival, capture the flag, dogfight, team deathmatch and defend the base which can be played against the AI. Multiplayer mode is also available.

Positives:
~~~~~~~~
- Challenging and really fun to play
- Plenty of modes including multiplayer
- Runs smoothly
- Stack of achievements
- Trading cards

Negatives:
~~~~~~~~
- Controls slightly sluggish

Overall, this game is worth $9.99 and I really enjoyed it!

8/10
