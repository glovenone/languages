About This Game

Zombies on a Plane is an exciting new survival game in the sky. Take on the role of the captain of a troubled jumbo jet carrying infected passengers intent on bringing the plane out of the sky, or pit yourself against hundreds of zombies in the interior of a jumbo jet or even walk along the outside as you fight to regain control as one of the passengers in this innovative and interactive gaming experience like no other.

Use the plane as a weapon or shoot your way through the aisles as one of the last surviving passengers as you to fight to stay in the air against hordes of zombies at 30,000ft. As captain in control, players must maintain the health of vital key parts, such as the engines and rudder, by knocking the zombies off the plane. As a passenger, players use an assortment of weaponry and traps to halt the zombies' advance and can manually repair parts to keep the plane in the air.

Key Features

  • A Multitude of Modes - 6 different game modes to test your survival or flying skills, including modes like 'Defend the Cockpit' and 'Flight of 10 Waves'

  • High Replayability - 5 different characters and 3 planes to choose from, all with their own abilities and playstyles

  • Smart Gore System - Severe the limbs off zombies to slow them down and watch as they react

  • Zombie Variety - A multitude of intelligent zombie types to survive against, all with different playstyles

  • Unique Blend of Weaponry - 10 different weapons to unlock, including zombie shredding traps, with smart score-based weapon progression system

  • Rewards - Innovative combo reward system keeps the game fresh by rewarding players with large combos an array of exciting game changers

  • Manage the Plane - Repair parts to keep the plane in the air and keep doors sealed to prevent new spawn points opening up

  • Never-before-seen gameplay - Battle for control on top of a flying jumbo jet or knock zombies off the plane and watch them fall with glee as they realistically react to plane movement

  • High Visual Fidelity - Beautiful graphics powered by Unreal Engine 3 provide stunning and destructible game environments

  • Supports Steamworks features

First of all, I’d like to list following issues I’ve met playing this game for an hour (equally in 3 modes: shoot zombies in plane, shoot zombies on plane and control plane)
- game crashed for me 6 times, twice in each mode (even when I completed 10 waves survival in flight mode, game froze at 0 zombies left, then game crashed) Game tends to freeze/crash either when you complete all waves, or when 7-10 mins of game passes in no-wave mode. Of course, after crash all progress is lost, not steam achievements, though
- game can't detect that I own Helicopter DLC like half of times I tried to use it in flight mode, so I had to restart game until I managed to use it
- game granted me achievement of 50x combo when I did 25x, not a big issue, but still
- game doesn't seem to be really good optimized, sometimes I have fps drops when like 3 zombies near me, but when there are like 20 - it's ok.
- you will ALWAYS be attacked by some zombie, when everything seems fine, and you won't be able to see where is he, but surprise, look directly under your feet - there's crawling zombie! yay?
- you'll have to find proper camera mode in flight mode before you can actually play it (3rd looked ok for me, that was the only reason I couldn't survive 10 waves earlier)
- getting a trap power up while you shoot-off zombies is REALLY annoying, like you shoot incoming crowd, then suddenly your weapon disappears and you have a trap in your hands to use (also I couldn't figure if it's any useful)
- defending and repairing passenger doors makes no sense, I stopped doing it at wave 7, went straight to cockpit door to defend, it didn't make game any harder, maybe even easier, since I didn't have to run anymore

Now to the good part:
+ game idea is actually interesting
+ different modes
+ game process is addicting
+ views in flight mode are awesome (especially thunderstorm!)
+ nice graphics and sound

Suggestions:
1. Please fix freezing/crashing and optimize game (at least a little bit)
2. Consider adding co-op after fixing game issues itself (would be pretty cool 3-man coop, (1) shoots off zombies inside of plane, (2) shoots zombies on plane, (3) controls plane)
3. Crawling zombie should be a bit further from character's legs, now it looks like you simply stand on him
4. Make trap power-up just be another weapon, so you can switch to it whenever you want
5. Consider possible relation between passenger doors and amount of zombies in wave for (10% per door might be ok, like if you have all doors - 50 zombies in wave, 5 doors destroyed - 75 zombies in the same wave)
6. Consider adding more heavy zombies in late waves, now they start to appear even in waves 2-3, but wave 10 still has majority of weak zombies

Recommending this, because it actually seems to be a nice game, and if developers manage to fix at least crash issue - it would be pretty nice.

Full Site Review (Click for more detail)

It pains me to not recommend a game I WANTED to recommend. This should have been stupid-fun. This should have been mindless zombie splattering. This should have been comical. Newcomer developer Shangi-La Game Studios has worked hard to improve on the prerelease copy I tested for 2 weeks. Even with this update, there is still a lot to be desired but the game HAS improved.

Enemies AI and abilities are flat out ludicrous. Animations are horrible. Its premise and enclosed zombie action SHOULD have made it a fantastic little indie shooter in the same vein as Dead Effect. Which is a far from perfect mobile port, but entertaining. Thanks to the release day update, this game is more so. Flight mode maps to 360 controllers well, and on screen prompts will have you shaking off waves of zombies into the sea in no time. Survive the Horde is enjoyable, and the challenges for the wave FPS modes are more manageable. There is still much room for improvement. But at a budget price this game could fill a niche for zombie FPS action junkies. Currently this game is a hard sell for with serious gameplay mechanic flaws.

PROs
- Survive the Horde mode is decent.
- Decent use of particle effects at times.
- Flight modes offer a nice change of pace from mindless shooting.
- Wave challenges are manageable, and offer a decent challenge.

CONs
- Animations are terrible
- Wave modes are still cumbersome, and frustrating. Can only play in short bursts.
- Following the update the game was slowed down, now it might be too slow.
- Highly illogical game. Not enough fun for replayability.
