About This Game

Nancy Drew®: Legend of the Crystal Skull is a first-person perspective, point-and-click adventure game. The player is Nancy Drew and has to solve a mystery. Explore rich environments for clues, interrogate suspects, and solve puzzles and mini-games.

Bruno Bolet was the proud owner of the “Whisperer,” a crystal skull rumored to protect its holder from almost any cause of death — except murder. When Bruno passed away, his nephew Henry came to wrap up his affairs, but he couldn't find the skull among the clutter of the creepy Bolet manor. You'll need to team up with Nancy's best friend Bess Marvin to find this mystical artifact before it falls into the wrong hands! Dare to Play

  • Play as Nancy and Bess Marvin
  • Outwit Bruno's pet iguana, alligator, and spider
  • Infiltrate a secret society, unlock crypts, and collect glass eyeballs.
  • Play arcade-style games — bowl, blast angry wasps, and dodge chomping teeth.
  • Play Junior or Senior detective level and erase mistakes without starting over

♥ So creepy!

I wasn't expecting how creepy the game would be until I hit the opening scene. This game, at least for me, is a lot more scary than the others despite it being one of the older titles. It deals with voodoo, rainy weather, exploring in a grave yard, etc. A bit too spooky for me!

♥ There's an iguana.

Gator. Tarantula too. Since I have "exotic" animals myself (a bearded dragon, pumpkin patch tarantula, and crested geckos) I really liked seeing the little guys in the game! The gator did make me jump since it snaps at you.. ;;

♥ Filled with puzzles!

Like all the Nandy Drew games, it's filled with mystery, puzzles, and interesting characters. I don't think I'll be finishing this, the dummy in the attic was too much for me but I do want to know who did it...! It made me guess and guess and guess again.

✘ Steam overlay...

It may just be glitched for me but Steam overlay never worked when I played the game. I either had to play it windowed (too small to properly see anything) or have it in full screen and was unable to reply to messages. Not a huge deal, just was a bit of a pain >.<;;

✘ The eyes.

They made me feel a bit watched, everywhere I went, a feeling that I found I couldn't shake. Eeek!

✘ If you want to play a Nancy Drew

that focuses less on horror and more on mystery, I would pick a different title before playing this one. I think they did a wonderful job with the game, this isn't quite a "con", however it IS something to keep in mind...especially if you're thinking of getting this for someone younger.

Overall if you're older or/and looking for a creepy game to play, Crystal Skull would be perfect.
