About This Game

Color Assembler is an abstract color mixing puzzle game. Connect the starting colors to various shapes that manipulate colors in their unique way. The goal is to create the target color.

Each of the 9 shapes manipulates colors differently. It's up to you to find out how and use them to achieve the solution.

Put your combination skills through a test of 84 levels, none two of which are solved the same way, while your combinations create a colorful nebula in the background. In order to not disturb you from the experience, the game contains absolutely no text.

Because color channels are also represented by shapes, Color Assembler is fully accessible to colorblind people.

DISCLAIMER: Most of my in-game time was spent a non-Steam version, thus my low hours listed here on Steam.

Color Assembler, developed by Purple Nebula, is a brilliant atmospheric puzzle game that will both calm your senses and sharpen your brain.

As seen in the trailer here on Steam, the gameplay consists of linking together mutliple colored spheres through one of 8 different filter shapes, which all influence the source colors differently. The goal is to match the color of the target shape on the far right side of the screen. What starts as a simple exercise quickly becomes a true mind bender as the player needs to eventually start linking color spheres to multiple filter shapes to arrive at the correct shade.

Although the challenge ramps up quickly, the atmosphere of the game helps the experience to remain a mostly relaxing one. The design and execution are fantastic, and anyone up for an atmospheric yet challenging puzzle experience will feel right at home.
