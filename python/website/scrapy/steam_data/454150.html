Why Early Access?

“Was established with a Game ready models now early access and for all of the models we chose early access because for a long time, the process of change.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“Come out of the game early access 2 months will average over a period of time such as.”

“• New Weapons

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

Warbit new category of popular war games, the first series of the game of top level game graphics and equipped with new game map. Warbit game, you are given more than one using the best equipment you will encounter on the map where you are shooting all enemies you have to disappear.
