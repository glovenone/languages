15 June

Disc Jam @ E3 and PS4 Officially Announced!

And… we’re back! First off, we’d like to apologize for being absent in the community for the past few months. During that time, we’ve been hard at work building new features, polishing existing features, and focusing on biz-dev.

Today, that work pays off and we’re happy to announce that Disc Jam is officially coming to PlayStation 4! While Steam remains a priority for us, Disc Jam will launch simultaneously on both platforms and we’re excited to extend the Disc Jam community to PC and console gamers alike. Disc Jam has always played best with a controller, so bringing it to PlayStation is a natural fit. Check out how far the game has come in our official announce trailer.

If you’re going to be at E3, come check out Disc Jam in person! We’re going to be hosting a playable demo at the MIX event tonight at LA Live.

Lastly, to everyone that has helped us test the game so far: THANK YOU! We know that many of you have been asking for more playtests; rest assured, they are just around the corner. Shortly after E3, we will be posting an update detailing all of the new features as well as a new playtest schedule. Stay tuned!

Patch Notes
We're very excited to share the BIG changes that went into this build! Our top feature request on our roadmap was adding our next character so we're happy to announce that Makenna has joined the fun! Small but speedy, Makenna covers a lot of ground and can be a crippling defensive player. Another massive change is the addition of SKILL CURVES! You can now use the left stick during windup to add spin to your throw. Check out our blog post for more details and see below for the full list of added features:

  • NEW: Added Makenna to the roster
  • NEW: SKILL CURVES added! New throw mechanic for adding spin to the disc during windup
  • NEW: Disc physics overhauled to enable disc skipping and spin-based ricochet
  • NEW: Added end game screen with rematch flow to get players back in game faster
  • NEW: 32-bit Windows is now supported
  • IMPROVED: All new full body throw animations
  • IMPROVED: Throw curvature is now indicated by tilt angle
  • IMPROVED: Full UI reskin
  • FIXED: Lobby countdown not resetting
  • TWEAKED: Power throws are now harder to track and should be more effective
  • TWEAKED: RT/LT curve throws updated to be "quick curves" with faster release time than all other throws

Want to watch and live chat with us?
We will be discussing our changes live on our twitch channel from 2pm to 4pm PST

What do I do next?
In the meantime, make sure you've redeemed your Steam key and downloaded the game client. If you haven't yet received your key and you're unable to get one from http://discjamgame.com, please check out this post.

How do I play?
Check out our 'How To Jam' page for a description of the rules, the controls, and some tips on how to jam like a pro. Once you're playing, you can also press Start or ESC to open the in-game menu and get a refresher.

Can I use a controller?
Yes! In fact, we recommend using a Microsoft Xbox 360 or Microsoft Xbox One controller for the best experience. If you don't have one, no worries, you can still play using a traditional keyboard.

Can my friends play?
Of course! If your friends want to play and they haven't signed up yet, send them over to http://discjamgame.com. They'll receive a Steam key as soon as they sign up and verify their email address.

Can I stream this or post videos?
Definitely! Everyone is encouraged to stream the game and upload footage wherever they wish. We're excited to share the game with the world, build a community, and get everyone involved in helping us make Disc Jam the best game it can be.

About This Game

DISC JAM

A fast-paced action-sports game that pits 2-4 players against each other in a unique combination of air hockey and tennis. Players scramble to retrieve and throw a glowing disc while unleashing devastating abilities and defending variable-point end zones. An exciting blend of strategy and skill, this arcade-inspired title boasts a zany roster of characters to compete with friends both on the couch and online.

FEATURES

  • Fast-paced arcade-inspired gameplay
  • Proprietary netcode designed to deliver fluid and responsive gameplay online
  • Support for splitscreen and couch play
  • A zany roster of characters, each with different styles and abilities
  • Invite your friends to a party and play against other teams online

ABOUT HIGH HORSE ENTERTAINMENT

High Horse Entertainment is an independent studio formed by two industry veterans with a combined 17 years of AAA development experience. Building on a strong history of online game development, High Horse plans on launching a line of fluid, robust, and accessible multiplayer titles.

System Requirements

    Minimum:
    • OS: Windows 7
    • Processor: Intel Core i3-4160 @ 3.60GHz
    • Memory: 4 GB RAM
    • Graphics: NVIDIA® GeForce® GTX 480, GTX 570, GTX 670, or better
    • DirectX: Version 11
    • Sound Card: DirectX compatible soundcard or onboard chipset
