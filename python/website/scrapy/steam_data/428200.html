What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“Attack Heroes has been in development for over 16 months and we invited some players to experience our game during the development. They inspired us a lot. Now, the game enters new stage, we want to gather more feedback from different regions. It will helps us to understand the regional difference, and make the game more fun.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“Attack Heroes is scheduled to come out of Early Access by Summer 2016. But the development is always variable. The feedback would bring us new ideas, we never think our game is perfect.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“Planned features:
1. More story
2. The Chinese voice
3. Stage 9 and 10
4. Boss Dong Zhuo
5. Improved UI”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“Attack Heroes is fully playable now for 8 hours gameplay. Including :
1. 13 scenes.
2. 32 animation clips of scene
3. 6 animations of story
4. 39 levels
5. 17 boss
6. 13 heroes.
7. Stage 1-8”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“There is discount in Early Access, and no discount when the game come out of early access.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We have created a QQ Group for Early Access to get touch with our gamers.The dev team will be active and available in the Steam forums.We really want more and more players to join us to make the game to be as polished and user friendly as it can be.”

Synopsis:
Attack Heroes is a funny-hot blood indie game which requires your abilities to finish all the challenging stages. The Story is based on The Three Kingdoms, telling about the adventure of Xiaomu, a guy with mysterious antecedents. Implicated by LiuBei, he and his fellow started a journey escaping from the capturing of officals. There's not only fun on this journey, but showing the loyalty of these guys. Gradually, they found the answer about the huge secret why the world became more and more strange...

Takes the 'operable acting animes' as a core, we use the battle style of Japanese manga and combine many manga-acting techs with lens-using techs, for showing the strong feeling of skills' actions. In music, we create 8 different songs originally by LuoTianyi ,making you battling with particular rhythms, like enjoying animes' openings.

The battle mode is easy but strategic. It requires your reflex and switching strategy because the heroes will come on stage one by one as a tight loop. There're 52 special skills for you to create your own combo style. Never forget, the fierce, fascinating, blooded, tricky Boss on each stage is waiting for you as a hungry wolf.

main features:
1. 13 heroes with characteristics is now for you to own.
2. Countless stages and over 20 special Bosses' battles are ready for you.
3. Every hero has 4 unique skills. You should deal with different situations on the basis of different skills' CDs.
4. Every hero has his own ultimate skill. You can fill your energy to use it during the battle.
5. Defeat enemies by your continuous combo, which needs your strategic hero-combination.
6. The good using of anime-lens and story connections.

Outstanding game! This truelly is a gem for all who love fast pase, thinking 2 steps ahead, cute cartoony character beat em up games... BUT! This game have a slight twist then the normal beat em up game...

There are no direct control of your hero's movement, they taking turns jump into then fight then withdraw when the next hero enter the battle, and the control have been split for both left and right hand...

Left hand WASD = Controlls the roaster of heroes in your team, game will auto rotate your heroes if no extra command was given, but you can use WASD keys to force select a hero from your roaster so you can unleash one of his skill that is not in cooldown.

Right hand IHJK = Controlls the 4 skills of each hero. J is the basic attack, that every heroes start with at lv1. K is advanced tier attack, and H is higher tier attack, Heroes unlock this 2 at higher level. I is ultimate attack, and also the last skill to be unlocked, they are not just dealing large amount of damage, they also laster way longer then any other attacks, and the lasting effect extend into 1-2 more heroes's turn.

The goal of this game is to plan and form combos with the attacks of your heroes, and use those attack to ether stunlock or push back waves of enemy, but enemy do have special ability or will be located at areas that some attacks just won't be able to touch them, so it will be up to the player to form up a team of heroes to bring down those annony buggers.

This game have a really fast gaming pace, really nice graphic for an indie game, funky cartoony design... It is fun to play, and even the music make me want to record it for my MP3 player!!


Okie.. I forgot to mention the down side... Due to this is a Chinese indie game, so far only some of the dialogues (most of them) and menus were translated into English, other in-game signs like "Let The Battle Begin!", "Show them what you can do!" and "Victory!" are still in Chinese... So it might be eyesore for some Internet Nazi, but not a big issue for normal game player. :P

Attack Heroes is a stylish non-stop action game where the heroes take turns attacking. During a hero's attack friendly heroes can join with their helper attack if available. Also: cute anime style.

https://youtu.be/8-1R83Mg3S8

The PROS outweigh the cons
- Unique gameplay!
- Many enemy types
- Cute style with big Chinese text on the screen
- Fun enough story. 2 of the main characters have enough personality

CONS
- Just 4 girls in the game? And 8 guys . . .
- If you think you play a level, and then continue to the next level and continue with the story, no! You usually need to play the same levels again to complete challenges that are only available after beating the level once, and your character may not be strong enough to stand enough of a chance to beat the next level.
- Combo challenges can be literally impossible if your characters are too leveled up and kill enemies too quickly.
- The leveling up system plays a bit too much of a role for replayability: If you just want to play a much earlier level because it was fun, it won't be challenging any more.
