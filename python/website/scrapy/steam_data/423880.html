Support The Sequel

Just because it crashed doesn't mean it's over

After so many requests, and after a few months of trial and error, I finally came up with the perfect story for a sequel and I'm ready to do it. I have started writing the story but I thought I'd give you guys a heads up and a few information about it.

It will be titled 'Carpe Diem: Reboot' and, as you may have guessed already, Ai... reboots. It will continue exactly where the first one left off, but this time the story will revolve around Ai and her struggles. The last part of 'Carpe Diem' will even be included in the sequel as the prologue, but with better art, music, and interface.

The first one did really well but it had its shortcomings which I'd like to make up for in the sequel. It will be longer (about 3~5 hours of gameplay), and will have multiple endings. No more MS Paint-tier background art. Characters will have more sprites. There will be more than two soundtracks, and they're going to be way better. Last but not least, we will finally hear Ai's voice!

However, this project will be bigger, and I won't be able to do it solo like the previous one. This is where I need your support. I've started a Kickstarter campaign for it where the funds will be taken from. The funds will be used to pay for the assets that the sequel needs. To show my thanks for your support, you will receive a reward if the campaign turns out successful.

Kickstarter Link: https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/178238554/carpe-diem-reboot

If you want to support the sequel but can't back it on Kickstarter, fret not. You can still show your support by upvoting the project on Steam Greenlight.

http://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=681290328

I hope this gets enough support from the people because I'm really excited to show you what I have prepared for the sequel. If you think the first one was sad... then I'm even more excited.

About This Game

Synopsis

What makes someone perfect?
Experience this short bittersweet story in the shoes of the main character, Jung, where you will spend your time hanging out with a girl named Ai. Ai is everything Jung wishes for in a woman... except for one very important thing. A thing he prefers to disregard in order for them to have the best time of their lives.
You have a day to enjoy your date and see the world from Jung's point-of-view.

Background

This is a free short visual (kinetic) novel made to show the importance of setting the right priorities in life. This is intended to be a single playthrough kind of novel. The choices all lead to the same ending, just taking different routes.
