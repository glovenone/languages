About This Game

"I Am You From The Future! No Time To Explain, Follow m-OH CHRIST!"

Chase your future self through time and alternate realities while fighting giant monsters, collecting hats, and eating cake! No Time To Explain is a game about Time Paradoxes, Jetpack Guns and Ribs In People's Eyes. It's a comedy platformer featuring great music, voice acting and gameplay that becomes more ridiculous as you go.

Key Features

  • Completely rebuilt from ground up in Unity! The Remastered version of No Time To Explain is how the game was originally intended to be played.
  • Now with local multiplayer!
  • USE THE POWERFUL FUTURE CANNON as a gun AND a jetpack!
  • PLAY AS NEW CHARACTERS to blast, beam, slingshot and catapult yourself through fire, acid and fairy-cakes!
  • TEST YOUR GUN-GLIDING MASTERY: There are 60 wearable hats hidden around these levels, but where are they?
  • FACE OFF against giant monsters from alternate timelines, including the most horrific of all: your evil alternate self!
  • Kill giant alien crabs!
  • Eat cake!
  • Fight sharks!
  • Jump on stuff!
  • Play fullscreen!
  • Perform awesome dance moves while wearing collected hats!
  • Play different versions of yourself from the future!
  • Fully original Soundtrack!
  • Create and share your own levels in the built-in Level Editor!

About tinyBuild


We're an indie game development and publishing company. No Time To Explain is our first game. It came out of a successful Kickstarter and Greenlight adventure. Since No Time To Explain, we started helping other developers with their games. After getting enough muscle behind our internal studio, we came back to No Time To Explain, and completely remade the game in a proper engine. No Time To Explain Remastered is free for everyone who owns the original!

Being a fan of the original No Time To Explain, I was pleasantly surprised to have received this for free.

Contentwise, No Time To Explain Remastered is just what one would expect from a remaster: Smoother and faster. Innuendos aside, it's a more stable and enjoyable experience than the original.

The frames have improved, to name one example, but whether or not that's a consistent among every computer is debatable.

The laser gun, dimension hopping madness fun is still prevalent, and with the smoother gameplay fixes, they serve as fun, if not short and sweet challenges that ooze with a uniqueness that only comes with in a giant robot crab package, and I would recommend it to those looking for a funny and fun little game.

No TIme To Explain is a 2D action platformer where you have a laser that can be used to damage enemies, and has enough recoil to act as a makeshift jetpack. You use that recoil to jump and fly your way past increasingly dangerous obstacles to get to the end goal on each level. The game spices things up by giving you other characters that each have their own way of getting around, but that's most of the game.

The story is simple and manages to be humorous the whole time. As you go on it becomes an increasingly silly tale of aliens, alternate realities, and time-traveling evil twins.

Is it worth it? Maybe not for 16 CAD, unless you REALLY like platformers. But on sale this charming title is definitely worth picking up.
