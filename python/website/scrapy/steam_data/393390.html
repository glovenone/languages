Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“Cat Simulator is currently under development, but releases regular updates. We are adding new levels, characters and graphics. We are also actively working on fixing bugs. Early Access will help us to get feedback right away and continuously improve the game until final release. Check back to see what each new update brings!

Cat Simulator "Early Access" currently features lots of fun game play and we plan to add more levels, characters, and fun to the game..”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“The game will be in Early Access for as long as it takes to make the game the best it can be. Regular updates are planned!”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“We will be constantly adding game features suggested by the community.

Even though most of the foundations of the game are already in place - there are still many planned levels, characters, and graphics that we plan to add until the full release.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The Early Access version is stable and playable.

The core foundations are already in place.

We will be adding new levels, characters, and graphics until the full release.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“We expect we will raise the price on official release (after Steam Early Access). We may gradually raise our price as new levels/characters/modes are added and we near final release.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We will be involving the Community in the development process!

Community feedback and suggestions will help us prioritize game updates.
Community feedback will also help us identify bugs.

Please send us your feedback and suggestions! Thank you very much for your support!”

This is a stupid idea for a game and, unsurprisingly, the game itself is stupid, and stupid in the worst sense of the term. It's one of the most boring, most unimaginative ideas and pieces of execution that I've ever played and a desperae grab especially considering it was made up of assets: https://www.assetstore.unity3d.com/en/#!/content/18139

The premise of the game is that you're a cat, you eat rats and knock over stuff and that's basically what cats do and it's the only thing they do OBVIOUSLY, because it's in a game where you need to be one with the cat so you do the thing every cat does, you eat rats and knock over everything and stack up on points. In case you couldn't tell from my description, the game gets really old, really fast. The game is also extremely buggy including the objects that you need to knock over have only one designated path to go so they can go in the direction you're coming from and maake u go flying away. There is also no skybox, as the unity asset map in the game is meant for a top down RPG, not a Cat Simulator game. You can barely climb the stairs as well, just as the cherry on top of the sh*t. The devs might argue back as one of their main promotions for this game is this "-LOTS of BUGS!" That's them basically admitting that their game is f*cking sh*t. Bugs don't = a good game. Some bugs can be funny, but it doesn't = a better verdict.

The game looks horrible. This game has a lot of horrible textures in the map. It even looks horrible on the "Fantastic" graphics quality. I want to also bring up that I never fealt like I was a cat, it could have been called "Baby Simulator" and I wouldn't be able to tell the difference. The only thing that reminded me I was a cat was the often *meow* sound made.

I hate this game even more because I can't really talk much about it because it's just a simple game "You're a cat, you knock things over" that's it. I'm finding it quite hard to justify how much I hate this game out to you, I'm lucky I justified it this well.

Pros
+ Simple

Cons
- Boring gameplay
- Bugs
- Bad textures

Final verdict: 2/10

This is not even a demo!!
Here is what you can do in this "game":
- walk around
- touch rats to get 100 points (there is no "action" to catch rat; you just "touch" it)
- knock over various objects to get points

AND THAT'S IT!
You get ONE level, with 3 things to do, and that's it! Once time runs out, you can either exit to main menu or replay level.

And even that little they managed to mess up; there are various bugs regarding clipping (you can fall off map quite easily), objects physics don't work properly (all objects fly off in same direction, now matter from which side you push them), rat's just randomly walk around or stand still (they don't try to escape from your to give some challenge)

And worst of all, THERE IS NO CATS IN THIS GAME! Only cat you see is one on main splash screen and that's it! Occasionally in game you can hear "maow" sound, but beside that, there is no other indication that you play as cat.

Don't waste your time with this POS, no matter how much you like cats!

P.S. Don't trust all those "positive" reviews; they are all fake and made to fool you to waste money on this!

Early Access Review
Posted: 15 February
This game is broken and is in a barely pre-alpha stage, the game is full of bugs and in its current state only serves to make Early Access in general worse off than before by adding yet another poorly "developed" game here.

CONS
* Broken Physics, all the objects only move in one direction when your "cat" collides with them in ways that make no sense, if you approach them from the wrong side the "knocked over" objects will actually fly right at you and move your character in a completely immersion-breaking way.

* The level is small and ten minutes of digging on the unity store I found that most (if not all) of the level was actually bought from the Unity store under the name "Top-Down Interiors" and since this asset pack was designed with top-down levels in mind, there is no ceiling at all despite it very clearly being a room. It is worth noting that developer isn't doing anything illegal by doing this but it would be nice to see if they had added onto the assets for a bigger and better map.

* Multiple times throughout my own personal playthroughs I found that my "cat" would fall through the floor that required a restart to play again only to fall through again.

* In its current state the game is extremely boring with the small level and lack of content beyond some books, a couple rats and cups that frequently knock your "cat" over.

* The developers don't even care enough about the game to fix the skybox's black dots, an issue that is apparently easy to fix and shows how little attention to detail the developers had given to this "game".

Overall I would steer clear of this game as it will most likely never leave early access in order to justify how little content and nemerous bugs are in the game. Do not buy it even on a 95% off sale.
