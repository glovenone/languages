Overhaul: Update v1.07 is here!

Greetings all!

Whew, it's been a really long time since an update, but finally we have one, and it's large!
While we have mostly been busy with the main Viscera Cleanup Detail game, the collection of fixes and improvements made along the way have now finally been integrated into Shadow Warrior.

This means that the game has been almost completely overhauled, making it much more stable and fleshed out than its current version.

Among the significant changes are:

  • New "Office" system, where instead of getting an instant readout on your performance, you are now escorted to a separate level where you can collect items and receive a detailed review of your actions in the level, telling you exactly what objects you missed, what the consequences were, etc.

  • Also, the single "generic" character has been complimented with another 18 different characters, including 9 female skins.

  • New User Interface, complete with a bunch more system settings.

  • Improved multiplayer and networking; might still be some very stubborn issues here though I'm afraid.

  • Countless fixes and improvements to almost all areas of the game, some more obvious than others.

It was hard to keep track of everything that had changed as a result of the update, so there is no actual "changelog" I'm afraid.

So, there you have it! This should greatly improve the general game overall. Although not a lot has changed on the content side, there are several new features.
Enjoy, everyone! And keep those areas clean...for Zilla!

NOTE: A word of caution if you experience problems or crashes with the game. Much of the folder structure has changed and may leave your current structure messy and out of date. So if you have problems, it's probably best if you re-install the game fresh, removing all old files and then reacquiring them from Steam.

About This Game

After the battle is over, someone has to cleanup the mess! Introducing Viscera Cleanup Detail: Shadow Warrior from RuneStorm, a unique crossover mini-game that combines the worlds of Viscera Cleanup Detail and Shadow Warrior. Step into the rubber soled shoes of the hapless janitor tasked with cleaning up Lo Wang's mess after a devastating katana battle in the collector's temple from Shadow Warrior. Mop up pools of blood, dispose of body parts, and get the collector's gallery of antiques in tip top shape as quick as possible! Successfully clean the entire temple and collect all the scattered cash to unlock two Steam achievements!

Viscera Cleanup Detail: Shadow Warrior is free with purchase of Shadow Warrior.
