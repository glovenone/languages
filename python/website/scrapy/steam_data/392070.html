About This Game

Pumped BMX+ is a fast-paced arcade BMX game full of tricks, stunts and combos which originates from one of the most well-known BMX franchises in the world. Master over 500 challenges in this breathtaking sports game.

Originally developed by one-man studio Yeah Us!, Pumped BMX + is all about tricks, speed and skill. Players ride their way through increasingly challenging levels while pulling off crazy and impressive trick combinations. The game lets players choose their rider style and throw down their best stunts whilst racing through more than 500 challenges to reach the top of the leaderboards.

- Beautifully rendered and varied environments: Bike through four distinct environments, ridin the simplest dirt tracks right up to lengthy and complex tracks filled with half-pipes, loops and death-defying leaps.

- A new enhanced and intelligent control system: Play via the keyboard or with a controller, with easy to learn controls and an 'instant reset' button for those trickier stages.

- A fully licensed electronic soundtrack from some of the best upcoming artists and bands.

- Authentic BMX tricks which can be combined for almost unlimited creativity: Play with 8 basic and 8 advanced real-world tricks, and chaining them together to create a nearly unlimited amount of combos!

- Customisable Bikes and Riders

Pumped BMX + is a fast platformer that revolves around controlling your BMX bike and doing as many tricks as you can without crashing, while riding towards a finish.

Gameplay-wise it's similar to another popular indie game, OlliOlli. I've played it on Xbox 360 Controller and the controls felt really great, speed control is handled with one button, left stick is used for balancing and right stick is used to do specific tricks in the air. The game is responding to every button press precisely and I haven't seen any problems related to possible bad latency. (Don't worry, the game runs with 60 FPS without problems.)

Length of the game seems to be nice, there are 5 sets of levels and every level has a set of easy, normal and hard challenges - completing each level challenges for specific difficulty unlocks the harder challenges. This way, you'll gladly replay all levels multiple times and you won't even mind, as you really have to try to be better and usually do some specific tricks. This is a delight option for players who don't care about the score, they still get enough motivation to replay levels.

You might be afraid of higher difficulty, which obviously you can expect on hard challenges, but the frustration doesn't come so easily, as you can restart any level in a second. There's no time to rest, you can beat everything!

Visuals look really nice for the game (check screenshots and make your own opinion), the soundtrack contains mostly electronic music with electroswing influence. Achievement are well designed, as there aren't any laughable easy ones - each achievement really requires to do something challenging.

It's been several months since the game have been released and it's sad that it didn't get much attention from players, because it's a really nice game that's entertaining even for people who doesn't play bike/skateboard platformers. Recommended!
