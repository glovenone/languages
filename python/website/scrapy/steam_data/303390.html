About This Game

Dead Bits is a colorful world, made out of Cubes and Alien Zombies! A first person shooter with platform elements, fast and destructive!

Quilly is a lonely creature. He lives in an invisible world, mysteriously, he was abducted by aliens. Now, Quilly has to fight for his freedom! Dead Bits is a first-person shooter for Windows and Mac. Contains Pixeled Art Design and an original Dubstep Soundtrack.

    Play with the Rhythm!

  • Original Dubstep / Electronic Soundtrack produced by Frost Orb.

    Pixel Art Style!

  • Dead Bits is entirely made out of Cubes.

    Weapons and Enemies!

  • There are 3 main weapons and a lot of Enemies, but think fast! Agility and Strategy can be weapons too!
[/h2]

I see alot of negative review about this game and I am unsure as to why this is. This game is no triple A Title as most people seem to expect. Most are going to get this game with a coupon for 10 cents as I did. Is this game worth the 1 Dollar? If you like first person shooters that will frustrate you to point of having to look up how to complete a level, then yes it is. When you buy this game remember that you are going to pay either a dollar or less and you will get a game that you will beat within 2 hours, are the guns underpowered? Not really they do the right amount of damage for the enemies. Is the music too loud? To start the game yes but most games are so you can turn it down with windows or whatever OS you are using and you willl be fine as the gameplay audio scales with the music.The things I liked most about the game is the fact that each level is different, with a new set of challenges. You have to move swiftly to get to your gun or you are being swarmed by enemies. It will frustrate some but this is no excuse for a negative review. I would definitely recommend this game for the price.
