About This Game

TDP4: Team Battle - a Free-To-Play 2D multiplayer flash platformer shooter game with RPG elements!

* Features various game modes: Deathmatch (DM), Team Deathmatch (TDM), Capture the Flag (CTF), Last Man Standing (LMS), Domination (DOM), Elimination (ELM) and Duel mode!

* More than 40 different weapons: from pistols to futuristic laser weapons!

* Powerful skill system. Train your character and make him stronger!

* Receive medals and in-game currency prizes for completing different achievements. Get high rating to become the best in TDP4 and the most respected!

* Create you own or join an existing clan. Fight for your clan brothers and gain strategic points!

TL;DR: Pay-to-play microtransaction game. Community is abhorrent, staff are ignorant hypocrites.

I played this game for about 2 years. It quickly became very very P2W. DO NOT PLAY THIS GAME. The gameplay itself is good, but past about level 12 it becomes extremely P2W. You can acquire effectively 3x or 4x more health and armor than your enemies by paying about $25, and get twice as much every stat by paying roughly $50. And then the high majority of guns cost over $15 or $20 each. About 60+ cash, and 40k+coins, which will cost roughly $25. And if you don't buy those yourself, I can completely guarantee that you will be dominated by someone with the defence and the health. You would not beat them even if it was 6v1. I played this game for 2 or 3 years because I had friends on it, and the gameplay itself was fun, mostly because I had these weapons and bonuses. But it is completely pay to win, and I quit it ages ago. There is a slot machine, and the prizes are: 50, 300, 500 coins, 3, 5 cash. (don't know chance but you never seem to gain money if you do it for a while) Besides that, the only reward is the best gun in the game. But the chance of getting it is below 0.001%. A TDP4 moderator known as LiP was supposed to of said this. To back it up, I spent approximately 400 cash (sold all my guns and bonuses and quit the game for good) and didn't get the gun. (I know many people who spent over 1000)
Also, each day you come on, you get to open a briefcase with a very high chance of getting the highest bonus. The bonus of getting a discount on buying things in the game.

The reason the game rating is high, is based off the Kongregate rating. http://www.kongregate.com/games/javelinou/tdp4-team-battle
The reason the rating from high is just an educated guess. The reason is because most of the people are new, rate it after maybe an hour of playing. Game-sites in my experience tend not to have reviewers,critics, and long time gamers like Steam does. They rate it after about an hour of playing, then they either stop playing, or they pay for bonuses and weapons, and don't touch the rating. Neither group touches the ratings, until the realize like so many people including myself has about the obvious truth. The gameplay itself is good, and it is enjoyable. But once you see the community, and you get to the P2W time, it's just over.

Most of the community are jerks, complaining about killsteals, noobs, hackers, buyers, etc. It's one of the games where if you use the wrong gun, or if you use a certain tactic, you will be cussed out, called a hacker, etc. Most of them will just call you a b**** for killing them twice in a row. The main demographic of the community is Russian. (I'm not trying to be prejudiced towards the Russian nationality, I'm just stating the demographic)

The developers are pretty careless. There is a glitch where you can shoot 2-5 bullets from almost any weapon, including the best snipers in the game, and the very high majority of the medium level+ players use this "tactic." There is a glitch where if you hold down 2 buttons, you lag out the game for everyone,(easily 4000 ping) (plenty of people do this) resulting in a game crash, and disconnection. These people are 90% of the time never banned, from my knowledge. I will confirm with my friend who used to be a moderator (not saying his name) for confirmation. The admins are extremely lenient with certain aspects of their terms and conditions. For example, it basically says "no killfarming" in their Terms & Conditions, yet literally any time you go on the game, you'll find at least 5-40 rooms obviously named something related to a kill-farm. People killfarm in order to get KDR, cash, and coins, because they can't earn it legitimately due to the game being P2W. Yet I got banned for doing a combo before, despite 3 moderators disagreeing with the ban. One of my friends in the room was accused of being a bot...Despite playing for the past year, and typing plenty, etc. He had no affiliation or connection to my name, and we lived in completely different countries, so we should of had quite different I.P addresses. There are other examples of this, too.

I never expected to have to see this game again - especially not on Steam.

I used to play this game - a lot in fact. I used to play is so often that I was on the leaderboard for goodness knows how long. In fact, I'm probably still there if I ever feel the need to log back on, which I can promise everyone here, I won't.

The game is riddiculous, the moderators were awful (I assume they must have been replaced by now at the very least) the developers are obvious cash grabbers and the community itself is downright toxic.

Not once have I been so dissapointed by the Greenlight system to actually begin to condemn it.

But enough about my life story, let's get down to business.

The game is P2W and I think that's obvious to everybody who has played the game for longer than an hour. In fact, I spent quite a jolly well amount on this game over the years that I played. The chances of somebody acquiring enough cash and coins to be able to compete with the top players is just stupidly low. If anything, the best chance you have is if you befriended an incredibly skilled player so that you can mooch off of that person during the tournaments, even then it would take weeks if not months to have enough to purchase anything worthwhile.

The developers are an absolute joke: I left the game approximately a year ago and I took a look today just so I could make this review on Steam and the updates are just so damn awful it's an embarrasment. From what I can see is that they've added more guns and made the place look pretty along with a couple of incredibly dull gamemodes which aren't worth anybody's time.

I noticed a few reviews earlier complaining about the farming and cheating. I generally believe that to be overhyped nonsense in most games myself, but this time it's actually true. In fact, it's so bad that the developers created a system where players find other players who cheat or hack and are rewarded for doing so. I remember it being so bad that I along with the majority of the population were having to complain to the moderators who simply were not fixing this issue and rewarding people their well deserved money. Bare in mind that these were 20 odd pages of this stuff. I'm not even going to bother counting the amount of reports there.

The moderators themselves were an absolute joke. Again, I'm basing this on the previous moderators so they're probably different people now. One fellow who managed to become a moderator was simply because he was a popular fellow within the game. He was a 15 year old child. You don't give children that amount of power over people - and he was no exception. Another fellow who was a moderator was somebody who I had never even heard of. Do bare in mind that the community was very close to one another even if they were a bunch of toxic individuals who I'd never offer a cup of tea to, so this came as a surprise to me. What happened? This individual became easily the worst moderator I have ever seen to date - laziness was out of this world. I can only hope these two are gone for the community's sake at the very least.

Community is toxic - I don't think I ever had a day during my years of playing without being insulted repeatedly on a daily basis. Now you can remove the chatting function so you don't have to read people's comments, but it does become rather tiresome after a while. It's a child's game, not that that's a bad thing, but it's run by children for children. Expect extremely childish comments to be made.

The clan system is pointless. I did run several top clans at one point or another. In fact, I remember it very well indeed. They were the only reason I stayed around for so long, eventually I just got tired and left however, one can only stand so much boredom in a game. There were tournaments which took place which were interesting even if I didn't take part in any of them for my own reasons.

But of course, I leave the worst till last. My opinion as to why the game is essentially dead: comboing and flag farming. People level up too quickly and leave the game immediately after. I was one of the few people at the time who never did any of these things. In fact, I even had a clan which was completely against either of these things. You did any of that, and you'd be gone without a second's hesitation I abhore it and I find it pathetic that the developers thought that this should have been allowed to occur even when they knew this game was dying. Absolutely shameful and I feel no pity for them whatsoever. They brought it on themselves.

Honestly, this game shouldn't be worth anybody's time. I have no ill feelings towards it in the slightest, but it simply isn't worth it. P2W, awful developers and obviously a cash-grab. I'm sure the only reason it's still running is because of people paying money because of simple nostalgia.

I loved it at one point, and it helped me through a lot of problems, but God damn is it a joke that it's still around and on Steam of all places.

2/10 and this will be the last day I ever open TDP4 again for any reason.

And if anybody wants to know who I am, I'm sure you can figure it out eventually.
