30 June

Viking Explorer Update now available!

Lords and ladies, the Vikings have landed.

Total War Battles: KINGDOM 1.2: Viking Explorers is now live!
See a full list of new features and bug fixes below.

New Features:

  • Compete with other players weekly in the new game mode: Viking Outposts.
  • Bolster your army with five new versatile Viking units.
  • The new flood relief system will help you avoid flooding your Realm.
  • Enjoy improved matchmaking in PvP.
  • Compete for gold in the new monthly archive entry (Pledging and PvP)
  • Retrain your troops by resetting their Unit Skills.
  • Make more of your improved fishing grounds.

Patch notes:
  • Fixed a bug where changing seasons caused animals to disappear from the Realm
  • Fixed a bug with waterfalls causing heavy frame rate drops
  • Fixed an issue where selecting units becomes more difficult in battle as unit count drops
  • Fixed an issue on Samsung Galaxy Note 4/ Galaxy S5 where push notifications caused the game to drop to a low frame rate count
  • Fixed an issue with Players receiving too many push notifications
  • Fixed a bug where Raiders could not be interacted with and remained in a paused state performing no actions
  • Fixed a bug where the “Have 60 Walls” quest would not complete
  • Fixed a bug where players would get the info panel when clicking on farms and no other UI buttons to interact with
  • Fixed a crash occurring when the game was left in a paused state overnight
  • Fixed a crash occurring when two users connect to a duel
  • Fixed a crash when entering battles on 1GB ram Android devices
  • Many smaller bug fixes and stability improvements

For a more in-depth look at the new features, head here:
https://blog.twkingdom.com/viking-explorers-update-available-now/

For a quick overview of the new units and Outpost mode, checkout this playlist: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uciUax878Bs&list=PL5JTpHmlaJ7ghu1bjdpg9dI6O1XzmzMB3
To read more about the 1.2 update in detail, we've got a comprehensive blog post for you: https://blog.twkingdom.com/viking-explorers-update-available-now/

The first Outpost mode competition starts tomorrow, so we recommend using today to get acquainted with the new units and features.

About This Game

Shape the land, build sprawling towns and recruit and train a powerful army.
Conquer new territories to expand your realm and defeat rival lords and other players in epic real-time battles.

FEATURES


  • Build and expand your Kingdom, with farms, quarries, blacksmiths and more.
  • Alter the land by creating rivers, lakes and mountains.
  • Command your army in large-scale battles.
  • Battle other players in real-time.
  • Cross-Platform - Play on Phones, Tablet and PC, whenever you want, wherever you want. Actions in your Kingdom will carry over onto any device you play on.
  • From the creators of the award-winning Total War™ games.

PLEASE NOTE


Total War Battles: KINGDOM is free to download and play.

Additional Gold can be purchased using real money. More information on in-app purchases is available here

So Total War Battles: KINGDOM is a mobile game that you CAN play on PC.

And the mobile game design is integral to this game. Basically this is an incremental base builder.
Gather resources, build and upgrade buildings, recruit units and play missions/battles. This gameplay happens over the course of days. Time walls and cooldowns take a lot of time, and the reason for it is...
A. To reinforce habitual gaming (rather than binge gaming), so the player keeps coming back
B. And to play against the player's patience and need for immediacy, so that they pay real money to get 'gold' and speed up progress.

It's important to know games like this aren't made to be games and have fun, they're made to take your money. It's market researched and psychologically tested to get you to spend money.

And the game REALLY wants your money, like all games of this type. I quickly hit a wall in which to progress I had to spend perhaps another week gathering the resources in order to upgrade buildings over the courses of more hours so that I can get units that aren't trash and can continue fighting battles fairly.

The sad fact is that the game is so close to being fun and interesting, but it's just stuck in a mobile time-sink design.

----------Who cares past this line the review is basically over----------

I have a theory for as to why Total War is in the title... And that (theory) is that the SEGA publisher is interested in expanding the brand name recognition of Total War which could make the real games sell better.
