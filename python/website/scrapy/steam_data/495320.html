Update #2

Thanks all for the discussions and remarks, here is a list of the current outstanding issues and remarks


Known issues :

#1 Not starting in theater-mode
solution : Run Steam as normal but turn off Steam VR , and then launch your game

#5 Random 'not starting'
Please drop us an email when you are experience startup issue, we would lik e to receive more information about this. So please provide us some information so we can look into that.

About This Game

Do you remember Thrill rides in amusement parks or local fairs?

What if such an experience would be available for VR headsets ?

Now you can enjoy the same experience in your chair if you own a VR headset ( Rift / Vive)

Experience an unique thrill ride / roller coaster in an ancient abandoned environment .Feel the thrills of the heights, speeds in this wonderful experience exclusive for VR headsets on steam!


Notice: Requires a virtual reality headset. See the VR Support section for more info.

System Requirements

    Minimum:
    • OS: Windows 7 or newer, 32-64-bit
    • Processor: I5
    • Memory: 8 GB RAM
    • Graphics: GTX970 , equivalent or newer
    • Storage: 1 GB available space
    • Sound Card: default sound device
    • Additional Notes: HTC vive required , Oculus DK2 or CV1 required
    Recommended:
    • OS: Windows 7 or newer, 32-64-bit
