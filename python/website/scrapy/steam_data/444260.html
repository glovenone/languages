About This Game

Mindless Running - it's a classic runner with story mode. Explore a randomly-generated world while listening to a unique soundtrack.

Our hero will have to go off on a dangerous journey, go through thick and thin, overcome hardships and obstacles to get to his Granny (who has baked his favourite Pedigree snacks for her beloved grandson ). Due to player needs motivation, during the journey the lives of the hero and his friends will be permanently in danger. See if you can take that!

Despite a chosen difficulty level it will always be hard as HELL! Test yourself and complete a number of challenging levels to enjoy to the full one of the main features of the game - its system of narrative dialogues in the spirit of modern role-playing AAA projects with a shade of delicate irony.

Earn as many points as possible while playing normal game mode to unlock Steam achievements! There are no ratings and best scores in Mindless Running - you're too unique and it's no need for anyone to know your results. Anyway you are the smartest and the hottest here.

Mindless Running is a fun enough little endless runner, and a pretty good value provided you tailor your expectations so that you expect nothing else. "The impossible pet run"
Mindless running is a clone of "The impossible game". Instead of a jumping square you have squared cute animals that run through a sprite landscape. For a quick reactions game is just the story mode with the simple mechanics OK. But in endless mode you die trying to reach the 10k points.

Reasons:
1. Game runs laggy as hell
2. Newly in endless mode introduced double size spikes
3. Sloppy hitboxes - sometimes you die before even touching the spikes or when you are already on the platform above or only close by some water
4. Spike sequences become unfair at 8k points

The main problem is like in every kind of this game type: The controls are way too rudimentary. Means you can just control the start of your jump but not faster going down or in-flight adjustments are possible. So if you try to ascend spiked stairs every jump must be fitting perfectly or you'll explode instantly. Another bad are the sometimes low exposed spikes like in yellow desert brown spikes. 2/8 chooseable characters are completly useless. Turtle & Dolphin cannot jump over long gaps because of slowness.

Update: Dev introduced a new character - the puppy dog - with which is the 10k achievement much more easier (more steaks, faster points gaining). I think it's a great gesture and makes the game completely fun now. Thumbs up!

In this game you'll repeat the classic running game phrase, "but I didn't even hit the spike!", over and over again.
With great regret, I just can't recommend this game.
As a runner game, it should make the player want to keep playing with its replayabilty. This game doesn't manage to do this, which has deeply disappointed me. Then again, it's not too horrible either.

Pros;
-Interesting combination of adventure, story and running games.
-Steam trading cards, achievements and partial controller support.
-4 playable characters
-Potential (its something new)
-Two modes (story and run)

Cons;
-No replayability
-Every run feels very much the same.
-The most annoying death sound of all time.
-Slow process from death to running again.
-No checpoints (only really an issue in story point)
-You can't save any progress in story mode.

3.5/10
