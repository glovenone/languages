Elsword is a fun, active, side-scrolling beat down that is very addictive. You have a lot of different character choices to choose from which gives the game easy replay ablility and for the player to enjoy it again with a different style. It also has 3 classes for each character that lets you replay a character and find which class of those you may like more than the others. It does limit characters but you receive slots when another character comes out which happens quite often as the developers pump out content at a good rate. You can also go challenge your skills against other players with their characters in pvp. In the begining you roll easily through the story and level at a fast pace but later on you do need to go grind Xp to level at the mid to higher levels. There are also many quest to do that will help level you and get you more goodies in the game. The only problem I find with the game that burns me out quickly is the high level grinding as the game makes you have to play it a lot to achieve a single level. Otherwise its fun to run through with different characters for a way to play so I highly recommend it for its high replay ability and fun style.

WHAT YOU NEED TO KNOW ABOUT THE GAME

GENRE
2.5D Action

HOW IS THE GAMEPLAY
It is pretty addicting while at the same time repetitive. Each character have their own large skill set. You can perform full string of combo and use mount as an extra assist to help you along with tough battle. You can either farm either in dungeon or field and the experience gain rate is just right.

IS IT PAY 2 WIN ?
To tell you the sad truth, it is. You have limited space of item inventory and skill slot. You only get 4 skill slot and 3 consumable slot in default. You can upgrade the skill slot using in-game money but it pretty expensive considering certain characters have many useful skills.

HOW IS THE EVENTS ?
I would say some of the events are pretty good where you can earn good items in which you can sell for a high price. While others, it pretty mediocore. I would rate it 7/10 in terms of them handling the events.

DOES THIS GAME PROVIDE BOTH PvP & PvE ELEMENT ?
Yes it does though I advised you to stay away from PvP as the matchingmaking is horrible + certain characters is very unbalanced especially the new characters. Reach at least level 65-85 then you can play PvP without much hassle.

HOW MANY CHARACTERS YOU CAN CHOOSE ?
Currently there are 11 characters with more to come as more huge updates comes by. Each of the characters play differently and have unique active and passive skills.

GAMEPLAY

A lot of the soundtrack is very enjoyable to listen too A stamina bar where you can't do dungeon if it is depleted

WITH SO MUCH NEGATIVITY, WHY DO YOU STILL PLAY IT ?
I guess a I am a sucker when it comes to 2D games since it my favourite genre. Give it a try at least and probably you may appreciate the game despite some flaw. The gameplay is addictive while yet repetitive is someway. It would enhanced your gameplay experience if you play it with friends. The pay 2 win is not that bad as long as you stay away from PvP.
