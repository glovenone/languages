About This Game

As a UFO pilot in training, you have been tasked with the overwhelming mission of invading Earth, terrorizing the locals, and returning home in one piece. Use your tractor beam to abduct helpless Earthlings and defeat aggressive foes. It will take all your skills, wits, and intellect to prove your alien worth and triumph over humanity.

You're a terrible UFO pilot who has failed most of its basic tests but your sister has just married the alien commander: this means you now have enough connections to pilot the best UFO in five missions to annoy, terrorize, abduct and kill humans.
Abduction Action! Plus is a pretty basic and short game that sees you playing through five missions abducting innocents, killing armed humans and generally wreaking havoc as you complete tasks set by your commander.

PROS:

-good amount of humour both in dialogues and mission tasks

-gameplay is pretty fun overall

CONS:

-basic graphics

-amateurish sound

-I havn't tried the keyboard, but the controller felt a bit unresponsive while moving around and trying to drop objects on the tiny enemies

-very short, you'll be done in an hour at most: it's also quite easy

-while it saves unlocked mission it won't save your highscores and gained lives, this means that if you quit and then select the last played mission you'll start with the basic 2 lives and a score of 0

http://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=531749240

I got this through a bundle and it wasn't too shabby considering it was made by a few people: it's very basic both in gameplay and production values but it was funny and silly enough to make my 50ish minutes with it a decent experience, hence the thumbs up.

I understand many people will, and in fact should, skip it since it's pretty forgettable overall and quite light in content: but if you find the premise interesting and find it on sale or in a bundle you'd surely do worse.

Kind of disappointing overall.
Was hoping for something a little more than 5 levels of very basic gameplay!

No key rebinding makes for an awkward playstyle with arrow keys for movement, space to use the Abduction Beam, Y to Boost and B to drop bombs! End of each level you have to press X to progress to the next level as well, so I think that this is a VERY lazy port that hasn't even bothered to change the inputs from XBox!
I tried an XBox controller as well, foolishly thinking that it might be a better way to play...
Nope, no response at all using a controller!
It goes to show how truly lazy a port this is when, in the options menu there is a setting for controller shake and next to it is a message "Not on PC"!! So why is it even in there???

The gameplay is mildly amusing briefly, but only consists of picking things up in your abduction beam and either abducting the defenseless, dropping enemies to their death or dropping rocks and crates to kill or damage enemies. That's pretty much it.

For a mere £1.35 I suppose it's ok value, I got just over 30 mins for mild entertainment out of it.
But it could have been so much more!
Every level is the same, a flat plane with a very basic background image and a bunch of crates, rocks, civies and enemies.

A bland and disappointing game, rather than a bad one, and a lazy port indeed.
