About This Game

Play 8-Ball, 9-Ball, Snooker, Billiards, Pub Pool, 27 games total!

“So realistic it will make your real life pool game better!”

Two career play modes, trick shots, video tutorials, multi-player online. Virtual Pool 4 is the most comprehensive cue sport game ever!

Compete for a season in Pro Tour Career. Play against real pros and top amateurs as virtual opponents. Start on the Local tour and work up through the Regional, National, and finally the World tour. See tour rankings and player statistics. Try to earn all the tour achievements for each tour game. Each season includes several tournaments with different formats including single elimination, double elimination, and special invitational. Tour games include 9-Ball, 8-Ball, Snooker, Black Ball, 10-Ball, 8-Ball Pub World Rules, and Straight Pool. Play levels of Easy, Normal, Hard, Extra Hard, and Good Luck.

Start in the Garage and gamble your virtual bankroll through six locations and hundreds of opponents in Hustler career play. Beat the room boss to advance to the next location. Opponents become more challenging in later locations and the betting stakes go up! Some locations occasionally have tournaments for a change of pace in this pressure packed gambling odyssey. Use some hard earned virtual cash to buy break cues, jump cues and low deflections cue shafts. Careers setup includes seventeen different game choices and five skill levels.

Learn to play better pool! Video tutorials with Steve Daking cover all you need to know to improve your real life and your virtual pool game. After each tutorial segment, you can play and practice each lesson shot in Virtual Pool 4!

Setup trick shots or just play them from the pool and artistic billiard trick shot libraries. You can also play friends on the same computer, practice by yourself, or play any computer opponent.

Use a custom Play Cue to show some style and change the shaft to a lower deflection model for better aiming accuracy. Get a Break Cue to smash the rack harder and make more balls. Jump Cues can be used to jump over obstructing balls.

Compete in online tournaments or play with friends. Single and double elimination tournaments with or without handicap. Compete as singles or teams. Player profile, friends list, awards, statistics, rankings, calendar of events, shot upload. Online lobby where players meet and chat. Great for both the friendly and competitive player.

Purchase includes 1 year online as a premium player (full access) and 500 V$ which can be used to purchase cue sticks and enter tournaments. Ongoing premium status requires $9.95 yearly minimum.

Grab a cue and play now!

The Virtual Pool franchise has been going since 1995

Vrtual Pool 4 is the latest in Celeris next installment of the series.

How to sum up Virtual Pool 4? Yes its a game,but it goes far deeper than that when you break it down into its constituents.
If you like simply to bang a few balls around against an AI player from time to time and set the game level to your specific choosing you can do so.

If however you want to develop and learn about billiards and pool in general then it also can cater to your needs.

From the Video totorials by Steve Daiking you can learn the very basics in the art and skill Of Pool to the more advanced positioning and cue ball control and once you think you have mastered that phase you may want to start a hustler career and gamble your bux against the Poolsharks at the various and sometimes seedy locations available to you.

You also have a varied and comprehensive career mode at your disposal.Start of as a lowly amateur and see if you can progress your way through the ranks to take on what seems to be a brutal Champion level in the game.

You have a trick shot mode,you can set a table and the balls the way you want and try perhaps to recreate a shot you seen one of your heroes play in the past.

In short there is a whole host of things to keep yourself interested in the game,above all though this is not simply a game,its a learning tool that anyone who has a mind to can progress and advance their own understanding of how to shoot a ball and and makes runs and start winning.

Virtual Pool does all this with the best control,method of all,the mouse.
Your mouse controls your cue stick,you draw the mouse back and push it forward to take a shot,it could not be simpler.

Using the mouse is the simplest and best way to give you the weight and feel of a shot and its also through time has proved Celeris have got it right since way back in 1995 when Virtual Pool first saw the light of day.

Other games of this nature have always used power bars and meters that take so much longer for you to set a shot up and actually hit a ball.Virtual Pool does not mess about and very quickly you will see that they got it right.

It can improve your reallife game but only if you want it to as its down to what level you want or think you may be able to acheive,the sky is the limit as far as that goes.

Ok some will say the graphics although a vast improvement on the fore runners in the series, they dont jump out at you like some other nicer looking games of that ilk.You may be right and there is still room for improvement as the game will be a work in progress and is being improved all the time.

What stands out by a country mile is the game play and once you have mastered all the key controls as that will take time I think you will find that Virtual Pool 4 has got all the rest beat in comparison
Its not simply a game its as true a simulation as you can possibly get on PC and for the time being still hold onto the title of Best Pool and Billiard sim of all.

If you are new to this type of game please dont judge it after a few hours of struggling around and feeling your not getting anywhere.Use the tracking lines as a help if you need to,use the ghost ball to show you where to hit the ball.

The most important thing is to give it time and if you want to learn and progress,you will and it will grow on you all the more.You will keep coming back to it and you will develop as a player and you also will get a better understanding of the art of billiards and Pool in General.

You have tried the rest....now try the best. Virtual Pool 4 Its what sitting up to 4 am in the morning is all about.
