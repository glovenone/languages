About This Game

Take a trip back to Jill Evans'™ hometown of Bakersfield in Cake Mania Main Street™, the highly anticipated fourth chapter in one of the most popular time management series of all time!

Help Jill and her closest friends earn enough money to revitalize Main Street by opening, managing, and upgrading four unique downtown shops and building must-see tourist attractions. With all-new shop mechanics, 50 shop equipment upgrades, 40 unique mouth-watering recipes, and hilarious new customers, Cake Mania Main Street offers players four truly addictive time management games in one!

  • 100 levels of fast-paced action
  • Purchase and upgrade 4 different shops, all with new gameplay mechanics
  • Buy and sell over 50 unique shop upgrades
  • Unlock and purchase 40 delicious, mouth-watering recipes which include over 35 unique ingredients

System Requirements

    • OS: Windows XP/Vista/7
    • Processor: Pentium III 800MHz or better (1.0 GHz Vista)
    • Memory: 256 MB System RAM (512 MB Vista)
    • Graphics: 32MB Video RAM
    • DirectX®: Direct-X 8.1 (10 Vista)
    • Other Requirements: Internet Explorer 5.0+, AOL 5,6 or MSN browsers

Cake Mania Main Street is the fourth game in the Cake Mania series, a highly popular time management series. For those familiar with the Cake Mania series, you'd usually take on the role of Jill, baking cakes for various customers in differing locales and such. Although you can still play as Jill in this game, there are 3 other shops besides the bakery, allowing for more variety. In addition, a burger restaurant, flower shop, and sushi restaurant are now available to play. Since the main storyline involves bringing life back to Jill's hometown of Bakersfield, it does make sense that she would have others help by running other businesses.

As this is a time management game, the gameplay is pretty simple. Basically, various customers will enter and give out their orders, and you simply need to fulfill them. There is a tutorial to help out for those unfamiliar with this kind of gameplay. In addition, after each level, you can buy more equipment, upgrade it, and buy new recipes, allowing for more variety. Apart from the levels and upgrades, you can improve on the town of Bakersfield by buying various attractions and upgrading existing shops. This allows you to bring more income to the town and attract more people to shop.

I enjoyed this game, as I'm a fan of the Cake Mania series. The gameplay was lots of fun and a bit challenging at times. The characters are entertaining, and I liked the art (in a comic book style) and music. I only wish that there would be more Cake Mania games on Steam, as I believe that fans of time management games would love them. (I'm not sure if it would be possible to bring more Cake Mania games on Steam, though, since the developer has since sold the rights and I have no idea who has them now.) Even without Steam achievements or trading cards, I would totally recommend this game, as I found it to be a lot of fun. ^^
