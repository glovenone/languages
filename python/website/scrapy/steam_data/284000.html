About This Game

Help Putt-Putt® bounce his pal Pep into the air to pop colorful balloons--some filled with hidden surprises!

Balloon-O-Rama features 120 levels of action and hidden bonus rounds. It even has a construction mode, so kids can build their own challenging levels! The junior arcade games are jam-packed with fast and furious game-play that will provide hours of fun.

Features:

  • Challenging arcade-style games
  • 120 levels
  • Construction mode allows kids to create their own levels of balloon fun
  • Save your game after each level and track scores for up to 75 players
  • For kids ages 3 to 8

Junior Arcade games are jam packed with fast-and-furious game-play that satisfies kids' hunger for action and surprises! Starring lovable characters and the high-quality, hand-drawn animation that families have come to expect from Humongous Entertainment, this series will provide hours of action and fun for your children.

Product received for free
Putt Putt and Pep's Balloon-o-Rama is a mixed bag which I cannot recommend.
My friend sent me this game as part of the anime sale and while I love the anime of this franchise the Putt Putt game cannot live up to it.

Story - 0/10
While I love the story of the Putt Putt anime it's a shame there is little to no story in this game at all. I wanted some backstory between Putt Putt and Pep but unfortuntely many of my questions are left unanswered. I wanted to know why Putt Putt could talk and why Pep is okay with being kidnapped by a car. These questions still bother me and will continue to have a negative effect on me for a long while.

Gameplay - 10/10
The gameplay of this game is fantastic. I have never had more fun throwing a dog at balloons. It's even more fun than it is in real life just without having to hide the corpse afterwards. It's a bit unrealistic though as the police never knock at your door in this game and you never have to lie about you never seeing the dog that went missing.

Music - 1/10
The music is very bland and sounds a lot like the JoJo's bizarre adventure soundtrack so you know what to expect; trash. This low quality of audio does not suit this high quality of gameplay.

Graphics - 4/10
I really like the Minecraft look that these games go with nowadays. While I would've preferred the animation from the anime the pixelated Minecraft inspired look really gives it some charm.


Overall: After 62 levels I called it a day. ?/10
