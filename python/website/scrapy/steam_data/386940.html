24 June

Online Update, A New Block, & Official Soundtrack Release!

It's the Steam Summer Sale!!!

With it, Ultimate Chicken Horse has gotten some major online updates: there's no more "Beta branch" for online play, we've improved connectivity, added clearer messaging, and generally improved the whole online experience to make it much more playable. We are still keeping the online mode labeled as "Beta" because we will be constantly updating it, improving it and fixing the remaining bugs. You can check the full Update Log for a full list of improvements.

In this update, we're also including a bit of a preview for new content to come! We've added a new block, the horizontal moving block, which should open up a whole new world of possibilities of movement, placement and screwing with your friends:



We're planning on rolling out more content once the online Beta is out of Beta, but we wanted to give you a quick tease.

Last but certainly not least, we're announcing the official UCH soundtrack with a great new bundle!



The soundtrack was made by the amazingly talented Vibe Avenue, contains 12 tracks and over 40 minutes of sweet tunes. It also features live instruments: trumpets, saxophones, flutes, whistles, banjos, guitars, harmonica and more!



Enjoy, and you can expect some more big updates coming soon!

4 April

Online Beta Update - Easier To Connect With Friends!

Hello everyone!

It's been a while since our last announcement, where we mentioned that the online beta would be coming soon. Well, we've got good news for you! It's actually been out for a little while, but previously people had been having issues connecting and had been experiencing game-breaking bugs. This announcement brings some important changes :)



Online Improvements
  • No more need for port forwarding
  • Scoring system works properly and games end at the right time
  • For a full list of fixes, check out the Steam discussion here

There are definitely still bugs though, which is why it's still in Beta. If you're experiencing bugs that aren't on the known list, please submit them here.

Known Issues
  • No sudden death when there's a tie game
  • Two coins can't be picked up at the same time online
  • For the full list of known issues, check out the Steam discussion here.

Next Steps?

We're working on making sure that the online play is seamless and there are no game-breaking bugs, so that's the highest priority. We've also started looking into setting up server lists so that you can join a game online without needing to have friends! I mean... without needing to invite friends to play online. This will act as a sort of "matchmaking", and we may look into doing real matchmaking at some point, but that will come later.

For now, enjoy the game, and if you want updates, be sure to follow us on Twitter. Thank you!

About This Game

***ONLINE IS STILL IN BETA!!!*** Expect some bugs in the online mode! We've made major progress with our newest update and it's getting close to where it needs to be, but if you think you'll be frustrated by some connection bugs, feel free to wait a bit while we fix it and perfect it. Thanks for your interest and support.***

Ever wish you were a wall-jumping, arrow-dodging, trap-setting horse, besting your animal pals in a race through a peril-laden obstacle course that you all built together?

Wish Granted!

Ultimate Chicken Horse is a party platformer where you and your friends build the level as you play, placing deadly traps before trying to reach the end of the level. If you can make it but your friends can't, you get a point! Play online or locally with your animal buddies and experiment with a wide variety of platforms in all sorts of strange locations to find new ways to mess with your friends.

Key Features

  • Online and local play (Online is currently in Beta, expect some bugs)
  • Unique game flow, from strategic block placement to twitch control platforming
  • 9 levels with different features (for now)
  • Huge library of blocks to create an infinite variety of levels
  • Play as a chicken, horse, sheep, and other wonderful animals
  • Fun, cartoony art style
  • Sweet funky soundtrack

Please note: there is no "single-player" version of this game. There is online multiplayer, where you can join random lobbies with friends or strangers, so you can still enjoy the game alone!

Review changed from negative to positive. Originally, the functionality of online play was so ridiculously awful that I and plenty of others found it impossible to play a full game without at least 50 random failures first, but a lot of work has been done since then. I still have trouble connecting to servers sometimes, and I occasionally can't move after an online game starts, but it's nothing too major anymore, and the developers are continuing to work on it.

The actual game itself? It's absolutely wonderful. Half platformer, half stab-your-friend-in-the-back-with-cheap-tricks simulator, and fully hilarious, it's easy for anyone to really get into the game and have lots of fun with it. It's the first multiplayer game I've found where I don't even care in the slightest who wins at the end, because every round, every increasingly impossible setup of objects, and every trap placed by a player in a mean spot all works together to give me a good laugh, so much so that I'm too busy cracking up to even pay attention to the scoreboard most times. How someone can make a platforming game all about competing for the finish line without the overall 'feel' of the game being competitive, I'll never know, but it's truly refreshing to find a game that's so simple and easy to get behind, and always so entertaining.

It's also pretty fun to build deathtraps out of conveyor belts, honey, and sawmill blades.
