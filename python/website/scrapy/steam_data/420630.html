About This Software

Music Maker 2016 Premium is a unique virtual studio with 6,500 included sounds, intuitive drag & drop controls, 17 instruments and high-quality pro features for mixing & mastering.

The most important features:

  • All the features, instruments & sounds from Music Maker 2016 plus extra exclusive features
  • 6,500 new sounds & loops
  • Free companion app for effects and transport controls
  • 17 virtual instruments (synthesizers, drum machines, choir)
  • Exclusive Premium instruments: Soundtrack Percussion and Concert Guitar
  • 10 Soundpools including 4 Premium Soundpools: Chart Hits, Movie Score, Trap, Metal
  • VST3 and VST2 interface

6,500 new sounds & loops

Music Maker 2016 Premium contains a total of 10 Soundpools with sounds, melodies and rhythms from genres like rock pop, 80s, hip hop, deep house, techno and chillout. Only available in the Premium Version: Chart Hits, Trap, Metal and Movie Score Soundpools.
Sounds from different Soundpools can be freely combined with each other.

Virtual Instruments

All the instruments in Music Maker 2016 Premium can be played with the mouse, the screen keyboard or a USB keyboard.

Premium: Concert Guitar
The wonderful acoustic sound of this concert guitar combines Spanish flair with first-class sound design options.

Premium: Soundtrack Percussion
A huge percussion section for creating cinematic rhythms, dramatic climaxes and exciting grooves.

Choir
This new, sonorous instrument gives you full control over a choir with female, male or mixed voices.

Cinematic Synth
Cinematic synthesizer sounds straight from the dream factory. The best choice when creating film music with powerful bass, inspiring melodies and epic sounds.

Folk Instruments
Melodic house music with minimal folk influences for conquering the charts and DJ sets. With the new folk instruments, you'll be on playlists around the world.

France is in the air
The virtual accordion can be used for twirling club beats or for telling traditional folk tales through song. The creative direction to take is up to you.

More virtual instruments
BeatBox2, Drum Engine, Jazz Drums, Robota drum computer, Synthesizer Revolta 2, Synthesizer DN-e1, Atmos, LiViD virtual drummer, Vita 2 Sample Player, Vita Sampler, Loop Designer.

Additional New Features


New Effects Area
The new, touch-optimized effects area has been directly integrated into the user interface, offering tons of sound design options.

The latest standard: VST3 interface
Integrate external effects plug-ins and virtual instruments into Music Maker 2016 Premium via the revolutionary VST3 standard.

New time stretching & pitch shifting features
The new Élastique Pro algorithm from zplane is one of the world's best time stretching and pitch shifting algorithms and is integrated directly into Music Maker.

Screen Keyboard
The touch-optimized screen keyboard now features a new mode with 7 musical scales which only plays the notes that match the song you are working on. The ideal tool for improvisation and composing.

Mastering
The new Mastering Suite gives your music a more lively and impressive sound. The built-in Auto Mastering feature offers optimized sound analysis and a handy monitoring feature.

Companion App Controls

Connect the free MAGIX Audio Remove app to Music Maker on your computer via Wi-Fi.

Download MAGIX Audio Remote for free

Recording controls
Use the app to start recording band practice on your computer.

Effects controls
Use the app to experiment with the room sound and filter controls for virtual instruments.

System Requirements

    Minimum:
    • OS: Microsoft Windows Vista | 7 | 8 | 10
    • Processor: 2 GHz
    • Memory: 2 GB RAM
    • Graphics: Onboard, min. resolution 1280 x 768
    • Storage: 9 GB available space
    • Sound Card: Onboard
    • Additional Notes: Supported import formats: Audio CD, WAV, MP3, OGG Vorbis, WMA, Quick Time, MIDI, FLAC, Image: JPG, BMP, Video: AVI, MXV, WMV / Supported export formats: Audio CD, WAV, MP3, OGG Vorbis, WMA, Quick Time, MIDI, FLAC, Image: JPG, BMP, Video: AVI, MXV, WMV, Note: Only non copy-protected audio CDs can be imported.
