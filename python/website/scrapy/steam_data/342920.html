A great party game that's easy to pick up and start right away

The controls are very simple, so the learning curve is pretty easy in the beginning. However, it can get very technical with the timings if you play long enough, so it's not just a one-time game. It's very fast paced, and can get a bit hectic at times, but after a few games it should be easy to get the hang of where to watch.

The art is very cartoony, and I think it looks great with the rest of the game. The maps are a bit less cartoony, but still fit the theme very well, and look great. There's a selection of about 8 or 9 maps, provding many unlockables and difficult achievements to work towards

Being a party game, it's best to play this with friends, otherwise you'll be playing against bots which definitely gets boring. There are single-player challenges, though best played with two players.

Overall, it's a great game if you're going to play with friends. Alone, however, you may want to watch some gameplay and see if it's what you want to be doing for an hour
