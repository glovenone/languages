moto RKD dash
Timeless arcade racing action from the future of our past! Moto RKD Dash revives old electromechanical toys like 'Tomy Turnin' Turbo Dashboard' Driving and 'Sega Moto Champ'. Ten levels, simple arcade gameplay, extremely fast 3D graphics at 60FPS, a bonus game and amazing soundtrack by Kliment and approxima astrolabos!

About This Game

Classic 80s motorcycle racing for the next generation. Blaze across the 9 beautiful locations of Moto RKD Dash universe: from 'Zenith Hill', through 'Ocean Gate' all the way to 'Cañon Del Silencio' and beyond. Turn your gaming into a journey between worlds and emotions with the amazing trance music of world renowned composer Kliment and the cosmic sounds of Approxima Astrolabos. Full HD graphics, 60 FPS, innovative controls, deep sound!

• simple arcade gameplay
• extremely fast 3D graphics at 60FPS
• deep trance sound
• 10 levels
• unlockable bonus game
