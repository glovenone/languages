About This Game

The cult racing series created in 1988 by Titus Interactive is back with a brand new version faster than ever before!
Rediscover the sheer thrill of arcade gaming with this amazing racing game, taking bends at max speed or massive jumps that can go hundreds of meters high.

You'll be right in the middle of all the action, racing some of the best rides out there. Criss-cross different states of the USA at over 200 mph! Just make sure the cops don't catch you.


CAREER
Risk it all to win as many $ as you can:
  • Master the speed of your ride and slalom through the traffic.
  • Blind your opponents with clouds of tire smoke.
  • Drive in reverse, nudge and dodge other rides, or slipstream past your opponents to recharge your boost gauge.
  • You can use trucks as trampolines for unbelievable jumps, which also comes in handy for avoiding police barricades.

GARAGE
  • Win all your challenges and you'll unblock loads of upgrades and new rides.
  • Pimp your ride with engine tune-ups, hub caps and spoilers.
  • Choose the color of your ride and customize it with stickers.

FREE RUN
  • Become the undisputed champion on 10 tracks in a variety of scenarios (Snow-capped mountains, Downtown, Beach, Desert and more...)

FEATURES


  • An arcade game featuring major speed and sky-high jumps.
  • Experience the intensity of urban racing as you slalom through traffic, avoiding obstacles along the way.
  • Smash through police barricades. The cops will do everything in their power to try and stop you...
  • A range of challenges in every race.
  • Use the garage to customize your vehicle. Pimp your ride!

Crazy Cars is a retro racing game that doesn't really deliver much, even for its low price point.

At its heart, this is basically something akin to Racing USA where you take a car racing across various "courses" which are open roads supposedly modelled after a certain sections of the USA, albeit the cars and terrain are 3D now. It looks like it was built in Unity3D. No licensed cars, merely cars that somewhat resembles real cars. There are cars coming the other way down the highway, and if you do something really naughty you may see police roadblocks but you can cut between the cars and go through. There are other cars on your side of the road, and some roadside obstructions like logs (!?) and stopped cars.

You do have handbrake (for drifting) and nitro. Nitro requires jumps and drifts and other moves to fill up.

There really isn't much handling. Cars don't feel much different, and you are encouraged to buy upgrades from your winnings, which aren't much. You are also requested to fulfill certain addition conditions for additional 'stars' per stage and some of those can't be done with the starting car, but only cars with lots of upgrades or better cars.

The only "good" part about this game is you aren't limited to a "circuit" and traffic's a bit random. And it's cheap. But it's just not very good. There are some customization options but those cost $$$ in the game that should have gone to upgrades.

All in all, there are much better good racing games out there, even at this low price.
