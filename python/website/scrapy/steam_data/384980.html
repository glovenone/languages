While this is not the most amazing game I have ever played, it is a fun match 3 combat game. The main reason I felt compelled to write the review is from reading the negative comments about the game that had been written by people who clearly did not actually play the game long enough to be fair about evaluating it.

This is a free game that DOES NOT REQUIRE MONEY TO WIN. You just need some patience to farm the coins you need to pay for things. Here is why I say this: The only thing you can buy in the game is coins and lives. There is NOTHING you can buy that you cannot earn. All units and "items" are unlocked just by playing. EVERY level you play will earn you coins. So, if you have time and patience and upgrade the right units as you go, you have to spend NOTHING to progress. I even saw some people claiming that upgrading your items with coins was "hidden" to get you to spend money. FYI, there is only one way to upgrade anything: coins. So, you cannot do the upgrades even with money without finding that "hidden" screen. Also the "hidden" screen is just another tab on the upgrade screen.

Now I have gotten that out of the way I will describe the game. This game is a match 3 driven classic castle defense game: you must keep producing better troops faster than your opponent to crush their base before they crush yours. The troops are just melee or ranged and there is no variation on how they work. The cleverest part of the mechanic is that each unit has a % associated with it. Each level has a certain number you have to fill to play it. So, the stronger the unit, the lower the % and the more different units you have to have. The more different units you have the more symbol types you have to match and the harder it is to make matches.

In the end, it is an easy to play and a bit grindy free system that is worth playing for a bit. Currently, the achievements are semi-broken and there are some rough edges, but it is basically solid. Those who are willing to try will enjoy it if they like the genre and those who bash a game just because of a freemium model get over yourselves as you could play this game all the way to the end for free if you are willing to spend the time.

EDIT: I have now completed the game without spending one cent. They have also made a few quality of life changes since I have started. Once I completed the game up till now I purchased one of the coin packages to support the game.
