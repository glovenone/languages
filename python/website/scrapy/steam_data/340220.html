About This Game


Shadows of War is a 2D Real-Time Strategy sidescroller game.
The game combines classical RTS elements with new ideas into a 2D game.


Fight in massive battlefields against your worst foe!
Challenge your friends in a 1v1 battle, show them who is in control.
Some maps also have unique elements, that requires you to change your tactics. The bigger the map, the harder it will be. You always need to have an eye on every section of the map. Be fast to take counter measures of the enemy's attacks!


The game also offers the Hardcore mode, for old-school gamers that needs a challenge.
You are low on money, which means you can order less units onto the battlefield.
Support strikes will be rarely used, since they're costing too much.
Keep track of your tickets, you can't lose a single one!
You really need to do everything to prevent the enemy from marching into your land.
Always do your best! Else, loosing can't be prevented.
Remember the good old days, where games hadn't got any XP system?
You only played for fun, not for unlocks or that stuff? Like that?
Then SoW is the perfect game for you, no DLC, no XP, no Micro-Transactions.


1944, a German Science project, was sealed and hidden.
It was never meant to be found. But in 2013, a new Federal Chancellor was elected.
Then, old research projects were activated again, this is where everything began...
One of the projects, were a bomb, not a casual one.
They built the bomb after the blueprints they've found.
Then they tested it, the bomb detonated successfully, with a devastating effect.
A world was left, that were pushed into the shadows, no hope, no life as it is known.
Nations in despair, began to wage war. The world, at the brink of total destruction.
Now we are here. No alliances, all fighting for survival.
Russia, now facing Germany again, on the battlefields of Europe.

Review Updated 8th March 2015

I originaly wrote a review a short white ago and since then the dev was kind enough to message me about a patch addressing some of my concerns. Since I last played there have been two patches.

Current observations 15/02/2015

Upon loading game all saved progress has been lost, at last play I had completed the campaign to day 7 I am now back at day 1.

Started up a skirmish map, as before dead enemy units are still showing on map. Ordered up a bunch of infantry units they stoped moving across the map about 15% of the way across, yet ordered up an MLRS and went all the way. During wave 2 game crashed back to desktop.

Game previously did not crash back to desktop, it just got very slow the longer a game was played ( 1 second took around 3 seconds of real time on sped up mode even worse on normal time progression by wave 16-18 and time between waves starting took around 5 mins in campaign mode, wave start delay seemed ok in skirmish ).

Given the game crashes back to dekstop so quickly I can not comment on if these issues have been fixed.

The dev is active which is a good sign and I have hopes that one day I will manage to complete the game.

Sadly as of today I can not reccomend this game in its current state.


*** 8th March 2015 3rd update to review ***

So my 3rd time trying this game, I still notice that previous progress is missing, I now presume this lost, along with progress towards acheivements.

Started up a classic skirmish, ordered up 20 soldiers, they met up with 20 enemy soldiers with a slight crest between and both stood there for 6+ mins with no casualties, didnt seem to be able to shoot each other. Started up skirmish again on a different map, same again ordered up 20 to face off against first wave of 20 enemy soldiers. Again they faced off with a crest between them. I took a screen short of it this time, nearly 13 mins they just stood opposing each other with no casualties.

3rd Time tried skirmish on a flat map, thankfully this time they were able to shoot each other.

I have played the game almost finishing the campaign and never noticed a LoS issue before, during this play test there did appear to be LoS between the troops and bullet animations were clearly showing hits between the units, so unsure if this is a new issue or just random bad lack that has not happened to me previously.

Crashes seem to have stopped. Fair play this was resolved quickly and caused by a patch that was not intended for release.

Mini map / radar is still largely pointless and quickly stops indincating your units and then shortly after enemy units.

Your own troops still randomly stop moving across the map bunching up in a mass group, this can lead to loosing a large number of units to a single tank / arty shell.

Once a large number of troops that had stopped moving and bunched up on the map just vanished ( well behind any fighting and only on 3rd wave no no arty in play on either side ), can't say I noticed that happening before either.

I would classify the current state of the game as working but frustrating. I have not tested the campaign, since Feb so can not comment regarding speed slowdown issues that occoured previously
