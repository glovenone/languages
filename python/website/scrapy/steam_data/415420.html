About This Game

** Official Nyan Cat Game **

Play with the most celebrated flying cat in human history. Drink milk, eat ice-cream, collect coins, buy gorgeous boosts, fly like a superhero (sorry... supercat), jump onto platforms made of cake, avoid angry dogs, and discover the infinite space. And that's not all, you can choose from multiple game modes, where you can play with Nyan Cat's evil foe Tac Nayn, learn to fly on different planets with Nyan Wings or visit parallel universes. If you're tired, you can even train and feed your own Nyanagotchi. Oh, and last but not least: you can enjoy the wonderful Nyan-song as many times as you want! All this funky stuff can't be missed.

Features for Humans:
- Tasty COLLECTABLES (energy drinks, donuts and more)
- Crazy POWERUPS (rockets, bubbles and more)
- Freaky POWERDOWNS (dynamites, flashlights and more)
- Evil ENEMIES (dogs, UFOs and more)
- Leaderboards & ACHIEVEMENTS
- Stunning boost ITEMS (score multiplier, shield and more)
- 5 GAME MODES, including Nyan Wings
- NYAN EDITOR!
- NYANAGOTCHI room: keep your Nyan happy to earn gifts! The happier he is the quicker you get your prize.
- Different background THEMES (Halloween, Xmas and more)
- Different forms of NYAN CAT (Supernyan, Zombienyan, and more)
- TAC NAYN
- Nyan nyan nyan nyan NYAN nyan (nyan, nyan and more)

Features for Nyans:
- Nyan nyan nyan nyan POWERUPS nyan nyan nyan ACHIEVEMENTS nyan nyan SUPERNYAN nyan ONLINE HIGH SCORES nyan nyan nyan BEAUTIFUL PIXEL ART nyan nyan nyan

Nyan Cat: Lost in Space is a platformer released in December 2015, developed by isTom Games Kft. The game focuses on the cute loveable meme , Nyan Cat.

Gameplay takes on the role of a very simple but highly addictive platformer. Nyan Cat is constantly moving across the screen and you control when to jump and how far. The aim of the game is to collect as many objects as possible to boost your score and multiplier rating. The game has a very infinite runner vibe to it as there is no actual end on the classic mode. As you progress through the stages, you will encounter objects which are not always so kind to Nyan Cat, such as bombs, dogs and UFOs which attack you! There are a few other game modes, but none of them come close to being anywhere near as fun as the classic mode.

Graphically Nyan Cat is really pleasing on the eye. The cartoon art style and cute animations really do the game justice. Of course it isn’t going to be super realistic visuals or high resolution, because well, it’s Nyan Cat who has been drawn on something like MS Paint. The classic Nyan Cat sound track is played constantly in the background, so if you are easily annoyed by it, you may want to mute the sound, however if you are the sort to be annoyed by it, you probably won’t be the sort to play this game either.

This isn’t a really hard game; it just takes a bit of practice to get really good at it. There will be moments where you just get recked by some random event that you had no control over, but that is part of the joy of this game. The game features loads of powerups which help you collect more objects to boost that score, but there are also plenty of downgrades too which will hinder you.

There are a couple of different backgrounds and themes to play in, all of which have a neat look which is represented very well. Not to mention there is an editor program that allows you to customize how your Nyan Cat looks, as well as Nyanagotchi, where you are able to look after your Nyan cat by washing him, tickling him etc. You are rewarded with gifts for making your Nyan happy which may be cosmetic items.

Nyan Cat is a very casual, pick up and play game, you won’t be able to play it for hours on end, but it is really fun for 15 minutes here and there. Whilst in total I have only played Nyan Cat for just under an hour, it is certainly a game I will keep installed as it always brings a smile to my face when I load it up. If you are really into your endless runner type of games, this is obviously not going to be better than the likes of BitTrip, but it is a really nice little competition in that genre. With the addition of leader boards for friends and globally, it adds a little edge to the game which may be overlooked in others of this type.

I didn’t have any technical problems whilst playing. The game can be played either using a controller or mouse and keyboard. Either is fine due to the simplicity of the controls. I personally opt for the mouse option but both works just as good as each other.

To sum up, Nyan Cat is your run of the mill OK game. Its part of a genre which already has some really bit names but the low price tag makes this a really good entry level game for the endless runner genre. You of course have to find amusement in the Nyan Cat meme to get anywhere near as much of a smirk from the game though. £1.99 is a great deal and I would recommend you consider it today even just for half an hour of fun.
