About This Game

The Great Escape


The Great Escape is a Infinity Runner, where the player guides a spaceship through a narrow tunnel filled with incoming obstacles. Maneuverability is pretty nice, but it will be necessary great reflexes and a lot of patient for this game, considered very hard for it own devs. Great Escape is procedural, the more you advance harder the hazards become.

Badges :

The main goal is achieve high score and record your name in our world leaderboards.


The Great Escape is another game from brazilian indie game developer XiNFiNiTY Games.

System Requirements

    Minimum:
    • OS: Windows XP, Vista or 7
    • Processor: 1GHz or faster processor
    • Memory: 1 GB RAM
    • DirectX: Version 9.0c
    • Storage: 500 MB available space

Game starts up well enough ,no in-game key-config. but as it's a Unity game you can change keys via the small box that pops up just before you play the game.

I'll be honest and say i didn't have a clue what to do in this game .
You are piloting a ship and have to avoid certain obstacles (i think)
You get some help about what keys to use for movement and slowing or speeding up your ship.
The problem is none of it seemed to work .
Apparently you're supposed to do some specific mouse clicking at the first screen ,i only found this out after reading another review here.
I still haven't quite figured out how to get pass the first screen using the mouse , i think a little more info at the beginning would be useful.
Like most people who've played this i kept dying in the "orb" .
Before you get a chance to figure out what you're supposed to be doing you get sucked towards to this "death orb" .
The graphics seem perfectly ok and the game appears to run well ,it just needs a bit of info at the beginning or maybe an easier introduction to the game-play before the dying starts :)

Maybe hardcore gamers will get more out of it than i did so i won't give it a negative mark as it could just be me

Initially thought that this was a game over simulator due to controls not working. Saw a comment by the devs about how this is actually a cutscene and we need to do actions during the cutscene. After several attempts, I managed to work out what was intended - fill the meter by clicking top emp button, keep clicking bottom emp button and hope it works (doesn't always) then keep clicking machine gun button and hope it works. This then brings you into the game.

I died before my first obstable by pushing left and crashing into a wall. I had an option to place my highscore, the flashing icon didn't match the text box. I begun again and pushed escape to see if there were any options, this caused the game to freeze and I was forced to close the game. Many spelling mistakes too.

An endless runner in which you need to perform an overly complicated, poorly explained and ultimately unecessary opening cutscene and in which you can die by crashing into the surroundings of the screen in addition to obstacles in front of you? I've given up trying before reaching my first obstacle.

Not worth playing until some serious fixes are made.
