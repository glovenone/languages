Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“Steam Early Access is an excellent opportunity for us to begin the next stage of development for Tower Unite.

There are still a lot of features we plan to implement, and releasing on Early Access will allow us to exceed the expectations of our fans and our community.

We want to ensure our community is part of our development throughout the process. Our game is unique due to it's diverse, varying gameplay. We want to expand the game based on feedback in ways that we could never imagine on our own.

Early Access allows us to realize our dream game. We want to create the best possible experience for a wide audience, and we can't do that alone. We will use Early Access to address concerns, gather additional data from gameplay experiences, and balance the game.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“We will leave Early Access in 2017.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“We plan to ship with more Game Worlds, all the various plaza activities, a fully functional casino and arcade, additional player avatar customization, ocean exploration, more advanced condo customization tools, additional PC settings such as key binding, Workshop support, and the features we promised our backers. We also want to ship the game on Mac OS X and Linux.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“After 10 months of development, the game currently features:
  • 4 Game Worlds: Minigolf with 4 playable maps, Ball Race with 5 playable maps, Virus with 3 playable maps, and Planet Panic with 1 playable map
  • 400+ unique and unlockable items
  • Full Steam Inventory support
  • P2P voice chat
  • Dedicated server hosting
  • P2P Condo to Lobby communication
  • 13 different in-game item shops
  • Game World matchmaking (traveling to and from Game World instances)
  • The condo with furniture/wallpaper editing
  • Hosting a condo for friends to join easily
  • Theater with YouTubeâ¢ support
  • Playable instruments such as a piano and a drumset
  • Basic character customization and accessories
  • Basic gamepad support (not inventory)

    The game is currently Windows only.
”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“The price will not change during or after early access.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“Since it's inception, Tower Unite has been all about the players.
By listening to our community, we have been able to build a game that has broad appeal
across multiple genres.
We are constantly welcoming new people into our diverse community to contribute ideas to the game.
If we find ideas that our players will enjoy, we start developing those concepts into new features for Tower Unite.

Our game has always depended on it's fans, they crowdfunded us to bring this to life.
In return, we will be constantly updating the game with new features, and bug fixes.

We also have a bi-weekly live stream with production updates from the developers.

You can join in on our community today by heading to our forums: http://forums.pixeltailgames.com
We are very active on our forums and are there to answer any questions you may have.”

Patch for Update 0.1.1.5

New to the Condo: Item Finder!
Tired of losing track of your items? Well, look no further, as weâve added a new item finder to the condo management menu. Just hold C while in your condo, click "Find Items," and the menu will display. You'll see a list of all the items that are currently placed down in your condo. Hover over them in the list to visually see the item or stash it back into your main inventory. Finally, never lose those pesky beach balls.



Changes:

  • Item tooltips now appear below the inventory UI as we noticed they get in the way of finding specific item icons and that's just terrible
  • Made Lobby 1 plate inventory icon a little bit better

Fixes:

  • Fixed inventory items (and pets) teleporting to the entrance of the Condo wrongly
  • Fixed a bug where the inventory would instantly close if the key binding was changed in the DefaultInput.ini
  • Reduced water splash sound effect volume
  • Fixed a bug where if you were noclipping and hit a ceiling fan or trampoline, you would fall forever

Please note that servers do not need to be updated for this patch.

About This Game


Tower Unite is a living, evolving world, fueled by the community. Play online games with your friends or make new friends from around the world!


Tower Unite is a large online multiplayer game that spans genres.

  • Play minigames such as Minigolf, Ball Race, Virus, and more!
  • Earn items
  • Hang with your friends in your own personalized house
  • Connect with others through a massive Plaza with various attractions and games
  • Party with friends!
  • Watch synchronized media such as YouTube or Soundcloud
  • Suggest new features - we listen!
  • No microtransactions or subscription fees



Tower Unite has many games to play, with more to come, such as...


  • Supports up to 16 players online
  • 4 courses with 18 holes (72 holes total) and more in the works!
  • Based on the original 2009 minigolf hit from GMod Tower that inspired other games
  • Hole-in-one fireworks
  • Wear tiny hats on your golf ball
  • Putt in snow, japanese gardens, mines, and even in the sky!
  • Simultaneous putting
  • Player hosted servers


  • Supports up to 20 players online
  • 5 unique levels
  • Roll around in a giant ball
  • Speed past tricky obstacles
  • Player hosted servers


  • Supports up to 12 players online
  • Fast paced old school first person shooting action
  • Survive in 3 unique levels
  • Blast infected away with 12 sci-fi weapons
  • Team tag elimination, with weapons
  • Player hosted servers



Once you've played some of the games, head on over to your personal space...


In Tower Unite, everyone has their own personal space, called a Condo.

  • Place tons of different furniture and items
  • Watch synchronized media such as YouTube
  • Place images from the internet directly into the game synced with online players
  • Listen to synchronized music with Soundcloud
  • Customize wallpapers, lights, and time of day
  • Invite friends to party, chat, voice chat, drink, and eat virtual pizza!
  • Play instruments like a piano or drums
  • Interact with various weird and cool items
  • Build unique creations
  • Get a pet cat or dog
  • Cook/craft food and new items


From the Condo, you can exit through your front door into a bigger online experience called the Plaza. The Plaza acts as a gathering point, like a shopping mall.

  • Gamble at the Casino
  • Play games at the Arcade
  • Walk along the Boardwalk and enjoy Bowling, Laser Tag, Bumper Cars, or ride the Ferris Wheel or Rollercoaster
  • Watch synchronized YouTube videos in the Theater
  • Shop for new items at different stores
  • Connect to the bigger Game Worlds
  • Go fishing and explore the ocean with your friends
  • Voice chat and text chat
  • Player hosted servers

DO MORE THAN OTHER VIRTUAL WORLD GAMES

Tower Unite isn't just a walk-around-and-chat simulator. You can run, jump, crouch, use jetpacks, get drunk, dance, sit, throw-up, lounge in pool tubes, and much much more.




  • Play multiplayer games of various genres online with friends.
  • Play online Minigolf with up to 16 players.
  • Customize yourself with accessories.
  • Furnish and design the layout of your personal space (the Condo) with furniture and interactive toys.
  • Invite other players over to your Condo for parties and mini-games.
  • Watch synchronized YouTube videos in a virtual theater or your Condo.
  • Fly around in jetpacks.
  • Imbibe alcohol to an irresponsible extent.
  • Join official servers or host your own server.
  • Hang out with your friends or find new friends!
  • Enjoy free DLC, no microtransactions, and developer support.
  • Collect tons of unique items with Steam Inventory support.
