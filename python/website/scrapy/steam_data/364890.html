About This Game

A Simple Side-Scrolling Shooter set in the Western Fronts of World War 1. Choose one of three heroes and customize your Battlecard loadout. Featuring Arcade, a score chaser that consists of eight different levels. See you on the front!

Honestly, I think the people giving positive reviews for this game have vested interest in the game or something. Most of them sound very forced and don't give any substantial reason as to why the game is good, other than "it's fun" or "the devs are nice." The game simply isn't very good. It just seems like a game that you'd find on any free flash game website - but far rougher around the edges. For example, there's actually no end to the levels (I had to read the other negative reviews to figure this out) - when you get all the way to the right side of the level, there's just an invisible wall that prevents you from moving any further. In the Streets of Peril level, on the far right side, you actually get stuck in a small area on the dirt because the game prevents you from jumping for some reason. In the arcade levels? You just instantly die when you go too far. The entire game has just such low production quality - and it definitely seems like one of those "first try" game efforts that might have come out of a project course in high school.

To be frank, I don't think this game should have been released on Steam. It would never have passed the quality controls of previous years.

UPDATED REVIEW

Updated 6/8/2015.
Since the game updated, I decided to play through a second time.

Update 6/25/2016
The game is looking polished. Though the artwork is cute, it feels atrocious at the same time. The simplicity look feels like South Park, and it would look 10x better if some of it was cleaned up and polished a bit.

The game has a fun arcade mode now.

6 ability cards that work similar to Overwatch's abilities. (Cooldowns, specialties.)

3 heroes as well to choose from.


Pros
+ Cute graphics. (Though could use some tweaking.)
+ Mostly responsive controls.
+ Very responsive controls.
+ Fun and easy click and shoot arcade.
+ It launches.

Cons
- Dull and slow gameplay. (Arcade is an arena of never-ending enemies. Never a dull moment.)
- Other troops do nothing except wander. Not even good at blocking enemy fire. (Lemmings are better at blocking damage.) (No other troops around, except for the buddy card ability.)
- No level progression? (No levels in current version.)
- Fall through the floor randomly, "killed by the hun." (Hasn't happened in newer versions as far as I know.)
- Jump on another troop member and you suddenly can't jump. (Fixed.)
- Get to the "end" of the level and you can't jump. (No levels.)
- Bullets not hitting their target, despite the clear line of sight and crosshair. (Firing is a lot more accurate now. Barely miss shots.)
- $1.99 seems a bit steep still, but then so do 80% of the games on Steam.

How to improve
// Get more sounds for the voices?
