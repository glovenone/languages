Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“At the moment, we are almost done the technical part of the game and finished to writing the design document. Also we are found the basic style in which we will go further and keep improving it. All forms that are available for play is working and ready to play(here is we mean that all forms which you will see is works, but more will be added). More forms hidden under the hood because there are still bugs in them. But for the further development we need to share what we have right now with you. And if you have any ideas feel free to share with us. Because we have two different ideas for match calculation. One is based more on analytic data, another based on strategy data.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“From 3 till 6 months until the end of the developments, plus from 1 till 3 month for bug tests and localization's.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“In full version will be:
  • More than 500 teams.
  • More than 5000 players.
  • More than 50 organizations.
  • More than 50 heroes.
  • Managers, trainers, scouts, commentators and other stuff.
  • Battle system and match simulation.
  • Around 300 pro tournaments and 1000 semi pro tournaments.
  • Better players training.
  • Financial reports and other data for micro/macro management.
  • Transfers.
  • Contracts.
  • Statistics.
  • Team positions.
  • Creating, monitoring and control your own organization and teams inside it.
  • Team meetings and relationships between players.
  • Around 100 items for match(heal potions, helmets, swords, etc.).
  • Treasuries.
  • Heroes bestiary.
  • Better UI.
  • Better email's and news system.
  • Different monitor resolutions.
”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“Currently you can:
  • Create yourself as a manager.
  • Choose the team.
  • Offer a contract to new players.
  • Setup your team's play style and training's frequency.
  • Analytic data for your next opponent in next opponent's report.
  • Match simulation.
  • Send submission to any tournament or the tournament may invite you itself if you are playing well.
  • Assign player's by lines to define where they will be play.
”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“No”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“Will be involve using following methods:
  • Game forum.
  • Events in social networks.
  • Communicating thorough streamers and game networks.
  • Communicating via email with anybody who have any questions or any submissions about our game.
  • Also will be a big plus if you are reported to us about any bugs which you found.
  • By the way will be a big plus if you share with us moments where you have difficulties which do not allow you to enjoy the game.
  • Also we are planning to share with you our development plan which will may change if we will receive a new and fresh ideas which don't have in our design document.
”

9 July

Alpha update 09.07.2016

Hello dear players,

Swiftly after the last, we’re following up with our newest update. It’s been nearly half a year since we began regularly updating the game, and we hope those who have been with us from the beginning can see how the game has evolved overtime. In this update we have added another 2 sets of hero skills. We’ve also populated skills for all heroes. (just populated not implemented). We also added music and hero images (and fixed the problems with displaying heroes and player images) to the game and started to work on skills (with a new 2D artist). In addition, we added the ability to level up heroes during a fight. We’ve starting working on FPS mode, making templates for the players and teams. If you’ve found any bugs feel free to leave it in a discussion forum).


More patch notes:

  1. Added music into the game after the many reviews. The initial idea was to play your own music of choice, but we added an unobtrusive soundtrack for popular demand...
  2. Added skills for following heroes:
    • Hedgehog
      • First skill reduces victim's armor, max stacks = 4.
      • Second skill damages enemy heroes in an area.
      • Gives hedgehog a chance to reflect damage if attacked from behind. Damage based on 2nd spell level.
      • When hedgehog cast spells, he becomes the only targetable unit by enemy heroes.
    • Rebel Knight
      • First spell gives stun and damage.
      • Second spell gives splash attack.
      • Third spell gives armour to all friendly heroes.
      • Ultimate gives percentage of bonus damage.
    • Stone Giant
      • First spell stuns and damages enemy.
      • Second spell gives damage.
      • Third spell gives a stun chance for melee attacks.
      • Ultimate grows giant which gives him damage bonus attack speed reduction.
  3. Fixed bug with displaying player's photo on team's frame and on transfer's frame.
  4. Replaced old hero images.
  5. Fixed the problem with displaying hero images.
  6. Changed the UI on pick and ban frame.
  7. Fixed the player count for each team. Guess will be correct if each team has 6 players (5 main and 1 in reserve).
  8. Added the possibility to fire players.
  9. Fixed the problem with displaying hero images in the fight frame.
  10. Added ability to level up the hero.
    • Hero kills and assists are rewarded with experience and gold.
    • When the hero receives required experience.
      • Level up hero by 1.
      • The ‘New Level’ button appears.
    • When you click ‘New Level’ you are given the option to level up a skill by 1.
    • Max skill level is 4.
    • The squares under the skill shows proper values.
      • Unmarked for unlearned skills.
      • Shows one gold square for each level of skill.
      • White square shown when skillpoint is available.

What we plan for next month
  1. Add images for remaining heroes.
  2. Add skill images for 5 heroes.
  3. Add skills for 4 more heroes.
  4. Fix the level up ability, meaning you can only level up ultimate on 6th, 11th and 16th level.
  5. Fix the problem with resolution.
  6. Need to add hero statistics panel.
  7. Update tournaments.
  8. Add tournament bracket and bo2/bo3 series.
  9. Add contract requirements for players.
  10. Add scouts and change the transfer process.
  11. Add skills for 5 more heroes.
  12. Add ranking system, where players will play and from where scouts will take the hot prospects for your team.
  13. Add the ability to level up your hero during the fight.

The next update will be near the end of July 2016.

If you’d like to share your thoughts or ask any questions about the game, please contact me here:
email: cyberteammanager@gmail.com
Twitter: @CyberTeamManage

Sincerely yours,
The Tambourine Team

20 June

Alpha update 19.06.2016

Hello dear players

It was a very productive month for us. We’ve been added the steam cards for Cyber Team Manager and changed UI to new style, also reworked teams and players (names, stats). We are plan a lot but made only a half, the other half will need to do in the rest of month. Also we are back to our CG work, so soon will be done unique images for each hero and skills. Also some of the heroes will be removed because of different reasons, but it’s will be only the 30% from total count. Also when will add the skills for 15 more heroes then will hide others (which dont have implemented skills) until the implement skills for it. Further explanation of this and upcoming patch read below.

What has done:

  1. Added steam cards
  2. Changed to new UI style following panels:
    • Before match panel
    • Emails panel
    • Positions panel
    • Transfers panel
    • Team panel
    • Main panel
    • New game panel
  3. Also has changed the team info when you are selecting the team.
  4. Added ability for adding training's second tactic. So from now you can to add another tactic for team, which team may use in match. But before add, you are need to set this tactic as primary.
  5. Formula for calculating training's stats which you are assign from trainings panel has changed.
  6. Each day the player will have fixed amount of points based on percent which you are selected. So here is 6 common groups which will be afflect on different player's stats.
    • Attack: Reaction, Brave, Aggression, Creativity
    • Defence: Technique, Team play, Creativity
    • Tactic: Technique, Anticipation, Flair
    • Team play: Creativity, Decisionmaking, Leadership, Team play, Work rate
    • Micro game: Last hit, Reaction, Concentration, Decisionmaking
    • Macro game: Positioning, Technique
  7. Fixed player's conditions calculations. So each day the player condition is restored after the condition decreased (for example when the player back from vacation or after the hard matches). Also in the day when team play's match the player condition restore less effective than in the day when player have a rest. Also if you are set training's intensity to high, then the player less effective restore his condition too.
  8. Added unique teams with names and players instead of current randomly generated teams.
    • 40 teams in 4 regions.
    • 200 players in this 40 teams.
    • 200 available players for transfer.
  9. Also has changed the process of assign player’s photo. So right now has 52 different modifications of this photos which assigned to players.
  10. Changed the teams assign to the tournaments. So it's 10 teams in each tournament with both top/mid/low players. But it will need future fixes.
  11. Added organizations. This is will be first step before implementing FPS mode. So the next step will be implement FPS mode :)
  12. Added the microeconomic for teams. calculation salaries / sells / profit, etc. Also you can track your balance change in finance tab. Also will need future fixes for this tab.

What will need to do until the end of next update(at least half):
  1. Need to add heroes statistics panel.
  2. Change tournaments to new one type of implementation.
  3. Need to add tournament bracket and bo2/bo3 series.
  4. Also need to change contract requirements for player's.
  5. Fix the player's count for each team. Guess will be correct if for each team will be only 6 players (5 main and 1 in reserve). What do you think?
  6. Need to add scouts and change the transfer process.
  7. Need to add skills for 5 more heroes.
  8. Need to add rankings system, where players will play solo games and from where scouts will take the hot prospects for your team or you can do it yourself.
  9. Need to add the possibility to lvl up your hero during the fight.
  10. Sketch up the FPS mode inside the game. It will be demo version and guess unplayable, so it will be hidden from you.

The next update will be near from 1st till 10th of July 2016.

If you’d like to share your thoughts or ask any questions about the game, please contact me here:
email: cyberteammanager@gmail.com
Twitter: @CyberTeamManage

Sincerely yours,
The Tambourine Team

About This Game

DON'T RECOMMEND TO BUY THE GAME IN CURRENT STANCE. BUY ONLY IF YOU WANT TO SUPPORT THE DEV PROCESS.

Select and manage pro gaming team
Do you ever dreamed of creating a cyber sports team? Or dreamed of when yourself play your favorite video game at the professional level? You will be able to experience all these feelings in our game and gain experience in manage activities. Create yourself as a eSport’s pro team manager. Involve in tournaments, win trophies, assign players to positions, rotate the squad, play MOBA and many more.


You can choose where to play your players and what the role of Support, Mid, Carry, Offline or Courier :)


Also, if your command plays well, then you get an invitation to the various tournaments, which will have an incredible amount or you may send invitation to it myself.


Play MOBA
And the most funniest part of the game: play in MOBA. You must be to analyze the opponents keep track of who is where and who plays where he can play, statistics and tournaments. Watch matches and the live simulation in real time.


And many many more will be further.

System Requirements

    Minimum:
    • OS: Windows XP SP3
    • Processor: AMD Athlon X2 7750 or Intel Core 2 Duo E6700
    • Memory: 256 MB RAM
    • Graphics: Nvidia GeForce 6600GT
    • DirectX: Version 9.0c
    • Storage: 120 MB available space
    • Additional Notes: You need a wide screen monitor (1360x768, 1366x768, 1920x1080)

This is about as bare-bones as bare-bones gets. I'm not going to condemn it as a bad purchase yet since it was $7, but this is the reason I can't stand Early Access.

The game, in total, has about 10 screens. The information you have is minimal. The only real options you have to do are to pick up free agents, edit tactics, which are three sliders, and press continue. Any sort of stats are minimal to nonexistent. On a lighter note, I don't want to completely write off this game. I think it has a lot of potential, it's just cursed with the evil "early access" label. The best description for the game is: wildy incomplete. Give it a few months before there's any sort of substance to the game.
