About This Game

Rogue Warrior is a character-driven, first-person-shooter, featuring Richard 'Demo Dick' Marcinko's explosive personality in an action-packed single player campaign, and intense multiplayer combat.

Playing as Richard 'Demo Dick' Marcinko, you go behind the iron curtain to disrupt a suspected North Korean ballistic missile program. Soon into the mission, you uncover a conspiracy that could turn the tides of the Cold War in the communists' favor. You must then take the mission into your own hands as you enter into the bowels of the USSR to destroy a technology that could change the balance of geopolitical power, leaving a trail of destruction in your wake!

  • Play as a Real Life Legend - Play as Richard "Demo Dick" Marcinko â a real-life American badass. His defiant, crude, and sometimes ruthless behavior has earned him both glory and infamy in the history books. Now you take on his explosive nature as you engage in black ops and go against the rules of conventional warfare to get the job done.

  • Brutal Kills - With more than 25 uniquely brutal kill moves, do what it takes to take out the enemy. No trick is too dirty as you maintain the element of surprise whenever possible - find and kill the enemy before they find you.

  • Targets of Opportunity - Be an instrument of destruction as you create tactical advantages by eliminating any and all targets that would otherwise benefit the enemy.
  • Multiplayer Mode - Go head to head against others online and even perform brutal kill moves on your friends.
  • Award-winning Talent - Golden Globe winner and Oscar nominee Mickey Rourke voices Dick Marcinko's in-game character.

Two Clicks
( 38.6 hrs on record )
Posted: 30 April
THE DOWNLOW:
Rogue Warrior is an old-skool corridor shooter with limited cover mechanics and minor stealth elements. Your objective is to one-man Rambo; an eight mission campaign, set in the Peoples Republic of North Korea. Choose to kill by stealth but be seen and its time for gun-toting action. The campaign is single-player but it has a multi-player arena style deathmatch with a few maps.

THE GOOD:
+ Simple gameplay. Other than picking up weapons/ammo, there is little to interact with.
+ Fast-paced action.
+ Cheesy voice-over from Mickey Rouke. Some great one-liners. Sounds like it was made while sitting on the crapper!
+ Who doesnt like taking on PRK enemies!? ^^
+ The music is old-skool tacky, gradually getting boom-tastic at the end. Perfect for this game!
+ Nice range of achievements which are fairly fast to gain.
+ On sale its very cheap.

THE BAD:
- Its very short. Too short!
- Cliche backstory. But who doesnt like them right!?
- Scripted levels are purely linear.
- Rudimentary graphics give it a minimalistic ambience. Well its an old game.
- Mostly similar enemies that just carry different firearms.
- Enemies attack in waves but even on Elite difficulty numbers are very low.
- Some MP achievements require other players. At least four. This is a problem to get them even if there are a few die-hards on the discussion boards.
- Very limited replayability. Everything is virtually the same the second time around.
- No matter what weapons you pick up in the previous chapter, they are reset at the start of the next one.


AND THE ORDINARY:
* Gameplay is fairly one-dimensional apart from minor stealth and cover mechanics.
* A.I is adequate but nothing to write home about.
* Short 8 mission campaign.
* 3 difficulty levels. Just go to elite. Its really not that difficult!
* Lite in-game story-driven cutscenes.
* Arena based PvP multiplayer. If youre into that sort of thing. Unfortunately all but dead.
* Carry 2 weapons but can choose from 13 different weapons (pistols, shotguns, SMGs, assault rifle, sniper rifle, LMG and a grenade-launcer. Unfortunately none are realistically modelled or very satisfying to use.
* Decent K/M controls. Unbidden tendency for weapon-switching to occur with mouse-wheel.
* You will need to complete the game three times; once at each difficulty to get 100%.

VERDICT:
Rogue Warrior is frankly terrible! Its a mediocre FPS. Ultimately limited by its age its levels are designed to be completely linear. Gameplay decisions mostly fall as to whether to kill with silent takedowns or alert enemies and blast them with whatever weapons you have to hand. However Its enjoyable in a minimalistic kind of way. Just dont expect anything like a modern-day shooter or a AAA title and you may find enough to saite your appetite. It will appeal to fans of brainless, easy to beat FPS.

Definitely one of the 'Its so bad, its good!' One to miss unless you love old-skool beer and pretzel-style shooters or if you are a sucker for punishment!


EDIT:
- After spending 4hrs trying to get 8 ppl to connect to a single host I can tell you this game has the worst MP facility of any PC game I have ever played and should be avoided at all cost, just for that alone. I still recommend the game but only for a cheesy SP experience, dont bother thinking about the MP achievements. They are nigh on impossible to get because you cant even get 4 ppl to consistently connect in a 2v2 game. Abysmal.
