17 May

1.3.2 - Crash on Startup + Achievement Bug Fixes

The two major bug fixes this patch are:

Crash on Startup:
The root of this issue was with GameMaker: Studio, in one of its updates it caused an issue with GameMaker and how it communicates with the Steam API. I hadn't thought that the reason for the crashes would have actually been on GameMaker's end. Hopefully this also fixes the black screens on startup. If it doesn't, let me know and I'll continue to look into it.


Achievements:
The root of this issue was a mixup between GameMaker: Studio and myself, for the since the Feburary Update, the only way your stats would update would be if you were to open and close the In-Game Steam Menu (Shift + Tab). That was why some people were having issues and other weren't. If at any time you were to Shift+Tab and then return to the game, it would start counting the stats, if you never opened up that menu and scanned 1,000,000 items, none of them would have counted.

Both of these bugs, to the best of my knowledge, have now been fixed, sorry about any inconenience that these issues may have caused.

There are some really minor bug fixes in here too:
Some typo fixes, audio tweaks, and experience system tweaks.

Thanks for the continued support,
-Brady

About This Game

Shoppy Mart: Steam Edition is the answer to the full grocery store cashier experience that many have dreamed about, and dozens asked for.


Shoppy Mart puts you in the shoes of a grocery store cashier, where it is your job to scan items and stuff them into bags. You'll be required to hand out flyers, chat up the customers, and ask for club cards all day, every day!

Here are some of the things you'll get to do in Shoppy Mart:

  • Nail the interview!
  • Deal with hundreds of unhelpful customers!
  • Bag hundreds of groceries for people you don't like!
  • Get paid minimum wage!
  • Unlock new customers!
  • Burn all 45 produce codes into your head, forever!
  • Hot moms!*
  • Talk to people all day without having a real conversation!
  • Find all of the cheat codes!

*'Hot' is a relative term and as such, can not be completely guaranteed.

Did you ever dream about being a cashier? Me neither. Yet, this game attracted me because I may have seen an educational side to it. That's why I activated the link offered in a bundle on itch.io and that's why the game is in my steam library too.

So, you bet that your job is to scan products and to put them in the bag while making sure that nothing get crushed because of the weight. Also, you greet people, you take their Shoppy Mart card if they have one, coupons if they have one, etc.. And for fruit and vegetable and seasonal items (Halloween, Christmas for now, I suppose we'll get Easter and bunny chocolate), you have to enter a product code that you can see by pressing SHIFT and your numpad.

That's the core of the game: the more you do levels the more xp you collect and you can unlock new customers like the Stoner, the Pringles look-a-like, the Hobo, etc... Besides, thanks to the weight now shown on the cash register, you can finally avoid more crushing bags and loose a customer. Because after 5 lost, you're fired and you're going to start again the game at first day.

Anyway, don't worry for the money you need to give back to the customer if they're paying with money instead of a credit card, you have the right number shown and what you still have to give.

And the game is mainly put into a world where everything is a parody: from the credit cards, to the memo given by your boss to the customers.

Another little thing: I've spoken about the game as if you had your time for each customer.... because I played it in Easy and that I'll wait until launching me in Normal where you have a timer and that greeting, asking for the card, coupons, scanning them, etc.., is buying you time.

Shoppy Mart is a fun game, even if in the end, the educational side isn't present (unless to see the difference between weights or if giving back money is also interesting). Pezomi, the developer, is present and answering you when you have bugs (like I had with achievements unlocking out of the blue, the game freezing because of a disappearing receipt or money or game that didn't launch anymore correctly).

So yeah, for the price, you have a little good game!

Cons :

Upgrade handbook, add much more items and barcode codes. Money exchange system is not that bad, so pass it.

I waited a long time before finally deciding to buy this..here is my review:

PRO's:
*Unique and there is not another like it that I have seen. Graphics are old school BUT that is made up in many ways.

*Customers are unique and each have their own personality, and it's just awesome when some come in the store saying "grumple grumple" and are in a bad mood..this makes it fun and adds it's own personal touch.

*Easy mode for those who do not like to be pressured with a time limit

*New items unlock as you play..as well as your choice of a new customer.

*Christmas theme update from the dev is nice, but I hope it gets replaces soon as it's getting old fast. But it's nice to see the game still being updated

CON'S:

There aren't really too many..but there are some.

*No save function..there is a save/load button but it only loads the last day you were playing, it will not save. And a few times it has actually started my game over.


*Controls are not smooth. For a game that is in it's final phase I would think most bugs would be gone..however browsing the forum to post about MY issue, I see nothing but error posts from other people. For example, picking up items sometimes takes a few times before it actually works, same for dropping in the bag, sometimes it's annoying that it just falls on the counter when you clearly had the bag open when you dropped it. Trying to put money into the register or handing the receipt to customers most times takes a few tries..and it isn't that I am doing it wrong, because sometimes when handing the bag of groceries to customer it completely gets stuck in mid air. This can be a huge issue when playing in normal mode and you're down to the last 5 seconds before the customer gets mad and you get a strike..after I think 5 strikes it's game over, period.


Overall the game is a nice one..and the dev is still updating it. I def reccomend this game 9/10
