About This Game

Join your favorite YouTubers on an Epic Quest in PewDiePie: Legend of the Brofist.

Probably for fans only really, and even then for those who like bland instantly forgetable stuff.

PewDiePie: The legend of the Brofist is pretty co- No I'm lying, it's not cool at all.

Remember back in the day when controllers had like, three buttons and roughly the same amount of pixels? Remember those really weird marketing games that came out like McKids and CoolSpot and that one game about the noid? This is essentially the modern day version of that.

You play as Felix Arvid Ulf Kjellberg, a Swedish man who, like me, enjoys yelling at children. But unlike me, Felix gets paid to do so. Suddenly, Felix's home is attacked by...Barrels for some reason and apparently all those kids he likes to yell at were kidnapped. Now, Felix is forced to go on an adventure where he meets people I don't know while making jokes I don't understand so he can save kids I don't like while fighting an enemy I don't care about. Also, he has two dogs that I sacrifice for my own gain on a regular basis.

PewDiePie: The legend of the kidney stones is a 2d side scrolling adventure that will take you around 3 hours to finish. The gameplay is as simple as moving forwards and then jumping with two buttons for powerups that aren't very good. At first, I thought the game was kind of fun, but the longer the game went on and the further I got, Felix's slidey and floaty jumping really got on my nerves. It feels unresponsive and clunky once there are more than three things to dodge on screen.

Now, I will admit, I enjoyed myself for a total of twenty minutes. before all of it got on my bad side. This game made me itchy if that made any sense.

I did not finish PewDiePie: The legend of the wasted lunch money, and I have no intention of ever finishing it. I got to the last boss and that was it for me, I had no intention of finding out what happens at the end. I'd ask who this game is for but there are roughly 40 million people subscribed to the Youtube account so I guess that question is already answered for me.

Should you play: PewDiepIe: The legend of the ♥♥♥♥thisgame?

I honestly don't even know how to make this review entertaining, I'm pretty much just rambling and hoping all of this makes sense, I don't know it could be the huffed paint talking.

Don't play this game unless Felix is your surrogate best friend, I got it as a gift from a friend and he is currently clawing at the inside of a coffin desperately gasping for air at the bottom of a lake.

Pewdiepie doesn't get a score, no, I'm not doing it, you can't make me. I'm sure this scathing review will hurt Felix plenty, he can cry himself to sleep on his bed made of money.

You get nothing out of nothing.

I'm going to go stare at a wall and think about Robocop for a few hours.
