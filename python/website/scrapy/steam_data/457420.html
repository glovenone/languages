About This Game



Surgeon Simulator VR: Meet the Medic – be the Medic and operate on the Heavy in this free exclusive for HTC Vive!

You’re leaning over a strangely familiar patient in dire need of a brand new heart, alongside an operating table full of terrifying implements you’d hoped never to have to pick up for yourself. What will you do? Which one will you reach for first? The bonesaw, or a sandvich…?

Now, you have the chance to get truly ‘hands-on’ and test your backstreet surgery skills in VR. Surgeon Simulator VR: Meet the Medic, allows you to step into the shoes of Team Fortress 2’s, The Medic, where only you can save The Heavy as his life ebbs away before your very eyes.

This brand-new virtual reality experience from Bossa Studios reunites two of TF2’s most-loved characters, who were originally brought to Surgeon Simulator back in 2013. Now, fresh into the VR stage, where you perform life-changing surgery under the gentle gaze of Archimedes, the dove from Valve’s hit game. Poke with a pencil, slash with a machete, chop with an axe, perform delicate tasks with a baseball bat; but be careful...time is ticking.

If you start the game and are staring at an empty black room, there is a glowing circle right behind you that you need to stick your hand in to start the operation. I don't know what they were thinking.

Surgeon Simulator was designed to be played with incredibly clunky keyboard and mouse controls - much of the charm was in the fact that it was so difficult to do anything, that you'd screw up everything in a highly entertaining fashion. Getting the operations right required extreme precision and lots of practice.

The VR version seems like a natural step, and it's definitely entertaining, but it's simply far too easy when you have full natural control over your hand(s). I beat this operation on my first try in less than three minutes, where it would have taken me a good 10-15 minutes with the original version. Even though it's free, it's hard to justify such a large download for 3 minutes of gameplay. If you haven't played SS before, you'll probably get more play time out of it - but this version doesn't have a menu or explain how to do anything and you might be very confused.

Essentially all you need to do is remove the ribcage and internal organs without making the Heavy bleed too much. If he runs out of blood, he dies and you lose. You can see how much blood is left in the elevated jar behind him. Once you have removed the organs, well... watch the "Meet the Medic" promo video to understand the rest of the operation.

As with the original game, the worst thing about this level is the ♥♥♥♥ing joystick that controls the ♥♥♥♥ing medic gun. Its axes aren't aligned with the medic gun itself so the medic gun doesn't move how you would expect when you move the joystick. I recommend getting the medic gun pointed at the Heavy's chest before you do anything else - if you save it for the last step, you'll never get it pointed at him before he bleeds out.
