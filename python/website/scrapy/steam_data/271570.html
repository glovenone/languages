About This Game

This is a Double Pack - you'll get 2 copies so you can give one to a friend.


Space Farmers is a co-op game only and has no single player mode.

Grab a friend, grab a gadget, grab a gun and get ready. Space Farmers is the most fun you'll ever have in a co-op game.

You're trapped on an alien space station and forced to share your secret British agriculture secrets. With no means of escape or tea making facilities this is all very uncivilized!

Luckily a hoard of hungry robots are attacking the station and your captors have vanished. Stopping only to pick up an Energy Shotgun and a Pie-O-Matic you begin your quest for freedom!

In Space Farmers you and a friend will encounter gadgets, guns, puzzles, pigs, a horde of killer robots and more. This one of a kind co-operative action adventure will make you laugh, cry and bring the human race together.

Every Type Of Co-Op

Space Farmers supports Online (peer to peer), Local Lan and Split Screen Co-Op. Don't forget that it's a double pack so you'll be able to give a copy to a friend and start playing right away!

Free Updates

We're continuing to add new levels and features to Space Farmers. We won't be releasing any paid DLC, all updates will be free. So get involved and let us know what you'd like to see in the game!

Posted: 30 November, 2015
This game has some pretty good ideas and some parts of it are fun, but unfortunately the implementation is very poor. The main thing that makes a lot of it not fun to play is the repetitiveness. Many of the levels have the same pattern: a super easy part in the beginning of the level (to get you used to the mechanics?) and then a harder part you need trial-
and-error/good RNG to solve. So then you end up repeating the easy part over and over while trying to figure out the hard part once.

The repetition wouldn't be so bad if your character wasn't so slow. It's like your character is walking in molassas. The default walking speed should be at least twice as fast as it is now, with a toggle key to make your character slower if it's needed for precise movement.

The other big annoying issue is that the hard levels are hard for the wrong reason. The puzzles themselves are actually a bit too easy. What makes some of them hard are unexplained mechanics due to the level order being randomized, the floaty controls, and the fact that enemies can spawn right on you. That said, most of the levels aren't really that hard, even the ones that are called "Hard". The one actual difficult level (and again, it's difficult for un-fun reasons) is about 10 times harder than the next hardest level for some reason.

Positive things I can say about it: the graphics and music are charming in an unpolished way, and we didn't have problems connecting to servers (not too much lag either). The levels that weren't badly designed were actually pretty fun and creative. But overall, I'd say you should only get this game if it's on sale or free.

Online co-op or split screen local co-op game with up to around 30 possible characters to play as square block figures. Has a total of around 25 co-op maps, a set of 5 different survival co-op challenges and 1 singleplayer survival challenge map. Depending on connection with co-op partner, each level has sufficient variety and tests the movement, puzzle-solving skill and teamwork in you and your co-op partner. Achievements are relatively easy to get with a decent co-op partner. The singleplayer map is also relatively easy to finish and unlock achievement and playable characters.

Among the cons, there is one very difficult level that is much harder than the rest, and online connection can be troublesome sometimes as the Steam game invite function does not always register in the other person's game.

Overall, I would recommend this game only if you have a friend or family member who can play the game online or local co-op with you and only when the game is at discount as it takes less than 10 hours to finish all maps and unlock all possible playable characters and character accessories.
