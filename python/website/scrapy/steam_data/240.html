CS:S. I really love this game. It's basicly a Source Version of the original Counter Strike, yet slightly different.

Pros:

Great Graphics and Shading, even on medium.

Good Hitbox (People say it's bad but I don't have any problems with it.)

Learning things such as Tactics like spraying or ninja bomb defusing is very fun with a challenge.

A community even better than the current CS, CS:GO. (Not %100 free from toxic.)

If you like Garrys-Mod and usualy have missing texture problems, you're in luck! CS:S offers textures for GMOD (Does it for ya, so no work needed besides clicking a button kek.)

No skins that you pay real money on, seriously CS:GO is horrible.

Cons:

The price of $20 is a bit too much, best off at $10 to $15.

Some guns when being reloaded such as the sig (You can see a completly black mag inside the gun as you reload, it's horrible looking.) it can get a bit buggy depending on the gun, mostly sig, once again.

Having to pay for a big server with addons and stuff.

Counter-Stike: Source is a game made by Valve and is a remake of the original Counter-Strike. I got this game on sale with the Source Multiplayer pack. This was my first game into the legendary Counter-Strike series (and the beginning of hours and hours of time wasted but still worth it).

Pros:
+ADDICTING Gameplay - This is what makes all CS games so fun. From the pluse pounding seconds of defusing a bomb with Terrorists hot on your tail, or the intensity of being the last man standing outnumbered by the opposite team where every move you make will determine whether you will clutch or be kicked.
+Well Balanced guns - All the guns in this game are well balanced. Guns like the AWP or DEagle give you power but reduce your speed or mag size. All guns are good, you just need to master them
+Fun Maps - All the maps in CS are fun. All maps are well balanced and don't really give one team an advantage over the other.
+Community Maps - The Community maps are easy to access and a lot of fun.

Cons:
-...

Overall, (10/10) CS: Source is a really fun game. This game will give you endless hours of fun, love, pain, and glory. Also, the game runs smoothly on my PC. If your PC can't run CS GO, then this is a great alternative.
