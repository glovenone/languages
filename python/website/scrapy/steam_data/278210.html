About This Software

Designed for a new generation of creative professionals Vegas™ Pro 13 Edit - Steam Powered delivers pure Vegas Pro core technology in a cost-effective package. New features include advanced archival tools, new audio metering options, and full synchronization with the Vegas Pro Connect mobile iPad companion app. Vegas Pro 13 offers more formats, more workflows, and more creative control than ever before.

About this Software
As your video skills grow and projects get more creative, step beyond Movie Studio 13 Platinum with Vegas Pro 13 Edit and the additional power of:
• Unlimited video and audio tracks
• Go beyond HD with 4K and UHD projects
• 32-bit video processing
• 24-bit/192 kHz audio processing
• Pro video scopes power up your final color correction phase
• Nested projects for longer form organization
• Application scripting for automation of frequent tasks
• Bézier masking for creative compositing
• Collaboration and remote control with Vegas Pro Connect for iPad
• 390+ Video and 40+ Audio key-frameable filters and effects
• Animated titles and media generators
• Support for multi-layer Adobe Photoshop® PSD files
• Closed Captioning creation

Edit standard definition, high definition, 2K, and 4K material with simple drag-and-drop functionality. The Vegas Pro 13 Edit interface provides an impressive array of innovative tools and thoughtful touches with a fully customizable workspace for accomplishing a wide range of production tasks. Quickly edit together that clutch moment in your game play or your latest quadcopter video with stunning video and audio effects and titles, post to your channel, and share with your subscribers.

Rapid Editing
Vegas Pro is known for being a rapid editing environment. Vegas Pro provides a multi-tool paradigm which organizes frequently used tools in a convenient pop-up menu directly below the timeline to provide fast access for a more efficient editing workflow. All editing tools have keyboard shortcuts so you can edit even faster.

Powerful FX
With over 60 Sony video effects and seven specialized video effects from FXHOME, and over 45 audio effects and filters integrated directly into Vegas Pro 13, you can enhance your video in any number of ways. Combine up to 32 video filters and effects per effects chain and apply them at the event, track, media, and project levels. Easily match color characteristics from one clip to another using the color match plug-in. Import layered Photoshop® files with each layer on its own track. Process and render videos faster with GPU-accelerated, OpenCL™-supported devices.

Add Narration and Closed Captioning
Enhance your game-play projects for the web by recording your narration as you preview your video. Add closed captioning to follow lockstep with your narration.

Masking
Don’t have a green screen? Bézier Masking to the rescue! Creative masking combined with the compositing available in Vegas Pro can dramatically enhance—and in some cases save—your project.

Vegas Pro Connect
Control Vegas Pro remotely from your iPad with Vegas Pro Connect, a free app for your iPad. Send your video to a friend’s iPad where they can leave comments and suggestions on your project. Reconnect and instantly bring those comments and suggestions directly to your project timeline as frame-accurate markers with text.

Add Animated Titles
Creating animated titles and credit rolls is as easy as a drag and drop. Place the titler on the timeline to create lower thirds or main titles. Use animation presets or design your own. Choose from a variety of credit roll presets and customize anything from font to color and background.

    Minimum:
    • OS: 64-bit Microsoft® Windows 7, Windows 8, Windows 8.1, or Windows 10
    • Processor: 2 GHz processor
    • Memory: 4 GB RAM

    Recommended:

    • Processor: 2 GHz processor (multicore or multiprocessor CPU recommended for HD or stereoscopic 3D; 8 cores recommended for 4K)
    • Graphics: GPU-accelerated video processing and rendering requires an OpenCL™-supported NVIDIA®, AMD/ATI™, or Intel® GPU with 512MB memory; 1GB for 4K
    • Storage: 500 MB available space
    • Sound Card: Windows-compatible sound card

Posted: 28 June
If you're serious about video editing, you want either After Effects, Vegas Pro, or both. After Effects is more geared towards super fancy effects editing, and don't get me wrong, it can do some amazing stuff if you've got the magic touch. Vegas, on the other hand, is perfect for more common usage; editing clips together, adding video and audio filters, tiling, crossfading, transitions, text and image overlay, etc. For me, Vegas turned the former nightmare of video editing and exporting into something I actually find easy.

It's just a bit system intensive once a project is underway, I've seen older versions crash from time to time (and the project autorecovery only sometimes works), but it's far and away the most impressive and reliable of the Sony software products I've tried. I would recommend getting the Lagarith Lossless codec to export with and running the final project through an external program like (freeware) MeGUI if you want it to look its best, as Vegas tends to chew up the quality a bit with its included codecs.

As a note to the cost savvy: Owning Movie Studio 13 Platinum on Steam will qualify you for a 25% discount on Vegas Pro which, as of time of this writing, DOES stack with other discounts.
