About This Game


Hit game mobile device is now available to the Steam! More than 150,000 amazing levels created by users!


Draw Rider - a classic racing game, made in 2D. Your goal is to get to the finish line within a certain time.

In the game you will find a huge variety of levels, the passage of which will open various options for character customization. In the beginning, there will be nothing difficult, but as soon as the training finished, will be a real hardcore! If You tire of the standard tracks, you can always create your own level in a special editor, as well as playing on tracks created by other players.

Lots of fun, incredible levels, crazy vehicles, all this Draw Rider!


  • Fun ragdoll physics
  • Destructible bike
  • Seven vehicles
  • Character customization
  • Mode Challenge
  • Slow motion mode
  • More than 150 exciting tracks
  • Level editor
  • The ability to share your creations with your friends!
  • Inversion colors mode
  • Online mode

Draw Rider is a game for extreme sports fans who dont mind getting their hands and legs dirty on a computer screen playing an indie game,every now and than.The game is for those who have a few hours of spare time.But before you read this review be advised PG(parental guidance) needed while playing this fun game.

The game itself is a lead based game as in you play on a terrain which looks simmilar to a hand crafted pencil lead drawn background.Also the game leads you to various types of situatuions and even though the application of physics is minimal. The game has seven levels and 175 chapters, each adding its own distinctness to the game.Every level leads you to a new circumstance.In some there is high gravity,some has Earth like systems,while some are designed for the hardcore extreme sports fans amongst you.So much is the vastness of the game that it allows you to edit your own custom maps for you and your friends in the editor mode.

There are typically 25 chapters in each level on an average,with the Earth level having a minimum of 6 chapters and the bonus level having a maximum of 41 chapters.Be warned though as you finish each chapter you face an even harder chapter.In Earth based system you play in a situation simmilar to that of what you face on Earth.In hardcore you get to do extreme stunts based on the limitations of physics applied to the game.In gravity you face extreme gravity,while black and white is for fun,Beginning is for the absoulte starters.(Had some nice time in it).Challenge is a bit more punishing than the beginners and lastly the bonus is for the kid amongst you.As mentioned they make up together a complete game.

Its not just one vehicle you can have fun on but there are bikes,sleds,unicycles,you can even walk,apart from cycling.You die a plenty of times during the game play or your vehicle breaks ,so advised dont try this anywhere.The game itself is fun and is sure to entertain you for hours if not days being a 2d game its a refreshing change from all the other 3d games or even from the ones in its class.

PROS

+The levels themselves are exciting and varied
+Good music,simple yet enticing
+So many levels to choose from
+Various kinds of vehicles to chose from,from cycles to bikes.from sleds to unicycles
+Its pocket friendly
+you can edit your own levels

CONS

There are no noticable cons to this good game but if i have to mention

-each chapter is locked and you cannot step jump
-Its a bit unpolished and the game can do with a bit more polishing (coloring)

Apart from that its an extreme sports fans dream come true And as I mentioned in the beginning PG is advised for if you try out this stunts even in this game you die a gory death some times.(Trust me I have died plenty of times and would die again !) All in all A solid 6.5/10 for presentation. Definitely a go getter for an extreme sports fan.
