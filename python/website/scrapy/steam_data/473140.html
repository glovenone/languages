About This Game

The classic maze based strategic bombing game is back.
Earth is invaded by aliens and you are in charge of your robotic soldier to defend it.

Navigate your way through the maze and destroy the aliens and their bases by strategically placing bombs.
Unleash a devastating Energy Blast to destroy everything around you.

Become invincible and destroy everything in your path, once you fill your adrenaline bar and activate a Tornado.

Features:
- Various mazes to navigate and play
- Collect coins and use them to customize your game and get power-ups.

Available customizations:
- 5 different soldier types
- 16 different types of bombs
- 7 upgrades (Atomic Bomb, Explosive football, Gun powder keg, Infinite bombs, Jetpack, Tornado, Protection Shield)
- Energy blast charge

Product received for free
Yeah, this may not have a great story like Bioshock and neither spectacular graphics like Crysis. But I'm sure as hell that you'll find it amazing if you just try it out. This game is like Bomberman and Subway Surfers had babies. It even has a nice old-school arcade touch on it. The goal of the game is to survive as long as you can, while blasting enemies and destroying enemy bases in order to get a better high score. I don't really know why people don't like this game, I really love it, it's exactly my type of game! If you've used to waste at least 2 hours per day playing Atomic Bomberman with your family like me, then you'll have a lot of nostalgia and fun moments with this game. You can even play it with your kid because of the simplistic and beautiful graphics it has, and both of you will get some fun out of playing it.

Pros
- A lot of mission objectives to let you entertained.
- The game is still getting updated. The developers even added achievements and trading cards after a few requests of them. They are also thinking of implementing multiplayer in their game.
- The music changes accordingly to the map you're in. And let me tell you that the rock music fits the game perfectly, it keeps you motivated.
- You can proceed in different zones by destroying enough enemy bases. Right now, there are 4 themes for them. The forest, the desert, the christmas and one I didn't have the luck to find yet.
- The mazes are procedurally generated, so you can go around the world as many times as you would like, there will always be a new maze and new enemies waiting for you.
- The Tornado power is badass as hell!
- You can upgrade the powers you receive.
- You have the opportunity of upgrading your powers even more after you've leveled up a few times.
- It's a very relaxing and simple game which keeps your mind away from real life problems.
- The sound won't keep hearing if you've Alt+Tabbed the game. This is a big plus for some guys!
- The settings page has options for: Quality Level, Screen Resolution, Windowed Mode, V-sync, Anti Alias, Music Volume and Sound Volume.
- Friendly graphics for all the ages.
- Collect coins and use them to customize your game, BomberZone includes 5 different soldier types and 16 different types of bombs to choose from.
- 7 different power-ups to help you in your quest of torturing those aliens.

Cons
- The UI screen looks like a mobile game, which is not entirely bad, but it feels a bit weird on PC.
- I've searched a while for the Leaderboards page but I can't seem to find it.
- It lacks multiplayer, which would have made this game a lot funnier than it already is.
- Still needs a bit of polishing. I've encountered a few bugs while playing it. Bugs like:
1. There was a time when I've detonated a bomb but the bomb was still showing up on the screen and I could just pass throught it like I was a ghost.
2. At random times, the game will tell me I've come to a new region even though I was still on the same map.
3. If you die exactly when you've received the Tornado power, you will continue to respawn spinning infinitely, one funny bug till you learn you'll have to exit and re-enter the game for it to fix up.

Conclusion
If Bomberman games are something you've spend much hours of your life on, I would gladly recommend you to try this game right on. It has the fully potential of being a next big clone of Bomberman, only a bit different. I hope that the developers will keep updating this game with new stuff. A new multiplayer mode will surely attract a lot of new people in the game. And heck, it will become the new addiction in our life.

Review

Well as you may think this Game is similar to the classic Game Bombeman, and I can guarantee it is as much fun as playing the Original. So what makes it different? Well you got different powerups like huge barrels that do extra-long explosion, atomic bombs that destroy a specific area, stuff that lets you run around extra fast, and you can kick bombs with a football upgrade. Also the main goal is not to beat the 3 other players like in Bomberman. Now you have to survive in a world with endless spawning enemies which makes this Game really challenging. If you like challenges and achievements you can also try to get those 4 hard achievements, which mainly ask you to do missions available.

Anyways I would totally recommend this Game to every Bomberman fan, but also to anyone that likes challenges and only got a few spare minutes.
(For the achievement hunters out there, the developer mentioned in the discussion section that he will add more achievements!)

7.9/10
