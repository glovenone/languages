Hyper Box - A real challange


Hyper Box is a challanging platformer with simple mechanics that you can use in different ways to complete the games levels.

The game contains unlockable skins and levels. A lot more will be added during the games current development.

Posted: 2 April
Having just completed this game, I can say without a doubt that it is one of the more challenging platformers I have played in my time. Despite some ragey moments, the game was very much a positive experience for me. I enjoyed the level design, Pixelated Cupcake did a lot with a little, as he constructed engaging stages that always helped the player grow.

What you'll find in this game depends primarily on what you are looking for. Do you want a rage game? If so, this game will definately scratch that itch, not by being unfair though, simply by being brutal in what it demands from you in terms of execution. Do you want a precision platformer? Again, this game will give you that experience, if you are willing to forgive the occasional glitch. Do you want a game that guides you? This game does indeed guide you, it just does so by punishing your failures and nominally rewarding your successes.

Basically, if you are seeking a platformer that will test your mental abilities as well as your reactions, then you should certainly give Hyper Box a try. Don't expect much in the way of unfair deaths, but don't expect to be able to skate by on luck either.

Early Access Review
Posted: 2 February
Hyper box is an extremely fun, addictive, but frustrating 2-D platformer. The difficulty ramp is insane. They give you three easy levels to make you feel good, and then they just throw a punch at you that you were not prepared for which you take to the face. I loved it! I was stuck playing it for awhile just trying to pass one level. One level for 30 minutes!

Pros:
-Increasing difficulty
-Addictively fun (you just want to get to the next level!)
-Different costumes so that you can pimp out your cube
-More expansions to come, and hopefully new challenges
-The creator made WASD available as an alternate to the arrows, which is astounding.

Cons:
-Gets kind of repetitive after some time of playing
-The difficulty ramp seems a little intense (I got stuck on the 7th level)
-Music is rather loud, and there is no options in game to make it softer (Only a mute button)

I know this game is early access, so the control scheme will probably become more customizable and the options added as time goes on. I believe this game to have a lot of potential, and I personally would love to see it thrive. If you would like to see how it plays before you buy it, I have a video up on my youtube channel that I welcome you to check out if you are interested! I hope this helps guys, and good luck getting past level 7 :p
