Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“Journey of the King is a traditional 3rd person RPG with hack&slay elements, embedded into a rich story. Using Early Access, we are offering our customers the chance to influence the development of Journey of the King. We think that the opinions of our players are extremely valuable. So with your feedback, we want to produce the game you really want to play!”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“We expect Journey of the King to be completed by August 2015.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“The full version of Journey of the King contains the complete story. Whereas during Early Access there is simply a long quest series.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“Journey of the King is in alpha stage, version 0.1.4. It currently contains around 60 minutes of original content. During Early Access there will be weekly updates that will include new content.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“We do not plan to alter the price of the game after Early Access ends.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We are happy to accept your constructive criticism. Your ideas are always welcome! You can post your feedback in the Discussion section or send us an email: silvesterlangen@me.com”

About This Game

Journey of the King


Hello dear friends!

Please bear in mind that Journey of the King is in an early state of development. Every day we are enthusiastically stepping closer towards our goal of finishing the game. We are incredibly enthusiastic about our project and are excited to share our creation with you.

In Journey of the King, you are an adventurer in a medieval Fantasy world. You are shaken awake on the side of a mountain, where you have been lying unconscious after a bandit ambush. Your rescuer is an old man from a local village who slowly nurses you back to health, and to repay his efforts you start to carry out small tasks for him. There is one task in particular however, that rekindles your adventurer's spirit... and so begins the Journey of the King.

During your journey you will gain companions and fight alongside them against old and new enemies alike. With every step into the unknown world, you will discover new adventures that will entertain and challenge you. Through all your adventuring, you will delve deeper into the mysteries of Journey of the King.

Early Access Review
Posted: 13 January, 2015
I re-reviewed this game on March 30, 2015, over ten weeks after my first review made this game look so bad that the developer immediately filed a copyright claim against me on Youtube.. Just to get the video removed ASAP!!

So.. how much has this game improved in the past ten weeks?

There is nothing new, it's the exact same bugs, problems, glitches, and nearly ZERO content as back in January when I last played it. I've made a Pro and Con list for you to look at, and despite the developer saying he's made updates since I last played it, it's still a horrid, boring mess.

Here's a big clue.. When you watch the preview trailer above, it looks pretty impressive. However, any time you see a preview trailer showing fly-throughs over his terrain rather then actual true gameplay, it's likely for a good reason.. because the developer is embarrased to show you the actual horrid looking combat, animations, and true gameplay.

Also at this time, there are 10 reviews. Only mine is negative. The developer is filing copyright claims on anyone on youtube showing the actual gameplay, hmmm. A developer willing to file copyright claims of people showing actual gameplay is obviously dishonest. So, I would bet money that just about every single "positive review" here was given a free copy of this game in exchange for a thumbs up review. - Just sayin'


Pros:
- Decent graphics for the world and buildings, especially the trees.

Cons:
- Way over-priced for what you get, even if the game is on sale for $3 USD
- Combat is bland, boring, uninspired, and nothing more the mashing your mouse button.
- Tons of gliches, bugs, and weirdness (game-breaking)
- Nothing to do as almost every building is either empty or closed up with no entrance
- Quests are kill a bear or bandit or find 10 coins, of which two of the quests are still broken
- Besides killing the same bland, boring and horrid-awful looking bandits over and over and over, there is absolutely nothing to do.
- Even if I missed certain mobs somehow, there is no way in hell that I care to actually play this game long enough to figure out how to find them 'nor do I want to fight them with this games clunky, lame, and broken fighting mechanics. Bleh.
- Potions cannot be moved to your hotbar
- No skills. No leveling up. No upgrades to character. No special attacks. No nothing except mashing your mouse buttons.
- No loot drops found.
- Character goes invisible and cannot be seen. (yes, even in 3rd person view)
- Jumping is ragged and hangs in the air and makes you look and feel like a lame tard.
- No lore, no history, no purpose set at the beginning, nothing to tell you why you are there, what your goal is, or why you are even playing this game
- Just nothing fun to do anywhere. No chests to open. No crates to smash. No animals seen running around. No wind blowing the grass. It's just boring and bland and the only thing this game screams at you is "UNINSTALL ME NOW!"

* This game is easily one of the absolute worst and most boring games I've ever played.

My rating: 5% of out 100
