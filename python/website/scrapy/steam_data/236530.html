About This Game

Be the World's Best Driver

Gear up for intense challenges only Hot Wheels can deliver! Join the ranks of Team Hot Wheels, an elite group of drivers competing for the coveted title: “World’s Best Driver.” The drivers are split into four teams, each with their own disciplines: Super Fast (Green), Outrageous (Red), High-Tech (Blue) and Powerful (Yellow). Compete in thrilling events across four Hot Wheels Test Facility (HWTF) locations around the world. For the first time, Hot Wheels fans can experience the legendary world of HWTF where the latest and greatest Hot Wheels creations are born. Pick your team, choose your ride and GO FOR IT!

Key Features

  • Customize 24 iconic Hot Wheels vehicles
  • Test your skills in 50+ challenges across 4 Hot Wheels Test Facilities
  • Extreme motocross, freestyle mid-air stunts, precision drifting
  • Adrenaline-fueled driving only Hot Wheels can deliver!

If being the best driver means driving on an ice skating rink, the cars are all so floaty the slightest movement causes the cars to careen off screen or into walls. Right away all the other cars speed away at the speed of light making it impossible to win. The AI opponents it seems are set to challenge the racer as if they have all upgrades already which is impossible on the first track. The game has a bunch of tracks but who cares if their impossible to unlock since you need 1st place in the first 3 stages to unlock the next set. Basically for the price you get 3 races thats it.

These should be the little cars they sell in stores, instead of feeling like their little they just feel like regular cars with really bad controls. There is no super small driving fun in a world where everything is bigger. There isnt much selection of cars seems for each team there is 2 cars, there seems to be unlockable cars but it doesnt say how to unlock them and who cares since this sucks.
