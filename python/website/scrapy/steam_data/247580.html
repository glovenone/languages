About This Game

Exclusively for the PC version your wizard will get all in-game robes and staffs unlocked from the start in this enhanced square edition. Remove the grinding time and get right to the action with your choice of gear! All of these goodies will be unlocked on every other platform you choose to play on

The once wandering wizards have found their way back home in Magicka - Wizards of the Square Tablet, now available for PC. Experience the mobile hit on your squarish and almost certainly, stationary monitor. But fret not, if any wizards still happen to be wandering you can still accidently kill them with cross platform support across all devices.

From the publisher that brought you Magicka, it’s more Magicka! Wizards of the Square Tablet features the innovative and dynamic spell casting system from the original PC hit. Experience a brand new campaign set prior to the original tale of Magicka.

Adventure through the new campaign by yourself, or “accidentally” obliterate your friends in multiplayer co-op, which supports up to four players! Play together with fellow mages across multiple platforms, which may co-exist in the same game session thanks to a twisted union made possible by dark sorcery!

Cast your spells across multiple levels, and unlock new powers unique to Magicka – Wizards of the Square Tablet. Experience the humor and chaos of this satirical fantasy world.

Features

  • Cross Platform Gameplay (iOS and Android versions sold separately)
  • Combine elements to create thousands of spell combinations
  • All-new campaign for single or multiplayer
  • 4-player mayhem with many “accidental” team-kills
  • New items and powers unique to Magicka – Wizards of the Square Tablet

System Requirements

    Minimum:
    • OS: Windows XP/Vista/Windows 7
    • Processor: Intel® Pentium® IV 2.4 GHz or AMD 3500+
    • Memory: 2 GB RAM
    • Graphics: NVIDIA® GeForce 8800 or ATI Radeon® X1900
    • Storage: 2 GB available space
    • Sound Card: Direct X-compatible sound car
    • Additional Notes: Online Multiplayer: DSL internet connection or greater

Magicka: Wizards of the Square Tablet has everything that people have come to love from the original Magicka. It has the awesome friendly-fire co-op action, the various spell combinations, and a few mild challenges to boot.

If you loved Magicka, there's absolutely no reason to pass on this game. For the small pricetag, this game has been converted from a microtransaction payment option (on other inferior gaming devices) to a legitimate buy to play game. In short, it's an actual game based on the Magicka we love minus all the BS that mobile games offer.

Content-wise the game is not actually very long. It's long enough for a day or two of fun spent with friends but when you consider the price tag for it, it's not so bad. What's even better is that there are no DLC in this game.

It was definitely a fun little game! 4/5
