About This Game

Your kingdom is under siege by a evil cult of wizards ruled by mighty overlords! Protect your lands from the legions of the overlords with a variety of special attacks and upgrades! Complete bonus rounds to gather crystals to spend on upgrades for your wizard! Enjoy a variety of beautiful artist settings in a cartoon stylized world. Encounter a large variety of evil wizards to slay. Defeat the overlords and defend your kingdom!

Features:
Fast paced action game play
Upgrades to improve how you control your wizard
Special attacks to blast down massive amounts of enemies
Bonus rounds to accumulate crystals for upgrades
Overlord encounters to defeat and earn massive amounts of upgrade crystals
Large variety of beautiful cartoon style settings
Endless game play with save functionality to see how far you can make it
And More!
