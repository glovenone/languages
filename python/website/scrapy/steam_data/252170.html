About This Game

Anomaly Warzone Earth Mobile Campaign continues Steam's Anomaly Warzone Earth in extra 15-level campaign. Originally released on mobile devices, the campaign is brought to Steam for PC, Mac and Linux exclusively in Anomaly Bundle.

AWE Mobile Campaign turns tower defense on its head. This time you take control of the attacking force, pitting your heavily armored squad against the destructive towers of an alien horde. Carefully lead your squad through the invaders' defenses, shattering their towers and turrets with special weapons and power-ups in this experience of tower offense.

As part of the "Anomaly" game series, this plays as "more of the same", which can be a good thing if you really like the Anomaly series.

This doesn't continue the Anomaly "story". It's more of a re-hash of the same story, abbreviated, for those who haven't seen the story before (in the full "Anomaly Warzone Earth"). Which is understandable as the "...Mobile Campaign" was likely introducing the story to the mobile market. However, perhaps being a mobile-targeted app, the campaign is short. I was able to complete the entire 12 mission campaign in approx 2.5hrs, not defeating every turret on every map, but nearly so.

Anyway, I got used to the massive sized-for-smartphone buttons (having also played "Anomaly: Korea" which is also a smartphone-targeted game) so that part of the controls weren't too annoying for me. Quality-wise, to me, this game seemed a little better than Korea. In fact, this was almost as good as the full "Anomaly: Warzone Earth" game.

On the plus side, I liked not having the separate commander run around and get lost in all the action on the screen.

In the end, the Mobile Campaign is worth a play if you really like the Anomaly game series. 7 out of 10.
