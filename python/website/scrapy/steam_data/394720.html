About This Game

The Legend Of Tango is a short comedy JRPG that tells the story of how Tango Fiesta was born.

Early one summer's morning, the renowned swordsman known only as Smith is reflecting on his future when who should appear but himself... from the future! Warning himself of the darkness that is coming Old Smith nearly gets a migraine before disappearing but Young Smith sets out on an epic quest to save the world! He must gather his friends around him, battle his way through a devious tournament of martial skills that the devious Judges have set up to find the world's greatest mercenaries, and finally face his darkest fears...

...so what are you waiting for? Grab your sword, gather your team, and travel to the royal city of Mirmingham with Smith and his crew - enter the Yewrogamma Tournament and save the world from its dark future, if you can!

Tango Fiesta can be purchased on Steam right here: http://store.steampowered.com/app/276730/

It's a advertising game for an early acess title. You get a reference here and there and the option to be linked to the store page at the start and the ending of the game.

This game is made in RGP-Maker with just the basic tools and the standard combat system which is really not that great in my opinion. The advertised game has it's own engine as far as i can tell so that doesn't really help to sell the titel.
The story is... well... short. You can't put much story into 20 minutes of playtimbe but there is effort to tell as much as possible. This leads to more text than combat which isn't bad but if you don't enjoy the humor like me, you'll see it as something negative.

On the plus side i have to mention taht the game is solid in what it is. A very short rpg. No bugs, no microtransactions or stuff like that. But i still feel that it doesn't belong on steam.
If you check some RPG-Maker forums you'll find free games with more time invested into making them. Longer games with advanced or just differend combat systems and way more playtime.

After all i can't recommend it but it's free, short and only a few mb big so i you might give it a try yourselfe.

4/10 because the dev is a reasonable guy.
