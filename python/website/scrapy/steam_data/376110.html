Black Island is episodic cinematic point & click adventure game with pinch of puzzles, why pinch because they are fairly easy.

After 57 minutes of gameplay and finishing the game Il jump straight on pros and cons;

Pro's
+Great overall sound (music, voice-acting, interactions)
+Beautiful cinematic videos that are like 50% of the game ^^
+Easy puzzles if you arent good at them

Con's
-Although it will have sequels it is still too short for my taste
-Easy puzzles if you are looking for challenging game


Overall;
It is fun and interesting game on short run. If you ask me is it worth 6,99, that depends on your budget.
I would instantly say it is worth, if it had achivements and trading cards.
Game main focus was put on movie storytelling and it was made to perfection, but for rest of gameplay, puzzle solving, items using.. I think developers should put more time in to it. But we will see next episode what will bring us ^^
