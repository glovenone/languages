A direct sequel to Cherry Tree High Comedy Club. This time it's a kinetic visual novel. The plot this time around is that the club is now formed and a pop idol has decided to transfer into Cherry Tree to follow her lifelong dream of comedy. It's more or less a slice of life anime in visual novel form. It's cute & well written overall. I was able to read through it in just under 2 hours. If you're a slow reader then this will probably take around 3. It definitely does not overstay its welcome. I also love how the game is split up into "Main Episodes" & "Sub Episodes". If you just want to read the main plot and avoid any sort of side story or filler you can easily. Sub Episodes pop up after every chapter or two and you can read them if you wish. They don't effect the main plot in any way and are just cute little scenes to break up the main plot. I loved how they handled it.

If I were to have any complaints it's that there is no way to view previously read text, save mid chapter or set the game at any resolution besides 640x480 (Windowed or Fullscreen). The steam overlay also doesn't seem to work either. It's nothing serious and it barely hurts the game at all! Would reccomend to all fans of the first game!

Although it didn't involve any interaction like its predecessor, it was still enjoyable and was able to be finished a lot more quickly than its predecessor.

The story is told through chapters with there being 12 in total, 7 of them the main story and 5 of them being side chapters.

Some points in the story were a bit boring, but overall it was a good game.

Now moving on to the important question;

Can I recommend this?

Yes and no;

What I mean is, while yes I can recommend this to some people, there are others who would not wish to try this game as they don't consider it as a "real game", its more of a visual novel but that doesn't mean its a bad thing.

As I said above, it was enjoyable, and if you have nothing to do on a day that might be rainy or boring, then sitting down and playing this through would be a good way to pass the time.

So if you like visual novels, then give this a try you might enjoy it, if you don't enjoy visual novels well then this probably isn't for you, but who knows if you do decide to try and give visual novels a fair chance you may like them, although I wouldn't recommend this to be your first visual novel :P

But to sum everything up;

Cherry Tree High I! My! Girls! a silly yet enjoyable kinetic visual novel that picks up where Cherry Tree High Comedy Club left off. 8/10
