About This Game

RFLEX is a fast-paced and challenging neo-arcade game. Frantically dodge your way to the high score in a series of rage-inducing levels while enjoying a kickass, original soundtrack. Experience trippy visuals while being pulled into a never-ending abyss of addicting, non-stop action.

  • 5 insanely challenging levels
  • Eons of addicting gameplay
  • Simplistic, trippy neo-arcade aesthetic
  • Rapid electronic soundtrack by Valiant (AKA Adhenoid), RollingFacade, and ForeverBound
  • Obsessive global / friend leaderboards
  • Full joystick support
  • Geometrically accurate inter-polygonal warfare
  • Realistic drunk mode
  • And much much more.

Follow @_wessles_ on Twitter.



For those with photosensitive epilepsy, an epileptic friendly mode has been added. If you are not satisfied with this option, and are unable to play this game, you are entitled to a refund.

RFLEX at first glance looks like a simple game (in some ways it's like frogger, you have to dodge oncoming traffic) but eventually you'll come to realise it's tough. If you're a fan of super hard games like Super Hexagon you'll definitely enjoy the challenge but for some it might not be your cup of tea (sadly wasn't mine).

You have 5 different levels (+5 Drunk level modes) and each one is more challenging than the last, timing is crucial as one slip up can result in having to start the level again. All you have to do to pass a level is let your little pentagon survive for 60 seconds, which may or may not be easy depending on your reaction speed.

The sound design and music is fantastic though and is probably one of the high points I found in the game, if you can get the soundtrack I would recommend it.

Is the game worth the price? I'd say yes, despite the fact I wasn't any good at it doesn't mean everyone is going to be as bad as me.

In future I'd like to see more levels added or different challenges though!
