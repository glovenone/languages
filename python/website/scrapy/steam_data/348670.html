Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“We rely on the early feedback of our players to develop the best possible survival online game. Join our Community Hub forums on Steam and tell us what you want to see in Immune!”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“Around 2016/2017, depending on player feedback.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“There's lots of content on our TODO-list like items, buildings, clothes, skins, vehicles, special places etc. to finish this game giving it more depth.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“Almost all features are already implemented and working, though sometimes in a rudimentary manner. The game world isn't finished and there's still lots of content missing.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“The game is and will be free to play.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“Join the Community Hub forums on Steam and discuss your ideas with the developers and the other players. We are very open and encourage players to talk to us.
Player feedback is very welcome!”

About This Game

Experience true survival with a wink in the world of Immune - the new open-world online game.
Survive the wastelands with the help of your friends and your hand crafted weapons. Build your own fort to defend yourself and your hard earned loot from evil mutants, bandits or reckless players.

Features:

  • Multiplayer RPG system with XP, levels and missions.
  • Optional PVP and PVE with wild animals, soldiers and mutants.
  • Craft and build weapons, armor, tools and even your own forts.
  • Plant, grow and harvest your own food or just go fishing with your crafted fishing rod.
  • Health conditions, medicine, vehicles, trading and much more to discover.
  • Open cases and collect, trade or win rare Steam Inventory items.

RPG: Role Playing Game
XP: Experience Points
PVP: Player Versus Player
PVE: Player Versus Environment

What does Early Access mean for Immune?
Immune is stable and rather bug free. The game is not finished, because it still lacks long term content. We are improving the game world and its contents with every update, which occur about once or twice a month.

Immune - True Survival seems like a pretty neat survival game in concept, but from playing it I feel like it is really rough around the edges in a variety of ways. Visually it seems a little bland, but on the gameplay side of things the way you interacted with the game seemed "off". This includes clicking on things to do actions but having your character miss if you are a few pixels off. or just lock up entirely. I would chalk it up to latency or something but it happened to regularly and was too reproducible for me to just call it lag.

On a positive note there seemed to be a variety of things to craft, and even though the visuals were rough I liked the art style and the top down view. I read that there are player skins and cosmetics now as well, unexpected but not a bad things, I do enjoy cosmetics.


I feel like Immune is worth a shot, so I would recommend players at least give it a try. I feel it needs some work to really feel like a complete game but not enough for me to just completely blow it off as it is now.

Early Access Review
Posted: 25 March, 2015
Played the game a few hours now with friends and really enjoyed it so far, despite some downsides (see below). Would recommend.

Pros:
  • Fun co-op topdown survival game
  • survival elements - start with gathering wood, build wooden club, go harvest food, make fire and cook meat.. take over house to be save at night etc.
  • loot - weapons, armor, ammunition, hats! :D
  • cars for 4 people!
  • client/server stable so far, ping also good (EU server, connecting from Austria)
  • challenging - should think twice about attacking that NPC which is armed with a submachine gun ;)
  • friendly players - easy to group up
  • ranks/achievements
  • some PvP
  • admin online and helpful

Cons:
  • Content still a bit limited
  • Map still quite small
  • car bit difficult to handle
  • some bugs here and there - get stuck with car and explode e.g.
  • no sharing function for doors - might want to get a door each if you are 2 or more people

Would definitely recommend if you like coop/social games, especially survival.
