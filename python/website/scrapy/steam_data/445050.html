Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“Because Early Access allows me to get user feedback while the game isn't finalized yet.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“Shouldn't be longer than 3-5 months.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“The final version of the game will have better general polish, some game modes and goals in-game and I hope to get online multiplayer in too, being able to paint the city with multiple users at the time.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“Playable, you can have fun with it, but there's no proper goals yet, this may change in the future.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“Unsure! People seem to think the current pricing is slightly on the high end so I will have to see how things go.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“By listening to user feedback and suggestions, and fix any bugs they may find if possible.”

About This Game

Paintey is a freeform do whatever you want painting simulator!
You can paint everything from your face to the entire environment.

Key features:
  • Paint all the things!
  • Paint balloons!
  • Googly eyes to stick onto anything!
  • An innovative (Optional) climbing locomotion system!
  • Birds!
  • Twitch chat integration! - Optionally spawns users from your twitch chat into the game.

Coming soon:
  • More levels
  • More interactivity overall
  • Multiplayer!
  • Time trial mode?

Paintey does not have any sort of predefined goals, you just have fun and mess around!
