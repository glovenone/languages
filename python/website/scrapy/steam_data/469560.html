About This Game

Can't make it to the bowling alley? No problem! Try your hand at Hyper Bowling VR. Play in stunning and breathtaking bowling alleys as you try to achieve the perfect game!

Grab your ball, choose your lane, and start bowling. Hyper Bowling VR is loaded with customization options for ball design, music selection, and scenery. Tired of bowling and want to relax? Kick back at Paradise Alley. Summer heat got you down? Take a trip to Icy Lanes and cool off. Enjoy aquariums? Head on over to Riptide Bowl.

Hyper Bowling VR is the only fun and easy way to enjoy bowling from your own gaming room. Accurate physics ensure a realistic bowling experience. Or, since you are the boss of your own alley, go wild and throw the ball wherever you want!

Choose from 7 different lanes with more to come:
Alien Alley, Riptide Bowl, Paradise Alley, Icy Lanes, Mansion Bowl, Disco Lanes and Red Pin Lanes!

Requires an HTC Vive
