Risky rescue is a good looking game with a simple premise - rescue people in a helicopter which can only fly in four diagonal directions, using two buttons.

There is a 50 level campaign across three "worlds" and an endless mode. The endless mode is where you keep flying up rescuing people without crashing into static buildings and mountains and moving objects likes rockets, aeroplanes and wind turbines. Oh and make sure you're quick too or the ever encroaching tidal wave will catch up with you.

You earn cash by rescuing people successfully and get to spend it on new helicopters with different handling characteristics; speed, acceleration and deceleration.

It is a perfect game to pick up for 10 minutes on a lunch break, and certainly has that one more go addictive nature.
