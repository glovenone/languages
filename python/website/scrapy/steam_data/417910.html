Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“We would like to encourage steam community to help us achieve best quality of the game.
We really need to know all best ideas and fix all known bugs before the master version.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“About 6 months”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“- Most issues resolved
- New, cool ideas implemented
- Clan wars feature implemented
- New items added
- Final items prices and points rewards for matches”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“- The version is playable for 8 vs 8 players on every level
- 2 of 2 game modes has been tested on our internal tests
- All UI pages (player improvements) are functional
- We need massive tests in order to find networking issues
- No clan wars feature yet”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“We plan to gradually lower our price and eventually become FreeToPlay with microtransations only”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We plan to add in-game bug reporting system and reward players that find and describe most important bugs.
We also want to make competitions and reward players that propose new levels, clothes or other items.
We also want to reward players for new gameplay features and modifications that we will find interesting.”

About This Game

Enter the world of endless street battles in Street Warriors Online - unique fighting game for PC.

- Compete with players from around the world using only real, close combat fighting techniques.
* No shooting or magic but a hail of swift blows of kicks and punches !
* Easy, one-click matchmaking finds best suitable fight for Your character.

- Control Your character in TPP mode using dynamic combat system with intuitive action keys.
* In Street Warriors Online You can dynamically attack or block incoming attacks as opposed to mmo games.
* You can provide standard, special or powerful sprint attacks that can cause different hit reactions of Your enemies

- Take part in battles up to 8 vs 8 participants in 3 different game modes
* Fight on many battle arenas of different player capacities ( 2v2, 4v4, 8v8 )
* Select one of 3 game modes:
* Skirmish: Beat up all enemies to win a round. Win three rounds to win the battle !
* Conquest: Capture bases by spraying over Your team's graffiti. Captured base generates points. Reach points limit to win.
* Capture The Bag: Capture the bag and deliver it to Your van. Reach bags limit to win the battle.

- Gain experience and respect to develop Your characters.
* Each finished battle gives You Experience as well as Respect Points reward.
* The reward is proportional to Your involvement in battle.
* Respect points is Your primary currency to buy different items such as clothes, power cards and new attacks.
* The moment, You get on the next level You'll obtain 1 attribute point to spend.
* Higher levels unlocks new items to buy.

- Configure appearance and combat style to make Your characters become unique !
* Compose Your appearance from over 150 clothing parts of 7 categories per character class.
* Prepare preferable attack sequence ( rotation ) that suits Your combat tactics.
* Spend Your attribute points to achieve best fighting results.
* Buy and socket different Power Cards that alters Your combat skills.

- Own up to 8 characters with different class and attributes build.
* Get new characters for golden currency or from golden premium bundle.
* There are 3 different character classes to choose from: Swarmer, Slugger, Bruiser
* Swarmer is a female class with extended speed and energy regeneration
* Bruiser is a slow heavy fighter with increased damage and health
* Slugger is a most balanced class

- Use elements of the environment as weapons to gain temporary advantage.
* On almost every arena You can find different items spreaded around.
* You can throw heavy objects like garbage bins or tires to hit many enemies at once and make them fall on the ground
* There are also light weapons like baseball bats, pipes and planks that You can use as one handed weapon.
* Throw bricks, stones and bottles at Your enemies to make them stunned and vulnerable to Your attacks.

- Buy and use energy drinks to scramble out from critical situations.
* There are 8 different energy drinks that You can buy from the store.
* You can take 2 different drinks on a battle and trigger them when desired.
* If You have more drinks of a kind - drink will be available again after Your respawn.

- Team up with Your friends to play together !
* Play together with Your friend in a 2-person group on free for all battles.
* You can create and customize Your unique clan of warriors and fight against others to climb up in the rankings !
* Higher clan ranking enables all members to buy special, unique rewards.

Street Warrios Online is a game where you play as a hooligan fighting in illegal brawls.
There are 3 character classes in game where you can choose from:
  • Swarmer: female, fast and with more regeneration.
  • Bruiser: male, heavy, slow and with more damage and health.
  • Slugger: male, balanced.
After picking a classe, the real game begins, you can select one of 3 game modes to start fighting:
  • Skirmish: beat up all enemies to win the round and the first team who win 3 rounds (doesn't have to be in a row), wins the battle.
  • Conquest: capture bases by spraying over your team's graffiti and avoid that the enemy capture your base until reach points limit to win.
  • Capture the Bag: just like capture the flag but instead of the flag, you have to deliver the bag to your van.
You can customize character, cloths, skills and the way you fight. During the fights, you can pick up weapons (bats, bricks, metal pipes, garbage cans, etc) to help you beat up your enemies.
The interface is pretty good compared to others early access games.. I didn't have trouble with the controls and the gameplay is nice, there are a few bugs here and there but nothing bad.
Also there's a rank system but since it's early access, there isn't much players do have a good pvp fight with. Most of the time I played with AI, maybe it's because of my region.. I don't know.
Btw, the soundtrack is amazing.

Overall:

Graphics : 8/10
Audio : 10/10
Sound Effects : 9/10
Controls : 9/10
