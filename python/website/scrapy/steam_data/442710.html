Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“Fantasy Tales Online is already the length of a full title with over 50 hours of content for your first play-through. However the expectation for a MMO title is much higher and early access is the next stage in our development process. We’re ready to grow the community and take on a larger player base of early adopters to join and help us shape the game.

A key policy that we have always stood behind is that players have a voice in helping bring our game to life. We have already stood behind this throughout numerous testing phases and are now ready to begin to scale our product with additional players. There are a lot of different gameplay features that require us to have a larger audience to help fine tune mechanics and gameplay.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“As long as it takes. We take your feedback seriously and want to make sure we’re proud of this game. We understand the complexity that goes with releasing an MMORPG title especially with a smaller team size. Because of this we want to ensure that we gather as much feedback as possible in order to properly balance and polish the game before we release.

We will release once your feedback is confirming that the game is as polished as we envision it to be.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“Here’s our roadmap for features we’d like to introduce, complete and polish:

  • New boss fights that you would expect in a AAA MMO title requiring players to strategize to tackle large obstacles.
  • Continue to raise our level cap by adding new zones, quests and unique items.
  • New puzzle elements and dungeon mechanics.
  • Improvements to the random dungeon generator.
  • Elaborate expedition quest chains such as sieging instanced towns.
  • Extended the crafting system recipes to allow crafting top tier items.

We’re also looking into giving users access to tools to create 3rd party servers where they can create their own worlds. We believe that 3rd party server can bring an incredibly creativity and diverse content to a game.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“All the core MMO mechanics are already in place. We’ve got classes with unique builds, a rich item system, quests, guilds, engaging combat and party system all in a persistent world. We’ve had a public test available for the last year while we continued to add content for players. Already our users have logged a combined year of play time with several users singlehandedly clocking in between 100 to 200 hours.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“Fantasy Tales Online is a free-to-win title and will remain that way. Early Access will feature a discounted Frontier bundle for early supporters.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“Keen players can watch our progress in (semi) real time by watching and engaging us on:

  • http://twitter.com/ftostaff - We post updates for most player visible changes as we queue them for the next content patch.
  • http://www.twitch.tv/ftostaff - Watch us code the game live. Players love to quiz us on combat, drop and mechanics during our steam :D. A great place to discuss balance changes
  • http://reddit.com/r/fto - Updates straight to your front page
  • http://forum.fantasytalesonline.com/ - Sign up and join our forums

Or get a summary of the changes as they go out by watching our patch notes on our Steam Page or tumblr (patcher) page: http://fantasytalesnews.tumblr.com/”

About This Game

Fantasy Tales Online is a retro MMORPG where you must form parties, collect gear, fight through dungeons and defeat bosses. Featuring puzzles, a rich item system and complex encounter this isn’t your run-of-the-mill grindfest MMO title!

Build Your Character


Pick who you want to become. Choose between thousand of customization options for your character.

Raid Epic Dungeons


Randomly generated dungeons featuring puzzles, bosses, traps... the works!

Build Your Home


Join your local player town. Collect blocks and objects to place in your home. Band with friends and upgrade to a guild house as the base of your operations. Elect your town major and contribute to it success by defending it and going on the offensive.

Explore a Massive World


Enter a massive world filled with quests and secrets. Start in the town of Bluevale where you must prove yourself before working your way to the large dungeons. Find all the hidden treasures, fight through the Cauldron sewers and explore the southern desert.

Unleash Your Powers


Stun, obliterate, vanish with your characters' unique abilities. Use them strategically to turn the battle in your favor.

Unique Monsters


Fight unique creatures and take down epic bosses. Employ strategy and party up with your friends. You will be rewarded for your efforts.

Free to play


Enjoy the game without paying, and if you want to support the development team purchase something from our Cash Shop that does not supply any competitive advantage.

After a few hours of play.... Over 100.... I can finally do an overall review of FTO!

Fantasy Tales Online
Free to Play w/Cashshop
No pay to win elements, Cashshop only adds items with no advantage to combat.

FIrstly I will add that this of course as you know Early Access. Now we have all grown tired of these games because we tend to expect more than we should. I will tell you that this game is very smooth and polished for an early access. The only issue is amount of content at this point. The dev team is small and is trying hard to add content constantly, however after about 2 weeks you will done with it all. That being said that doesnt mean you wont have anything to do! Now for my review

Graphics 7/10- If you like 2d Games(zelda style) this is the game for you and shoul be a 10/10, however because this is an older style of graphics I have to round it down a bit to be fair :). Overall pleasing to the eye and simple. The devs have done a great job adding detail to the maps.

Sound- 7/10- Game has charming music and very simple sound effects. Not advanced and not so dull you want to mute it. The usual 2d mmo music style.

Controls- 8/10- Typical MMO controls WASD and Number skill hotkeys etc. The controls are awesome and when refined will be a solid 10/10 for a 2d MMO.

Gameplay- 6/10- The gameplay is smooth as butter and the physics between you and the world are great. Not alot of bugs and the game runs at a solid 60 FPS on even the simplest of computers. Whip out those old Laptops because this game can play on it! The combat is fun and simple but can become VERY grindy as the higher lvl need EXTREME amounts of XP. However this is done on purpose by devs to make the (end Game) Lvls be much harder to obtain while any player can hit the soft lvl cap with ease. This game is a PURE Grindfest as it stands right now which I do not think is the direction the devs want to go, however because the 4 or 5 best pieces of equipment are EXTREMELY rare random drops in 1 or 2 raids (Instanced mazes of rooms with repeated mobs and a final boss) that means the ONLY way to compete for dps etc is to grind for those weapons which I cannot lie gets very stamina draining and boring... IF you can deal with the grinding for a few pieces of equipment than this will not bother you that much. Also currently there is NO PVP in this game.. it is on the to do list of the devs but doesnt seem to be a priority which is causing some frustration with alot of players because this is a great way to add unlimited gameplay to the servers.

Community- 5/10- This game has great potential to have a great community however the player count is very low ATM. The devs are very busy but they do try and monitor the forum and in game chat often. The devs listen to the members and try to move the game in the direction off of player input. There are a few bad apples in the game as far as players go but you tend to ignore them. Once people start playing this score has alot of potential to climb higher.

Summary: Overall this is a great little game that just needs its moment to shine. Once it gets some spotlight the servers will fill up fast! Right now they sit at anywhere between 25 to 100 players at any given time. As far as 2d Free to play MMORPGs go I consider this one of the best! I play alot on US Server 1 Diamond Shard so hope on in and gives this great game a try! Its FREE!!!

the inventory limits are a little harsh you get a set amount of items slots for your bank and carry ons and account bank (you get more if your a subscriber) with 4 slots for bag expansions for your bank and carry ons not for the account bank though but if you do all the quests you only get 2 small bags for your carry ons anymore than that or anything bigger is cash shop only

pros

about the only good qualities to the game are the devs trying to add new content even if its just a minor dungeon or puzzle to solve and they try to fix bugs they have managed to get alot of the lag issues fixed lately thankfully it was almost unplayable for a while

the quests and puzzles are pretty well done they will keep you entertained for a little while

overveiw

all in all it can be entertaining to play while lvling, but it quickly turns into a monster of a grindfest game after the mid 20's for lvling and trying to get gear, and a headache and a half to try to optimize your gear, all while dealing with a community that isnt interested in sharing or helping (not that i blame them when the drop rates are so horrible), maybe in like 6 months when the devs have filled in the quests gaps and finally learned how bad there drop rate and gear stat systems are and get them fixed, and it gets a bigger playerbase with decent people it will be worth trying again but for now avoid as anything but a time killer when your bored.
