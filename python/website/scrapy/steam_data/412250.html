Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“The Early Access platform gives us an opportunity to reach out to the members of the steam community to help us refine Empires of Creation. We strive to provide the best possible experience, and your voice matters to us. Our goal is to ensure that our game provides interesting and unique features that ultimately bring the player as much enjoyment as possible.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“We plan to ride out early access for 3 to 4 months before kicking into full release. This time will be spent exclusively on implementing community derived features, as well as refining existing systems. We plan on pursuing all features that appear to be within the community consensus during this time, within reason.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“Current plans for additions primarily involve combat expansion - e.g., more combat mechanics, more weapons, combat balance, etc. We also have plans on expanding the scope of Empire interaction and economy management.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The current version of the game has many of the systems planned for full release. Some are quite refined, and others are rather new and need more attention to perfect.
Players can currently:

- Explore a procedural generated spiral galaxy
- Utilize resources around you to forge your own empire
- Engage in Empire-wide wars
- Destroy legions of Pirate fleets
- Upgrade your fleets by destroying your enemies in combat skirmishes
- Assume direct control over any ship you can acquire
- Manipulate time itself”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“Our early access price is slightly discounted, as our multi-national team of developers will be working, quite literally, around the clock for the 3 to 4 month early access period to provide as much substance as we can manage.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“Our primary goal during the early access period is to add as much community influenced content as possible. We want this game to reflect the desires of our supporters wherever possible. Any and all suggestions will come straight through our team and a decision will be made on whether it's practical, and how to implement it.”

About This Game



Empires Of Creation is the debut title from Bundle Of Sticks studios.

Empires Of Creation is an up-and-coming Sci-Fi themed sandbox game in which the player takes on the role of a fleet commander, destined to make his name in the galaxy and ultimately conquer it. Forge relationships with surrounding empires and use your wit to establish a galactic monopoly. Colonize nearby habitable stars to expand your presence in the cosmos. The universe is your playground in this dynamic economy.

Enjoy fast paced, high intensity fleet battles with enemy ships as you race to the top. Whosoever can prove his superiority in the field and in the diplomatic sphere shall come out victorious.

-Gameplay-

After procedurally generating a spiral galaxy in Empires of Creation the player will find themselves with nothing but potential and an AI that has no regard for life programmed to do whatever is required to conquer the galaxy. Initially the player you will recruit ships from colony worlds that are barely able to scratch the armada of empire fleets, from this point you will proceed to upgrade them to formidable combat hardened battles fleets and eventually destroyers of worlds. The game turns from being a predominantly self centered ordeal, to a risk factoring strategy game as you obtain your first colonizer followed by claiming your first colony and beginning your own empire. Expansion and hostility will find you involved in galactic warfare with entire empires but this is essential if the player wants to obtain the ultimate goal of conquering the galaxy.

Empires of Creation is a 2D space shooter where you should be able to build a colony of your own, gain some resources and build yourself a humongous fleet.

Here's how you play the game:
1. Download the game.
2. Finish the tutorial.
3. Uninstall the game.

Seriously, this game is stated being in Beta while nothing really works (at least in Windows 10). Since this is not really a game review but more of a bug report to the developers, let me start by numbering problems I found inside the game while "playing" (testing if anything really works) for an hour:

1. Very incomprehensibly short tutorial that didn't really teach me anything useful about the gameplay.
2. What is it with this font size 6, make it 10 so I don't have to use my magnifying glass while playing with my right foot and left hand.
3. Control keys <> aren't working and you can't change them even if you wanted to.
4. Pressing ESC while in dialogue will freeze the dialogue on screen and getting out of the game safely becomes impossible because the ESC will stop working.
5. Closing mission tab causes ship to fire when in ship controlling mode.
6. Game crashed while generating background stars for small singleplayer map.
7. In Arena, I get constantly bumped by enemy fleet. Small little ships that are 1/4 of a size of my giant USS Enterprise are able to push my ship. Physics still apply even when in space!

If you speed up the fights fast enough this becomes a Pinball simulator with spaceships.
