About This Game

Addictive Match-3 game with exciting levels and super-cute graphics!

Who doesn't like Match-3 games? That's exactly what 'Clover Tale' is about. Get ready for pure amusement and peek into an amazing fairy-tale world!
Explore a beautiful world, complete quests, unlock new levels and gather collections! There's always something going on in the Nifflings' Valley. Sometimes it's funny, other times very dangerous. Even now, when everything seems so perfect, a black mist approaches their lands. What will happen to the peaceful valley of the Nifflings? Only you can solve all the mysteries and save the Valley!

Growing up with casual games, I sometimes miss playing nostalgic games like Bejeweled and Bust A Move. After a few minutes of playing the tutorial, I couldn't go on any more.

From what I have seen, the game play is pretty standard for the match it puzzle games but...


Micro-Transactions aside, these are some of my overall impressions:

- The mouse cursor/movement response time is quite clumsy - resembles that of the Hidden Object Games
- Feels like I'm playing a game encased in a jello castle: eg. "Resistance" when I tried to drag/match the wobbly tiles
- Too many items and clumsy procedures before moving onto the next round.
- I'm aware that the dev are trying to fix the issue, but the game crashed so hard that I had to do a force restart.

Conclusion:
Please don't take my monies. If these things don't bug you, go right ahead.
