About This Software

PowerDirector Ultra is an unbelievably fast and flexible video editing software solution, offering a unique combination of high-end performance and advanced video editing features. Its intuitive interface makes it a video editor accessible to users of all skill levels, from beginners to experts. A huge selection of customizable design tools, including unique alpha transitions, animated titles, particles and PiP objects, give you the freedom to explore your creativity and reflect it in your video creations.

Features:

  • Customizable Effects & Templates
    Hundreds of Amazing Effects
    PowerDirector includes over 500 effects, including animated themes, title effects, transitions, animated particles and PiP objects. Users can customize and control realistic non-linear motion for PiP objects and titles, and add new opening and ending effects.

  • Action Camera Tools
    Extreme Toolkit for Sports Video
    The all new Action Camera Center brings together the ultra-powered features action enthusiasts need to create high impact videos. Use it to stabilize shaky action video, correct fish-eye distortion, and fix color problems. You can also apply slow motion effects, or freeze & zoom to footage highlights.


  • Express Projects
    Pro Results, Fast
    Express Projects are pre-created video templates that provide all the basic content blocks of a video project. They are ideal for those new to video editing, allowing users to see how titles, effects, and transitions are arranged on the timeline and how they work.

  • Unmatched Speed with TrueVelocity™
    World’s Fastest Rendering
    TrueVelocity 5 leverages hardware and software technologies to speed up video processing and outperform all others. TrueVelocity is optimized for modern CPU and GPGPU hardware platforms from Intel, AMD and NVIDIA to maximize memory allocation and usage.

System Requirements

    Minimum:
    • OS: Microsoft Windows 10, 8.1/8, 7, Vista
    • Processor: AVI Capture/Produce: Profiles: Pentium 4 3.0 Ghz or AMD Athlon 64 X2
    • Memory: 2 GB RAM
    • Graphics: 128 MB VGA VRAM or higher
    • Storage: 7 GB available space

The Best Around....
There are few programs that I use with everyday operations and this is one of them. This is hands down the best video editing software on the market. I have done countless hours of research on this subject multiple times and Cyberlink PowerDirector is always #1.

The thing that I love about this software is how easy it can be to edit and produce your videos. Comparing to other programs this is very user friendly and really easy to pick up and learn. There is not a big learning curve when trying to learn how to edit your videos.

In this version of the software are a lot of new features as well as the ability to handle the new HD formats such as 4K HD, H.265, XAVC-s, 3D, DTS audio and Blu-ray discs authoring and so much more.

This software has great "Action Camera" features if you have video footage from a GoPro. It can help you quickly stabilize shaky footage, correct fish-eye distortion, fix colour problems and apply slow motion, freeze and zoom effects.

Pros:
  • Action Camera Center
  • Content-Aware Editing

  • Menu Designer
  • Motion Tracking
  • MultiCam Designer
  • MultiCam Editing
  • PiP Designer
  • PowerDirector Mobile
  • Screen Recorder
  • Theme Designer
  • Title Designer
  • Transition Designer
  • TrueVelocity 5 Engine

Cons:
None that I can think of, but there are times when the program will freeze when you are doing a lot at one time, make sure you have a strong enough system to do everything you would like to do at one time.

This software has great support and a great community forum (Non-Steam) where you can ask questions and get answers from experienced people.

I have used this video editing program for a long time with many different previous versions and it doesn't compare to other competitors. I have used also many different other video editing software and I just keep coming back to Cyberlink PowerDirector for ease of use and how great the software runs and edits videos for school projects, home videos, and gaming videos. There is just so much you can do with this software!
