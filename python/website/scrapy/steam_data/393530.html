About This Game

Been looking for the thrill? Then you've come to the right place! Here is a terrifying survival simulator of an abandoned hospital with elements of horror, which will keep you in suspense throughout the game! Insanity Clicker is a time-killer game full of horror and fear that will make you tremble in a couple minutes!

Imagine: you are alone, the sounds of heads and fists beating behind the bare walls around you can be heard even through them... What's going on here?! First thoughts: I want to get out of here as quickly as possible, but... Am I strong enough?


One click, two clicks - and you're not so weak and vulnerable as you were a moment ago! More clicks, more power! Hitting the wall becomes automatic with time! The one has to be hardy, need to adapt to this place. Pills, mutagens - consume everything you need to stay sane! But they also have to be found somewhere, so clench your fists and step into the unknown!


Still not scared? Then we got tell you about the inhabitants. Maniacs, madmen and mutants – are just a small part of the population of this place. And now the most interesting part - each of them is ready to eat you alive, tear you up into pieces or just kill by the injection of a deadly vaccine. Yeah, deranged former doctors of the hospital will also join the party!

So I decided to uninstall this clicker game a few days ago after hoping that some features would be fixed so they'd actually work.

As there does not seem to be an update with fixes to the major problems I'll assume they have not been fixed yet;
Items: So once you hit floor level 100-101 you have a CHANCE to gain an item and a supposed guaranteed item.

The items themself aren't even working properly. The affixes on these items, such as "reduces cooldown by x%" do not work at all once you cap (75%) the Illness that does the same thing.
Certain other affixes are not working properly either and in fact do the opposite of what they're supposed to do.

Then there's the "crafting". Basically you need a certain amount of "parts" to reroll the stats on a certain item with a chance for it to either get a better version or get a worse version, fair enough I guess and that's not the issue with it; besides having to get the parts (you need a higher amount to reroll higher quality items, which is ridiculous due to the sheer lack of items you'll be getting) you also need CRYSTALS. Now these crystals can either be obtained by BUYING them or have a chance to come out of briefcases that spawn from time to time.

This is a major design flaw. It appears they want you to buy crystals to then wasted it on some RNG thing which has a chance of screwing you over that also needs a high amount of another currency (parts).

The devs bring out new "sets" (note: no set bonuses) of items while neglecting to fix broken affixes and now they're even throwing in a "DLC pack" of €20 with items (of which the affixes frequently don't even work as intended or at all).

I've rebirthed (Amnesia'd, whatever) a bunch of times only to slowly progress on to a few additional floors after taking forever to get there again and while they state they "raised the chance to gain an item at higher levels" I have not seen anything of the sort. In fact it's actually the opposite.

My last 2 rebirths, after passing floor 101, I did NOT get ANY item whatsoever.

Moving on to the actual unlockable monsters/heroes; Eventually you'll get slammed into a ceiling as you'll need a substantial number of teeth to unlock the next monster which'll take forever to get (and a heap of rebirths) and once you unlock it you'll get slammed against a few more ceilings all the way until the last monster.
Then when you unlock the last monster, it supposedly trashes everything for you and you can basically sit afk and level it up until you hit "MAX" on damage, teeth and whatnot.

The floor level limit is 4825. Been there, done that and after rebirthing the game stopped giving me items.

So, for those that are reading this game's reviews and are wondering "should I play this?" My advice to you: Unless you don't like your life and wouldn't mind going full ♥♥♥♥♥♥, stay away from this game and play another clicker instead. Feels more like a cashgrab thing (you need 200 crystals to gain a certain amount of souls. Then when you're 1000+ floors higher than where you were when you spent those 200 crystals you'll noticed that you can now gain 5-10 times more souls from the same amount of crystals and the pace you gain them from briefcases, due to "chance to" is way too slow) without much needed fixes to certain features of this game.
