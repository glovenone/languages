Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“First, I had to fill up my piggy bank to give me the opportunity to focus on my passion. After two years of working full time on this new project, I realised that I would run out of money sooner or later. In other words, of time ...

I would like to fund this game in the "Minecraft" way and not like big companies do. Those who sell an unfinished product, line their pockets and run away.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“I don't know exactly but I think that in 2-3 years, there will be a big part completed.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“Here is the list of coming stuff

- Zombies, Badass Ranger, Dinosaur AI
- More Vehicles + Parts (Off-road, Cars, Helicopter, Bike, Monowheel, Skateboard, Weird Stuff and More)
- Weapons & turrets
- Alcool, Cigars and Explosions
- Lots more items
- More Crafting Content
- New Game Modes
- Random Generated Islands
- Steam Achievements & Trading Cards
- Performance Optimization
- Authentic Canadian Voice Acting


All updates informations will be listed in the forum at http://forum.clumsymooseseason.com”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“Clumsy Moose Season is fully playable. You can craft, draw and build structures, unlock bike tricks, customize vehicles (Motorbike, Small Van, Shopping Cart), race with your friends and bet on the winner, chop down trees, share and clone online maps, upload custom player skin.

Players can expect many hours of replayability and sharing of creations.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“The game will be lower priced through Early Access, relative to its final full-version retail price.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“I really want to create this game with the community. To bring something new in the gaming industry by listening to the players. Try different angles and see how far it can go. Everyone have the possibility to post ideas and suggestions in the forum, share videos and discuss about the game.”

About This Game



Clumsy Moose Season is a funny and silly game that allows you to draw entire maps with your friends. You play as a stupid moose living among the islands inhabited by several angry rangers. It's moose season but nobody realize it due to their low IQ.

You want a motorcycle? Kill zombies? Build houses? Ride a dinosaur? Hunt weird animals? Do pajama party? Do amazing bike tricks? Craft tools and vehicles? Go into space or burn in hell? This game is for you and the only limit is your imagination!

WARNING: This game contains cartoon violence and adult humor.
