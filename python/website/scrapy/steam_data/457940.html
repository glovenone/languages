About This Game

A new action game that can be played in 2D mode or 3D mode. A bounty hunter out for krog credits, it's your mission to destroy everything the universe has to throw at you. Complete bonus rounds to rack up tons of krog credits and use them to purchase ship upgrades and special attacks. Use your upgrades and missile attacks to defeat crazy boss encounters and see how far you can make it. Enjoy varying play styles, endless missions, and awesome upgrades.

Some Of Krog Wars Features:

You can play in 2D or 3D it's up to you!
Awesome ship upgrades that will help you on your missions
Use multiple styles of missile formations to get yourself out of tough spots
Tons of awesome cartoon spaceships trying to destroy your ship
Super bonus rounds filled with krog credits
Fun boss fights that will test your skills
Endless play see how long you can last
And More!
