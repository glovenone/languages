Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

““There are no real games to play, even though there are so many endless-quest-requiring MMORPGs and extremely easy Mobile RPGs, why? Why there are no enjoyable games at all?”, “Those are not the games I was really into.” That’s why we started developing Tree of Life. We have focused on the issue for a long time, day and night, over and over again.

Finally, after 3 years of development, Tree of Life was released in Early Access. With your valuable suggestion, we would like to make Tree of Life near perfect to build the one beautiful world for everyone.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“We have a plan to launch Tree of Life officially at the end of 2016 for now.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“Most of all, mastery will be fully implemented, therefore you will be one of important members in Tree of Life world, whichever you choose. Plus, the guild system will be fully completed, you will be able to enjoy various contents like The Mermen attack or Guild War. Then, we’re going to add various enjoyable event, items, etc.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“Construction, crafting, fishing, farming, ranching, adventures, The Mermen Attack, bounty hunting, etc. Tree of Life are full of lots of entertainments already. You can be anyone you want to be, depending on how to combine 24 classes in Tree of Life. You can be the last warrior in the world, or legendary master in the world. You can be a wanderer, fisherman or bounty hunter. Whatever you want.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“There is a possibility that it might be changed after launching Tree of Life officially, due to the fact that Tree of Life is on beta now.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We will refer to the community’s suggestion whenever we face important decisions in the future. For instance, if there are an important balance adjustment, we will analyze players’ data first, after that, we will adjust it.”

About This Game



  • Make your own village, anytime, anywhere.


    Thinking of building your own village to show your workmanship? Oh no, you feel like that all buildings on the street are kind of grand castles? If so, it’s time to play Tree of Life. You can make your own village anytime, anywhere. You can also be anyone. You can be a lord who rules a huge grand territory, or make a knight guild to protect the ToL world. What about a village for wanderers? Whatever you want!



  • The one beautiful world.


    Yes, our world is not perfect. It is full of dangers. So, it could be a good way to explore the ToL world in your room. What about the desert? Come and feel the sunshine, chase a mirage, find unique cactus and shooting stars… If you prefer the North Pole then the desert, why don’t you explore the snowy field on the North? Please keep in mind that you should bring your warm leather clothes. (… be careful with bears!) Can you feel it? Just explore the world!

  • Player-based Economy


    Are you tired of endless battles? But you still want to support your guild as one of guild members? If so, be a master, then move the entire economy of ToL. Build and maintain your own farm, then generate food to support your guild. Or just be a grand merchant, then transport goods across the whole world, or be a master who make good weapons in legendary level. Whichever is fine. That makes the economy.

  • 24 Class combinations & No restrictions


    World best warrior in the realm, legendary master, 5 star chef… You don’t really know which one fits you? Well, have you ever thought why you can’t be a mighty warrior and 5 star chef at the same time? Like... a chef who catches a big brown bear with his bare hands. As you want, you can be both of them N.O.W, since there are 24 job classes in the world! With no restrictions! YAY~~~ You can make your own character as you like.




  • Be a friend, or be an enemy. That is the question.


    The real world is full of difficult choices. Yes… but what if the Tol world is, too? You can be a good friend with other players, or just be a night watchman to protect the new world order, or offer a reward for criminals to bring the justice. Depends on your choice. However just be careful. Anyone could offer a reward for you!



  • Fight against your enemy!


    Grand castles always win a lot of praise. Even monsters will envy you, thus please keep calm and don’t panic, even though they begin the war to take your territory. This would be a superb chance to be the best knight in the ToL world! Be a hero by defeating your enemies who try to take your grand castle.


    So, get ready for Tree of Life? Just play your own game, make your own world. In Tree of Life world, you can be anything whatever you want to be, and you can do anything whatever you want to. Yes, it’s time to play REAL RPG. Don’t miss the chance to live your second life. PLAY NOW!

I've put off writing this review for a long, long time because I've very mixed feelings about it.

I've played this game since early Alpha release (around July 2015) and have around 1500 hrs in game over two accounts (this takes me up to about 6 weeks after the Beta release).

Firstly I'll start with the good / what I like about the game:

GOOD
1. The game has awesome scope to build a base / castle / home. You have pretty much got total freedom to make any layout of your choosing and you can design the base for looks or to be safe from raiders / thieves from a strategical standpoint.

2. There are a variety of skills / professions to chose from... around 20 (some not fully completed though at the time of writing this). So for a guild of about 8-10 people, everyone is valued and becomes a valued member of the team, which is important for the social aspect of this game.

3. The environment / different biomes looks great.

4. The crafting variety is very good.

5. The variety of weapons and corresponding weapon skills is quite diverse.

6. The map is relatively large.

7. The community liaison person (OddAlice) is very nice and works very hard.

Here's the bad / what I dislike about the game:

BAD.
i. The lag. There servers are often laggy when a moderate number of players are online (say 400-500). There are also random lag spikes and FPS drops for no apparent reason. Note: I am aware the game is not optimized yet (I'm running a top of the line Alienware rig, so it shouldn't be an issue for this game).

ii. The crafting system is currently a little buggy and its almost impossible to craft higher quality items.

iii. There is a desync when fighting (both PvP and when fighting PvE monsters). It is almost impossible to hit someone who is moving, so unless you both stand still and take it in turns trading hits, battles are pure frustration and become boring fast.

iv. There are many small bugs that are not very significant but very irritating that have been around since Day 1 and have not been fixed (despite people calling out for them to be fixed on the forums).

v. The developers do not communicate with the player base. They are silent for long periods and tend to only reply when there is a massive uproar with the players. Then the response is often vague or scant on content.

vi. The developers appear to be more focused on tweaking existing features / graphics, rather than fixing the small bugs mentioned before or adding new content.

vii. There is practically NO END GAME CONTENT.

viii. The profanity filter on the game chat is overly sensitive, so censors non-offensive words.

iX. At Beta release the developers covered 75% of the map in mist. You cant view these areas properly and you cant build on these areas. It's a huge annoyance to everyone who plays.

X. The game is a little too grindy. I dont mind grinding in a game and this is Korean so I understand and expect it to be a little grindy. The grind in Alpha was just about right, but in Beta they made it way more grindy.

Xi. Forever chopping trees... many joke this game is actually a lumberjack simulator. This is ok when you are in a large guild, but because a serious pain in the a$$ if you dont have 5+ players.

Xii. The recent milestones released by the developers appear to take the game in a very different direction to how it is currently or how it is referred to in their reviews and promotional videos. I know this is early access and things change, but this could take the game in a totally new direction.

Xiii. Mice infect your base if you are offline for about 12 hrs. After 24 hrs your base starts losing HP. This is VERY irritating.

Xiv. The devopment team are very small so new content is VERY slow and recent comunication suggests patches for new content will be every 2-3 months.

Xv. Patches / hotfixes to date appear rushed out and untested. This results in very buggy content and usually every patch is followed up by a hotfix.

TL:DR:

PvP is broken due to the desync. Its been like this since Day 1 and little has changed in over a year to fix this. There is no end game content (guild wars etc.)

PvE is currently broken as the crafting is buggy, you cant build on 75% of the map and there is no end game content (like quests / dungeons / bosses etc.)

So all in all I give this one a thumbs down. This is on the basis that over the past year, not a lot of core features / funamentals have been added and major bug fixes are still not dealt with (desync). Also, the last 2 months of Beta suggest a similar approach will be taken this year.

I suspect the developers dont have the resources to deliver on their promises so I am doubtful this game will make it over the line or it will be realease in much the same state it is in now.

I would LOVE to be proven wrong though...

I've tried unsuccessfully to write a review for Tree of Life for some time now. I originally added this to my wishlist because I was greatly interested in the sort of "World of Warcraft" style graphics coupled with the idea of an engaging survival game. Unfortunately for me, the game just doesn't live up to what I was looking for. In fact, this will probably be my only negative review on Steam so far. I tried my best to like the game, I really did, but here are some brief points about the game, some positive, most negative:

-Completely abandoned community/island/area. Nice if you like being alone, but still oddly errie to see hundreds of abandoned buildings.
-Lack of single player, lack of private servers. This is the main reason I dislike the game. I like playing alone sometimes, and frankly would like a private server with friends.
-Too much relies on other people. Not enough options for people who want to form small clans.
-Combat is clunky and broken.
-Music is very good but annoyingly repetative. Drives me crazy after a few hours, and have to turn it off.
-TONS of typos, TONS of spelling errors, and complete failure in translation. I give them a break on this since they aren't an English-based developer, but it's so annoying. The description for the french fries actually made me roll my eyes so hard I fell out of my viking ship.
-Good graphics, good animations, doesn't feel dated. I like the cartoony feel.
-Game requires WAY TOO MUCH wood. Everything takes wood, lots of wood. Game should be called "Tree Cutter Simulator 2016"
-Misty areas/foggy areas can seriously go take a hike. They provide no value-add at all.
-Mining sulfur makes you want to /wrist. In fact, mining iron, or any "high tiered" anything makes you want to /wrist.

The list goes on, but yeah, at it's current state I don't recommend this game. Maybe in a few updates, maybe in a few months, maybe in a year, who knows.

I spent basically 300 hours on this game. As a level 45 sewing character i can finally craft myself some crude linen cloth.

If you know what crude linen cloth is, you will understand my review.

If you don't, let me explain...
When you first start the game and you kill something, you get yourself linen cloth. This can literally be achieved in less than 5 minutes after starting a new game. After you get 6 pieces of linen cloth you can "sew" them into one linen cloth roll. This is the first crafting thing you make for your armors, less than 30 minutes into the game. Consequently this linen cloth roll is bad quality ("crude") because your skill level ("sewing") is only 1 out of 50.

After spending 300 hours you will be level 45-50 sewing. Very high right, there is no way you will make bad "crude" quality linen cloth right? WRONG. 300 hours later you will still make bad quality ("crude") linen cloth rolls.

In other words, you have just wasted 300 hours and your first ever crafting recipe at level 1 is still giving you garbage because the devs are too busy partying in hawaii to care.
