About This Game

Learn to fly radio controlled (R/C) models with aerofly RC 7. When it comes to learning and improving R/C flying skills, aerofly RC 7 is the number one choice for R/C pilots. Our state of the art physics simulation gives you a stunning level of realism. Together with our superb model details and awesome scenery quality you will feel as if standing on a real flying field.
You may choose from a wide variety of models, like aerobatic airplanes, helicopters, jets, gliders, scale models and quadrocopters.
After practicing on aerofly RC 7, youâll fly confidently and instinctively at the field.
Many features and game-like competitions will test your piloting abilities that put real fun into your virtual flying!

THREE EDITIONS TO CHOOSE FROM


aerofly RC 7 is available in three different editions. When you buy this game you will have aerofly RC 7 Standard Edition with a choice of 30 different models and 5 different sceneries. This edition is a good starting into radio controlled flying. However you may upgrade your edition at any time by purchasing an upgrade to the Professional or Ultimate edition using Steam DLC. The Ultimate edition features more than 200 models and over 50 sceneries!

FEATURES


  • Choose from over 30 models and 5 sceneries (Standard Edition)
  • Awesome sceneries with full Retina Display support
  • Highly detailed aircraft with state of the art physics simulation
  • Stunning dynamics simulation for a realistic flight feeling
  • Set wind and time of day for challenging training conditions
  • Different model types: Quadrocopters, electric and gas powered airplanes, helicopters, gliders, jets, and scale models
  • Suitable for many R/C transmitters and joysticks
  • Fly with friends all over the world in Multiplayer Mode (Professional and Ultimate Edition)
  • DLC available: Upgrade aerofly RC 7 to the Professional Edition ( 170 models / 40 sceneries ) or Ultimate Edition ( 200 models / 50 sceneries ) both including additional features like freely scalable models, aircraft editor, helicopter hover trainer and torque-trainer.

IMPORTANT NOTE ON USING THE RIGHT JOYSTICK, GAMEPAD OR R/C CONTROLLER


It is STRONGLY recommended to use a joystick, gamepad or dedicated R/C controller with 4 axes. This will give you the best flying experience.
Some models ( especially helicopters ) can't be flown properly when using a joystick with only 3 axis or less!

Visit the aerofly RC 7 website for a overview of supported input devices:
http://www.aeroflyrc.com

Nice little gem here, definitely pick it up if you're into this kind of thing. Aerofly RC7 is a flight simulator of RC aircraft and can also be used as a training tool for those interested in the real hobby. Aircraft range from miniature indoor helicopters to full scale, fuel-guzzling beasts, every one is easy to fly yet tough to master! The sim includes beautiful photo-realistic scenery from real locations all over the world with many different airparks to fly from, which all have their differences, my favourite being the variation in runways (gradient, length, width etc). Every one makes for a challenging flight without even considering the wind yet!

The hud is great and easy to interpret, it includes vital instruments in a simple easy to read format which can all be adjusted to your liking from the dropdown menus, everything works very smoothly and everything feels like how it should feel. From the menus you can also customize each aircraft to your liking e.g. throws and whatnot. I've found nothing annoying about this sim yet but If I do I'll be sure to come back and update this review.

To sum it up;

Pros:
Great physics
Awesome interface
Stunning photorealistic scenery
Smooth running, high fps on ultra settings
Easy super simple control setup(Regular Joystick, Keyboard or RC transmitter of the simulator type)
Plenty of great aircraft
TrackIR compatible
Very customizable (Aircraft flight surfaces, throws etc..)

Cons:
None other than the cost aspect, I got the regular edition yet moved to ultimate as I noticed a few of my favourite birds were in it. Ended up costing me well over $100 all up.

I guess in the end if you really are into flight in general, flight simming or RC aircraft the enjoyment had is definitely worth it. Like all flight sims especially those with payware they are not cheap, I'd honestly say this one is worth every penny, and I personally love it!

A must have for any AVGeek!

Aerofly RC7 is an RC aircraft simulation game/training tool from simulation developer IPACS. In Aerofly you learn how to fly a wide variety of RC aircraft ranging from small quadcopters to giant military jets in a number of different locations mainly across the US and Germany. With a huge amount of customisation and realistic physics this is a must have for anyone interested in RC aircraft.

While you read my review please take two things into consideration. Firstly, I played entirely with an Xbox 360 controller throughout my time with it. Secondly, I own the ultimate edition so some of the things I will talk about may not apply to the version of the game you're interested in.

+Great flight models, they all look very realistic especially on ultra graphics settings
+The physics are very realistic and from my little experience with real life RC aircraft the aricraft look like they behave as they should
+Super simple interface that's easy to navigate and stays out of your way when you're flying
+Lots of optional instruments and displays to make flying easier
+Over 50 different locations to fly at most of which are photrealistic while others are fully rendered 3D locations which allow you to use different camera views
+All locations have a number of different starting positions
+Before takeoff you're free to move the aircraft around
+Over 200 different aircraft which have a good variety of size and type of aircraft
+Some cool 1:1 scale aircraft
+For the most part it runs fantastically, I'm running it all just below max settings at around 120FPS
+Lots of customisation options for aircraft including their size and mass
+Can be played using KB&M, a controller, a flightstick or an actual RC controller all of which are easy to configure
+Smooth controls, the aircraft all feel very responsive
+You can play it splitscreen

+/-Sound effects are hit and miss, some are excellent while others don't sound real at all

-As has been said by other reviewers it's a bit too forgiving when it comes to stall recovery
-You can't zoom in on the stationary camera (the only one available for most locations) so it can be very easy to lose site of your aircraft
-The in-game smoke system available on some aircraft has a huge hit on performance, when the aircraft was close to the camera with smoke on I noticed a drop of up to 100 frames
-Helis and gliders show very little to no visual damage when they crash apart from their rotors looking crushed or damaged

Verdict:
9

If you have any interest in RC aircraft then this is a must buy piece of software with a wide variety of great looking models and great physics. Whether you're new to RC aircraft like myself or a veteran RC pilot you're gonna like this a lot.

Well as you know there are 3 top RC simulators (RealFlight, Phoenix and Aerofly RC) in the market and this simulator is one of them. I have both RealFlight and Phoenix RC simulators and I just bought ultimate edition of Aerofly RC to compare with them.
To be honest, It is very hard to compare all those 3 top rc simulators with each other, they are all good and they have all pros and cons. However,instead of comparing all those 3 games, I will try to explain my opinion about Aerofly RC.
The Graphics are really good and it will probably satisfy rc sim fans.
The Gameplay mechanics are really good and this simulator will really help you in real life if you are beginner. Especially, I have some of the in-game plane models in real life and game really gave me feeling that I am flying in real life. By the way, I am using Art-tech 6ch simulator transmitter which is cheap and easy to use on your computer.
Sounds are good enough for you to feel realism and sucks you in the game.
The aircraft details are fine and realistic
The weather is good. Especially if you fly your rc models at noon you can set up the time of the day and practice not to fly in to the sun.
The wind is effective and normally in my city its rare to see weather without wind, so in the game I am using wind settings to be better rc pilot in real life.
Summary:
Best part; In my opinion, the hardest part of the rc plane is landing and this game will really help me to increase my skill about landings
I wish; there are some kind of missions which can add more playable options with friends, the game is a bit expansive and you really need to buy ultimate edition if you want more and you will want more... In some sceneries you can not change the views. There can be more planes, especially some electric motored planes are not enough for me because in real life I am just flying with electric motored rc planes.
In conclusion: this simulator is really great and it will give you both pleasure and training for RC models. I insist that you should use simulator controller (with cable if you can) instead of xbox or other type of controller. The other simulators are also great. However, this one is enough for you if you are searching for a really good simulator which will train you for real life or just enjoy multiplayer with friends.
