PATCH 4.62 is here!

"Terror from the Deep Hell" event returns!


The event lasts 6 days.

Playerâs job is to collect 4 kinds of Coin fragment - they can be gained by raiding the event camps (and Portals).

About This Game

Are you interested in MMORTS fantasy oldschool wargame? Take part in the strong cooperative features, trade with other kingdoms, conquer and defend your provinces and lead your alliance to victory!



YOUR CITY
  • choose one of four different races (Orc, Elf, Dwarf, Undead)
  • create powerful economy
  • research new units or spells



STRATEGY
  • conquer new territories
  • defend your fortresses
  • setup alliance, create pacts, destroy enemies
  • PVP (strong teamplay element)
  • PVE (regular events)



HERO
  • 90+ unique artifacts
  • 90+ hero skills to choose from
  • 20+ spells



WORLDS
  • new realm starts every two weeks
  • 97-day long server ending with a challenging bossfight
  • many special events with unique artifacts and units
  • possible to play on web / iOS / Android

You can spend real money to speed up training time.
You can spend real money to buy weapons and armor for your hero.

It is more like Travian where the higher levels raid the lower levels 5 minutes after the new player protection wears off in 5 days. There is no bunker, so when you get raided you will lose 100% of all your resources.

Some good things for a browser game, forge, magic spells, basic research.
