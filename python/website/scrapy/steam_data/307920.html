About This Game

Farm World is the famous farm-extension of the successful and award-winning Wildlife Park 2.
Wildlife Park 2 - Farm World will completely satisfy animal lovers and farm fans: an incomparable natural experience â right in your living room! Become a zoo, respectively farm manager and take good care of your animal charges!
In Farm World, players can build their own farm and have to take care of their farm animals such as cows, pigs, sheep and chickens. The breeding of many new animals is a major challenge. At the same time the production of food is important: The fruit and vegetable gardens should always be well maintained. These farm workers and harvesters can be hired to assist the players best efforts.

Farm World is fully independent, Wildlife Park 2 IS NOT required - but works together!

Posted: 2 August, 2014
Overall I like the game. Has a few improvements like choosing skin varients and being able to sterilize animals. The missions are a little more challenging. I liked the new buildings and decorations. I loved the new maps. There are many new maps to chose from that vary in climate and set up. If you like plants, it has crop farming which can replace your greenhouse. I liked the animals better and caring for the plants wasn't very fun for me.

A little buggy for me. The skin varients didn't work for me in freestyle, only in the missions. The narrator for the missions didn't speak (which maybe you prefer, but I would rather hear him talk). Also all the animals constantly vocalized so if you went to first-person mode near a large group of animals you might find all the noise quite annoying.

The expansions for WLP2 don't tie together very well. For example, if I wanted to play Crazy Zoo, I could still get the Farm World animals, but thats it. None of the plants, buildings, or decorations would transfer to the other expansion, which is kind of annoying.

I enjoy playing the game, it would just be a lot better if they fixed the buggy stuff.
