3 April

Update 13 - New Hunter: Red Officially Released

Hey hunters,

Today our new playable hunter, Red, is finally out of Beta state and officially released!

To unlock Red, you must break open 20 Kami Cages on Adventure or Endless modes. I hope you have a lot of fun with her!

I have also fixed a long-time annoyance for gamepad players: sometimes you start to aim down sights, and the camera locks into a certain direction, but them you want to turn to the other side while still aiming… and the camera stays there!

This is fine for mouse+keyboard, but could be improved for gamepads. Let me know if you like it right now!

And here’s our usual links:

www.relichunters.com.br

www.relichunters.com.br/forums

www.roguesnail.com

My twitter: http://twitter.com/markventurelli

Happy hunting!

Cheers,

~Mark

Red can be unlocked by freeing Kamis from their cages 20 times;
3 new Steam Achievements: "Here Comes The Rider In Red", "Legendary Red" and "Freedom!";

*Gameplay*

20 March

Update 12 - Polish, Fixes, New Gun

Hey hunters,

Feels good to be back to weekend updates on Zero!

This weekend I have polished Red, our new hunter, a little bit. She is getting some fixes, as well as a slight nerf. This should put her more on par with the other Hunters.

Betu is at GDC since last week (and will still be away for a few days) and I need him to complete some missing art assets for Red. So she’s still considered Beta. Expect her full release in Update #13!

As a bonus, I have put a new gun in the game: the Blast Pistol. It is a heavy ammo pistol that fires exploding shots. It is… a blast ;)

And if you want to make mods (and can forgive me for that pun I just did) maybe you’ll want to check out our new tutorial video. It teaches you how to create a rocket that throws monsters.

Here it is: https://youtu.be/sAAkmbrNKw0

And here’s our usual links:

www.relichunters.com.br

www.relichunters.com.br/forums

www.roguesnail.com

My twitter: http://twitter.com/markventurelli


I have also started work on the “Community Pack”, which involves a bit of code refactoring and other boring stuff, but the end goal is to gather all our favorite stuff from what you, the community, have created last year and pack it all together in a massive update to the game!

Happy hunting!

Cheers,

~Mark

About This Game

A 100% FREE shooter from the creators of Chroma Squad, Knights of Pen & Paper and Dungeonland





Relic Hunters Zero is a FREE game about shooting evil space ducks and evil space turtles with tiny cute guns. It’s fast, it’s tactical, and feels delicious and smooth to play. Unlock new weapons, characters and relics to defeat the Ducan Commander.

The game's source code is also freely available, so you can find or create your own mods very easily!

This is 100% a labor of love from two developers. If you have a suggestion for the game, let them know - if they don't do it, maybe the community itself will!




  • "Updated Forever" - open development lets anyone contribute to the game’s future;
  • Six playable characters;
  • Local Co-Op for up to 2 players;
  • Play with mouse and keyboard + Full Xbox360 gamepad support;
  • Amazing lo-fi soundtrack by Goto80;
  • 12-level "Adventure Mode" with unlockable items, characters and relics;
  • Challenging "Endless Mode" with unique mechanics and economy;
