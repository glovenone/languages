Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“As always, projects such as these are a risk for the developers. But after seeing the level of interest and excitement for Dead Sea, we decided to quit our day jobs and pursue the game full time! The funds raised will be used to employ more developers and artists who possess the necessary skills and experience to deliver a quality prduct. Phoenix Studios is currently a small team that is based in Bangkok, Thailand, in which we hope to expand. Our country and government are not keen on video games, and we plan to change that with the help of all our fans and supporters. You have all given us an immense boost in motivation and confidence, and we are dedicated to producing the best possible game!”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“The Game is still in development, and we need your contributions in order to reach completion. Our goal for this game is to give the players mysterious clues and puzzles, with the freedom to make decisions in a vast open world setting. We have come a very long way and plan to improve current mechanics, as well as implement many new features. We are shooting for a full release by the end of 2015.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“
  • Full Survival System
  • 3 main story-lines and the ability to choose paths.
  • Intricate crafting system.
  • More areas and locations on the map.
  • Co-Op Mode
  • Each creature/monster will have its own life cycle and routine.
  • Giant bosses and several more creatures.
  • Save your progression by sleeping on the bed that is located in your camp. You will re-spawn to the nearest camp location when you die.
  • Avoid starvation by hunting different animals. Eventually, you will acquire a skill to farm your own food.
  • Customized Vehicles
”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“Early Access v. 0.4”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“Dead Sea is currently $5.00 until it’s next update that contains a full survival system which is more intricate. It will be priced at $15.00.
How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“Players/Supporters are free to ask questions and request ideas via our facebook page and live streams.”

About This Game

Dead Sea is an open-world, first-person survival game. It also contains elements of horror, adventure, and puzzle solving that creates a hardcore gaming experience. The game is difficult but not overkill; surviving isn’t supposed to be easy. On your way you will encounter many mysterious story, clues and puzzles leading to various paths that can be explored. Your main objective is to overcome the harsh and brutal desert. Not only will we make a great game, we will make it an enjoyable experience for everyone.


    KEY FEATURES IN Early Access 0.5 *
  • The power of CRYENGINE with 4K resolution support.
  • A large desert containing mysterious and customizable assets.
  • Small, medium, and massive creatures with different agendas.
  • You will need your wits and guts to survive the harsh desert.
  • Day/Night system that affects the player directly.
  • Random goals and missions.
  • Hidden and secret loot to search only for * Early Access 0.5 * version

      DEVELOPMENT ROADMAP

    Planned Key Features

  • full Survival System.
[/h2]
  • 3 main story-lines and the ability to choose paths.
  • Intricate crafting system.
  • More areas and locations on the map.
  • Co-Op Mode.
  • Each creature/monster will have its own life cycle and routine.
  • Giant bosses and several more creatures.
  • Save your progression by sleeping on the bed that is located in your camp. You will re-spawn to the nearest camp location when you die.
  • Avoid starvation by hunting different animals. Eventually, you will acquire a skill to farm your own food.
  • Customized Vehicles
