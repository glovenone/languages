About This Game

The #1 mobile game of all time blasts off into space!

Play over 300 interstellar levels across 10 planets! Whether it’s the icy orbs of Cold Cuts, the volcanic explosiveness of Red Planet, Utopia – a planet made entirely of junk food, or the new Brass Hogs episode ruled by evil Hektor Porko, each episode offers intergalactic fun at every turn! And the low-gravity environment of space results in spectacular gameplay ranging from slow-motion puzzles to lightspeed destruction. With brand new birds, special abilities, and a whole galaxy to explore, the sky is no longer the limit!

The Angry Birds series is somewhat simplistic and repetitive by definition, some (myself included) might say to a fault. There might be more of a challenge here, but you've got to slog through dozens of levels worth of mind-numbingly easy gameplay before you get to it. Compared to the original release, this installment doesn't really do anything particularly ground-breaking, basically simply adding an alternative physics system and a couple new shot types. That said, if you're a fan of the original Angry Birds, you'll probably find plenty of enjoyment here. Conversely, if you're not an Angry Birds fan... Well, this game is not going to be the one to change your mind.
