About This Game

“Of course there are worlds. Millions of them! Every star you see has worlds, and most of those you don't see.”
― Isaac Asimov, Pebble in the Sky

"Forbidden Planet" is a Space Strategy and Tower Defence like Game.
You control a small spaceship and your main goal is to protect your own home planet.
Explore the universe for resources.
Colonize other planets.
Defend from the asteroids.


----------------------------------------------
Forbidden Planet features:
• Combination of 2D and 3D graphics
• Deep Strategic gameplay
• Planets, Spaceships, Asteroids and the deep Universe.

music by Projecteur.

System Requirements

    Minimum:
    • OS: XP, Vista, Windows 7
    • Processor: 1.6 Ghz
    • Memory: 1000 MB RAM
    • Graphics: 256MB
    • Storage: 200 MB available space

Forbidden is not Always Alluring
Really, you don't want to waste your time on this game! Instead of just trashing the unclear directions, bad grammar, elevator music, and general pointless gameplay of the Forbidden Planet; let me give you a list of 18 "better" uses of your time in space, fellow Star Voyager!

18. Anomaly Series : Really old school strategy with great artwork

17. Energy Balance: Math puzzler in Space with great art

16. Asteroid Bounty Hunter: Space Shooter with Beautiful comic book art & good narrative

15. Apollo 4X: A very good 4X space game

14. Luna’s Wandering Stars: A planetary physics game

13. Habitat: Sandbox Space Ship Builder using space junk for crafting in actual outer space

12. Universe Sandbox 2’ed: A Strictly Simulation that lets you imagine “What if?” Great graphics & Art!

11. Kerbal Space Program: Simulates an actual Space Race for all ‘Kerbal-Kind’ to visit the planets

10. Spore: Strictly for building and designing aliens and alien ships (No action/ just art) Also has action gameplay for evolving creatures.

9. Sid Meier’s Starships: A 4X game that is at the top of it’s game… using a fleet to conquer the universe

8. Rymdresa: Space story in poetry with awesome graphics & art

7. Rymdkapsel: Minimalistic Strategy game that will make you forget why we ever wanted more graphics

6. Planetbase: Build & manage a planetary base, with mini colonists to order around & keep alive

5. Empyrion – Galactic Survival: Survival Sandbox builder with planet to planet travel & base defense

4. Opus: The Day We Found Earth: A Mystery Adventure with excellent narrative & star exploration

3. Offworld Trading Company: An Economic Base Builder Simulation with multiple paths to Corporate Success

2. Stellaris: Grand Strategy meets 4X in a beautiful new space exploration simulation

1. XCOM: Enemy Unknown/ Enemy Within / XCOM 2: The Strategy & 3rdPerson Shooter that lets you rid Earth of aliens with a highly personalizable squad

Heed the warning
& stay away from the ‘forbidden planet.’ You only have so many years on this planet to play video games. Of course, if you just want to spend your money on this to get the cutesy background art… there it is.
