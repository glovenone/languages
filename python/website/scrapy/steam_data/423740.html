4 February

Sad news, the game won't be buyable for a while

Hello, fans and buyers of Save Your Mother. I come with very sad news for a time being.

Effective as of 5.2.2016, Save Your Mother will no longer be able to be purchased from the Steam store. If you find this game on sale during this time, it's a scam and I do not earn a single cent from it. Steam was the only place I sold the game at


My reasons are personal and I would wish people to not pursue asking me about it, especially the ones who know me in real life. I will bring the game back sometime in the future, hopefully this year still. Maybe. This issue is major and affecting me on multiple levels, so I cannot make promises like that about when the game will return. Anyone who purchased the game earlier will keep the game of course and I wouldn't want anyone to take something that supported me through a hard time in my life away from them.


I am sorry for everyone who'd have wanted to buy the game under any of the number of the sales that'll happen this year but this is an absolute necessary and it really tears my heart and I wouldn't do this unless it was an absolute necessary. I worked on this game for over three years and dedicated so much of time, only to have to remove the game away because of issues that are out of my hands, especially when I had just barely broken even in my budget that had taken me years to pay up with my poor income. Once the game will be made purchasable, it'll most likely have a vastly reduced price for good.



Thank you for your support and understanding of this serious issue that has honestly, changed my entire life maybe forever.


Sincerely,
Tuomo Laine, creator of Save Your Mother.

Version 1.07

Bug fix

  • The random error with Final Pancake crashing the game has been fixed.
  • In some rare cases the mana did not work as intended, this bugfix should resolve every issue with mana.
  • The skill/item hud when in combat did not cover big enough area, this has been fixed.
  • Lava transport background sometimes scrolled with the player's movement, this has been fixed.
  • "Evil Transport" overlay sometimes on certain conditions scrolled with the player's movement when it shouldn't have, this has been fixed.
  • Talking to Glamour again at "Evil Transport" would leave her music playing after the discussion had ended, this has been fixed.

Dialogue

  • Changed cure's description to explain it works on the user.

Gameplay

  • Added tutorial for fishing.
  • Added steel bridges to Lava Pit in softcore mode for safe passages. Hardcore mode does not have safe passages. This also brings much needed diversity between Hardcore and Softcore modes in the Lava Pit.

Visual changes

  • Changed the color of the save points in the Ice Hell, to better stand out from the blue ice background.

About This Game

Embark upon an unconventional quest to Save Your Mother!


Alice Andersson, a world-renowned scholar, faces a dilemma of grand proportions when her mother falls gravely ill. No traditional cures have been able to save her; not even the most unconventional methods have worked! Join her as she discovers the legend about the Elohim Cube, a mystical artifact that might be their only hope. Long lost to the sands of time, the Cube is said to possess incredible healing powers. Over the ages, many have attempted to retrieve the cube, but to no avail. All were foiled by the long and arduous path that leads to the cube, and in turn, salvation. Will you be able to unravel the mysteries of the Elohim Cube?

A little info about this game


"Save your Mother" is the upcoming final version of a highly successful RPG Maker game; originally created within one month for the "Quest for Fun" contest. The original version won the first place and was downloaded by 39288 people before the download was removed at the end of the contest. This new version improves on every single aspect of the game, including a rework of many key components in preparation for the game's upcoming Greenlight debut, but still staying true to the charming spirit of the original game. Nearly three years of work and love has went to making the game as lovable as humanly possible!

Features


  • Save Your Mother! is a turn-based RPG with puzzle elements. While this combination may seem all too familiar, the fast- paced combat scenes and intricate characters set this game apart.
  • Rapid combat with epic special moves!
  • Hand painted cutscene art and character with a variety of emotions, blinking and mouth movement complements the intense and emotional plot.
  • The inspiring but eerie soundtrack will evoke a variety of emotions from fear to happiness and will set the stage for intense battles and emotional story scenes.
  • Unlock the special "hardcore" mode to add challenges and replay value!
  • Steam achievements and Trading Cards are planned, so happy collecting!
