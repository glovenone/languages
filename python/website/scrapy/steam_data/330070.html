About This Software

Action happens all around us and now we can easily capture it, harnessing the fun, energy, and love so that we can share it with others. Smart phones make it easy to capture those moments, but why not take the time to make a video that's as special as the day it was shot? A video with finesse. Effects. A great soundtrack and a title. But most importantly, a video that reflects you. Show your best side with Movie Studio Platinum.

  • Making movies in Movie Studio is easy. With Help Bubbles and interactive Show Me How tutorials, video creation has never been this much fun.

  • What do 4K, stereoscopic 3D, MP4, and AVCHD footage all have in common? They can all be edited in the same Movie Studio project!

  • Round out your movie with exciting soundtracks and beautiful titles. Create the perfect soundtrack by importing your own music from CD or MP3, or record your own music and sound directly into the program. Add elegant animated or simple text with the titling tools. Cut through the clutter and produce the perfect video.

  • Boost colors, apply slow motion, or add other effects to enhance any video project. Use the new Color Match tool to achieve a consistent color look from one scene to the next.

  • From standard dissolves to sensational 3D transitions, add artistic style between scenes. Adjust and preview your transitions in real time.

  • Movie Studio Platinum provides everything you need to produce spectacular movies. With 4K support and two distinct editing modes, video creation has never been easier.

WARNING!
If you are hoping to get a proper video editing application that provides GPU accelerated rendering, you might want to look elsewhere. I have an NVidia GTX 780, and apparently CUDA is only supported with the older Fermi architecture chips. I don't know about AMD cards, but if you have a newer NVidia card or plan to buy one, you will not get GPU accelerated rendering with this product! There are numerous forum posts throughout the internet, if you are interested about what exact cards support hardware accelerated rendering. Alas, Sony's site says nothing about this problem, and advertises the program to support basicly any current NVidia GPU.

From the product's homepage (http://www.sonycreativesoftware.com/moviestudiope/techspec):
Requires a CUDA-enabled GPU and driver 270.xx or later.
  • GeForce GPUs: GeForce GTX 4xx Series or higher (or GeForce GT 2xx Series or higher with driver 285.62 or later).
  • Quadro GPUs: Quadro 600 or higher (or Quadro FX 1700 or higher with driver 285.62 or later).

NVIDIA recommends NVIDIA Quadro for professional applications and recommends use of the latest boards based on the Fermi architecture.


While the program works great for basic editing, there are a couple of minor problems which I didn't realize before buying it. These may not be such downside for you though.
  • If you buy it through Steam, be prepared that it requires some inconvenient workarounds to be able to open two instances of the application. Also, starting the program requires Steam to be running. These are obviously Steam related problems, but I noted them anyway.
  • Velocity envelopes are only available in Vegas Pro, so creating slow motion with variable speed is harder than necessary. This again is something missing from their product comparision page, and that's why I write it here.

In the end, I suggest everyone looking to buy this to first try out Sony's trial, and decide if they can cope with the above mentioned issues after using it for some time.
