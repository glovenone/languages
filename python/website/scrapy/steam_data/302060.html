About This Game

Trucks & Trailers puts your driving skills to the ultimate test with 50 individual truck driving challenges. Compete against the clock to complete intricately designed courses and tasks that put you firmly into the driver's seat. Can you avoid obstacles whilst reversing a trailer? Do you have the lateral thinking required to sort a series of trucks & trailers in the yard into their correct positions? Can you park with precision?

As your skills increase, upgrading the difficulty level will guarantee that there is always a task that will put you and your truck to the ultimate test! At the highest difficulty setting external camera views are disabled, forcing you to maneuver like the pros using only in-cab mirrors. A test that will award you the gold medals and the honor of being a real trucking titan!

Key features


  • 50+ challenges, with achievements and awards, guarantees you will want to keep on truckin'
  • Incredible 3D graphics that bring beautifully detailed trucks and trailers to life
  • Multiple camera views of an intricate 3D world
  • From the makers of truck simulation games in the 18 Wheels of Steel series and the Euro Truck Simulator series

Great, if HARD, sim game. If you want a challenge getting trailers around into some difficult parking places, then this is the game for you! I picked this up as part of the ET2 deluxe bundle, and have been enjoying playing it.

Pros
- variety of tasks.
- able to choose several parking jobs if you get stuck (not limited to doing in exact order).
- basic difficulty setting allows crashes to simply 'finish' level if you want to progress and come back later.
- real sense of accomplishment when you do it right :)
- decent physics engine.

Cons
- getting gold in all tasks is almost impossible (I've yet to get silver in all). If you NEED 100% achievements, you might find this one too hard.
- some parking restraints are insane in pro.
- in game radio turns off between levels, so generally more trouble that it's worth.
- reversing mirrors don't work like ET2 (just go on popup mirror)

Rating 7/10

This game definitely wouldn't suit everyone, as the difficulty of all the tasks might end up on the high side. As an experienced ET2 player I started on pro, then quickly dropped the difficulty. Some parking locations seem just unfair (WHY would you park a trailer there??), but then the challenge can be quite satisfying. So if you want a game to test your backing skills, and pull some insane speeds to go for gold (or even silver), then definitely consider this game!

Any idiot can press the accelerator to go fast but that does not make them a good driver. Being able to maintain control while negotiating corners and avoiding obstacles is what takes skill. Doing that in reverse with massive trailer on and still beating the clock is a real challenge.
The game is basically Euro Truck but confined to warehouses and frieghtyards etc. There are a few obstacle courses on roads, but there is no highway cruising here. It all about parking trailers. The tasks start out simple enough but soon get pretty challenging. On Pro mode the constraints on accuracy get a little too tight on some challeneges causing some serious frustration, but ultimately all are doable.
The different camera options and angles are a bit limited and sometimes it gets pretty hard to see what you are doing. Mouse look is available but awkward to use since you are on a time limit and usually dont have a third hand to be able to make use of it. Pro mode becomes even harder as you can only use driver views and the mouse, which means you will need to be an expert and using your mirrors.
Overall this game is a great test of driving skill, coordination and situational awareness. A good buy for any true driving enthusiast.

This is a great game for any Euro Truck Simulator (ETS) fans. While ETS focuses on endurance driving and the freedom of trucking, Trucks and Trailers (T&T) focuses on the technical side of things.

T&T is basically a collection of complicated trucking scenarios which are organized as mini-games. It is very similar to Scania Truck Driving Simulator. Challenges range from parking in small confines, navigating through tight corners, and mastering brake control. The goal is to complete the task without causing damage to your vehicle. This can be a lot of fun for truck enthusiasts, and there is little doubt that oneâs trucking skills will improve.

Difficulty
SCS decided to award Gold, Silver, and Bronze medals based on completion time. A good driver should have no problems achieving the Silver medal for most challenges, but the Gold times require slightly rushed maneuvers that should never be attempted in real life. Achieving Gold often requires many attempts, even for the best drivers. It adds a nice challenge, but in real life, parking isnât supposed to be a rushed process.

Replay Value
Donât expect much replay value on this game - unless you want to repeat the challenges over and over. Despite this, the challenges will take you a fair amount of time (I am still working through them). If you can get this on sale for less than 5.00 USD, itâs a fun little game to have. A steering wheel or controller is recommended, but the default K&M controls are more than sufficient.

Graphics
Expect this gameâs graphics to be somewhere between ETS1 and ETS2. It looks quite nice, and most low to medium spec PCs should have no problems running this. Youâll probably get a higher FPS on this game compared to ETS2.

For the features it has, and its price point, this is a great game.
