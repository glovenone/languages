About This Software

Might and Delight invites you to step into an interactive diorama that expands on the world of Paws and Shelter 2. Welcome to The Lonesome Fog!

---------------------
The Lonesome Fog can be found in the software section of your Steam library.
---------------------

The Lonesome Fog is a living book that seeks to expand on the concept of storytelling and explores the plight of a young cub trapped in a cycle of fear, cowardice and uncertainty.

What becomes of you when you let fear dictate the way you live your life?

The roughly twenty page living book hopes to uncover that.

Main features:
  • Well established sounds and tracks from the Shelter series.
  • Environmental elements such as trees and water will be brought to life.
  • Navigate around animated scene to breath life into the illustrations
  • Seamless animated page transitions add depth and character to an already detailed experience.

The Lonesome Fog

This is a very nice short heart felt interactive visual story, great for the young ones, about a Scared Lynx cub who learnt how to brave the elements as she became lost in the wilderness, and finally find her lost family.

I certainly would have liked more of this interactive story book, it is way to short. But it is a lovely story none the less. Great for the very young children with shorter concentration span's I think :)

I feel the price is a little to much, for such a very short story, the Art is lovely though. So if your interested in this story for your younger children or maybe Grandchildren, perhaps wait for a decent discount, unless these developers lengthen the story more.

I got the Pitter Patter Edition of Paws, and didn't quite know what I expected from this. All I know is that this turned out a lot better and different than I first imagined.

If the Shelter games are something you really enjoyed, I recommend that you read The Lonesome Fog. It's an amazing add-on to the story, and shows off some about how the Blob works. It's a truly stunning experiance.
