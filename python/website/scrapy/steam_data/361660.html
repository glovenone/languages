About This Game

TIRED OF EASY STUFF? WILLING TO FEEL ANGER AND FRUSTRATION? THEN THIS IS YOUR GAME. I BET YOU WON'T BEAT IT. NOT EVEN THE FIRST LEVEL.





An 80's challenge with todays visuals

Accidental Runner is an Arcade Action game set in the desert where you play as a stubborn businessman cursed to run forever and punished to do it without forward movement after planning to build a casino in the pyramids.

Prepare yourself for a really challenging experience in which you will have to survive all sort of hazards, including flying carpets, heavy cars, V-Tols and hordes of goblins.

No loading times between stages! Enjoy single player, local coop and first person camera modes and toggle them simply pressing a button!

Accidental Runner is developed by Albert Espín and features music by Jordi Altayó. It's powered by Unreal Engine 4; I would like to thank Epic Games for the remarkable variety and quality of UE4's content for subscribers; this game makes a massive use of it for its visuals.

Well, first bug is when the EULA pop up and you see steamstore home page instead of it. Ok, I signed steam page, fine EULA for me.

Next problem is resolution - it is fixed and it is low (kinda 1366x768).

After that finally the game. Ok, how do I control it? Mouse doesn't work, arrows as well, appeared that A-D keys move your character sideways. Can you change these keys? No. Are there any options at all? No. Is there exit button for this game? No, you have to Alt+F4.

About gameplay. It was claimed to be challenging, at the end the challenge is game design. There are no clear hitboxes, fancy coat make you unable to tell if you hit something or just was suddenly blowed off, some levels are under construction (level 10 or 12, for example).

By pressing random buttons you can got general idea that q is kinda pause, c - first person view, caps lock - ghost run (oh, it's not a ghost, it's player 2 to be controlled by arrows, ffs), n - next level, x meant to do something but it doesn't, 1...9 teleport you to level with the same number, o teleport you to level 12 and i to level 10. After you die you can spawn tons of warlocks at level 10 with the same i, also this level is not designed to be beaten at all. I didn't find jump button yet..

You are expected to run, but to be honest, the character is running on the same place, and just some objects run towards you. Not really a problem, but this looks ugly.

Optimisation... this game used up to 1300Mb of my GPU RAM, which is insane for such a simple game.

Overall the game is raw, badly tested and unfinished, claimed challenge is not a challenge so don't get baited. By the way, what is the key to jump?
