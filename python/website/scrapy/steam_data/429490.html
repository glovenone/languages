QUICK START GUIDE!

When you start a World you will see a Box with green smoke pouring from it, you can use F or mouse click to open it and collect all the building parts.

You will only need to do this once, as the Inventory will save. (TEMPORARLY USING 1 INVENTORY ACROSS MAPS). So you will only need to collect the items from one map once.

To use an item press ''' i '' to open your inventory then drag the items you want to use to your hotbar and use the corresponding numbers on your keyboard. (e.g... Drag Foundation to slot 4, then use number 4 key to use)

To remove, make sure you have nothing in your hands then simply look at the item you want to delete and press the '' delete '' key on your keyboard.

To rotate use mouse scroll wheel.

To save! Autosave :D

About This Game

Resilience: Wave Survival is a physics based endless wave survival shooter set in an abandoned village, being taken over by Alien Invaders, Zombies & Robots.
Explore to search for guns and ammo to help defeat the enemies, better find food and water too if you want to survive for longer. Beware of the Tripods stalking the lands.

Customize your Atmosphere with world settings and combine with the many colour grading options to make your world truly your own. Every round can be a completely new experience! Pick from Sky Presets and them further Customize to your liking.

Customize Player Settings to your play style. Pick from 6 Load outs or start with just a knife.

Key Features!
* Building Mode with Autosave, Inventory and Hotbar (Includes 4 Unique Maps only for Sandbox Mode)
* HoverBike for Sandbox Mode
* Customize your experience by changing Player & World settings
* Fully Customizable Sky settings with Day/Night cycle (with fully adjustable speed) (Now with Snow!)
* Unique Recoil and Bullet Spread
* Physics Based
* Scoring System
* Hunger & Thirst (can be disabled)
* Easy, Normal & Hard Difficulty Modes
* Slow down time for them Epic Slo-Mo Moments!
* Gravity Force Options
* Weapon Testing Range (now with option to Spawn Random Enemies for practice)
* Xbox Controller Partial Compatible

FEATURES
-You can slow down time
-Able to change the gravity
-Very deep settings to change the game to how you want to play it
-There is a gun range
-A survival crafting system in progress
-JIMBOB sends out patches or updates almost daily!

GAMEPLAY
-The waves steadily get harder with 10 more enemys every wave, as well as 4 kind of "Boss" monsters that roam the corners of the map.
-There is a food/water system that functions pretty well (can be toggled)
-There is a stamina bar now so you can't run forever (can be toggled)
-Regnerating health (can be toggled)
-God mode for people who hate playing games (can be toggled)
-Whole lot of weapons for you to find
-Pretty big map, with another one being worked on
-Secret location or two
-Easter Eggs
-Loadouts you can choose from or you can start with a knife
-More powerfull variations of weapons

MAPS
There are four maps that we know of so far:
-One that can't be played yet but looks like an alien ship based on the picture
-Grimlock Island which is a test for survival crafting and building
-Flooded Grounds which was the first map
-Rendlesham Forest is a new wave map

ENEMYS
There are 8 enemys so far:
-A small brown alien with demon eyes
-A big Alien that looks like a yellow Gorilla without skin
-Humanoid aliens with green eyes
-Zombies
-An alien that shoots a gun
-A deformed giant white lobster
-A another deformed giant lobster, but green
-Tripods, pretty hard to kill unless you have a rocket launcher. (Takes 6 shots from a laser shotgun, AKA the slugger)

WEAPONS
This game has a lot of weapons, and there may be more more later on.
The Game currently has:
-3 Melee weapons
-4 Pistols
-A Crossbow
-2 SMG's
-2 Shotguns
-2 Assault rifles
-2 LMGs
-A Semi-Auto Rifle
-A Sniper
-A Rocket Launcher
-A Flame Thrower
-A Minigun
-2 Alien Weapons

GRAPHICS
I think this game just looks awesome, especialy the ocean in the shooting range, since the sounds of the waves are synced with when they crash down.

SOUNDS
JIMBOB did a pretty good job with the sounds, for example, I may seem crazy, but I think I hear some bird noises in wave survival. Either way, The sounds are great except for the aliens footsteps, which tell me exactly where they are coming from (Perhaps there could be an echo effect indoors later?), as well as when you swing a melee weapon, it sounds like you hit something even if you didn't (Maybe it could be a swishing sound?). One last thing, with shotguns the sounds that plays when you kit an enemy is overpowering the sound of the actual shotgun, which kind of feels like a bummer since you should see them ragdoll away with a big bang. At least in my opinion.

ANIMATIONS
Even though the enemys kind of flop when they die, it's awesome in my opinion You can see yourself as well as your flashlight isn't fixed to a single spot since it's attached to your gun.



BUGS
-None at the moment

Recomendations
Just some things I think would be cool for the future, you don't have to add them though. :)
-The blaster aliens could drop the weapons they have when they die
-A kind of boss monster that appears in every X number of waves (not the tripod)
-Maybe a debuff for the slugger, since it kills everything in one hit (on hard) except the tripod
-Maybe secret ways to get into the currently closed off areas for better weapons
-A kind of armor system that takes away damage, but has to be repaired. You could put in an item that's rare, but you can find 2 around the map, that breaks after a certain amount of hits. It could be limited to a game mode or not.
-A weight system that determines how fast you are moving because of how many/which guns you are carrying. For example, if you have the minigun, that could make you go much slower. But if you have a smg, you can move much faster. This could also influence the stamina system.

You should get this game
I really like this game and I recommend that you guys get it, especially if you're looking for something that doesn't cost much and will give you at least a couple hours of fun, get this.
Keep up the good work JIMBOB!

EDIT: A TON of new content has been added expanding upon the game, for example a new Building/Crafting mode which is like a Survival mode type thing, lots of new maps (Like Rendlesham Forest, Arid Wasteland, etc) a New gun, and loads more I can't put into good detail.
Also, loving the new Snowing feature.

Don't let the bad reviews fool you, this game is darn good.

Now you might be thinking.

But it has bugs and bad AI!
No game is ever perfect and AI should be better really soon.
I personally find the AI fine, they might get stuck on something ever once in awhile but it's okay.
Bugs, I have not encountered any yet.

The physics and animations are bad!
Physics are very good. It MAY seem like they flop dead when they die, but seriously, is it really that bad?
Animations are really good... Expect for some of the enemy animations. Those are a bit wonky but Jimbob can improve them, it's hard to make animations anyways.

Unity Assets are the worst!
Okay, I hate when people say a game is bad because it uses Unity Assets.
I myself am a Unity3D game dev working on my own game and I am using some Unity Assets, what's so bad about someone using a simple Unity Asset? The game has a lot of assets, I know that. But JIMBOB can't make hundreds of models himself so he used models from the Unity Asset store. Why is that so bad? If someone can't model, they are still a game dev and that's what matters.

B-But S-Something else is bad...?
What else is wrong? The game has bugs, okay every other game does too.

Now for the review. after explanation why it isn't bad.
The game has a ton of weapons right now. Enough to keep you occupied for awhile. There are about 6 or 7 Alien types so far. The game has several maps and the game looks BEAUTIFUL! Nice survival food and water system and good detail on everything. When you look down you can actually see your body and the Flashlight is very realistic, by that I mean when you run with the Flashlight, wherever your gun is facing the light goes onto there.

Overall Rating 8/10

The dev really tried to make a great game, and almost did so.

There are alot of options for weapons, a good amount of enemies, some bosses, and CONSTANT updates, which make the game good, but the main issue is two things:

1) There are many "Filler Items" in the game. For example, there are a vast selection of guns, but some do a small amount of damage, while the rest do the exact same (A Sniper rifle, magnum, LMG, and Assault Rifle do the exact same amount of damage, they all just have different clips and distances they can shoot). There are alot of enemies, but many of them, once more, do the same amount of damages and are basically the same monster reskined to look different. And the Food and Water system is honestly out of place and just feels like something the dev added in to be able to say "And look! Survival!"

2) Enemies are honestly overpowered, while you are horribly underpowered. Every single enemy (Including the boss) Runs slightly slower than you and NEVER stops running, meaning that you cannot stop to reload at any point during a wave, and you cannot run while reloading. Some weapons, such as the magnum, are totally useless further on in waves because it does the exact same amount of damage as a sniper (The sniper would be useless as well, but it can shoot longer ranges than all weapons, which barely gives it enough slack to make it ok), and becuase you have to wait 2 seconds in between each shot. There are 3 enemies that do a large amount of damage: Rock giants, Aliens with blasters, and the mini bosses.

They all run, once more, slightly slower than you, so you can only sort of outrun the giant. The aliens will stop and shoot at you, and their shots are IMPOSSIBLE to avoid, and they do a good amount of damage. Same with the mini boss who shoots a lazer at you, high damage, runs ACTUALLY FASTER than you, and unavoidable attack. What is even worse about this is that the boss glitches through all placed objects (Minus buildings), so if you try to hide behind a tree, it just passes through it and hits you anyways.
