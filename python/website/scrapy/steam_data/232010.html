About This Game

The original Euro Truck Simulator - the game which has become the classic of the genre and is still relevant today. The first truck simulation game in a European setting, with European long haul trucks!

Drive freight from London to Rome to Berlin to Madrid to Prague - and many more cities - in realistic vehicles. Faithful reproduction of driving trucks on the European road. Pick up a variety of cargoes, and deliver them on time! Highly realistic, meticulously detailed models based on real trucks. Actual working instruments such as flashing indicators, temperature and low fuel warning lights, wipers, and a full set of gauges. Pan the camera around the cabin, just as if you were actually sitting at the wheel.

Note: Unofficial "mods" are not supported by the Mac OS version of the game! (file formats are not compatible with Windows PC version)

The predecessor to the amazing Euro Truck Simulator 2, this game is pretty great in its own right.

While it doesn't possess the graphical fidelity and scope that its younger brother has, Euro Truck Simulator 1 nevertheless features a detailed rendition of Europe and it's still a relaxing driving sim. It has some nice music as well, especially when accessing the game's map. The only real complaints I have about ETS1 involve the flawed physics engine; the engine itself can be fairly glitchy.

I would recommend this game, especially if you can't afford ETS2 and still desire something similar. I'd also recommend it if you're interested in seeing how the series itself has progressed over the years.
