About This Game

Vault-Tec engineers have worked around the clock on an interactive reproduction of Wasteland life for you to enjoy from the comfort of your own vault. Included is an expansive world, unique combat, shockingly realistic visuals, tons of player choice, and an incredible cast of dynamic characters. Every minute is a fight for survival against the terrors of the outside world â radiation, Super Mutants, and hostile mutated creatures. From Vault-Tec, America's First Choice in Post Nuclear Simulation.
  • Limitless Freedom! Take in the sights and sounds of the vast Capital Wasteland! See the great monuments of the United States lying in post-apocalyptic ruin! You make the choices that define you and change the world. Just keep an eye on your Rad Meter!
  • Experience S.P.E.C.I.A.L.! Vault-Tec engineers bring you the latest in human ability simulation â the SPECIAL Character System! Utilizing new breakthroughs in points-based ability representation, SPECIAL affords unlimited customization of your character. Also included are dozens of unique skills and perks to choose from, each with a dazzling variety of effects!
  • Fantastic New Views! The wizards at Vault-Tec have done it again! No longer constrained to just one view, experience the world from 1st or 3rd person perspective. Customize your view with the touch of a button!
  • The Power of Choice! Feeling like a dastardly villain today, or a Good Samaritan? Pick a side or walk the line, as every situation can be dealt with in many different ways. Talk out your problems in a civilized fashion, or just flash your Plasma Rifle.
  • Blast 'Em Away With V.A.T.S.! Even the odds in combat with the Vault-Tec Assisted Targeting System for your Pip-Boy Model 3000! V.A.T.S. allows you to pause time in combat, target specific body parts on your target, queue up attacks, and let Vault-Tec take out your aggression for you. Rain death and destruction in an all-new cinematic presentation.
  • Mind-Blowing Artificial Intelligence! At Vault-Tec, we realize that the key to reviving civilization after a global nuclear war is people. Our best minds pooled their efforts to produce an advanced version of Radiant AI, America's First Choice in Human Interaction Simulation. Facial expressions, gestures, unique dialog, and lifelike behavior are brought together with stunning results by the latest in Vault-Tec technology.
  • Eye-Popping Prettiness!*: Witness the harsh realities of nuclear fallout rendered like never before in modern super-deluxe HD graphics. From the barren Wasteland, to the danger-filled offices and metro tunnels of DC, to the hideous rotten flesh of a mutant's face.

Honestly there isnt really much purpose to getting this game anymore since the game of the year edition was released. I bought it because it was on sale for like 2 bucks and regretted it later when I had to pay for all the DLC. The biggest problem with this release is it is buggy. It will not work on windows 8 or above, but If you look around online you can find numerous ways for you to be able to run the game by editing the files. Even after that expect occasional crashes. Other than that, this is easily the greatest game I have ever played. I have played it at least over 100 hours (I used the Fallout Script Extender so it wont actually log my hours on steam) and Where I usually lose interest in games after my second or third playthrough I have done at least 15 playthroughs of this game. This game allows you to

1. Destroy the US government
2. Nuke a developing city (or not but the explosion is pretty)
3. Enslave people
4. Find your dad
5. Kill basically anyone (except for a very small number of essential characters)
6. Join forces with a bunch of people.
7. Roam a sizable game world at your own pace and do what you want. You can be a â¥â¥â¥â¥ or you can be a saint, and the effects of your actions are felt.

With the DLC you can
1. Fight the chinese in a military simulation
2. Be a slave in pittsburg
3. Continue after the games ending and keep fighting the US government.
4. Go to some â¥â¥â¥â¥â¥â¥ island with nothing really there and a short, pointless quest.
5. Get abducted by aliens

There is no reason not to buy this game. This game is the greatest fallout game in the series. Better than the originals, better than New Vegas, and still better than Fallout 4. Also, you can get alot of mods for the game to customize your experience to your taste. I recommend
1.Enclave Improvement Project- Lets you join a group called the enclave you couldnt join in the main quest.
2. Sydney Follower mod- When you meet sydney in game, you will know why you need her as your follower.
3.Ultimate Amata Follower mod- Amata is cool, and this is a good idea.
4. Essential Followers- I dont know why the devs of this game want the followers to die. Get this and they wont.
