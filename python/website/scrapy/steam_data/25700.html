About This Game

The spiritual successor to the massively popular freeware top-down shooter BaboViolent2, Madballs in Babo:Invasion is an arena based 3D shooter offering players a satisfying and immersive experience in both single-player and multiplayer, including 'Invasion Mode' where players are able to design their own combat maps almost instantaneously by placing a combination of map tiles to create a never-before-seen map before gameplay starts. Truly, no two games are ever the same.

Featuring the recently re-released Madballs™ characters, Oculus Orbus™ and Horn Head™, from the popular 1980s toys best remembered for their outrageously bad looks and graphically colourful names, Madballs in...Babo: Invasion is a feature laden 'casually hardcore' action shooter.

A great multiplayer arena shooter. It doesn't take long to unlock all the class abilities and has plenty of game modes beside the well worn VS multiplayer most of these party games have. Multiplayer works and the netcoding is better than most on the indie titles I've played. Played against a friend in Austrailia and had about 200ms response time where 200 is what he often gets in Counter Strike.

I like the variety in guns selection the most. Each gun has an alternative fire that slightly changes how it functions while maintaining the theme of the gun. For example, the Shredder launches a saw blade that has slow projectiles while it's alternate fire shoots a slightly faster projectile that isn't as accurate and does less damage. Combined with class abilities, many guns are useful and can mix up the play experience.

Highly recommend this game and it is certainly worth $2-5 pending when you get it. You should get the 4 pack due to multiplayer being pretty much dead. That is the only real negative I have about the game.
