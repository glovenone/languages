About This Game

Moon Breakers is a free to play, multi-player 3D space combat game set in an alternate WWII-inspired future. As either a Government or Pirate pilot, take to the stars in single-seat starfighters and engage in epic dogfights for Helium-3 around the galaxy.

A modern multi-player take on the classic space combat genre of the 1990's, Moon Breakers delivers thrilling gameplay with simple controls that will have you flying and shaking bandits off your six in minutes. Coupled with a bold, retro-futuristic art style, amazing music by Bear McCreary (Battlestar Galactica, The Walking Dead) and a variety of ships to unlock and upgrade, Moon Breakers is pure space combat awesome. Sign up, climb in and get ready for launch... there are fortunes to be made.

Key Features

  • Free to Play!
  • Accessible Gameplay
  • Simple Controls
  • Large Scale Multi-Player
  • Unique Ship Customizations

Moon Breakers brings back the epic dogfighting of games like Xwing Vs Tie Fighter & Freelancer mixed with a bit of BattleStar Galactica style physics. Each ship has its unique tactical advantage, but winning a battle is more about your skills as a pilot and the strategies you use. This includes smart use of your afterburner (which also highlights your location when used adding another dynamic to the fights) and the presision piloting thru the awesomely detailed asteroid environments; this can be the key to winning many a dogfight. Its F2P but you can buy ship and booster packs or simply grind these out in game. Graphics are good but not jaw dropping.

The game had an excellent balance, was accessible, and most importantly was a heap of fun! There isnt a lot of variety in this game though and regrettably devs seemed to have dropped support so what could have really been epic is now a game with server issues and zero community in 2013.

Try Star Conflict if you like this sort of game; this one is frozen solid, floating aimlessly in space!
