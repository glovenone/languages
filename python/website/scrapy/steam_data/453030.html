Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“We decided to put the game into early access to enable us to interact with the community as early as possible.
Most major systems are in place and the development from here on our will focus primarily on content creation and polish. For both, getting feedback from players to adjust the game in the right direction is vital to us.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“With our current plan, we estimate to work on the game for another 6 to 9 months for it to be feature complete.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“For the full release we plan to add some additional features that add to the depth of gameplay, such as equipment upgrades and medical treatment for characters and a corresponding system for injuries and long term health problems resulting from them unless they are treated.
There is also a host of new content to be added, from additional environmental objects and types of danger (like gas and liquids) to more and more different items as well as improved visual effects.
Another feature we want to implement is character customization, along with more options for their appearances.
Lastly, we plan on adding more different tile sets as the games campaign goes on beyond what is currently implemented, meaning the environments will start to look a lot different the further the player gets in the game.

That, and of course there is lots of polishing to be done, fine tuning mechanics and getting items to feel just right.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The game right now represents a baseline of functionality that we will build upon.
By that we mean that most of the systems that make up the game are completely implemented, but not every bit of content for those systems is in yet.

For example, the map generator is fully operational and will create unique maps for the player every time, even though the variety of enemies and objects to interact with is limited right now.

All starting gear has been implemented and is fully functional, but the amount of further equipment that can be found and used is limited right now as well, with more different and unique functionalities planned to follow sooner rather than later.

All characters are unique and have their own traits, strength and weaknesses, but with later updates we will expand on those, add further character customization options, randomization and include more traits for them.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“The game will be discounted for those willing to support it's development and buy it on Early Access.
Which is to say, yes, the price will be higher for the full release.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“Community Feedback is important to us and the development of the games content.
As such, we have set up a forum for feedback and discussions and we will regularly check back with steams forums and feedback threads for input from the community on what they'd like to see more (or less) of and adjust our game accordingly.”

About This Game



In the 100 years after the end of the world, the survivors and their descendants have settled in and around old, but still functional, production facilities for food and shelter.
When one of these ancient machines breaks down, the community that had settled around it sends out expeditions into the unexplored depths of the facility to try and bring it to life again.
The first 4 of these expeditions have never been heard from again, and the machine continues to stay silent.
Now, with barely any manpower or equipment left, the fifth expedition is the communities last hope for survival.
As the leader of the Fifth Expedition, you will have to overcome the dangers within the machine and guide your team of explorers safely through its treacherous depths.

Features

  • Tactical Exploration - Control up to 5 individual characters at once in real time and lead them to their goal.
  • Random Map Generator - Never see the same map twice, Every area is full of interactive & destructible environments.
  • Challenging Gameplay - Numerous traps, enemies and dangers have to be overcome in order to survive.
  • Survival - Decisions and their outcome have lasting consequences with permanent death and persistent injuries for characters.
  • Loads of Items - Several dozen different items to find and craft, each bringing a new possible solution to problems, each customizable through upgrades.
  • Character Advancement - Every character has their own unique strengths and weaknesses and can be leveled up and customized.
  • Player Abilities - Interact directly with the world, your characters or their enemies with your own, powerful abilities that can be unlocked and upgraded.
  • Alternate Play mode - Either play a campaign mode with a definitive end, or compete in the leaderboards for how far you can go before you die.
