18 May

Integrated Voice Chat - Now Live!



Hey everyone! We wanted to push this small update that includes a handful of minor fixes and one much requested feature: Integrated Voice Chat. No more waving your clubs and pointing like mimes (unless you want to)! Mic's are off by default. Turn them on using the large UI to the left in the Lobby/Room scene's or find the settings from the Pause Menu. We're unable to detect which mic attached to your system is the Vive mic, so you may need to go into the Pause Menu > Microphone settings and try out the different mics to see which one is the Vive one, or the one you want to use. Switching mics requires you to leave and rejoin the room which is a bit of a hassle and it's something we're working with the good folks over at Photon on remedying. Once you figure out what mic is the correct one, this shouldn't be an issue. You may also mute others from the same settings menu if you want some peace and quiet.

Music is also being re-worked. A few tracks have been created but we're not going to push them until all of them are done. We've set the music to default off for now (Music can be enabled from the Pause Menu).

Stay tuned for some sneak peek Course Editor pics coming in the next week or two.

About This Game


Welcome to Cloudlands : VR Minigolf! Cloudlands is a virtual reality minigolf experience like you've never experienced before. Be transported to a beautifully landscaped island archipelago above the clouds.


You will find no swing or power meters in Cloudlands. Cloudlands uses an intuitive 1:1 control mapping between the virtual putter and your Vive controller. Expect your real-life skills to translate into the virtual world (oh no!). Don't worry though. It's mini golf! Everyone can have fun and play.


Cloudlands includes one fantastical 18 hole course. The course is split between 3 different seasons so expect to see summer, autumn, and winter themed holes. Expect to be challenged by ball cannons, floating bridges, spinning obstacles, and multi-island holes. Fully equipped with local and global online leaderboards.


What could be better than playing Cloudlands by yourself? Playing with friends and family of course! We offer 4 player online multiplayer to play mini golf over the network with other players with fully integrated voice chat.


Local multiplayer (only 1 VR Headset required) allows you to play a competitive round from the comfort of your own home with up to 4 players.


Bored of the existing holes and want to play some new ones? Shortly after launch we will be including (for free), a course editor to allow you to build, share, and play other user created holes. Build in god mode and instantly play your course in real time. Lock your pieces to a grid or use complete free form to create some ridiculous holes never before seen or played before. Included with the editor launch will be 2 new 18 hole courses. A classic and beginner friendly 18 holes, and a difficult advanced set of 18 for those who like a challenge!


Over 20 challenging Steam Achievements to test your precision and accuracy with a putter
