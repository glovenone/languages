About This Game

Like Pharaoh's Tomb, Arctic Adventure is a CGA game containing 80 all-new diabolical levels, packed with mind-bending arcade action designed to keep you from ever succeeding. Nevada will face freezing waters, diabolical traps, slippery ice patches, falling stalactites, runaway snowballs and even abominable snowmen! There are many, many more obstacles for Nevada to overcome in this highly adventurous series.

Features


Click or Stick: Flawless keyboard and joystick controls.

High-Score Hero: A high-score table allows your to track your treasure-hunting skills.

Treasure Hunter: Find bonus items to increase your wealth.

Blast from the FAST: Utilizes the FAST (Fluid Animation Software Technology) system for flicker-free movement.

Controller Support: Partial Xbox 360 Controller support.

This game is from the old days and its not bad, but its a platformer. Pixelated platformer. You can move, jump and shoot from your pistol. Very limited ammuniton, but that is not a bad thing, because you can jump over enemies.
-Each level is a one screen level.
-One pipe marks the start and one pipe marks the exit. The exit pipe is blocked by a bunch of blocks that need to be destroyed and your quest is to find the exact number of pickaxes need for these blocks.
-There are also buttons that add or remove blocks from the level so if you dont know what to do, then look for a button.
-Jump height is fixed, doesnt matter whether you hold or lightly tap the jump button.
-Some blocks are breakable with your head.
-Some blocks look like walls but contain gold.
-Annoying blocks of ice(make you slide) and flying blocks are in this game too. Also spikes.

Challenging platformer from the old days.

A great 2D platform jumping game by Apogee!

You're an archaeologist who works for Dr. Jones (StarTropics or Indiana Jones reference [Or Both]!?)
Jumping on platforms and switches to unlock hidden blocks, hidden coins and access areas to gain extra points, pickaxes or bullets.
The bullets, which look like condoms on the lower right of the screen, are used to shoot enemies.
The pickaxes are used to break open the blocks in order to reach a key and exit the dungeon, you need all the pickaxes to exit the level.
The diamonds/circles are points for high scores.
One hit and you die, losing your points, items and bullets earned on the level you died on, restarting in the cave having to re-obtain everything again.

A very fun classic!

7/10
