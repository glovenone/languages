Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“We are gonna be totally real with you: we think our game is a lot of fun as it exists, right now, on Jan 15th 2016. Everything we add to our game after today is new content like cars, maps, pretty accessories, music, different ways to play and tweaks to tighten gameplay. That's gonna take a long time.

We want fans to help shape that content. We want to keep the community updated. Going Early Access will help us develop the game you really love. We are also going independent and we can maximize player feedback this way.

If you want to wait until the game is 100% finished, that is totally fine! This game might have some bugs, we won't judge you if you wait!

But the four of us, we believe that buying an Early Access title is not just about adding another game to your Steam Library. Buying Early Access titles is a chance to help work with developers to shape the game. There is a sacred relationship between us devs and you. We are now a team, shaping Tacopocalypse into something we can all sit down, talk smack, butt heads and still have an awesome time playing. Together.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“Tacopocalypse will be in Early Access for 8-10 months.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“The full version is planned to include:
  • 2 New Player Cars
  • 4 New Maps
  • 2 New Disaster Mechanics
  • Stunt Mode
  • Taco Tag
  • Time Trial
  • Free Roam
  • New Unlockable Skins and Music
  • Achievements
  • Fuller Stunt Library
”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“Following our planned full version, the current state is 45% complete.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“Buying Tacopocalypse in Early Access will give you about a 33% discount off of our final price.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“You can follow our Steam updates. All channels will be updated frequently.
Twitter Updates https://twitter.com/cherrypie_games
Facebook Updates https://www.facebook.com/Tacopocalypse-1578363912439335/
Kickstarter Updates https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/cherrypiegames/tacopocalypse
Follow our developer blog and newsletter http://cherrypiegames.com/”

About This Game

Tacopocalypse is a humorous 3D stunt driving delivery game. If a professional skateboarder retired to food delivery, it would look like this. You must deliver tacos, boost your earnings by nailing tricks to unlock new vehicles, customizations and cities; all while surviving the apocalypse!

Key Features:


Fast-Paced Delivery Stunt Driving
  • Play alone or with up to 3 friends
  • Boost cash by completing stunts
  • Avoid obstacles to make as many deliveries as possible

Customization
  • Choose from a selection of 90âs inspired vehicles
  • Unlock new paint jobs and accessories to customize your vehicleâs appearance
  • Customize your playlist by finding hidden Mixtapes

Exploration
  • Nostalgic 90's style theme and soundtrack
  • Experience a unique disaster per iconic city
  • Discover collectables hidden across each city
