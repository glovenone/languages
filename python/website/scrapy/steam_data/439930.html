About This Game

Control Craft 2



Control Craft 2 is a fast paced RTS, which brings quick thinking and clever tactics together for some planet conquering action.

Badges :

Enhancing everything which made the original a hit, with stylish graphics and addictive game play, Control Craft 2 will keep you coming back for more.

Take command of your troops and lead them into battle. Send in wave after wave of your soldiers to overthrow the enemy colonies and occupy their bases.

Battle your way through challenging levels and upgrade your weapons. Combine your different attack units (light, heavy and flying) to out wit the enemy and claim the planet as yours.

FEATURES :


*24 Unique Levels

*Clever 2D Platform Level Design

*Stylish Graphics and Smooth Animations

*The Ability to Upgrade Your Firepower

*Hear the Troops React to Your Orders as You Direct Them

*Unlock Levels and Choose the Non-linear Path to Victory

Control Craft 2 adds to the unique style and game play of the original. This game is easy to pick up but hard to put down.

Take Control and Smash the Enemy. Your army needs you!

Basic Information

  • Genres: Action, Casual, Indie & Strategy
  • Developer: Elite Games Ltd.
  • Publisher: OtakuMaker.com
  • Release Date: The 15th of February, 2016

General Impression
Control Craft 2 is a relatively nice combination of the action, casual, indie and strategy genres. The graphics are two-dimensional, but they are well-designed. The textures are simple but well-designed. The textures are pretty nice as well. The atmosphere varies between 27 levels, where on each level, there will be different background colors, as well as other decorative objects. The soldiers can move on both ground or fly to some bases. The audio is alright. The music is pretty nice, it suits the game. The gameplay is basically: capture all the bases. You must send soldiers to every base and capture each one of them. Be careful though, you need some strategy, or you are very likely to be defeated. You lose the level when you lose all your bases. Throughout the levels, you will notice that the difficulty will change randomly. That’s a pretty big disadvantage. But, the good part is: obstacles will make the gameplay even more challenging. The user-interface is relatively nice, there’s nothing special about it. It’s pretty well-designed. The controls are all done by the left click of the mouse. They are very simple, but yet well-chosen. The engines are alright, I haven’t met any bugs, crashes, screen freezes or frame drops. The mechanics are pretty good everywhere, even though they could move a little faster. A game-speeder option wouldn’t be a bad feature neither. Overall, it’s a pretty fun and challenging game, with pretty nice graphics and many levels to combat.

Positive Points
  • Relatively nice 2D graphics
  • Pretty nice soundtrack
  • Pretty fun and challenging gameplay
  • 27 levels to combat + some of them have bosses
  • The ability to upgrade your units
  • Some obstacles make this game even more challenging
  • Steam trading cards available

Neutral Points
  • Achievements would make this game better
  • A game-speeder would be a good feature, especially for easy levels
  • A level editor would be a great feature

Negative Points
  • The sounds made from the bases are bugged. Even if the base has 0 soldiers in it, it still makes the sounds of attacking soldiers. Basically, that’s likely a bug which should be fixed.
  • The sounds made from the soldiers are repetitive and annoying
  • The difficulty is very unstable, this could be easily improved by making harder and harder on-going levels, instead of completely random levels
