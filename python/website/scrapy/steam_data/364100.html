About This Game

We are not alone in the universe, its filled with Zombies! Millions upon Billions of Zombies! That's right, in mankind's effort to expand into the stars they discovered something so sinister that it stopped them DEAD in their tracks. To combat such daunting numbers mankind invented their most efficient weapon yet: the Space Robot Samurai Zombie Slayer. Using cutting edge neo-millenium technology, ONLY YOU can pilot the Space Robot Samurai Zombie Slayer. Control this deadly weapon from across time to stop the Space Zombie hordes!

Posted: 19 June, 2015
When I first bought this game, I assumed it was going to be a cheap knock off pre-made asset game by some bootleg company that is hardly worth .50 cents. I was dead wrong. (Get it? Zombies.) The developer actually made 1000 amps, a game I fully recommend along with this game, but let's talk about SRSZS game already.

Space Robot Samurai Zombie Slayer is the best combination of mindless clicking and fast thinking. The game has two parts to it, a user friendly movement system that any newcomer can get used to and a high paced skill oriented score system that any competitive soul could get involved in. The way certain enemies attack is frightening to new players but can be utilized by veterans to max out combos. The game doesn’t throw any new weapons, upgrades or DLC cheats your way. It’s just you, your skill, and zombies. The addictive gameplay and price tag along are worth a look.

During my time reviewing the game, I eventually found myself on the top 10 worldwide. So tell me fellow gamer, how many zombies can you kill?

This is a weird one... This is a game where you move your mouse around and kill zombies. The thing is that i remember playing a free browser game that was almost exactly like this. It was fun, and therefore so is this. But i just can't really see a reason in paying for it when i can just play a browser game that is the same.

However, don't get me wrong this game is very fun and addictive. It really is just a fun arcade game, and that is all it is. There is no campaign, multiplayer or anything like that, just an arcade mode. But that is fine. Remember Call of Duty Zombies? That was just an arcade type thing, but it was still very fun and repayable and the same goes here. I would like more sprites for the zombies and some more areas though. Maybe unlocked if you get achievements?

I would still recommend purchasing this game but only on a sale so you can actually get your money's worth. It is still pretty cheap now but considering what it is, not cheap enough.
