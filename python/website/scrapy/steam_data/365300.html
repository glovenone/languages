    About This Game

    Transmissions : Element 120 is a short single player experience set in the Half-Life Universe featuring a unique gravity defying weapon that allows you to jump buildings and sustain large falls. The story takes place at a mysterious date & location. Where are you? Why have you been sent?



    To help bring the Source Engine up to date several new features are included:
    • Enhanced dynamic lighting
    • Integrated Source Shader Editor 0.5 for enhanced post processing & lighting
    • Improved support for complex physics structures
    • Support for super massive physics objects
    • Improved AI and situational awareness on multiple AI types

    THE DOWNLOW:
    Transmissions: Element 120 is a Source engine developed indie game set in the Half-Life universe. It consists of four well-made if somewhat short chapters that provide a few encounters with your favourite crab-zombies and combine soldiers. The overall feel and setting is designed to be a mixture of horror and puzzle solving, with a little hint of combat at key moments.

    THE GOOD:
    + More Half-Life style play.
    + Great care and attention to create an immersive environment and detailed locations, take this small team's effort a step up.
    + The custom made soundtrack elevates the tension and key encounters to another level.
    + Loved the clever use of luminescent paint in certain areas.
    + Its short and can be played in two to three hours.
    + Achievements are simple to obtain but can be missed if not careful.
    + Its free.

    THE BAD:
    - Not much really. If anything there could have been a little more spoken narrative / dialogue included to help build up a better picture of the scenario.
    - Some of the AI routines stop working for combine soldiers and they just stand there doing nothing. Happened a few times.
    - Progressing through some of the physics-based doorways/exits can be confusing. So if thats not your thing make sure you are happy to consult a guide.

    AND THE ORDINARY:
    * The engine may be dated but the developers have maxxed out as much as possible with the tools they have to make this more than just a mod.
    * There isnt much combat with it being staged in key set-pieces towards the end of chapters.

    VERDICT:
    Short, well-made but enjoyable nonetheless. If you are waiting for Half-life (Episode) 3 you could do worse than check this out! Its one of the best free-to-play efforts that you are bound to find on steam. Fans of Half-life should enjoy this no question. Otherwise if you are after easy/quick achievements or just like to pass the time with free games then you would be a fool not to try this. If however you are after a pure or heavy-focused shooter then this is not for you.
