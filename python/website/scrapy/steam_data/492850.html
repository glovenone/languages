About This Game

Astroderps is a fun puzzle game for all ages. The objective is to solve puzzles that guide your Astroderps to safety. You can move blocks to create a path, extinguish fires, build bridges, and deflect projectiles. Timing and dexterity is key to get your Astroderps safely from one portal to the next.

There are multiple levels for users to solve, each more challenging than the previous one. Are you up for the challenge?

Like other reviewers said, the game is a bit like Lemmings: you have to get your astroderps to the goal, while they wander mindlessly into certain death or jump off of cliffs - thus the title lemmings. Instead of manipulating the astroderps directly, you change blocks and stairs and form a way for the astroderps.

Most of the levels are fairly easy. The art style, the controls and the sound are okay, but nothing more.

Only the amount of content is really frustrating - there are few level and I finished the game in under 1 hour! That's not enough playtime for a complete game. The price/content proportion is a no go and I can understand, when other players won't recommend the game because of this. But the game itself is okay, so I will give it a recommendation - but only barely and only if it's on sale.

Posted: 18 June
It's a nice little game that brings back some Lemmings memories. However, while in Lemmings you manipulate the little dudes to make them reach their goal. Here, you manipulate the enviroment by placing blocks, stairs or lowering bridges to make sure your little derps don't fall, get burned or drown and get to where they want to be.

I play in a small area (2,1mx1,5m) and I had no problems with the provided space. Standing next to the little world and placing blocks and making sure they all survive is fun and challanging.
Although, I think that this is not a game that really NEEDS VR to work, it definitely is is more fun in VR.

EDIT:
after it was pointed out to me that this game is indeed not an early access title, I unfortunately have to say that I would not recommend this game to everyone.

It is quite short and the price is definitely to steep for about 40 minutes of puzzle solving.
I do understand, that the VR market is so small that you need to charge a little "early adopter tax". But the framework exists and a few (like 5-10) more levels should not have been a problem.

So my ne final verdict is:
GET IT! if you want to support the developers (this is the reason I'm keeping this game). The idea is nice and I hope that maybe this game will get some free map packs... or a level designer would be awesome (you listening chronos VR?)

DO NOT GET IT if you expect an awesome Lemmings clone. It is too short to have any replay value and the price is too high.
