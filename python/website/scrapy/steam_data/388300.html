About This Game

Just Alone - adventure game, in which you become a crewmember of a spaceship, that crashed during its flight. Bill Yesterday is the only survivor of the crash. His mission is to survive on a planet, destroyed by its previous inhabitants, equiped only with a radio and pile of debris left from his spaceship. His team was sent to gather samples and do research on the life of people who inhabited the planet. Now, Bill cannot do a lot of research, because most of his equipment broke into thousand pieces.

To survive in this hostile place, Bill has to hunt, gather resources and try not to get in the way of mutants, who turn out to be far from friendly to our protagonist.
Apart from doing numerous missions, Bill can construct a variety of bulidings, hunt and explore the surroundings.

The game is still developing. New updates&fixes come soon.

I hate to say it but I cannot recommend this game at all. "Just Alone" is a survival game that takes place right after a spaceship crash and are the only living member abord you vessel. You are forced to survive in a uncharted location infected by ravenous mutated creatures. The problems with the game overwhelm the good things about it.

This game has a basic level tree system, its a good feature and it adds a sense of progression to the game, it gives a purpose to killing monsters and animals. Aside from that it is your basic survival game accept it feels unfinished and sloppy. There are numerous minor details that are missing from the game and without thoes details, it puts it below other survival games.

The only way I could reccomend this game, is if they add more small details, the base of the game is there but alot of small, but needed features are not. Even if this game was on sale you could probably get another survival game that is better, for the same price or a little more. Before closing I would like to point out that the developers are kind people, when I bought the game there was a probelm with the servers and they contacted me personaly to resolve it. They seem like they care for their game and they want to see it thrive.

I would suggest you to stay away from this game untill they polish it, if they ever do.
