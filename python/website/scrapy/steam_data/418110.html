Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“Face Off XL is the big sister product to the original Face Off game, released earlier this year. While the original game was always intended to be sent to mobile markets, XL is intended solely for desktop players. Numerous graphics upgrades have been made and many aspects of the user interface have been improved. We also have plans for new game modes and expanded game boards.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“We anticipate a 3 to 6 month Early Access period, during which we will finish polishing the existing feature set and roll out the additional features.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“The finished version will contain a completely overhauled user interface, a story mode, more visually appealing game boards, and game boards that feature more than 3 lanes. Additionally, more factions and cards will be implemented by the time the game is fully released.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The game is in a fairly polished state. The user interface still needs work, and the appearance of most of the game boards featured within the mobile game have been upgraded. We have verified that game boards with 4 lanes are functional within the existing code base, and the AI can use them, however no useable boards currently exist and the AI will need adjustment to allow it to properly deal with the implications of larger game boards on the overall strategy. We have only outlined the story mode.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“The game will always be free to play.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“The community is a major part of polishing the game. Their input will be used to improve card balance and to develop new and interesting cards. As new features are implemented, their feedback will be key to ensuring the experience is interesting and fun.”

About This Game

March of War: FaceOff
The Battlefield Card Game

Played out on a beautiful game-board battlefield, FaceOff offers many of the familiar mechanics of the typical card game; however, but the game-board play adds a unique âterrainâ dimension missing from other card games.

FaceOff not only plays different than other card games â it is the only Steampunk card game we know of on Steam.

Come join the FaceOff alt-history Steampunk wargame community - choose a faction, build a devious deck, and then put on your poker face as you Face Off across the game-board battlefield. Unleash devastating barrages, advance your army, and pulverize the enemy with a dazzling combination of cunning strategy and totally unexpected card combinations.

With hundreds of cards across three unique factions, no game is ever the same. Craft your perfect deck and play for free against the AI or human opponents!

Many FaceOff players are active chess players looking for entertaining new ways to exercise their chess skills. So if you like card games, and you like chess - then why not download FaceOff and give it a spin - its Free to Play.

Overall, despite the FPS problem, this is a very funny and interesting card game and I truly recommend it to anyone. ^^
