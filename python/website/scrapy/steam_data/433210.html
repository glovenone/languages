Version: 1.0.3

Intro
Rhino's Rage is a tactical turn based game about rhinos. There are four modes in this game: 1 vs many, many vs many, capture the flag, and 1 vs boss + many. The unique thing is, you can gear your rhinos and make them stronger.

Graphics
First of all, the screenshot in this game is slightly different from what's the game has to offer. The battle screenshot is mostly the same, but the menu texts and fonts are different. Also, I haven't see the upgrade popup in the game after playing the game for 14.7 hours .

The graphics in this game is cute. However, i can't really say the same with the UI. Although i can't say it's bad, it's not good either. It's worse than good, however. The text positioning for OK is placed incorrectly, and the main menus are unclickable before the animation finishes. Not to mention the awful text when you lose. "Sorry". That's all. Why don't you make something creative?

Musics
Both the bgm and sfx are decent. I don't really mind it in particular, but it can get boring, especially when you have to hear and do the same thing in battles (i'll explain more later).

Battle
As i said before, there are 4 modes in this game. However, all of them can be cleared by doing the same thing: kill them all. The maps for each modes are randomized, and each modes have their own level which can be raised by defeating the boss mode (there are only 5 bosses total). However, i have to say that the opponent's AI are too simple because they always do the exact same thing when you are doing the same move. This makes it

The controls are terrible. The instructions are not clear enough and even battling can make you frustated. You'll lag when you have a high speed, you'll lag when you try to move the map. There is even a button assigned to do three things: moving the map, undoing the selection, and looking at the character's stat.

At later levels, the many vs many becomes annoying because you have to wait for the enemies to finish their turn, and it can took more than one minute per turn, imo. Your allies' stat is weaker than your opponents', making the main rhino to be the only one doing the killing.

There are also tons of bug when you are playing the game, making game experience becomes worse. I have already list tons of them in the suggestions if you are curious. The main point is, what is the QA doing? (if you have no idea, QA is the one who test the game and check the bugs) If you are looking at the credits, there are a lot of people contributing for this game which should make this game stable. At this point, i think it's better to flag this as an early access game.

Pros:
- 4 different modes
- Equipment to gear up your rhino

Cons:
- Tons of bugs
- Unclear controls
- Predictable AI
- A difference in stats between allies' and opponents' rhino

Conclusion
This game should be flagged as early access, but it didn't. Note that the devs said that new update will be on the end of June. I'll wait till then.

Should you buy this game?
No.
At this point, there are tons better game with the same genre.

I wanted to like this game but it's just not a good game. I only played it for 15 minutes but it's enough. The idea is cool, the art looks good and the music sounds fun but the game just doesn't make much sense. I'm not even complaining about it being an obvious mobile port (It lacks a lot of things that should come standard on pc games. Like a button or toggle for fullscreen).

The game drops you in a match with only one vague picture as explanation. The level layout and enemies seem to be randomly generated in your disfavour. Enemies deal higher damage and have more movement. I beat the first level when it generated only 1 enemy instead of 3, taking all the health pickups for myself then resorting to a headbutting fest without any movement until one of us was down.

I won a piece of equipment and some bananas but cant seem to be able to actually equip it yet. Maybe the slot is unlocked later, that wasn't clear.

Perhaps I've been playing it wrong. I hope the game has more to offer. I'll come back to it sometime after it receives some updates. Can't recommend it at the moment, if ever. I went into it expecting a simple and casual tactical game with a heavy focus on positioning but it's not even that.
Nice idea but lacks in execution. I look forward to updates.
