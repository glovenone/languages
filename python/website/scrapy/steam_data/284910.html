Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“Putting our game on early access gives us a chance to receive invaluable feedback on certain functions of the game and the current direction we are taking.
As our game is all about playing with others, the community's opinion is held in high regard.
The game is in alpha stage, those who purchase it need to be aware they may encounter bugs, glitches and incomplete content. Early access is designed for those who like the idea of the game and wish to support its continuing production and be a part its journey from alpha to full release.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“The dates are never certain with an online multiplayer but we plan to move into beta phase within the year, from there we will be polishing and fixing bugs before a full release. As we hope we won't stop creating new content for a long time, the game will be an ongoing project even when fully released.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“Currently the game is in a very basic state, for the future we plan to fill the shop with new armor, weapons and spells to begin your matches with, as well as multiple miscellaneous items to increase your powers or abilities. There will also be all new modes which vary greatly from standard death match, new and more complex maps and much more. We plan to release updates on a regular basis, incorporating new features or fixing bugs.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“At the moment all players start with a default spell and can pick up a variety of different spells in the maps. The shop currently contains several weapons and spells for the players to aim for.
There are four maps in total, two for duels, and the other two for 4 player matches.
We currently have 1v1, 2v2 and 4 player free-for-all game modes, with larger number possibilities on the way when we create larger maps.
Players can currently gain affinity points, by using spells that are in a certain school, for example-
• Fireball - Elemental
• Devour mana - Arcane
Increasing your affinity with a school will increase the damage you deal with that school's spells, as well as your resistance to them.
The players also gain levels and soul fragments, which will play a large part in the shop aspect of the game.
It also features a taster of the traps we have planned, players can be launch into spiked walls for insta-kills with spells that have knock-back abilities.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“The game will be priced at $12.99/£9.99 for early access and $19.99/£14.99 for Full Release.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We will be checking the community forums daily for suggestions on all aspects of the game, whether they be viable changes to the current way it works or advice on cool things to add. As often as we can we will address any questions or issues players may have.”

About This Game

Purgatory is a fast-paced multiplayer fps game where you use a multitude of spells with a variety of effects to destroy your enemies.
Participate in duels, team battles or free-for-all in the mysterious realm of purgatory, where all arenas have a history which chains them to this world.

Key features
• Destroy your enemies with multiple spells, items and abilities
• Fill up your load-out with purchased spells or random spells spawned during matches
• Increase the power of your avatar with magic affinity and items
• Multiple maps for multiplayer mayhem in various match types
• Day/night cycle in appropriate maps
• Diverse fantasy environments

Players take on the role of souls cast into purgatory, where they will wage eternal battle to absorb the souls of the fallen, thus increasing their power and dominance.
Spells are categorized into 'Schools' of magic; Arcane, Elemental, Light and Dark.
Increase your affinity in the various magic schools by using their respective spells, gain experience to wield greater power, earn soul fragments and exchange them for greater spells, items and abilities.
