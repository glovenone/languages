About This Game

Starsphere is a challenging platformer. Jump and move together with Starsphere in ocean of dynamics and action. Incredibly difficult levels, bonuses, achievements, and of course rhythmic Starsphere music will give you a lot of fun (pain) to play.

Features

  • Continuous movement. Do not stop at nothing!
  • Dynamic levels, a puzzle on the reaction, time and not only.
  • Simple, but interesting game mechanics and control. No time to think! Jump!
  • Vertical and horizontal levels.
  • Starsphere upgrades.
  • Rhythmic and uplifting Starsphere music.
  • Caution! The most difficult and boring game in the Universe.

Pre review TL;DR:
It looks like a mobile port but in reality it's more along the lines of a free flash port. with tons of leap of faith moments and gameplay that punishes you for going fast.


Pros:
+Is frustrating if you like that kind of stuff

+Somewhat diverse music (all dubstep though)


Cons:
-Tons of grating music

-Cant rebind any controls

-It tries to be hard by being frustrating and bull****

-Community is dead nonexistent lol

-The devs have updated it twice, and one of those updates was a needed update to make the game run after it was unplayable for a whole month, in neither case was it more gameplay

-Very bland and lackluster artstyle

-Low quality graphics and no resolution settings

-The view doesnt go far enough so in a platformer that you want to go fast you need to continually stop to check if you are jumping into a pit or onto a platform (and sometimes even checking doesnt help)


Reasons why its frustrating
-The ball suddenly speeds up while falling

-Platforms dont reset each time you die

-No way to regain lives besides dying

-The screen shows far more on the left side than right, and you are always progressing to the right, this leads to TONS of leap of faith moments

-The ball is constantly and annoyingly perpetually bouncing

-Level 8 is INCREDIBLY annoying and getting any jump height "upgrades" make it worse (im missing it by 3 pixels EACH AND EVERY TIME)

-Checkpoints dont count if you pass over them but rather you have to hit them


Other issues:
-If you go to shop it plays music despite having music and sound turned off

-Tells you about mouse based control but doesnt mention arrow keys and doesnt use wasd

-For the unlock lines in shop, green applies to the unlocked AND the unlockable items which is annoying
