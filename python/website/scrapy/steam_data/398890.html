Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“We decided to release the game in this stage through early access to get feedback from the Community and make the game better together.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“It's difficult for us to give a solid release date, but we plan to release the full version in Q2 2016.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“The game should be well balanced with the feedback of the Community and as we work towards a full release we're hoping to add new features and more content.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The base features are implemented and fully playable.

Features
  • Name and Gender selection of your Zombie
  • five different needs each with many different interactions
  • 5 entertaining Mini Games
  • Panorama backgrounds
  • different 'Styles' for the Main scene (2.5 D), Bathroom and Bedroom
  • many clothing parts to personalize
  • many animations and emotions
  • direct interactions with your Zombie through gestures
”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“We will not be raising the price during or after the Early Access period. The price will be the same as it is now.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We hope to get as much feedback as possible from the Community to improve and extend the game. It would be great if many will post in the forums and be part of the discussion.”

About This Game



Adopt your own Zombie as your virtual Pet! Enjoy its countless Animations with Amazing Graphics with unique Charm. Create your own look with more than 80 clothing parts in fantastic 2.5D scenes. Play with your zombie different mini games to get him or her happy.

Features

  • select the Name and Gender of your Zombie
  • five different needs each with many different interactions
  • 5 entertaining Mini Games
  • Panorama backgrounds in which your Zombie can run around without borders
  • different 'Styles' for the Main scene (2.5 D), Bathroom and Bedroom
  • more than 80 clothing parts to personalize
  • many animations and emotions
  • direct interactions with your Zombie through gestures
  • get experience and unlock new Content
  • with Zombie and Humor

Early Access Review
Posted: 23 March
Even if it was free, I wouldn't recommend this to you. It's buggy, boring, and you can even find a game like this way better for free.

We're not in 2000, where Tamagotchi are popular, and there's nothing that can justify it's $10 price.

So far, I've got a virtual zombie and a rock-paper-scissor game to play. I feel like it's going to take a long time before I unlock a new minigame.

There's this whole item who's bugged, the herbal tea. Being one of the first object you start with, I noticed that after you use it, you can't do anything anymore, you'e got the relaunch the game. Wow. A+ programming.

The cursor is really bothering me, and it's no way helping me to know where I'm clicking, since it's a bit over it, for some reasons. (It also change size depending on the item in background, it makes it way more annoying)

Also, when I "delete" my zombie, I've got to close and re-open the game yet again because the cursor is stuck BEHIND the picture where I can choose my zombie's name and gender.

This early access is a joke, it's like a developer spent 1 hour writing a game and didn't even playtest it.

The graphics are a joke and the game lags way too much for this kind of thing.

EDIT: The dev blocked me from their forum for a simple reason: "hater".
