About This Game

Codename: Rogue Fleet is a multiplayer team-based game about spaceships combat. The twist is, you can play as huge capital ships, tiny squadron of fighters or anything in between.

Bigger ships are equipped with lasers, missiles, engines and various sub-systems that can be destroyed. To keep things balanced, smaller ships take advantage of that and can easily cripple them.

A match of Rogue Fleet will pit two team of 12 players against each other. At the start of a match, players will choose their ship. With the help of their team and by managing the strengths and weaknesses of their respective ships, players will achieve victory by destroying all enemy ships.
