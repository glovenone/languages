About This Game

An extension of the 12th release in the Nobunaga’s Ambition series, Nobunaga’s Ambition: Iron Triangle, has appeared at the Steam Store as Nobunaga’s Ambition: Iron Triangle w/Power-up Kit!
This game is the first in the series to employ a single 3D map for the game to play out on. On this map in 3D, castles and towns are built and developed, attacked and defended across Japan and grand naval battles take place on the Inland sea.
In addition, over 80 techniques can be developed using the “Tech Innovation System” and then used in battle to win a strategic victory.
Furthermore, the power-up kit brings you a variety of new and powerful additions, including the Foreign technique, in which using special goods for trade is the key, and many more.

First of all, if you don't speak Japanese or maybe Chinese, it's not a game for you b/c all the contents are in Japanese and can be understood as traditional Chinese characters, therefore it's not even friendly to Japanese learners who knows only kana.
There is a English version of the same game available on Amazon or some other online store, but I don't know the quality of translation since I've not played that version.

The best side of the game for me is its rich and interesting presentation of the history. Although that's not the main theme of the game, the short story in the personal view of a charater gives you the background and the personality of the historical figures, and the interactions between them. On the top of all these, you can shape the future of the country and the people all by your own hand.
Its control is intuitive as a strategy game since almost all the command and options are made via a textual menu with explanations, as long as you can read the language.
The battle is not so exciting in the later game because you should always have overwhelming troops with super powerful generals, but in the beginning of the game, the battle is thrilling, especially when you win your first siege.

One negative feature is mentioned that the battle in the middle or late stage of the game is easy because your superiority against your enemies. Second, the build, develop, expansion loop of all strategy games is somewhat boring, you do all the same thing for all cities.

I really like this game and I do recommend it of you are a fan of strategy games .
