Story

With Pang‚Äôs arrival on the pizza, preservatives began to attack him!

Mold

Create 21 different types of mold to occupy a pizza!

Preservatives

Fight 32 different types of preservatives that prevent mold from occupying a pizza, destroy a building and get droplets to create more mold!

Pickle

Pickles are granted after clearing every stage. Bring them to the lab for upgrades into even more powerful troops!

Topping Slots

Get slots from each theme after clearing every stage. Equip a mold with slots and make it more powerful!

Bosses

Posted: 26 September, 2015
Strange blend of genres but works well. Similar to a tower defense game minus the towers. Replaced with disposable units to counter an attacking army that you battle on top of a pizza.
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YLHuu5DXje0
Earlier levels don't last long but quickly increased in difficulty, perhaps even needing some tweaks. Got a little frustrated due to the perfection required for stopping the onslaught at times. Other then enemy units, the other challenge faced is the moisture level, energy used for spawning units and using their special abilities. While limited and slow to regenerate, it can be increased through completion of levels and spending acquired funds on passive boosts or unit upgrades.

No screen resolutions available instead scaling to fullscreen or running in a 720p window. Sliders were available for music and sound. Mouse or gamepad worked fine but could not be remapped and worth nothing was pad needed to be unplugged otherwise game suffered from odd menu behavior. Reasonable amount of content and didn't touch the pvp or co-op modes, believe the later may only be supported locally. Nice artstyle though graphics don't stay as crisp as I would like running fullscreen. Phone port but a good one, without any micro-transactions. Huzzah.
