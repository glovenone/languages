About This Game

The captain has told you to visit Twin Peaks? No Silver Creek Falls. You arrive at your first crime scene. There is blood all over the floor and everywhere you would expect and even where you could not possibly expect! You have been assigned as the police detective for this special case with a new partner. Everything about this day is foreign to you including your newly assigned partner.

Two city detectives are sent to a small idyllic town in North Carolina called ‘Silver Creek Falls” to investigate a strange series of events that the local sheriff can’t seem to get his mind around. What initially seems like an open-and-shut case unfolds into a truly terrifying mystery that will leave the detectives wondering what is real and what isn’t.

My third is that there isn't a WASD movement option and it's awkward for me to use the arrows.

*Artwork/Animation: 10/10
-Artwork: this is a 2D pixel game which is super cute and done well.
-Animations: The animations are done nicely and I haven't experienced any glitches. The only thing I did notice is you do have to be positioned in the correct spot in order to collect things such as blood samples. For example, say you walk up to a blood splatter on the ground. If you're on the left of the pile of blood and use the spacebar or enter button to interact with the object, nothing will happen. However, if you're standing in front of the pool of blood then use the interaction buttons, you'll collect the sample. This is minor but could still be improved.

*Sound: 8/10
I couldn't tell if there was background music or not because it was so faint! The sound for entering and leaving buildings was weird and when dialog took place no one talked. It was all reading. There is a point in the game you have to listen to a recording, this part the voice actor did a nice job and I liked that you could read along to what he was saying.

*Hours to complete: 8/10
I beat chapter one within 44 minutes. I did have to listen to the sheriff's recording again though (which you can't skip unfortunately) and that's about 2-3 minutes long. So if you don't make that mistake you can easily beat it within 40 minutes. I gave it an 8/10 score only because I wish it was a little longer! I really enjoyed playing it and I'm not sure when the next chapter will be released!

*Story Line: 10/10
You play as two detectives from out of state and travel to Silver Creek Falls (which is a real place, btw) to learn about a recent mystery case that has troubled the local police department. You must investigate 3 different crime scenes to help unfold the mystery located in this small town.

*Game Play: 10/10
I had such a fun time playing this game. I love 2D pixel games. It reminds me of my childhood. From the moment I started playing, I was intrigued and entertained. My inner detective was constantly trying to figure out what could have happened to this town. Before you play there is a note in the beginning to let you know to keep your eyes out for anything that could be used for evidence. This actually did help me because I literally checked every room, locker, shelf, etc I could and found things that could have been easily missed. I collected every blood sample (43 total, which is one of the steam achievements. The other is to drink one of the blood samples) and any other evidence I could find. At the very end of the game I never suspected what happened and I'm still very confused because even the cliff hanger didn't give any information and left me with more questions! I want more of this game!!

*Price: 10/10
The best part: as of right now, the game is free to play!! I would easily pay up to $10 for this game with more chapters being available and/or being released quickly. So get it while you can!

*Overall:
I would recommend this game to anyone who likes:
>2D pixel games
>Detective/mystery games
>Short, casual games
>Chapter/story games

**Note: This review was made for CHAPTER ONE.

This is an interesting game, not really much to do outside of collecting some blood samples, notes and a few other items.. It'll take you in and around 40 minutes to an hour to finish the game, it's short, and story driven. The first part in a three part series, this one starts the story, and a very good start to say the least.

The price... free.. free always works for me. You lose nothing by trying it out..

I'd recommend this game as it doesn't require much of any time investment, it's simplistic, free, and best of all interesting.

The Story... seems to be inspired by movies like Twin Peaks, or games like Deadly Premonition.

It's not scary, or overly complicated atleast not yet. The bones are there for a interesting story to be laid out, it's just a matter of how good the developer will keep the story moving forward. Like all great mysteries not much is revealed in the first part.

The gameplay.. Is your typical RPG maker fair, move around with either the K&M or plug in your controller and kick back.. The gameplay consist of you finding items in three crime scenes,, the items all lay in plain sight and no real puzzles exist just yet.. A few items you're required to pick up have no use just yet...

I'd recommend playing this one, if enough people download load and play hopefully the developer will finish up the three part series and the remainder of the story will be revealed...

This is a game made with RPGMaker. A mystery game with a potentially interesting Chapter 2, the end of Chapter 1 has an interesting cliffhanger.

As for the gameplay itself, it's a little buggy, but mostly it's "search everything". No combat at all (and due to the nature of the game there probably won't be any). If you like a story, this game seems like it has potential.

Maybe if this game was done as a visual novel or maybe a point and click adventure using AGS instead of RPG Maker would've probably made this game feel less grindy and more intriguing.

I'll put this at recommend because the story potentially sounds great, but the gameplay is slow and tedious.

So i just finished playing Silver Creek Falls: Chapter 1, & i thought it was pretty good, & i'm really looking forward to the next chapter (Sept.).

The premise of the game is that you are a female police detective, who is sent to a small town in USA, where you team-up with another detective (from England), to investigate a crime that has the local sheriff confused.
You move from crime-scene to crime-scene, searching for clues to the mystery. There's no combat, just clue searching & dialogues that advance the story, but the story is the best part.
Even though it was over way too fast for my liking, i thought that it was a really good intro, to what might end up being a very cool story.
I'm definately keen to see what happens next.

It took me almost 2hrs (i do pause quite a bit) to finish, & that included getting the 2 Achievements.
It's a RPGMaker game, so that should give you an idea of the graphics style. The Back Ground Music was (from what i could tell) mostly mellow piano playing, that was fitting for the story & setting.

You should definately try this game. I can't really think of a reason not to. It's free. It's a small download. It's an easy Perfect Game (100% Achevo's). And it's actually good.
Have fun.
