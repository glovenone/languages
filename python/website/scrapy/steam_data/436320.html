Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“The forefront of the VR gaming revolution has attracted an outspoken, tight-knit community that is as crucial to the development process as our own internal testing and development. After the positive reception we received at VRLA in January 2016, we’ve made player feedback a key element of evolving Raw Data from a co-op experience into a fully-realized Active VR game that everyone will want to play over and over again.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“Getting Raw Data out is definitely one of our highest priorities--but so is making Raw Data one of the most compelling and immersive Active VR gaming experiences available. While we’re aiming for it to be out of Early Access by Q3 2016, it’ll take as long as it needs to gather valuable community feedback, implement it into the game and nuke every reported bug or glitch.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“We’re aiming for the full release of Raw Data to be an ambitious, replayable smash-up of adrenaline-heavy action and engaging environments. You’ll get the infinite firepower of classic arcade shooters with weapon variety, including dynamic melee combat, tower defense, deployable defenses and ability upgrades to further immerse you in the game.

Additionally, we are putting a heavy amount of focus on new maps to further expand the experience. We’re also continually revamping the in-game models and experimenting with exciting new ways to eviscerate your android enemies.

Finally--because it’s very important to us--we’re actively working toward online networked multiplayer.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The first iteration of Raw Data’s Early Access build is our showcase build that’s been played by media and trade show attendees. It features one level packed with multiple weapons, several different enemy types and a handful of deployable defenses, along with local co-op gameplay.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“We have not yet finalized the price, but all Early Access purchases will retain the full product value of the final release price. By getting early access, you also get the full game on release.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“The main hub for community engagement will be our Steam VR discussion forum. For news straight from the source, be sure to follow our official blog at http://www.survios.com/blog, where we’ll be regularly sharing updates and in-depth content about Raw Data’s production. We’ll also be active in Reddit’s r/oculus and r/vive communities throughout Raw Data’s development.”

Welcome to RAW DATA Early Access!



Greetings, SyndiK8 operatives! I’m Spencer, and I’ll be your sensei here on the official Raw Data Steam community! With Early Access coming out July 14--officially less than a week--the Survios team wanted to connect with everyone and share what we’ve got coming up.

If you’re active within the community, you’ve probably already noticed that several of the Survios devs are already here--say hi, guys! Going forward, consider yourselves honorary members of the Survios dev team, because you guys are an integral part of the journey from Early Access to final release. We’ll be answering your questions and passing feedback along to ensure Raw Data is the most immersive, compelling, and complete VR title you’ve ever experienced.

Along with keeping you regularly updated here on Steam, we’ll also be sharing exclusive news and content* on Survios’ social media channels--Twitter, Instagram , and Facebook --plus prepping the official Survios Blog with dev insights, game feature spotlights, and more.


Shout out to @NoMercyLeroy for this brilliant example of VRBadassery

*That includes awesome content made by you guys. If you’ve got any amazing play videos, physics tricks, gifs, or fanart, we want to see it and share it!



WELCOME TO EDEN
What are some of the new features you can expect in Early Access? For starters, we’ve got four exciting missions you can undertake within Eden Corp’s expansive headquarters in Neo-Shinjuku. And every single one is packed with Eden’s deadliest killing machines, from standard-issue Automos to carpet-bombing Hornet Drones to heavy-hitting charging mechs and tons more.


Oh, and you can move. That’s right: teleportation has officially arrived in Raw Data. Get up close and personal with your flash blade, or blink away and riddle them with bullets from a distance: suiting every playstyle.



SYNDIK8 RECRUITMENT
Early Access also introduces you to the first two SyndiK8 heroes with whom you’ll be infiltrating Eden Corp: Bishop, the pistol-wielding Gun Cleric, and Saija, the telekinetic Cyber Ninja. Their official operative dossiers are coming soon!

SQUASHING BUGS & TAKING NAMES
Encounter something buggy or broken while in-game, or just have some feedback for us? Tell us about it! We’re also building out a support ticket system for exactly that; it will go live shortly on the RawDataGame.com website. We’ll share the link in a pinned post once it’s live!

FIST BUMPS!
The Steam VR community has been a huge source of inspiration for us getting to this point, so we wanted to take a moment and thank you all for sticking with us as we make Raw Data a reality. Trust us: we’re extremely excited to get this game into your hands, and we can’t wait to hear your thoughts on it.

Together, we’re going to make Raw Data one hell of a VR experience! Let’s do this!

Signing off,
Spencer & the Survios team

About This Game

Neo-Shinjuku - 2271. The massive and seemingly benevolent Eden Corporation owns the world. The elite hacker resistance movement SyndiK8 has unearthed the sinister reality behind Eden Corp’s newest line of robotic products. As one of SyndiK8’s top operatives, your mission is simple: infiltrate Eden Tower, steal one geopbyte of data, and get out alive--bringing Eden Corp down in your wake.

Built from the ground up for virtual reality, Raw Data’s action combat gameplay, intuitive controls, challenging enemies, and sci-fi atmosphere will completely immerse you within the game world. Go solo—or team up with a friend—and become the adrenaline-charged heroes of your own futuristic technothriller. You will put your wits, boldness, and endurance to the test.

Active VR gameplay turns you into a controller, with instant reflex access to an arsenal of advanced weapons and cutting-edge nanotech powers. Shared spaces with avatars and motion tracking encourage players to physically communicate through body language and environment interaction.

Cool Features
  • Active VR Combat: Utilize your body to pump shotguns, draw and fire explosive arrows, dual wield pistols, hurl grenades, slash and slice katanas, deflect projectiles, do gun tricks, or even use your fists in close-quarters combat.
    • Choose Your Hero:Play as one of two starting heroes--Gun Cleric or Cyber Ninja--each with their own unique weapons and nanotech powers, including bullettime, telekinesis, sword throwing, rapid fire, levitation, charged shots, and more. Holster up and pull weapons off your body with physical loadout and check your stats on the in-game forearm display.
    • Deadly Defenses: Hack into Eden’s munitions database to access weapon upgrades and deadly defense systems to amplify your shootout strategy. Transform your environment with turrets, barricades, dynamic cover, and traps.
    • Adaptable Enemies: Battle against swaths of murderous machines, including ninja stealthers, laser gunners, heavy rocket mechs, jumping crawlers, flying drones, chargers, and more.
    • Co-op Multiplayer: Team up in a shared co-op VR space that encourages social communication via body language, facial expression, blended animation, and simulated eye contact.
