Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“We decided the best way to create the optimal modern Savage experience was to take Savage 1 as our starting point, then continue developing the game in concert with our community through the Early Access program and beyond. At this time the game is very close to Savage 1, but we plan to begin testing improvements over the coming weeks.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“The game is already in a playable state; how long we stay in EA will be determined by working with the community to decide where the game needs to go, and how far we are from that point.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“During Early Access we plan to:
  • Work with the community to improve and expand our core gameplay*
  • Enable competitive rating and ranking
  • Expand our Map offerings
  • Create new, fun, game modes
  • Create new features, as requested by the community
  • Polish and tweak art and interfaces
  • Create desirable cosmetic items
”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“At Early Access launch the game has the following features:
  • Complete Savage 1 gameplay - the game is very playable
  • 16v16 Casual matches, including quick-match
  • 8v8 Competitive matches
  • Custom matches on official dedicated servers, or hosted locally, or fully offline!
  • Stats and Leaderboards
  • Cosmetic Items + Steam Drops
  • Bots! You can sharpen your skills in practice matches, or they can fill missing player or commander slots in Casual matches
  • 4 Maps
  • Player hosted dedicated servers

Being an actively developed title, there will likely be occasional frustrating or hilarious bugs and glitches.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“No, however you will get a few extra cosmetic items with your EA purchase.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We plan to use the steam forum and reddit primarily.”

About This Game

The rebirth of the classic action FPS / RTS hybrid "Savage 1" is here! Experience fast-paced combat and cunning strategic gameplay in glorious Unreal 4 environments. Engage in epic 16v16 front-line combat, or take Command to outplay and outmaneuver your team's way to victory.

Savage is a multiplayer-only game with a competitive focus. Combat includes both melee and shooter aspects, and should challenge even seasoned gamers. Servers are currently located in the US, EU, and AUS with more planned. Players may also host their own servers, use ours, or play offline!

As a member of the fearsome Beasts or crafty Humans, you must fight to:
  • Secure strategic resources and positions
  • Craft and expand your team's buildings and structures
  • Research and acquire new weapons and items, to gain the upper hand in combat
  • Crush your opponents with mighty siege weapons, sneaky sabotage, or by strangling their economy


At Early Access launch the game has the following features:
  • Complete Savage 1 gameplay - the game is fully playable
  • 16v16 Casual matches, including quick-match
  • 8v8 Competitive matches
  • Custom matches on official dedicated servers, or hosted locally, or fully offline!
  • Stats and Leaderboards
  • Cosmetic Items + Steam Drops
  • Bots! You can sharpen your skills in practice matches, or they can fill missing player or commander slots in Casual matches
  • 4 Maps
  • Player hosted dedicated servers
  • Most likely occasional hilarious and/or frustrating bugs


During Early Access we plan to:
  • Work with the community to improve and expand our core gameplay*
  • Enable competitive rating and ranking
  • Expand our Map offerings
  • Create new, fun, game modes
  • Create new features, as requested by the community
  • Polish and tweak art and interfaces
  • Create desirable cosmetic items

*We decided the best way to create the optimal modern Savage experience was to take Savage 1 as our starting point, then continue developing the game in concert with our community through the Early Access program and beyond.

Our continuing development is funded largely by cosmetic item purchases: there is no persistent power progression system, there are no paid upgrades or boosts. This is a competitive game: there will be no Pay2Win.

Early Access Bonus

Bonus includes:
  • 4 old-school weapon skins
  • Account icon flair
  • Taunt, and taunt image
  • 1 cosmetic crate key

Digital Deluxe Edition


Exclusive bonuses include:
  • 3 gold weapon skins

  • Unique in-game vanity effect
  • Soundtrack
  • Account icon flair
  • Taunt, and Taunt Image

I love S2 Games to death, but I regret purchasing this game and can't see myself playing this game anymore until major changes are introduced.

- Let me set my mouse sensitivity to 10 like in Savage 2 (My #1 biggest pet peeve. For a game that is combatcentric, and relies heavily on turning to block and turning to attack, being able to turn your mouse is EXTREMELY IMPORTANT!)

This is the game I have been waiting for since Savage 2 died, after the death of savage 2 I found Savage XR, this game is basically a reboot/remake of XR and it is amazing!

Everyone should try this game, not many people have ever played an RTSS(real time strategy shooter) as the genre and playerbase is usually so small with little to no advertisement. Lets get this game a healthy population!

Savage is a game about asymmetrical combat, strategic teamplay and raw skill.
Beasts(can leap and are more melee/attack focused) vs Humans(can block and are more ranged/defense focused)
both teams can use various types of ranged weaponry and have a melee attack, this leads to an easy to learn hard to master simplistic combat system that rewards skill and practice.

Each team has a commander that plays in a top down view rts mode that controls the building, tech upgrading, npc workers and overall economy of the team as well as carrying out battle plans/tactics (though you cannot draw on the minimap in this one YET) commanders can assign an officer to give a health regen aura and each team has access to 3 buffs after building the corresponding structures.

Humans get Shield 80% dmg reduction as far as I know, Adrenaline speed boost, and Electricity like x4 melee dmg.

While Beasts get flame shield(dps to enemies around you) rejuvination(full hp, stam, and mana which acts as ammo for beasts) and Gate, which is basically like a teleporter you can put anywhere on the map for ez back doors etc.

The Savage series has definitely always been one of my very favorite games and this is no different, I love this game and I will be playing it for a very long time!
