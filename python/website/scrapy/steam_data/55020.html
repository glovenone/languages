About This Game

Air Forte is a high-altitude game of math, vocabulary, and geography. Compete with friends or fly solo in the various arenas. Good luck, pilot!

Key features:

Play with friends: Up to four people can play Air Forte together. Find out who's the best pilot!

Stage variety: Play through multiple stages that explore math, vocabulary, and geography.

Unlockable game modes: Once you complete the Adventure, try the "Challenge" and "Infinite" game modes.

Online high scores: Compete against people around the world. Can you beat their scores?

Gripping story: Embark on a royal quest. Can you unravel the mystery?

Air Forte is Blendo Games' take on the educational genre and they've done it very well. It may not be the best teaching tool but it is an entertaining way to review math, vocabulary, and geography. The gameplay is simple and easy to pick up. The music is catchy and goes well with Air Forte. The adventure story is funny and surprisingly meaningful whiles maintaining the unique visual style and quirkiness of a Blendo Games title.

The one complaint is that this game is far too short. While it does have different modes after completing the main adventure, there's not much reason to come back to them after you've played for an hour. Overall, it's a good game but don't buy it unless it's on sale.

It’s an edutainment title with a very short (20mins) adventure where you practice maths, vocabulary and geography by flying into the right answers. I like the presentation and you can have four people play at once. It’s just a shame that there’s so little content. The breadth of what is tested is very limited e.g. maths is just about the twelve times table and there’s no ‘learning’ element e.g. a map to study geography or explanations of what nouns or verbs are.

It’s clearly a game that was made very quickly, even using sound and music from Atom Zombie Smasher. It's clearly not worth the full asking price as is, but with a bit more time put in, it could have become a fairly good game for young children.
