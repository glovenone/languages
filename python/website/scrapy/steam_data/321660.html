This is a full on Buinsess simulator managment game through and through, there isnt anything fancy going here and you wont be getting into the vehicles and driving them.

This is purely a numbers game and all about running a business, if not a slighty dumb down way of running a buisiness it is still interesting trying to balance between contracts and selling on the free market whilst trying to keep a positive company image through various means such as safety, PR, maintance and labour effectivness.

You must also keep an eye on the various assets your company has and see what the bottlenecks in your company is which makes you have to decide when you should consider ordering new equipment or refreain as the market seems to be not so favorable and could cost you too much if you did expand.

Â£17 seems a bit steep for this game considering there is only 2 forms of resources you can choose from (iron ore & gravel), it would have been nice to see others such Bauxite, Gold, Silver etc, with the various specalist machinery that come with these.
Perhaps updates will come with these in the future.

Mabey being able to pick the site you wish to start your company and having the resources specific to that area would have been a nicer touch then starting some generic company somewhere not exatcly known.

It is a fun game if your into purely Buiness Management games, if your expecting something like Farming Sim and running your mining firm by jumping into vehicles and having your freind come and help, this isnt for you.
