Let's review this work that seems to have all Milestone fans up in arms about Ducati 90th!

Let's address some complaints.
-I get it, to others it doesn't feel like a full game.
-Bikes are lacking.
-Tracks are lacking.(I don't understand this one!)

These are the main issues I see, so before I start this review I wanna say this, keep an open mind.
From what I know, Milestone had a contract with Ducati for this game, and I feel like they did what they needed.

Now the review.
It's a barebones version of RIDE, but to say it's just RIDE all together, or a RIDE Expansion sold off as an exclusive DLC is just stupid.
The game has had several tracks worked on from France, Wales had a fix to make the track feel more immersive in its hills in the game which lead to flat out speed and aggression with full control. Imola was thought out again and doesn't feel as tiny as the Imola in RIDE, it definitely has the length I expect from it now.
And if you know the series well enough you can find the other differences for yourself.

Bikes, the count came out to about 36.
Sure, we can work with this is DLC.

Camera bugs have been fixed since the release, and everything seems to be working fine.
All together I think the people who want to bully the game really don't have many valid points.

The game as of its fix is flowing a lot more smoothly, and if you love RIDE I suggest you grab this when it goes on sale, yes! On SALE.
It's a great game, and I can say I enjoy my time on it, I plan on keeping it as a game I go back to for the sake of playing with old classics, & Ducati exclusive bikes.
Personally, I feel it was only right to put all the classic bikes together in one game until Milestone can think of something like a RIDE Classic Edition or something else that addresses other bikes from the past.

Do I truly think the game is worth it, or am I just trying to sell you to increase sales for Milestone as they're a smaller company?
I do! I think this game has its own place in the world of Moto Games. But I see where people get upset at something like this being linked in as its own title, BUT! That price tag says it all.

What are the things I actually enjoy and think others would too?
The tracks are remastered, surely not replica tracks though, we might not ever see a track that's an exact spitting image of any IRL tracks, that's why it's nice to have devs who think out of the box to bring unique things to tracks.

The bikes yet being a small list still give you something to mess around with. And something I didn't touch on much, the slip boxes are bigger in this game, so any newbie can easily find it to be enjoyable.
Even though the list is small I can see Milestone tossing in a DLC Pack or two to try and increase the games lack of bikes.

All together I love the feel and for anyone who doubts on it, give it a go before you judge it based on others reviews. It isn't the "Full Package!", it's simply something to say congrats to Ducati.
Leave it at that.
You'll enjoy it if you're a true fan of RIDE, and you won't be too let down. But don't expect big things, just enjoy it for what it's worth. A few new tracks, a list of tracks that have been changed a small bit, a nice compact bike list, and a game that'll satisfy you for a small bit of time.
