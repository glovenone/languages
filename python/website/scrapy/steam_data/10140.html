About This Game

Whether you're a seasoned player or just a beginner, get ready to tee off for a quick and fun match with four animated stylish characters. Putt your way through 36 fun-filled holes of minigolf on 3D designed courses with elevated greens, bunkers, bridges and water hazards, among other crazy obstacles. Also choose from an array of different environments to fit your mood that includes the Classic Carnival, Old West and Outer Space.
  • Carefully Crafted 3D Courses  Sweeping, panoramic views of the holes and mind-bending course designs.
  • 4-Player Multi-Player Mode  Choose from 4 highly detailed playable characters, each with their own personality and distinctive animations.
  • 36 Challenging Holes  Based on three major themes: Classic Carnival, Old West and Outer Space.
  • Creative Game Additions  Collect over-the-top power-ups and avoid imaginative traps to keep the game pace exciting and unpredictable.
  • Customizable Course Editor  Build your own course design by mixing and matching combinations of tees, straights, corners, cups, junctions and ramps.

Not bad, not great.

I've had this sitting in my library for around 8 months now I believe. When I initially bought it, I had quite a lot of fun messing around with friends and as a whole wasn't so bad, but there are definitely improvements that need to be made. The graphics are meh, and it is quite glitchy on certain maps, too. It gets very repetetive after awhile, and hasn't been played in over 3-4 months I imagine, and if I have played it since, it wouldn't have been for very long. On the other hand, the first 3 months of the purchase I found was very pleasant, and of course makes it better when you do it with friends etc.

I've decided to not recommend this game, as the point of a game isn't to just be played for a couple of months and instantly abandon it, much like I did in this case.

5/10 - Big room for improvement, but not the worst game of all time.

Cute game nothing fantastic but there are only a few mini-gulf games on steam right now.
There are 4 characters with 2 outfits in 2 colors each to pick from that all play the same.
You can also change the ball color too if you want to spice things up a bit.
The courses have themes like wild west, Inca(or Mayan) temple, space, amusement park, and a few others.
Most have more than one way to complete them with off & hidden paths that make it easier to get a hole in one.
There will be power-up in most levels like jump, fire, glue, bomb and a few more but most don't seem to help much.
2-4 player mode is only allowed on the same system but there is no timer so you're not rushed swapping people.
The editor is vary basic and just lets you build on a 4x4 grid and you can't use any of the themed stuff.

Overall its not bad the themes are interesting and feel very different from each other.
Looking for the best way through some levels add replay ability.
It won't blow you away but it's a good mini gulf game.
