26 May

CARGO DROP #1



Summary
Attention, pilots! Cargo Drop incoming to Final Approach!

We are proud to present our first "Cargo Drop" with extensive updates, feature additions, and bug fixes! Thanks to all of the incredible feedback from you since our launch last month, we've been able to incorporate some great features into this update. We’ve also added several bug fixes and balancing updates to Final Approach with the help of fans participating in our Steam forums.

Mission accomplished, pilots. We couldn't have done it without you!

We're so excited to introduce three big updates to Final Approach: Steam achievements, challenge missions, and leaderboards! These are features that add an extra edge to the game and that we're sure you'll all love. What pilot doesn't love a challenge? You can test your abilities against the clock and show off your accomplishments to all of your friends. The team is looking forward to seeing how the community enjoys these new additions.

Now, without further ado, here is the list of goodies included in the Cargo Drop:

Featured Updates
    * Added Steam Achievements! *
  • Show off your feats of strength to friends with new Steam achievements!
  • 45 Steam Achievements across a range of difficulty

    * Added Challenge Missions! *

  • Introducing timed, objective-based missions in Challenge Mode! Earn a 1 to 3 star rating based on your performance
  • Challenge Mode is accessible from the menu select in the Hangar at the beginning of the game, alongside Story Mode and Free Play
  • Challenge Mode unlocks after completing the first tutorial mission

    * Added Leaderboards! *

  • Gain virtual fame by flying to the top of the leaderboard!
  • Leaderboards are available for each mission across multiple categories (best completion time, best score, most Final Approach Points) as well as Challenge Mode
  • Leaderboards have viewing filters for Top 10 Scores, Top Friend Scores, and Scores near your ranking
General
  • Added error message if save or load game fails to main Start Screen and Flypad
  • Level 2 Spreading Your Wings is now shorter with slightly fewer objectives in order to get players into other environments faster (City, Airport, Midway)
  • Level 2 Spreading Your Wings required score for 2 and 3 stars adjusted to accommodate fewer objectives
  • Level 15 SubOptimal: enemy missile speed reduced by 10%
  • Added splish splash water impact effects to bullets fired from turrets and airplanes
  • Added ability in the Airport to temporarily pause moving cars when touched by the Player
  • Updated to Unity 5.3.4f1
Bug Fixes
  • Fixed planes landing on runways backwards due to approaching runway from wrong direction
  • Fixed Airport actors running around above ground level
  • Fixed Airport airplanes flying low enough to collide with Control Towers if the skies were heavily occupied
  • Fixed the cargo container pickup in Learning to Fly being gripped by the magnet at an offset position
  • Fixed helicopters flying sometimes crashing into buildings after dropping off carried objects and the AI taking over to fly the helicopter away
  • Fixed lights on some aircraft wing tips being in incorrect positions
  • Fixed helicable objects respawning with velocity after timing out
  • Fixed firefighting zoom points sometimes having water spraying without holding the trigger down
  • Fixed firefighting zoom points not displaying correct UI information on fire hose when putting out rooftop fires
  • Fixed ability to takeoff helicopters on fire without putting fire out by repeatedly giving them instructions
  • Fixed rare instance where many planes spawning at the same time would cause planes to overlap on spawn and explode

About This Game

Take control of the skies like never before in a full Virtual Reality world! Airplanes on fire, commuter aircraft low on fuel, mid-air collisions, emergency rescues, and military exercises all need your immediate attention. Manage your aircraft and airports in "God Mode" as you tower over large metropolitan airports, tropical islands, and towering cities. Path your aircraft to safety, avoiding air traffic, fierce thunderstorms, and perilous landscapes. Get those planes refueled, repaired, and back in the air as fast as possible. You will need nerves of steel as you zoom down to "Toy Mode" to fight raging aircraft fires, man the guns and missiles of the battleship, and the worst task of all...Luggage Sorting. Don't waste any time, the skies are full of emergencies that only you can manage. Did we mention the Alien Invasion? Welcome to Final Approach!

Room-Scale VR
Never before has a game used the full capability and power of Room Scale Virtual Space! The sky is full of aircraft of every shape and size, from F16s to Blackhawk Helicopters, WW2 Spitfires and barnstorming bi-wings. We even threw in predator drones and stealth bombers for free! Enjoy over 6 hours of gameplay, mini-games, and free-play modes!

Final Approach gives you complete control of the skies around the airports and islands as you tower over the world in what we call "God Mode". Using the HTC Vive or Oculus Touch motion controllers, reach up to grab an aircraft, draw a path from one side of the room to the other, and get the aircraft to their designated runway. In addition to managing the aircraft, the player can use zoom points throughout the game to get up close and personal with various aspects of the terrain. Scale down to real world size, where you can fight roaring fires with water hoses, interact with the guns and missile launchers of battleships, sort luggage, scare off seagulls with air horns and solve puzzles to make your way through the game.

Game play includes:
• Flying military jets and helicopters in combat and rescue missions while navigating the decks of aircraft carriers and battleships. Take control of the boat guns and missiles as you fight off enemy combatants
• Take control of a city airport, managing large commercial and private aircraft. Pilot them down to their appropriate gates while you navigate helicopters and ground crew on various tasks.
• Fly your rescue and support helicopters around skyscrapers and large office buildings in a towering metropolis as you navigate your way through busy streets to clear traffic jams, fight towering infernos, and rescue stranded citizens.
• Aircraft include F-16 and F18 fighter jets, DCA-717s, A320 Commercial Jets, Messerschmitts, Spitfires, B2 Stealth Bombers, B17s, Predator Drones, Heli UH 60 Gunners, HH65C Dauphins, Sikorsky Ch-54s, HH-60 Blackhawks and so much more!

Story Mode:
Follow our heroic pilots as they work their way through dangerous missions and hazardous terrain. But they can't do it alone, they need your help. Keep them safe while they help you decipher puzzles and challenges that could lead you to the truth and knowledge of how to stop what could be the world's greatest threat yet, ALIENS!

A very good, challenging game that provides an amount of content and gameplay worthy of the price. For my full thoughts on the game, you can watch my video review here.

Please note that my playtime is not accurately represented here, as this isn't my primary account used for gaming.

A few other things I've noted since making the review:

  • The tutorial does mention that planes can't take too sharp turns, so not to draw sharp paths. Perhaps a warning (at the very least, in the tutorial) displaying when you draw a path too sharp would help people get an idea of what kind of turning angles they can pull off.
  • Free Mode stages don't unlock until you play in each environment for the first time, but challenge levels are all unlocked after playing the tutorial only. Not sure if this was intended or not.
  • Scaring off gulls becomes too easy, as they always appear in the same exact locations. It only takes a few times until you know exactly where you need to aim your air horn, making the task take easily under 2 seconds. This kind of makes it not much of a task at all, certainly no challenge. Perhaps make the seagulls spawn in random locations, or maybe in different group sizes. (Small group of 3 birds fly away quickly, large groups of 7-10 gulls take some more noise to scare off.)
