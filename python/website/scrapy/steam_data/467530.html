I'm not 100% dissatisfied, but I strongly recommend you to think again "Am I really the type who likes this kind of game?".


1. Repetitive.

Levels are too long, with quite repetitive procedures. Just boring. This is almost at a level of violence.


2. Way too simple.

Everything you do is (ultimately) just peek the timing, click. That's all.

This game is entirely based on timing your shots right. The game that comes to mind first when playing"atorb" is the game "Zuma" without the combos. Atorb is a game where you have multipe rings that correspond with a color, you then have orbs or balls that you must get ONTO those color corresponding rings. You have an orange orb you must get it onto the orange ring, you have a blue orb you must get it onto the blue ring. It is a fun and simple game that can test your patience at times (:

To go into a depth a little more there are 3 difficulty settings easy, normal and hard.
-Easy / You can shot the colored orb onto the correct ring in any area to pass the level
-Normal / You must shoot the orbs into predetermined spots on each ring (no punishment for missing)
-Hard / You must shoot the orbs into predetermined spots on each ring WITHOUT missing, if you miss you must restart the entire level.

I think the game so far from what ive played is fun the music is soothing and a nice color scheme. I purchased the game for 2.50 USD and the full price is 3.00. Not an expensive game and I think its well worth the money!

Solid 9/10 for what it is.
