About This Game

Summer break, a time to relax and have fun. But what's the difference between a good summer and a great summer? Spending money, of course! And what better way to earn some extra cash than a car wash? Unfortunately, the local branch of Ninja Car Washing Corp. is right across the street and they don’t appreciate the competition...

Wash cars quickly to keep your customers happy and keep the money coming in. But beware! Those pesky ninja will do everything they can to run you out of business. You’ll need to use your wits, reflexes, and collection of basic car washing tools to defend against their attacks while keeping your customers happy.

Learn to wield powerful items like the Garden Hose, Giant Sponge, and Reptile Wax both to clean cars and fend off the attacking ninja! You’ll start by defending against simple threats like muddy water balloons and exploding shuriken, but prove yourself superior to such petty tricks and your reputation will grow, earning you more customers and more attention from your enemies. Use the money earned to upgrade your stand and buy new gear such as the Polishing Cloth, Textured Sponge, and Fire Hose then put that equipment to work, taking on bigger jobs and fighting off mud firing bazookas, ninja assassins, and the dreaded trained pigeon attack squad!

Features:
Action/Casual Gameplay
A hilarious story with hours of fun!
Three fast and furious arcade modes for endless replay value!
Over 25 different tools and upgrades for your car wash!
Over 20 different ninja to fight!
Choose to learn the game at a slow forgiving pace, or skip ahead to the start of the action!

[[It appears slow paced gameplay is for the tutorial only, I have updated the review]]

Ahh Car Washer: Summer of the Ninja, a game originally released on the old XBLIG, the place where you had no idea whether a game would be a shining gem or a total mess, pretty much the entirety of Steam Greenlight.

Car Washer is an interesting title but it doesn't do much to hook you in, after 15 minutes of circling random cars and using my hose to stop ninjas from messing up the cars I'm trying to clean I have given up mainly because the game is too slow and doesn't have much in terms of telling you how clean one side of a car is or what side needs cleaning meaning you have to slowly circle around until you have to switch to the sponge and repeat the process. The game could have actually been pretty fun if the game was sped up a bit, allowed you to see when a side is finished so you didn't just sit there waiting for a few seconds hoping it was done.

For what it's worth it could have been a decent title especially for the time of when it originally came out but it falls short massively with the pacing and other big issues.

While I don't reccommend it It's not to say you won't have fun with it if you can get past the slow paced nature and lack of content early on but definitely don't pick it up for £3.99, get it in a bundle or on sale. But first Try the Demo to see if this sort of game is your thing.

I give the game a 5/10 for being somewhat unique and a different experience but lacking in content and too slow paced.

[[Update]]

Okay upon further inspection it appears you can skip ahead and then the game really begins, not much however changes apart from being able to clean cars faster, still no indication which is a big issue.

Even with this speed upgrade the game is still so tedius and boring, after finishing the first Non Tutorial mission I was greeted with a new piece of Gear, ANOTHER piece of cleaning equipment which operates the same exact way, circle the car until it's done. Brilliant.

Honestly playing the game more makes me want to drop the rating more because It's even worse, by trying to add more 'gameplay' to the game it actually does the complete opposite because it makes you do the same thing more and more and doing the same boring task is not fun. It literally adds nothing new to the game.

If I were to drop it it would now be a 3/10.

    Comments
  • I am going to say this right away: I would only highly advise buying this game on sale. Otherwise, it's up to you.

  • Gameplay is not as polished as it should be; slow and sometimes tedious

  • This game is orginally from 2010, and it shows age

  • The idea behind this game is great: just trying to earn some cash, you end up fighting against a rival car wash across the street that is owned by ninjas who constantly try and sabotage your customers with mud filled balloons. It is your job to fend off the balloons with your hose; all while washing cars and trying to keep your customers satisfied.

Summary
  • Graphics: 5.
    Looks like it was made in paint, or with a very generic program. Still looks decent.

  • Music: 6.
    Nothing much here, just a few moderately catchy soundtracks on loops. Fitting, but lacking at the same time.

  • Cinematics: 5.
    Also done with paint or a similar program. Not bad looking, but could be better.

  • Story: 7.
    As was mentioned earlier, the story revolves around a young boy determined to earn money. He ends up as the target of the ninja car wash accross the street.

  • Content: 6.
    Couple hours of gameplay for those who do not mind the graphics and have some patience.

  • Controls: 6.
    Hard to get used to at first. WASD to position around the car your are washing, Q and E to switch between tools (Hose, sponge, ect.) and arrow keys to aim the tool currently selected.

  • Gameplay: 5.
    Very slow at almost all times, takes patience. If slow gameplay bothers you, you may want to look elsewhere.

TL:DR
A game that shows age with somewhat under average graphics and soundtrack, solid story, controls that take getting used to, and slow gameplay at times.
Unfortunately, I can only recommended to buy this game on sale. 6/10 at best.
