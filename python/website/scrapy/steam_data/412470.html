Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“-We aim for Early Access in order to facilitate player's progression through changes that we are about to make in game. Seeing as we are going to introduce some serious updates in future, we find it convenient to apply for Early Access status.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“-Estimated time will be from 3 to 6 months, depending on update feedback and bug fixing process.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“-There are several different things we are about to change. One of most important is machines ability system; currently, each of in-game machines has 1 ability for every class. We want to create a unique ability for each machine. Aside from that, other notable updates include paint system - we plan on making it more accessible and simple, remove characteristics from paints and reduce their prices.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“-It is a playable game, however we plan on applying aforementioned updates and see players reaction on them.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“-There will be no change in price, since our game is FTP.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“-There are several ways to involve community, but we will primarily focus on requesting and processing feedback through all the available channels, as well as participating in games together with players to see how they react on gameplay within the game.”

About This Game

Join us in the free game "Metal War Online"! We invite you to the world of uncompromising corporate wars!
Unmatched dynamic shooter with a huge selection of futuristic technology! Millions of players. Only real people as enemies and friends.

The action takes place in the near future. Powerful transnational corporations embroiled in a fierce battle for resources. The battlefield became the whole world. Choose any of the powerful war machines, win the battle and lead your side to victory!

Posted: 27 June
Tried it out, got about 5-6 matches in. Good concept, though poorly and greedily executed. I love vehical battle games but this really couldn't do it. My issues below.

-Servers are in buttcluck Europe/Russia somewhere. I can normally get 120-160 ping from UK and germany, but I get 200-260 in this game.

-Super Pay to win. There are many things in this game that trump over non paid currency items and that quickly became evident when playing.

-Not beginner friendly. Practically zero tutorial. Almost nothing to give you the basics or what you should be doing.

-Not beginner friendly PT 2: Because the game population is so small (said 320 logged in when I was playing) you will be put up against much higher ranked opponents often. Items/weapons/vehicles in this game are everything, and I often got my ♥♥♥ handed to me by high tier players with larger vehicles that can one shot my beginner scout, or high tiers scouts that can two shot my beginner scout. Skill gap plus an additional MASSIVE GAPING equipment gap when you're starting off. Leveling will be an uphill battle unless you give them the sweet money they are trying to milk from you.

-Auto aim: Everything I had a chance to try on three different chassis are all auto aim. I am assuming the rest of the game is the same. Come on. Auto aim on PC? Even if you like your precession auto aim, this further increases the equipment/pay to win gap and diminishes any skill for the game.

-Heavy Russian population/salty player base. Anyone who plays games with large Russian populations is likely well aware how toxic and angry a good number of their players seem to be. Almost all players I met in this game were Russian. Out of 5-6 matches that I played, there was one where I accidentally bumped a big tank with my little scout. So what did the rest of the team decide to do? 3 Russian players on my team decided to team up and spend the rest of the match ramming and trapping me to walls and various surfaces. All I did was be a noob and accidentally bump someone (which caused no damage)

-Pacing is slow: This might have something to do with the beginner scout and tank, but both are painfully slow. and under armoured. Some non beginner scout class appear to be faster, but not by much. Considering most things have wheels in this game, I feel like I am going the speed of a WW2 tank.

So yeah, can't recommend. I love these kinds of games, but this one? Skip it.
