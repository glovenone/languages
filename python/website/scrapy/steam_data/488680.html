About This Game

Bored of playing over and over the same Tower-Defense games?

TD Ultimate is combination of Tower Defense with RPG's and Shooter's elements. From every genre we have taken the best parts, so player can take his own tactics and get trough challenging waves with his favourite style!
If you like to make some mess, use manual main turret and shoot enemies with Minigun or Plasma Gun. If you want to improve some skills with leveling, buffing or special ammo, change mode and play as Crossbowman to shoot some enemies down. After all, don't forget to build new, upgradeable turrets. Every turret has it's characteristic properties. Some of them sets your enemies in fire and the other slows them down.

Build, improve, get new levels and fight with mythological creatures, knights, mages and other beasts to unlock all of 9 different maps!
Become a Champion in TD Ultimate!

I cannot recommend this game because it hangs after only two levels. I actually paid for this and expected the whole game, not 2 trial levels.

I played the first 2 levels but then the game gets stuck in an endless loop during the 50th wave at the point where I should have won; an endless wave of spiders just keep coming and dying at the first turret after the main tough boss made it to my base. Base weapons seem to do zero damage to the tough bosses whether it is a machine gun, gatling, or lazer. Quitting and restarting the game after 3 tries still resulted in the same endless loop.

The beginning tutorial shows you how to move and a few things about upgrading, but, there needs to be a bit more of some explanation about how each upgrade works. A brief tutorial should be reinforced to allow enough time to learn the upgrades. I would also include some hint or guide about damage types and if they make any difference; machine gun versus lazers versus different monsters.

This game is not a FPS by design. I bought it because it reminded me of a phone game just like it and wanted to veg out.

The waves are tough and the balance between defenses and monster health is spot on. You can under defend and you will lose if you don't manage the towers and help kill with the home base or the hero on the ground.
