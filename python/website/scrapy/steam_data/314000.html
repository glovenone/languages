About This Game

Pool Nation FX, is serious fun!

A visually stunning pool simulation with the critically acclaimed POOL NATION physics engine. Play 8 Ball, 9 Ball, Snooker, Killer and more, unlimited Career modes with customised difficulty levels, Trickshots, Fast paced Endurance mode, Local and Online Multiplayer, spectate live matches and feel the hustle and bustle of the Online Pool Hall. On one hand Pool Nation FX is a serious Sim which can actually improve your real life game, on the other it can provide a more casual experience; it flawlessly blends reality with the impossible, resulting in the finest example of digital pool you will ever play.

Why are there four options to choose from?

Because Pool Nation FX is a HUGE game, we want you to play it your way: by providing you the option to have everything unlocked, play only online or offline, Or earn cash and unlock modes as you compete to win... In Pool Nation FX there are no subscription fees, no barriers to playing online, just pool with friends!

    - Free to Play version of the Game

    The whole game including both Online and Offline

  • The Unlock Offline DLC pack

    What is Pool Nation FX Lite?

    It is Free to Play, perfect for those who want to work there way up, unlocking modes as you go; with limitless 8 Ball Pool online an offline practice mode, a Daily Bonus Match, Daily Trickshot Challenge and full access to the customisable features including collectable cues, balls, decals and locations. Play with your friends online, and compete to win cash to unlock modes, the more you play the more money you will earn.

    Included in the free version:
    • The critically acclaimed Pool Nation Physics

Earn money in-game as you advance through your pool career offline to use to buy cues, balls and decals. Battle it out online with the introduction of all new modes including; Play for Cash, Leagues. Explore the fully customisable Trickshot Editor. Enrol in the Pool Nation Cup and kick start your Snooker Career. Pool Nation FX is much more than just a pool game. It’s the ultimate pool game.

    Compete for a season in the Pool Nation FX Tour Career. Start in the Qualifying rounds and work up through the Round 1 through to the Final, comepete across 6 locations and finally the World tour in New York. See tour rankings and player statistics. Try to earn all the tour achievements for each tour game. Each tournament level includes 3 star challenges to compete. Tour games include 9-Ball, 8-Ball and Snooker. Play levels of Easy, Medium and Hard, with a variety of aiming lines to choose from and Good Luck.

Early Access Review
Posted: 7 October, 2015
Pool Nation FX is a Pool Simulation game that can be seen as an improvement to the original Pool Nation. I have a few things to say as to whether or not I would recommend the game or not.

Positives:

It looks great. However, having spent so much time in the original game, at this stage in time...1.6 hours... I prefer the look of the original game compared to Pool Nation FX. But perhaps the new look will grow on me over time.


More content. Pool Nation FX features an all-new career mode which is a big improvement from the original Pool Nation. It boasts many new locations and the 'play with skill to win' approach makes single-player pool a joy to play. Snooker is a great feature in Pool Nation. Me and a friend had an enjoyable time and like the other modes, are great.

Negatives:

I think the speed of the cloth could be adjusted. Perhaps there could be a slider added to the game, or a 'slow' or 'fast' speed option could be implemented. My friend and I were almost always complaining how the speed of the cloth could be better. It is fair, yes, but variety will always be appreciated.

Setting up a game with my friend had been slightly difficult as my friend had a microphone switched on in the lobby but I could not find a way to mute him so I had ended up turning the whole game down in the mixer.

Compared to Pool Nation, aiming and potting has become a lot harder, with or without aim assistance. I wish as I was taking my shot that I could look down the table even further. I have been using the middle mouse button to aim the cue more vertically to line up the cue ball better... however, it is still difficult.

I have been looking forward to Pool Nation FX for so long now that I cannot help but want any improvement the game can get. However, the game is great and I am very satisfied with my purchase. Also, thank you so much for the loyalty discount!

Thank you, Cherry Pop Games!
