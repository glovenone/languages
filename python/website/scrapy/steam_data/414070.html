Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“Shut Up And Dig is not just a game for us. The mixture of hard teamwork and talent of inspired people has resulted in a great 2D adventure we can’t wait to share with our players. Furthermore, our main purpose is to make this game more exciting, catching thus we also want our players to influence the final product. We believe that player’s experience and opinions are the best guidelines for good game developers.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“The period of Early Access is planned until 2Q 2016 . Our team hopes that the game will be completed with player’s support”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“The full version will be more polished and will be rid of any last minute problems that our players may find. We will also listen to feedback and decide if anything needs changing before full release.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The first world of our game is completed, and there are also a lot of features to be implemented and tested before the final version is ready.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“The difference between the early-access price and the final price will be small; it will increase slightly when we leave Early Access.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“Players will be involved through our public web pages, Steam forum, e-mail, Twitter and any kind of feedback providing resources.”

About This Game

Shut Up And Dig is an exciting 2-d roguelike game made in fantasy theme. The main hero is an elf-girl, who is exploring the mysterious worlds of haunted dungeons.
All the elements of the magic world of this game is connected with each other. The terrifying world you have to explore is full of powerful artefacts, secrets, demons, monsters and ghosts.

• Presently, the first world of the game is completed : it's around 10hrs gameplay

• The game will have 5 absolutely different and also randomly generated worlds and more than 10 secret worlds. Every world has its own design, music and gameplay.
• During the game you will meet great amount of bosses.
• All the elements in this magic world interact with each other.
• The dangerous underworld you will explore is full of powerful artifacts, secrets, demons, monsters and ghosts.
• Every game will be unique. Get ready to survive in this dark and doomed world.
• The process of finding the artifacts and special tools is very interesting; you have to solve difficult puzzles.

This game is in Early Access so don't take these too seriously

- The pickaxe (your main weapon) has far too short of an attack range. You will likely take damage by weak monsters just because you have to walk so close to them for your weapon to even do damage.
- No sense of direction, goal, or story. You are literally wandering around aimlessly exploring.
