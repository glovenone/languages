Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“Garlock Online is a 3rd person shooter-rpg game. As a player you will get a lot of functions to immerse the world of Garlock. Take a look on a game which based on two genres. As a player you will get a lot of events, growing difficulty quests and a own crafting system. With a high number of different monsters the player can show what he can in PVP and the whole world of Garlock. for all zealos players are the King Quest's whre you can earn more experience to grow your level.
The combination of rpg and the shooter genre can be played from boys and girls, young or old.
to bring Garlock Online on a higher standard we will need your support to add more features on the early access.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“one year maybe to improve the game.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“to improve the number of king quest's, the playable ways through Garlock Online. On the end we want the graphic perfect that everyone tho plays the games has the "WoW" effect when he entered the first time a city or goes out to green fields.

At the moment you can grow your level up to 60 later you can level up to 98 and we want to add more areas, pvp battles and realistic sounds.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“At the momemt you can make a lot but at the moment not everything is on the newest version so you can still find some bugs what we need to patch.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“Garlock Online is a free to play game with an ingame item shop where you can pay money to get more characters for real money."”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“In the WIP we will bring out an forum for all fans of the game on the Galock Online Hompage, whre you can ask us your question, to talk with us and to get the latest news to events. On our homepage you can apply yourself as an GM.”

About This Game



About the game Embark on a journey in Garlock Online with your friends. Collect items, fight numerous monsters, and gain achievements throughout the game. Build, recruit and upgrade your summoned monsters together in battle for fighting and also in PvP battles. In Garlock you can choose between 3 class types and equip them with items and armor to level up throughout the game. Each character has the ability to summon monsters that they can use in battles. Keep them in good health to ensure their longevity and help them level up along beside you. Level up and become stronger than your foes. Defeat other players in PvP battles and be the strongest of them all! Donât forget to seek out a faction to join and receive bonuses. Play hard to get into mini instances and gain more to experience opportunities!
