Damnation City of Death
Damnation City of Death features survival gameplay, where you fight against monsters, cold, hunger, thirst and other players. The Gameplay is an action packed fight for survival, where you always need to be ready for the next encounter you face. You can also do various missions with friends.

Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“Early Access allows us to work with the community to make Damnation City of Death into what our fans want. The Game has already been in a playable state as some fans have already been playing the game. We want to develop the game with the whole community and see what the fans make of the game as we add new features and get near completion. Early Access also gives us more funding, which allows us to add more features to the game what we otherwise couldn't be adding to the game.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“We plan to release in 2016. We will work hard with our small team to reach this goal.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“Damnation City of Death features will be refined. We will add new features and refine the old ones with the community's feedback. Including at least these:

NPCS - Their actions are currently minimal and we want to bring them more life and give them a bigger role in the game.

Game modes - Survival has been our main target as it includes the core gameplay of the game and we will continue to develop it to have a long lasting interesting gameplay. Nuke and Alien Threat are in a early playable state, but still missing depth. Rescue is still in very early state and unplayable. We will make sure that all game modes have enough depth and interesting gameplay before we release the full game. The full version will have the rescue game mode, which we promised on our Greenlight Page.

Enemies - We will add more actions to bring more variety, when encountering with them. It is very important to us that our players enjoy fighting them and that they provide a good challenge.

Crafting - Currently it works nicely, but we will add more depth into it.

Stealth - This part of the game has been just added and we hope we can improve it with the community to be a viable play style.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The current state of the game is strong. The game can already be played for hours with friends without game breaking bugs. Some bugs and missing features still remain in the early access version, which we will try to nail down before 1.0 version.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“The price of Damnation City of Death will increase after launch without going into specifics.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“The community is the heart of the game and will offer us the required feedback on the game features and balance. Hardware performance of our players can be improved as we get information from the community, which would otherwise be really hard to nail on the scale of our team. We have already taken good feedback from our early adopters, but we would like to bring this to the next level with the whole community as we get closer to the release.”

About This Game

Damnation - City of Death is a survival game set in a procedurally generated city filled with content. Search vital items for your survival and deal with a load of zombies & monsters at the same time. But you don't have to do all of this alone! Join forces with other survivors and start to secure your surroundings. You can find everything you need to survive from the city. You just have to have the willpower to make the climb and start to gather your supplies. Make friends and enemies with other players to create your own unique stories.
Surviving is a choice you make.

Key Features


  • Dynamic enviroment - Procedurally generated city with day to night cycle.

  • Base building and crafting - Protect yourself by building a base. Craft items for your survival from items found by scavenging the city.

  • Enemies - Enemies vary from zombies to large monster to embrace the co-op aspect of the game. Other players can still be a large threat to you.

Game modes


  • Survival - Survive in the randomly generated city against monsters and other players. Find & create shelter and fight against cold, hunger and thirst.

  • Nuke - A Nuke has been set in the city to destroy it. The infection threat has been succesfully contained and a strike is no longer required. The bomb location is unknown in the city as the team guarding it disappeared and the bombs location transmitter turned off. The civilian casualties are enourmous if it goes off. You and your team have to disarm the bomb before it explodes and get in time to the rescue chopper.

  • Alien Threat - A Giant monster roams in the city. Military has ordered a special team to neutralize the threat before it gets out of the city and releases all the horrors that have been blocked away from the rest of the world.

  • Rescue (UNAVAILABLE) - The President has approved a plan to exterminate all living things from the city. Your team has been given the final chance to rescue all remaining civilians from the city before airstrike hits and destroys every single living thing that walks in the city.
