About This Game

Revel in a brilliant story, fast-paced puzzles and thrillingly designed levels in reportedly the first adventure of the action adventure platformer saga about Bob, a truly brilliant fellow from Sekotwana!
Featuring 50 levels in 2 astonishing worlds, in-game achievements for you to get and 10 unique skins for our hero - Bob. It allows you to change the camera angle so you can view Bob and his surroundings from any perspective you want to or have to in a specific level or situation. 8 different thematic enemies through the first and the second world, hazards, shooting cannons and a whole bunch of puzzles and skill-demanding level traps.
Are you brave enough to join Bob and help him in his quest of justice? Are you ready to become a part of his brilliance?
