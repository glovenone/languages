I can remember years ago when I was smaller my Mother loved this game. I recently searched up on Steam as to whether it offers this little bundle of joy. And well, it did.

A classic drag-and-match puzzle game. There are many out there like this. But I would have to say that this one is the grandmother of them all, as far as I'm concerned. The little cute, fuzzy, multi-coloured alien-ey things just help add to the appeal. The sound effects are incredibly adorable as well. You can easily spend around an hour completing classic mode on the hardest difficulty - possibly more. So, don't try and play for a quick 5 minutes or so. It's a trap; don't do it!

There are a pretty decent amout of achievements available in game - which will make a fun time trying to get them all. I, myself don't have all of them yet. However, it could prove quite frustrating trying to get them all. One of them is to complete a whole game without ONE wrong move. Surely, that's next to impossible, unless you strategically plan your moves beforehand. Which just adds to the beauty of this game.

It is extremely challenging at times, which is what we all look for in a puzzle game. Why else would we want the puzzle genre?

Overall, cute graphics, amazing sound effects, challenging gameplay with around 4 or so different modes and achievements to get that fire in your heart going just that bit more. I would definitely recommend this game to absolutely anyone; regardless of age. Fun for the whole family!
