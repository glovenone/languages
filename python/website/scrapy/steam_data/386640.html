8 July

AZT Update Released

An update to Affected Zone Tactics has been released. Included changes:

MEDICINES BALANCE

Watching for the effectiveness of medicines in the battle, we came to the conclusion that common and premium variations should be more balanced and should have a smaller effect on survivability. Medicine was extremely effective, allowing you to quickly heal wounds and withstand powerful attacks for a long time. This had a negative impact on the feelings of the battle and its duration.

Stimulants
We decided to remove the side effects of common stimulants, now they provide only useful effects. This will help to make them more popular and competitive.

Nanofixators
Now medicines heal limbs not at once but one limb every 2 turns.

Injectors
Injectors have also been revised. As a result their parameters have become more balanced.

NEW MAP: DRONES BASE

This map invites you to a factory for the production and servicing of combat drones for corporations. Base area has a very good guard on the perimeter, which is not surprising - there is some factories and fortified bunkers, where high-tech weapons are stored. Some mercenaries are ready pay a lot to get a couple of these drones in its arsenal.

Click to zoom

FIXES:
  • Fixed Commander XP receiving.
  • Fixed joining battle with overdrive.
  • Fixed user maps.
The update will be applied automatically when you restart Affected Zone Tactics.

Have a nice game!

About This Game

Welcome to Affected Zone Tactics, a tactical online game with cooperative turn-based PvP combats. Travel through the Tesla portal to a dangerous parallel world, lead the squad of reckless mercenaries, and outrage mighty corporations and cliques.

Key features:

  • Cooperative turn-based battles
    Fight together with other players in a unique mode of tactical turn-based combats using covers and capturing strategic positions. Unite into clans and play with your friends.
  • 4 classes and 12 specs
    Hire various spec fighters and build a squad that meets your tactical preferences. Every spec has its own set of equipment, skills and combat tactics.
  • Many types of weapons & equipment
    Fight, get experience, and study new types of mercenaries' combat gear. The game features more than 100 real models of weapons from pistols and assault rifles to machine guns and grenade launchers.
  • Commander's career
    Move your main character along the carrier ladder by making the squad grow and by taking new battles. Take over command over allied fighters.

Features we are going to add:

  • Clan hierarchy
    Development of clans and their players.
  • Clan wars
    Advanced clan clashes and clan ranking.
  • Player's and clan's bases
    Construction and development of your own base.
  • Story PvE missions
    Fighting against mutants and monsters, performance of combat missions assigned by corporations' agents.
  • Global map
    Global clan wars and territories seizure.

System Requirements

    Minimum:
    • OS: Windows XP, Vista, 7, 8
    • Processor: 1,4 GHz or higher
    • Memory: 512 MB RAM
    • Graphics: 128 MB VRAM or more
    • DirectX: Version 9.0
    • Storage: 1 GB available space
    • Sound Card: DirectX 9.0 compatible
    • Additional Notes: 1280 x 800 display resolution, 1 Mbit/s internet connection

- missing english transaltion in some places
- changes in the interface casuing impariment of functionaity
- server disconnections in some cases

----------------------------------------------------------------------
I played this game for a few days. It seemed to be fine, very similar to Fallout Tactics, but simpler.
Decided to buy packages from Steam.. and it appeared to be mistake.
At the beggining matches were fine, you could win, you could loose. Depends on applied tactics if you had any. But after buying premium wepons, games started to be heavily unbalanced. Of course, if premium weapons are used in battle.
Even if you update only secondary weapon, staying at rank 1, you are matched against much more armored players. I do not mean Heavy Infantry, but commandos - sometimes 3 or 4 of them in team of 8. Each sniper shot takes 50 to 100 health, so your practically dead behind any cover, which sniper can make a shot. I can handle it somehow, but I am not the only one there.
And here we came to bots. In any match I played, I had seen only bots on my team. They are no opponent for any player. The more higher level player, the more stupid behaviour of bots seems to be. These can do many ridiculous things ending their short and mostly not useful life.

They can:
- change desicion on where they want to go in last moment before reaching the preavious target
- shoot distant enemy leaving enemy behind his back (1-3 fields away) untouched
- run from battlefield leaving almost dead enemy, just to let him patch the wounds
- takig cover in direction to no enemy, when enemy is on the very close flank
- charge in the middle of enemy line, just to get killed in a few shots
- charge on the enemy position, leaving perfectly good cover, in the situation of imminent threat, for the fun of being killed
- and many other interesting behaviours, like steping into mine field, which was set up in front of his eyes

And I though it is a fine game because it is so similar to Fallout Tactics. More or less it is, but requires a lot of polishing on AI and team balancing (I didn't mentioned that in details.. but there is a lot to say here also, like getting almost only grenade soldiers, against team with a few snipers - certain death and miserable defeat).
What's more, it still can give some fun, but condition for fun is to not buy any special gear (for gold or through steam packs). I mean.. no problem with buying it, only no point to use it on battlefield.
I did that mistake. Lost money, lost fun, lost hope for good team balancing.
