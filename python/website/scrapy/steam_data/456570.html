I had heard of Muv-Luv before playing this, but it was never really something I thought about reading. I still don't know if I would, but with it coming to steam this VR demo has definitely put it on my radar. This lets you move through a brief section of what I assume is an action scene from the visual novels, and you make your way through a snowy area while large monsters try to ambush you. It's simple and short, but the visuals are pretty good and they do a decent job of startling you. Afterwards it plays the opening video for Muc-Luv Alternative, and lets you teleport to various positions on one of the large mechs to see it up close. It's not amazing, but it's a pretty interesting concept in that they're essentially making an entirely different product in a different medium for the sake of advertising another product. Being able to step into a scene from a visual novel in a 3D VR game did far more for me than any number of banner advertisements could ever have done, and I could very easily see this becoming the future of advertising.
