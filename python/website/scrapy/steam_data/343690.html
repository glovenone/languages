About This Game

Silicone-2 - a shooter with a first-person. In the story the player will have to quell the uprising of robots.
Features: mission both indoors and in the open spaces (sandbox), help Allied soldiers, vehicles, each scene is accompanied by a videoclip on the game engine for Silicone-2 written 20 songs.
