About This Game

For years, there have been documented cases of UFO sightings around the world – Buenos Aires, Seoul, France, Germany, China. But in 2011, what were once just sightings becomes a terrifying reality when earth is attacked by unknown forces. As people everywhere watch the world’s great cities fall, there remains one place that becomes the last stand for mankind in a battle no one expected. It’s up and your team to draw a line in the sand and take on an enemy unlike any we’ve ever encountered before.

Key features

  • Immersive first person shooter experience. AAA boxed product visuals and heart-thumping 5.1 surround sound pull you into devastatingly deep into the heart of the battle.
  • Defend Los Angeles. Face off against the enemy in familiar locations from a besieged City of Angels.
  • Relive the thrill of the movie. As Corporal Lee Imlay, you will fight alongside characters from the film, including Staff Sergeant Michael Nantz, Tech Sergeant Elena Santos, Corporal Kevin Harris and others on your mission to save humanity.

Basic FPS based on the B-movie "Battle: Los Angeles". You can shoot, throw a grenade, sprint a very short distance, jump (although there is literally never a need to), crouch, etc. The cut scenes are done in sort of a comic book style, which I enjoyed. Not terribly difficult when played on normal, which is what I beat the game on. Unlockable bonuses include still pictures and video, nothing exciting.

The number 1 thing you need to know is that this game is super short, and not challenging. I beat it in 1 sitting in about 45 min, dying twice in the process. I probably could beat it again in 30 min. It is not going to light the world on fire, it is derivative, there are some minor pathing difficulties, etc etc. This is not a AAA title.

However, all in all, this is a pretty good game. I got it on sale for $2 and I feel like I got my money's worth. If you just want to turn your brain off for an hour and shoot aliens, this is going to work fine and I would recommend it on that basis.
