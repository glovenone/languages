About This Software

Even if you don't have a band, you can realize your musical vision using virtual instruments. Music Creator and SONAR include new Cakewalk Sound Center, which gives you access to at least 150 different instrument sounds. It includes acoustic & electric guitars, basses, drums, pianos, synths, strings, ethnic sounds, and more.

Cakewalk Sound Center is bundled with the following Cakewalk products on STEAM.

  • Music Creator 6 Touch
  • Music Creator 7
  • SONAR Artist/Professional/Platinum
  • SONAR X3 Base/Studio/Professional

So I wrote a negative review on this software a while back but I was confused on what software I was using. So I was going to change it however after doing some more research I found out that the software I was confused on was made by the same company! Surprise, surprise the software that keeps asking me to register is "Music Creator 6 touch" a program that keeps asking me to register despite already registering. It's not a big surprise that software got taken down from the store. If you want to check the reviews for that software I recommend check out "Music Creator 7 touch". Ironically enough, even the software that I was confused with wouldn't work. It just doesn't work, I thought it was a steam issue but nope it's the program itself. After an hour scratching my head on why a 10 minute downloaded software won't work I just gave up. And no I don't want to register to access your fourms to ask why the software isn't working while the company spams my email to oblivion. Sadly the Cakewalk manager works perfectly fine. If you want a music software this software isn't recomended (although I'll admit Sonar looks promising). If you want something better splurge a little bit more on music software that actually works.
