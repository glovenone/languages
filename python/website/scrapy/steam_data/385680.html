Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“During the development process we have received valuable feedback through our forums, newsletter and alpha testing. Player feedback is at the core of our game development and to emphasize that we start the early access phase of our epic sci-fi strategy game, Galactic Conquerors.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“We leave early access July 30th 2016. At that point we feel that the game will only need minor tweaks and bug fixes to be ready for 1.0 release.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“We plan to add additional features, such as galactic council phase and game editor into the game to allow players make additional content into the game. Also new playable race and additional game modes will be added to the game during the early access phase.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The current early access version will include the core features of the game, such as planet management, battles with up to 8 players, 2 races with their commanders and units, with up to 60 different skills.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“Game will be sold cheaper during the early access phase than what the price of the full version will be. The price of early access version will reflect the amount of content in the game.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“People buying the game increase our belief in the game and allows us to add more resources into the development of Galactic Conquerors. We will listen to player feedback closely and take note of players' ideas and issues to improve the gameplay.”

About This Game

Galactic Conquerors is a simultaneous turn-based sci-fi strategy game with tactical gameplay and fast paced, action filled battles. You need to fight to control planets and resources by mastering different kinds of commanders, units and their skills in multiple on-going battles in order to overcome your enemies. Orbiting planets and other objects create an ever changing playground for epic and massive battles up to eigth players.



  • Simultaneous turn-based strategy gameplay.
  • Fast action based battles.
  • Immersive sci-fi world
  • Two playable factions (Human and Gramos) with unique commanders, units and skills.
  • Up to 8 player multiplayer games.
  • Cross-platform gameplay



  • User made content (editor)
  • One additional faction
  • More animations
  • Diplomacy to receive advantages over foes
  • Steam achievements and Cloud support
  • Dedicated servers
  • More maps, more balancing and several other features

I have high hopes for this but so far I am completely unimpressed.

Currently this is in a nearly unplayable (and frustratingly confusing) state.

If you know how to play other games of the genre such as Master of Orion then it wont help you here. The movement method consistes of: Click ship, drag a yellow line to your LINEAR ONLY destination, Right click. Hope it "takes". If it doesnt, (and normally it doesnt) repeat. Until you get a green line with another yellow line next to is. then right click and hope it doesnt change or erase your straight line flight path.

Combat is also confusing. I couldnt get my ships to fire on the first 2 tries at combat. So I played the tutorial. That was an exercise in frustration since 90% of the time I did what it said it wouldnt target the ship. It gave no explanation why or hints how to do so. then my entire fleet in the tutorial was obliterated on the first turn.

the main play screen looks alot like Galactic Empires 2 but less pretty. (this is not a good thing since that is one of the worst of the genre)

I sure hope this gets a lot better!
