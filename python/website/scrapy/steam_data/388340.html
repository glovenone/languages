About This Game

You can't beat this game!!! Super Hipster Lumberjack is a love letter to very difficult flash platformers. It is a collaboration of ideas I have had since I was a kid and the games I have always enjoyed playing. In this story Noah attempts to make a game for the first time. He then gets trapped in his own creation. He learns that making games is a lot harder than he thought.

http://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=695657772

While looking over the reviews before purchasing this game, I couldn't understand how the opinions of Super Hipster Lumberjack could be so polar, but after playing the game first-hand I completely understand.

On the surface, this game has pretty much everything I want in an Indie platformer. Uncommon visuals with a steady curb of difficulty without being disproportionately hard at times. That's really all I ask for from this game, pretty simple, but the issue I have is that its not really a "game" at all.

I'm thrown into a world without context or meaning. No story to drive me, and no reason why I should leave the 90's 2D paradise that is my room. I would be perfectly content there honestly, but I suppose that would make for an even more lack-luster game. Even a single speech bubble would be nice, or a booming voice telling me to go right would do this game a lot of good. But no dice.

The mechanics are pretty solid, and even though this "game" doesn't quit bring anything new to the table, its worth the low price tag for some cheap (good) rage, and entertaining visuals. Overall, I recommend this as long as you have the right expectations going into it.
