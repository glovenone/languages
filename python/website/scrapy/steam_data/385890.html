About This Game

Golden Rush is a rather unusual fantasy MOBA for four teams. Here you can become one of the legendary heroes fighting against monsters, dragons, and other adventurers for the treasures of the Golden Lands.

There are no lines or creeps and nexus here. Wealth is your only goal. You are to slay monsters and seize their chests with gold, become invisible, steal treasures from other teams, defend your own gold and triumph in the dynamic fantasy PvP. Four teams make the situation on the map unpredictable: victory, seemingly so close, may slip away in one second. Golden Rush is no place to relax â it's a treasure hunt you have to carry through!

Here's what else you will experience in the game:

  • Level up your hero after choosing one of six basic classes, be it a seductive witch or a furious barbarian.
  • Look for a unique tactics of victory in the new MOBA that is different from all the other ones.
  • Slay hundreds of monsters, defeat bosses and turn into a Bone Dragon that tosses enemy heroes around as if they were puppets.
  • Collect artifacts looted from monsters and assemble unique armour sets.
  • Take part in rating battles and monthly tournaments.
  • Organize well-coordinated teams of friends and fight against experienced opponents.

Golden Rush is a free to play PVP Action Battle game that is in Early Access. It worked, looked ok, sounded ok, played ok, was understandable in english. Needs more work, has rough gameplay issues. needs more players. Gameplay is Hard to compare to any MOBA game. Pure PVP, have only 3v3v3v3 map, no PVE practise. Needs Skills, Teamwork and Coordination to win battles. Needs better tutorial. You can earn the currency Black Gem to buy better gear by winning battles or buying it by paying real money. Try it, if you like action games. I applaud the devs for trying to make a different game.

- many leave the match after a few min, they get replaced by a useless bot who gets farmed by other teams
- at early access release you were able to buy full legendary set for 40$
- endgame meta is quite unbalanced (90% of the ranked leaderboard is either archer or mage)
