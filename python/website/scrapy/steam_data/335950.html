About This Game

About the Game
After the largest and most devastating biological war on record, human beings have become extinct and alien insects have evolved and morphed at a unfathomably rapid rate to become the apex predator. You play as one of these hybrids armed to the alien teeth with arsenals of high tech weaponry - the battle is on to control the last of the planets vital resources.

Insecters War will require both skill and strategic expertise. How will you level up? Which load outs will you choose? How will you defend your base? The choice is yours..Learn from victory, learn from defeat..learn from your opponents.

Feature
  • Multiple direction shooting, easy to play.
  • Moba model added, enjoy cooperating or competing with your friends!
  • Modified talen system; with less equipment requirement and more tactic skills.
  • Mobile suit included, more heat in battleground.

System Requirements

    Minimum:
    • OS: Windows XP or later
    • Processor: 1GHz
    • Memory: 1 GB RAM
    • DirectX: Version 9.0
    • Network: Broadband Internet connection
    • Storage: 200 MB available space

Insecters War has neat art and smooth gameplay, that being said however it needs a lot of work in many places including the ability to rebind keys, better AI, and above all it needs more players. It is almost impossible to find a game, and just playing through the tutorial and playing with AI is not enough to enjoy this game fully. I can see Insecters War being at least decent fun with more players but as it stands I think you will have a hard time enjoying it with the low population it currently has. Mechanically the game seems to be fun though, and I like it enough to say it could very much be worth while if given proper attention.

This game should have been released under the Early access option, since its current state as-is, the game is a mess.

Cons:
- Sloppy translation with numerous typos

-- Inability to re-bind keys, plus despite showing Gamepad UI in the store page the PC version doesn't actually display it anywhere in the game or settings. Gamepads do work, however you have to figure out what does what in game- cause again, the game only displays KB+M UI and tips during tutorial and in game.

- Sloppy formatting: When reading the expanded info about each class, nearly every description either continues beyond its intended bounding box or is just abruptly cut off mid-sentence. Same with Skill info boxes- cut offs and typos.

--- There's zero player base, max I've seen online at any point since purchasing is 7. The funny part with this is the game locks you from playing anything but 1 vs 1 until you're a certain Level- So I'm not even able to Que into those people games. Though I doubt they're public to begin with, most likely customs.

Also- the enemy AI is non-existent beyond run forward and shoot, then retreat once out numbered. It's honestly the most barebones it could ever be, and creates zero incentive to play bot matches.
