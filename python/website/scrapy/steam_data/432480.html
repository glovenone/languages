Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“It may sound unbelievable but we have been developing the game for more than 5 years so far. Quite a lot of time right? We had a lot of ups and downs during that time and even gave up a few times.

For the last 10 months we have been fully focused on finally bringing the experience to life. We feel that it is the right time to get a real feedback from players to find out if it was all worth it.

Even in the current development state, everything in the game is subject to change. We will be updating the game on a regular basis and mainly, we will be listening to your comments and will do our best to adjust the experience according to your feedback.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“We expect Grenade Madness to be in development as an Early Access for one year presumably. The final release should come sometime in Q1 2017.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“As stated in the answer below, we have a lot to add to the game, including game modes, weapons, maps and other content. We feel that we can make a lot better game with your help so the final result will be largely affected your feedback during the Early Access phase.

Beside new content and the gameplay adjustments we plan to create a whole new customizable character, which the game surely deserves and also better AI so you can enjoy the game even if there are no players online. Also we plan to create an in-game map editor.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“Grenade Madness will now be available as an early access game, because it is still under development. It is fully playable, however. Right now the game contains one mode, but the gameplay, we invented gives us a plenty of space for experimenting with many different modes. As we will be finishing the game, we would be very thankful for the steam community feedback. It will help us not only as an evaluation of how the game should look like, but also as a motivation.

As for the other content, such as weapons, power-ups and maps - a vast amount of it is already available in the game as it will be in the final release.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“We do not intend to change the price but as we will be shipping the new content and features (during and after Early Access), it could change.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We will share our project roadmap on the community forum, with the ability to vote on each feature you want to see us focus on next. We will also do our best to interact with the community via the forum and publish notes each time we release an update.”

26 June

Weekly News #4

The week is gone, the update is behind us and i have a short summary for you. Today it's going to be a little tungled, because we have run into some troubles.

Graphics

Ok, i won't beat about the bush. The Unity update won't be happening. Yeah I know, unfortunately we tried to move to the new version, but the Unity developers have not yet fixed the bug, that is strongly influencing the light rendering, which is making the game look bad.

But do not panic (like someone is panicing...), we surely have a lot to try out. Not updating to the new Unity is not the end and we have a lot of graphic stuff to implement and stylize the game.

Update 0.6

We hope that you like the new patch 0.6, it's an update that is mainly focused on balancing the gameplay and tiding up the features to make more sense while combining with each other. We will continue to make such changes and we will add two new features in the next one or two patches; a bigger one - the Madness mode, and the other one - Traits, that will replace the melee weapon slot. If we manage to be effective, the Traits are likely to be a part of the 0.7 update.

Madness Mode

As I mentioned before, the main purpose of this update will be the Madness mode. This feature is still in it's infancy but the basic principle is very clear already. I don't have a plan for the specific events, however. In this phase the technical implementation will also be important. But it should not be problem because, as I mentioned the Madness Mode is the main purpose of the update.


Visualisation how Madness Mode might look like in game (not final)

Today, as I noted at the beggining, the report is a shorter one, because not many things actually happened while realising the update 0.6. If you have any questions, feel free to ask me below this post (your ideas, visions or any questions for us about the development). I will reply to you as soon as possible. It's all for now and as always - good luck at the arena!

About This Game

Grenade Madness is a crazy round-based multiplayer shooter with lots of explosions. Start your room, customize your loadout and challenge your friends now! The game is running on Unity 3D engine.

Are you ready to test all the various explosives and humiliate your enemies? Choose your gear and find your own gameplay style. The arena is full of robots and itâs just up to you how long you are going to survive.

Boost your abilities using power-ups

  • Collect power-ups and get a huge advantage over your enemies
  • Combine your power-ups with different weapons to boost your abilities and become an invincible warrior

Choose the right weapon and find your own style


  • Try various types of powerful grenade launchers
  • Blow up your enemies with frag grenades
  • Demolish the box-based arena using sticky grenades
  • Place traps using tactical mines
  • Or do it the old way using different melee weapons
  • Unlock new weapons by gaining experience points and leveling up

Modify the environment using the crate gun


  • With crate gun you become literally untouchable
  • Trap your enemies between the blocks you build and let them suffer
  • Protect yourself when outnumbered and run away as a coward
  • Explore different ways to make the crate gun more effective with power-ups.

Accomplish the missions and win rewards

  • Learn to use the right weaponry in every situation by finishing the training missions throughout the game
  • You get reward for every mission you accomplish.

With Grenade Madness you will experience:

  • Epic battles and infinite loads of explosives
  • Original visuals and sounds including funny voice-over commentary
  • Powerful experimental weapons
  • Tactical and devastating power-ups
  • Advanced ragdoll physics
  • Level system based on experience points
  • Unlock new weapons and create the ultimate arsenal
  • Competitive leaderboards with players from all over the world
  • Unique training missions with special rewards
