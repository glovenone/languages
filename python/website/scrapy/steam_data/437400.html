About This Game

This Game is designed for Virtual Reality. must need a HMD Device.


SOLRAVEN is rail scrool shooter game for Virtual Reality.
Move Your HMD for Fly through the Valley.
Lock on your Enemy and Shoot'em!

---------- Feature ----------

  • 4 Campaign Mission
  • High Quality Musics
  • 8 types of Enemys
  • Free Play

SOLRAVEN was made to study the VR.
You may be disappointed with the content of the short game.
But I think this is better now than when the enemy will be randomly generated.

---------- Control ----------

  • HMD Control : Fighter Pitch/Yaw/Roll
  • Right Mouse Button : Fire Missile
  • Left Mouse Button : Fire Gun
  • ESC Key : Quit Game
  • Enter Key : Calibrate HMD Position
  • Left/Right Arrow : Tutorial On/Off (In Main Menu)

Except Oculus DK2 was not tested in the device.
But I think it would go well with most of the HMD.
If you have problems, please send an e-mail.

---------- Developer ----------

LuiCypher(Lee Jae Hyeong) / Inception Soft.

I understand that this game is for learning VR.

I used the DK2 for this review.
Unfortunately, the flying and aiming controls are handled by the same movement. That means you cannot aim at enemies while flying in another direction. This leads to you flying in the line of fire just to aim at your enemies, and dying as a result, when you're just trying to land a hit with the very weak weapons of your machine.

This also means that enemies on the ground can only be hit by looking up to fly up, then looking down to aim at them, consequentially flying into the ground just to take aim and shoot at them. You're more likely to pass missions by avoiding all of the enemies and their projectiles, and to not bother shooting anything.

The game looks 'decent' if not super basic, as is the music. There is nothing fun about this 'game'. I do not recommend anybody to play or try this. There are better games out there that provide a more interesting VR experience.

TLDR
1/10 Crash To Aim Simulator
