About This Game

The world's cutest creatures arrived...and want to play!
In Adorables you can play with 3 fully customizable cute characters in 6 different game types. Play against your friends, collect coins, customize your characters and win the Tournament or the World Tour modes. Adorables offer a lots of fun for you, don't miss it! But beware, it's very addictive

The control is very simple. You only need to click on the character to control and aim with the arrow.


Main features:
  • Platform free multiplayer

  • 3 single player modes (practice, world tour and tournament)

  • Fully customizable characters

  • 6 games (soccer, beach ball, CTF, Last Adorable Standing, Ice Hockey, Mummy)

  • Turn based and fast game modes

  • 8 languages (English, Hungarian, Chinese, German, Slovak, Spanish, Portuguese, Russian)

  • Achievements, leaderboards

  • You can select from the world's all countries to represent in the competitions

  • Controller support

  • HD graphics

  • Tooltip

Cute carrom-based strategy game for all ages

- You control a team of 3 players, where the aim is to beat the other team. You do this by specifying in each turn, how you want your 3 players to move.

- There are a few different game types to choose from: e.g. soccer, ice hockey, capture the flag, escape the mummy, knock your opponent into the water, etc, and you can choose to play in practice mode, online vs, hotseat, world tournament, etc.

- The goals of each game differ depending on the type of game you select. E.g. In "capture the flag", you must get to the other team's home base, steal their flag, and bring it back to your base, while making your own flag isn't captured first. And in "escape the mummy", you must try to make the mummy turn all the players on your opponent's team into mummies themselves, while making sure you have at least 1 player still alive on your own team.


Game mechanics

- Movement is free anywhere within the game board as long as there are no obstacles in the way.

- On each turn, you can specify 1 linear direction in which you want your player to move, as well as the amount of force.

- You can also choose not to move any of your players per turn.

- If players bump into objects (i.e. game board items and other players), they will rebound.

- I had no problems at all playing this game using my mouse, on my laptop.


Strategy

While kids may enjoy the cute graphics & sound and appreciate the simple physics involved, more mature players soon realise that, in order to win, you have to take into account how the other team is going to move, and also in which direction all players will "rebound" when they hit stuff.

Different game types require different methods of strategising. E.g. In "capture the flag", you may find it beneficial to not move 1 of your players and have it permanently stationed at your home base instead.


Conclusion

Adorables operates fundamentally like carrom, but with more options and a variety of interesting contexts.
This really is an adorable game that I would recommend to both kids & adults alike. I can see kids enjoying the colourful, cute characters and interface, and adults having fun thinking up various strategies to beat each game type.

There are 6 minigames in total in Adorables: soccer, beach ball, hockey, capture the flag, push off opponents to water and running away from a mummy. Soccer, beach ball and hockey can be considered as one minigame since the only difference is a friction of a ground (the ball and creatures moves faster or slower).
