Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“UPDATED 05/30/16: Grand Class Melee 2 is a fun & furious LOCAL MULTIPLAYER fight and we're taking it further with Steamworks integration so we can offer players a fully featured Steam game with achievements, trading cards, leaderboards, Workshop support, the whole shebang. Your interest now is important because it provides us the feedback and resources to better focus on building more game <3”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“New stuff has been coming together update by update through Early Access and sooner or later we'll be satisfied that the game fulfills the definition of full release. It's already been in the oven awhile and if GCM2 needs to cook a bit longer, so be it!”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“A fleshed out set of Steam features and additional game modes are the big ticket items in active Early Access development. In addition to good stuff like trading cards & achievements which we've been rolling out since Early Access launch, the whole thing's only going to become more polished, bugfixed and complete as development continues.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The current build (v1.1.1) offers a stable and thoroughly tested couch multiplayer brawl with full controller support, audio/video/gameplay configurability, near-finished assets and no shortage of fantasy fisticuffs to be found in that giant class tree. Have a peek down below at the store page to get the full scoop of features :)”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“During Early Access, all purchases of GCM2 4-Packs will enjoy deeper built-in savings so share that multiplayer goodness with your fightin'-ist friends and get 'em trained up! Single copies sport the same price today as they will once the game exits Early Access.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“As more people play and get to know the game, we're paying close attention to player feedback to help us prioritize features in development. In addition, bug reports received from the Steam community have been an invaluable aid towards this game being as seamless as possible and we seriously dig the exciting ideas our fans share with us for things they'd like to see in GCM2. Don't hesitate to let us know what's on your mind, we'd love to hear it!

Thank you for taking the time to read through this Early Access interview and for informing yourself on the status of Grand Class Melee 2's development. Here's to a game that's fun today, tomorrow and in between.”

About This Game

Grand Class Melee 2 is an RPG job system arena fighter featuring a huge roster of over 60 character classes to battle with. Drawing inspiration from JRPG favorites and the competitive classics of console multiplayer, players progress through 8 intensifying rounds of fierce combat, gain new abilities each round and build up their arsenals to the top of the class tree.

To bolster the slash/thrust/parry weapons-based melee, every player pairs the new ability of their chosen job with one earned from an earlier job for an ever-evolving, two-fisted loadout. With up to 4 players mixing & matching, the possibilities are sprawling!

The fight gets fleshed out further by bots, teams, custom scoring, procedurally generated battlefields and beyond with finer game tweakability.


GCM2 is in EARLY ACCESS (v1.1.1), offering a stable, configurable, extensively tested couch multiplayer brawl supporting up to 4 players on gamepads & keyboard.

Features in Early Access development include:


  • Steam Workshop Class Tree Modding that gives players the power to create & customize classes (live as of v1.1.0!)
  • FFA & Team Quickbattle Modes with leaderboard support to bring fame to your hard-fought victories (live as of v1.0.16 & v1.0.14!)
  • Spectator Mode to set the AI on autopilot and relax as the melee unfolds (live as of v1.0.12!)
  • Survival Mode where players stand their ground against waves of enemies
  • Practice Mode to climb the class tree in comfort and test abilities freely
  • In-game Unlockables that reward your blood, sweat and other fluids
  • Achievements & Trading Cards to round out the Steam game experience (live as of v1.0.16 & v1.0.13!)

In the tradition of Street Fighter II, Warcraft II, Bomberman 2, Seiken Densetsu 2 and other greats you may not have played until they hit with part two, Gigatross presents Grand Class Melee 2 -- a game for those who like a little variety in their job system arena combat.

(My game time does not reflect time spent pre-Steam) This game follows in the footsteps of things like Towefall and Nidhogg. It's a brilliant and frantic arena fighter that borrows mechanics and aesthetics from Final Fantasy Tactics, Bomberman, and Link to the Past. Yes, that's right. It has a job-mixing system. *swoon*

Right now, it's local MP only, but it does have AI if you can't get anyone to play this with you. But, if you have anyone at ALL around you, just make them pick up a controller and play. It features that beautiful balance that makes it obvious and simple to pick up while still being deep enough to reward a lot of play.

There's the tactical level of learning how to parry and maneuever and use your abilities well. Then, there is the strategic layer of trying to adjust your job choices to what your opponents are using. Give it a shot. It's nearly impossible not to have some fun with it.
