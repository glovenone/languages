Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“Although we have released several games, including Battle Fleet 2 on Steam, this is our first VR title and it's important for us to create a great experience so we need your help! To do that, we are opting to go through Early Access so that we can have the room to experiment with features, get feedback on what works and what doesn't in VR and make a game that the community will love.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“Around 4 months.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“We are planning to add a new "Fortress" game mode that will allow you to build a complex miniature snow fortress, man it's walls and towers with your own snowmen and see how well it stands up to an attacking army. We're hoping to also let you share your fortress with other players and see how it does compared to their fortress. Beyond this new mode, we are also planning to add an extra Snow Fort levels, improve the graphics across the game, add in a nicer ground surface, add more enemy types and more tools/weapons for you to use.

This is all flexible of course and depends on your feedback and what features turn out to be the most popular in the game.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The current state of the game features 2 game modes "Snow Fort" and "Survival", includes 2 types of slingshots, a water bottle to heal your fort and a shield. A few of the graphics elements are complete but most will be improved. The game is fully playable at this point and offers a few hours of gameplay.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“We will likely increase the price after Early Access but the exact difference will depend a bit on the amount of content and gameplay we have ready by then.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We are looking for your feedback in terms of the features and content in the game. We always enter into discussions with our community as you can see from the discussions page of the Battle Fleet 2 community. We want to know what you like, what you don't like, what kinds of interactions work in the game, and what kind of suggestions you have for new content, game modes, tools and enemies.”

6 July

Update 0.52 Now Live

We've just launched the 0.52 update to Snow Fortress with some fixes and new features:

- FORT SAVING! In Snow Fort mode you can now restart any level with the fort as it was when the throwing stage starts (ie: When "GO" is shown on the screen). This is the first step in this implementation and currently saves just the last built fort. You'll see a new Restart With Saved Fort button whenever you die or win, or in the pause menu. This will cut the built time to just 10 seconds for any last minute adjustments you want to make and disable the block spawner so you can't keep adding blocks endlessly. We'd love to get feedback on this feature so let us know what you think and if you have any suggestions.
- SNOW GLOBES :) the level select menu now has snow globes for each of the levels, they still work the same way just look cooler
- New scoring system for Survival Mode, hits to a snowman are 25 points, snowman kills are 100 points
- Fixed powerup (question mark) blocks so they can show cracking like regular blocks
- New Sound FX when the fox is hit by a snowball
- New snowball whoosh sound, so you can hear snowballs whoosh past your hear :)
- Fixes to some sounds
- Bug fixes
- Memory performance improvements

Next Up:
We'll be working on adjusting the blocks so it's not as easy to knock over your own fort when building. Thanks for your support and please don't forget to leave a review :)

About This Game

Snow Fortress is a room-scale Virtual Reality snow fort building & battle game. Relive your childhood by building snow forts as you prepare for epic snowball fights! Unlock tools to protect your fort and deliver a fury of snowballs at your opponents!

  • Build your snow forts and destroy the snowman's fort in 12 unique levels
  • Throw snowballs or use your slingshot to do some real damage
  • Build and maintain a fort while protecting it against waves of attacking snowmen in Survival Mode
  • Unlock a shield and other tools to protect yourself and your fort
  • Compete in global leaderboards
  • Build a miniature snow fortress and defend it by placing your own snowmen on it's walls (coming soon)

Snow Fortress is created from the ground up for room-scale VR and the HTC Vive. It takes full advantage of tracked controllers to let you build the perfect snow fort ... and then watch it get trashed by an army of angry snowmen.

Overall a really good game, lots of fun. It runs smoothly, takes advantage of VR, the throw mechanic feels really natural. I highly recommend you to try it. However, I do have some issues/proposals:
  • The game ramps up in difficulty really fast. It took me 4 attempts to beat Level 3, the AI's aim is just too good too early on. Survival mode felt better, it allowed for more time to get used to the mechanics before it got harder.
  • Setting up your snowfort at the beginning of each level can be tedious. I'd like to be able to save templates and just load those, or at least reset the snowfort to your previous one for each level when you fail.
  • I wouldn't recommend this game if you have a standing-only placespace. Mine is rather small (1.6x2.2m) and the blocks to build the fort were at the edge of it. At one point I had to pull a lever that was placed outside my chaperone bounds by default.
  • So far, the game is lacking music. While it's appropriate if you want to simulate a snowfight, I feel like the game would benefit from quiet music in the background.

Posted: 9 June
Product received for free
Snow Fortress is a really fun game that honestly took me by surprise. I figured this would be 5 minutes before I got bored but it ended up pulling me in for 2 hours of ducking, dodging, and throwing. Being early access it doesn't currently have a lot of content so I'll be excited to see more levels and items added throughout the process. Right now there are 2 modes, Survival and Snow Fort.

In Snow Fort you are trying to complete each level by defeating the snowmen behind pre built forts, as levels go up towards twelve they get more difficult by giving the snowmen better bases and vantage points. This was the meat of the game for me, the levels quickly became challenging as you have to think how to build your own fort and where to attack first. Currently I've completed 11/12 levels.

In Survival you build a base and then face wave after wave of snowmen that aren't behind any cover, as the wave number gets higher you'll begin facing more snowmen. The cool thing about Survival is that it has a global leaderboard.

Pros
Cute atmosphere and fox
12 levels with more to come
Constant 90fps
Dodging snowballs works great, never got hit when I felt like I shouldn't have.
Leaderboards in Survival

Cons
Few items although they plan to add more
Very quiet game, could use some more music

Overall I enjoyed the game a lot and certainly recommend it, judging by the trailer I assumed this would be a very easy game meant just for kids but the difficulty went up nicely and I bet newer levels will be even more challenging. If you want to see some gameplay I also have a video here. https://youtu.be/-Ak4Lim77QU
