About This Game

Gathering Sky is a playful, beautiful experience about a flock of birds on a journey across the sky. It starts with a single bird. Use your mouse to steer and grow your flock or let your birds find their own way. Dance in the winds of a far-away world and create an emotional story without words.

The world is brought to life by an original score by Dren McDonald performed by the San Francisco Conservatory of Music.

Gathering Sky is enjoyable with any skill level and can be finished in under an hour.

Fly with us.

http://gathering-sky.com

Buy this game if you want a short sit-down-and-play-to-completion experience full of flow, exploration and charm. Don't buy this game if you've already decided "what's the point?"

You are the unseen guide to a flock of birds on migration. Using your mouse, you set where you want them to fly to - you start with one bird, but you'll quickly find more birds, who keep joining your flock until it's so large that it becomes a challenge to maintain order.

And the point? Either take your time to enjoy exploring the level and finding lost birds, or push on to your destination at the far right of the sky, where entering a thick bank of clouds will guide you seamlessly into the next region.

Gameplay video:
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iAm_S0Achag

Get over your fears that this is a insubstantial non-game and you'll find this to be compulsive. It's got a great responsive classical score that uplifts when you're soaring along air currents, and makes you nervous when there's danger around.

And yes, there are dangers. Not "game over" situations, but things that threaten the unity of your flock, such as mountain peaks that split your flock in two, or eagles that chase your birds into a panic - providing the game's main heart-stopping moment.

The game is stripped to the bone to offer full immersion. There's one basic control - the mouse - and there's almost no HUD unless paused. Instead of telling you how to play, it allows you to work it out, using gentle prompts such as a wisp that you'll immediately realise should be followed. All this is why it works as well on mobile and tablet as on desktop. But you'll really want a large screen and some good heaphones to get the best out of Gathering Sky.

Gathering Sky is incredibly relaxing, immersive and cute.
A Gameplay & Review video showcasing features I'm about to mention:
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5JIPncexRNQ
You start the game as a lonesome bird, and slowly add to you flock by passing by them.
Before you know it, you'll have a bunch of birds on screen, and will find that the game pulled you in as you followed drafts of wind at high speeds, accompanied by beautiful music.

Seriously, the music in this game is amazing. The best part is it's designed in a way to match what you are currently doing in the game, be it calmly floating through the sky, being pulled by a fast current, or being chased by a predator.

The game's levels add new music, environments and mechanics. Which keeps the game fresh

The art style is simple, but really nice looking. It goes with the relaxing theme of the game very well.
The fact that you control the entire game using only the mouse (Or analogue stick for controllers) also keeps to the simple nature of the game.

I really loved the game, and if you like the look of it, you probably will enjoy it.

Enjoy!
