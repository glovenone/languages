About This Game

Hearts of Iron III lets you play the most engaging conflict in world history, World War 2, on all fronts as any country and through multiple different scenarios. Guide your nation to glory between 1936 and 1948 and wage war, conduct diplomacy and build your industry in the most detailed World War 2 game ever made.
Features:
  • Play as any nation from 1936 to 1948, more than 150 countries to choose from.
  • Control the oceans with aircraft carriers, submarines and battleships and use your air force to defend your skies, support your naval and ground forces, and to bomb your enemies
  • Thousands of historically accurate real-world military commanders and politicians.
  • Realistic military command AI with unprecedented levels of interaction
  • In-depth diplomatic and political system.
  • Historical accuracy combined with an unparalleled level of freedom of choice
  • More than 10,000 land provinces makes the game five times more detailed than HoI2 and the most detailed depiction of World War 2 ever made.
  • Customize your divisions in detail with more than 20 types of brigades
  • New economic system makes it possible to buy weapons from abroad.
  • Mobilization and reserves gives the option of surprise attacks, Blitzkrieg made real.
  • Government-in-exile makes it possible to continue to struggle from abroad with underground movements and uprisings against the oppressing power.
  • Strategic warfare system makes it more important to defend the skies and oceans against foreign attacks; leaving cities open to enemy bombing will now be devastating.
  • A completely new intelligence system, with several types of intelligence sources, makes it possible to get information about enemy reserves and troop movements.
  • Assign troops to "theatres" on the map to fight two-front wars more successfully.
  • The new AI system and more detailed map will allow for more strategic decisions.
  • Flexible technology system with hundreds of categories, where major powers get their own unique attributes.

First sorry for my english. Sotto la recensione in italiano.


Hearts of Iron III is a 2009 strategic game on a large scale, the third representative of the paradox about World War II.
You are free to choose from hundreds of nations between 1936 and 1948.
The game is based on several levels: the army management, industrial production, technological research, diplomacy,espionage and politics.
The game is in real time, but you can pause when you want.


Pros
  • Not high hardware requirements
  • Multiplayer and coop
  • Cards
  • Some fantastic mod
  • Light download and occupied space
  • Excellent longevity
  • Once mastered becomes extremely rewarding
  • Wide range of nations
  • The whole world to conquer
  • Three major expansions


Cons
  • High price
  • many dlc of dubious value
  • No workshops, cloud and achievement
  • Insufficient tutorial
  • Bugs and crashes
  • Graphics and sound
  • No demo
  • No linux and steam OS
  • Extremely difficult
  • Without mod loses points
  • Initial loading very long
  • Micromanagement
  • It requires much effort and time



Notes
  • Serious technical problems reported in some cases especially with mod
  • Easily activated cheats
  • Dlc Hearts of Iron III - German Sprite Pack in some bundle can not be detected
  • Mod downloaded via Paradox forum (it is a paradox policy)
  • The DRM free versions is present on other platforms
  • Not present holocaust management and other controversial parts
  • Suitable for all ages (no violence, at least not directly)
  • There are reported problems of downloading through Steam


With the release of the 4th chapter has lost relevance because of its extreme micromanagement
and not easy accessibility.
Strongly recommend modding because the basic version is not satisfactory.
Is present an active and expert community for the multiplayer, and the game gives wonderful emotions, if you enter in mechanical.
The air force in the game unfortunately has a secondary part, before you buy you should inform well for not abandon it at the first try (the fun-frustration border is very thin).
Compared to the 2nd chapter is divided into thousands of provinces, and the beauty of the game is to shape the story to their own will for example by bringing Italy into allies.
Probably many person will choose the major nations because they offer more tactical strategy, however for its size it remains a milestone of its period.
