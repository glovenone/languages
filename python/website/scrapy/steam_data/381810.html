About This Game

Tom Clancy’s Endwar Online is the RPG strategy spinoff game continuing the story of Tom Clancy’s Endwar.

Set in the aftermath of a massive global war between the biggest superpowers on Earth, resources and money have run dry and the factions are on the brink of collapse. As one of the last leaders, you are tasked with rebuilding your homeland and spearheading the final battle for all that is left. Create a hero general, build and lead an army made of the finest commanders and weapons, engage in challenging single player missions to reclaim your homeland from rogue armies, and put your skills to the ultimate test in intense player vs. player battles against the other factions’ generals.

Key Features:
  • Side with one of the 3 factions: the mighty U.S. Joint Strike Force, the high-tech European Enforcer Corps, or the ruthless Russian Spetsnaz. Conquer territories to reclaim your homeland in the PvE campaign, and join the global power struggle in PvP modes.
  • Strategically allocate command points, master the combat chain, and combo unit abilities to vanquish your enemies. EndWar Online combines the depth of RTS games with the heroes and abilities variety of MOBA games into a revolutionary, action-packed strategy gameplay.
  • Unlock, recruit, and train hero commanders from 9 different unit classes, each with their own unit, skills and attributes! With more than 80 commanders, you must battle overtime to recruit, train, and master them all to their full potential.
  • Level up your hero and your commanders through conquering territories and defeating other generals.
  • Use the precious loot you obtain from battles, and research new weapons and technology to reinforce your army and give your soldiers the edge during battles.
  • Rebuild your capital city from the ashes in to the ultimate military Head Quarters. Restore the energy supply and start rebuilding and upgrading new zones to unlock more unit classes, more modes, and increase your unit stats!

So I closed the game and never opened it again.

As for gameplay, it's your standard Plants vs Zombies mentaility, summon tanks and helicopters to fight other tanks and helicopters while clicking on power ups to do a thing, and then be assaulted by a "ALL OF YOUR ENEMY'S UNITS HAVE BEEN DESTROYED, CLICK TO WIN" button which makes you win. Rinse, repeat, rinse again, and then repeat until you are fed up with the game.

The game can be played in single player and multiplayer, but understand that this is fundementally a c l i c k e r game, and a "pay to advance" one at that.
