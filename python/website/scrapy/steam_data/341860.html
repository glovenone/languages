About This Game

SickBrick is an action-packed Sci-Fi FPS with vivid graphics and fast, frantic gameplay.

The game consists of 15 levels mixing indoor and outdoor areas. You’re forced to do tests for an unknown robotic mastermind. Destroy hundreds of hostile robots with a wide variety of weapons on a strange alien planet. Jump in a hover-tank and wreak havoc on giant boss enemies. As you go through the tests, you slowly unravel the story through log files of previous test subjects.

SickBrick contains the following:
  • 15 maps with a mix of outdoor and indoor locations on a strange alien world.
  • 5 unique and devastating weapons. Chainsaw, Shotgun, Lightning Gun, Grenade Launcher, and the Shock Rifle.
  • 10 deadly robotic enemies each with custom animations and sounds.
  • Explosive hover-tank driving sections.
  • Vivid graphics reminiscent of the golden years of first person shooters.
  • Advanced graphical technologies like depth of field, screen space ambient occlusion and light scattering.
  • A rocking original soundtrack.
  • Includes MapEditor and ResourceEditor for creating custom maps and mods.

Basic Information
Title: SickBrick
Genre: Action, Adventure, Indie
Developer: Mladen Bošnjak and Ron McDowell
Publisher: Mladen Bošnjak and Ron McDowell
Release Date: 30 Jan, 2015

Basic Overview
SickBrick is a very fun FPS with a satisfying Sci-fi genre theme that really works well. Some nice weapons, although the weapons to start off with aren't that great. A pretty easy and short game that mixes Portal and Serious Sam together to make this awesome game!

Game Play
I absaloutly love this game, although it's short and can be completed from anywhere between 1-2 hours it's still a gem of a game in my opinion. First off let's talk about the things that I don't really like about this game, and then we can get to the good things after. First off the game is way too short for the price that you are asking for the game. I completed the gmae in about a 2 hours, and was kind of suprised to see the game with such potential end off so short. I really didn't like that, the story wasn't all that good. With the lack of dialogue throughout the game, seemed like this game was just a shoot aliens that attack you. The way that the game tries to tell a story doesn't really work in my opinion, as the dialogue isn't presented enough for you to get pulled into the story enough. Other than some annoying jumping puzzles that's all the bad things I have about the game play of this game. But this compared with all of the really good stuff really makes it look good as there as so many good things to talk about. First off I want to talk about the variety of maps that are in this game, I really like it when a game really puts time and dedication into creating maps for levels. You can either be inside or outside and it really makes a nice change of atmosphere when shooting those robots. There are also some cool weapon designs that really fit the futuristic genre of the game. I really like the diffrenete coloured weapons that then corrospond to the colour of your bar at the bottom left of your screen. The overall pacing of the levels is good, there where some levels where walking through the corridors seemed to slow it down a little but as you got further into the game it started to pick up again. Really works well. Not only are you battling enemies on the ground with the weapon arsonale that you have collected, there are times where you can go into a tank which is really cool. Although it does change the perspective to third person when in the tank which (for me) took a little bit of the experience of a First-Person out of the game, but that's only really a small detail to complain about in my opininon. Nearer the end of the game there is a large variety of enemies and level design, which could've been used earlier in the game I guess but again another small thing I would only personally complain about. The last point I want to make, I wasn't sure whether it was negative or positive. I guess it'll be different from person to person. The game is fun, but challenge is really not a problem at all in this game. The enemies do negligible damage combined with the array of very powerful weapons and a lot of ammo you find throughout the game, it really does take the challeneging side out of this game.
Score (Out Of 10): 7 - Game too short, can be completed in 1-2 hours, the story isn't all that, dialogue isn't really used as the game progresses and some annoying jumping puzzles.

Graphics/Art Style
The graphics of this game are really nicely polished and well presented for a retro style and feel to the game. There are custom animtions for each of the different types of robots that you encounter along your way. It's also pretty simple, anyone can play it on almost any computer whether it's a bad laptop or what. For what it looks like it seems like a big game, with it's futurstic grahpics and stuff but really isn't that big of a game. One small thing is that sometimes the graphics can glitch out a bit and make the game not as good than it actually is.
Score (Out Of 10): 9 - Really nice retro style grahpics, with nicely presented animations for each different enemy robot. Sometimes the graphics glitch out a bit.

Music & Sound
The Music for this game is really nice, again fits perfectly well with the Sci-fi genre as does it set the pacing of the gameplay as well. The sounds are a bit of a dissapointment, just generic sounds but putting into consideration all the good things about the gameplay this game really is a gem.
Score (Out Of 10): 8 - Great music that suits the genre, sounds are a bit too generic for me.

Conclusion
I definatly recommend this game to any Sci-fi fans, and anyone who likes FPS. It has some really nice game play, along with a few bad things here and there about the game play but overall very good. The music really sets the atomsphere and pacing of the game, as does the retro graphics fit well with it too.

Sickbrick is a game made by... ahahah, I can't keep a serious face.

SICKBRICK: I AM SICK OF IT ALREADY

Lets totally ignore my usual review format. I am here just to give you a warning about this game which barely qualifies as one and definitely is not worthy of actualy being sold to anybody for any amount. This game in its total sum from beginning to the very end [Which is a mere 83 minutes by the way] feels like an amateurish student project and I actually played games made in a gamejam that actually feels fun and not like someone is constantly dragging my face through the ground and telling me to shoot cardboard targets that shoots foam at me while i engage them with a sniper rifle all the while yelling beside my ear that this game is "FUN".

-sigh-

Graphics
It looks like ♥♥♥♥. No wait, even some of the worse texture I have seen had at least some effort put in it to be cohesive like a dirt floor outside and a gravel road inside the military base. Sickbrick has a single wall texture copypasted onto the ceiling and walls, and sometimes a different wall texture gets used for ceiling, floor, and wall. All in all you will be a madman trying to find a something worth praising about the graphics, it looks so ancient and dated.

Sound
Why do I even try... There is no sound balancing for most part of the game. A particular guitar riff during the desert section actually blew up my headphones which is set to 15% by default. For comparison I cannot hear anything in the game above a whisper because it was giving me migraine with its robot babbling random gibberish and stage FX that looks incredibly bad it was giving me migraine.

So yeah, no good points here either.

Performance, Controls, Option
I dont even want you to buy it so screw the options menu.

It runs at 60 fps all the time.

Controls are horrendous for 'twitch' based shooters like this, you cannot even trust your own bunny hopping since it tends to bounce of every single item or texture off this god forsaken world. The aiming is alright but wait until you get into the tank. You will be flying in no time thanks to the great effort that goes towards the bounding boxes of everything.

Gameplay
You have a gun, shoot anything that moves, easy right?

It's not supposed to be easy because this is a twitch shooter which rely on you almost dying most of the time and only surviving on the skin of your teeth due to the awesome skills which you posses. Not in this game, you are built like a tank and the only reason I ever died is because I got shot halfway across the room and landed in a pool of acid or my tank got blasted upward into the nether because I hit a piece of scenery or or..... ♥♥♥♥ it.

That literally all there is to this game, occasionally collect 1/2 keycard, shoot everything that moves and don't die which you can't anyways since there is cover all over the arena, healthdrops every single room transition, and the third gun you get completely negates the entire challenge with its incredible ability to outshoot everything and fire faster than the entire room of droid combined, with a ♥♥♥♥ing large magazine well.

Tank section? Its a rekin version of on foot combat except sometimes you get to change tanks.

Rant
Dont even buy this game, I played student game that actually felt more polish than this. I half suspect that this was made in a shooter fps gamemaker engine what with the amount of bugs, enemies, challenges and other stuff.

There's a total of 3 different minor enemies with 2 turret and reskin version of your tank shooting at you with pew pew, bash your head against the wall and read that again, a total of around 10 types and only 3 that you will ever face in frequency. 83 minute is already starting to feel like torture to me.

So much bugs, with a story that felt like it was made in 5 min.

Conclusion
Dont even buy it, its not worth the cards, the cents, the effort or even the time taken to idle it. Its a literal garbage. Save your money and get something else. Something that feels like an actual challenge.

- S-Rank: excellent
- A-Rank: very good
- B-Rank: solid
- C-Rank: satisfactory
- D-Rank: inferior

Verdict: S-RANK [Shovelware]
