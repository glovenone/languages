About This Game


Build, dig, defend! Are hordes of zombies, demons and robots attacking the base? It’s not a problem, because we have more than 40 guns, turrets, tanks, IFVs, helicopters, infantry fighting vehicles in our arsenal and the entire wardrobes of super armor and skins!
Online shooter becomes a exciting battle for survival, with destructions, building of fortifications, arrangements and traps. Build maps for the game of survival with your friends, build modes of capturing the flag, assaults, play on your friends maps and get the general's epaulettes.
In addition to online battle, you will get 8 episodes comprising 12 missions! You will defend the city from the zombie apocalypse, unite against powerful bosses, seek the lost technologies, survive in the laboratories, and much more!
Join the liberation army of Madness Cubed!

THE KEY FEATURES:


  • More than 100 missions for playing with friends
  • Multiplayer up to 16 players
  • Game modes: survival, man for himself, team, domination
  • Build your map and play with your friends
  • Huge arsenal of weapons
  • Enemies: zombies, robots and mutants
  • Changing of the time of day during the game
  • Technique: tank, helicopter, airplane,
    quad bike, boat, turret
  • changing of character’s costumes

Right off the bat, the game gives off of this nostalgia feeling, possibly due to the similar graphic style as Minecraft. But this is more than just "another Minecraft clone" I can assure you.

You start with a default character which you could freely customize and a handful of cash to start you off with. The shop has skins and outfits available, which would add additional bonuses on top of your upgradable stats. There are also various weapons separated by several categories, (i.e Melee is really useful, when you find yourself surrounded by a horde of zombies, in need of a few smacks to get out of that situation. Not to mention that even though you might have a higher chance to get hurt in battle, melee does not require bullets and therefore could be a huge life savior when you run out of ammunition) each category serves a different purpose, just so the player could play the part and plan their own strategies to test out each weapons.

If you like to play with friends, online battle is a great mode to play in. There are 5 different types (Survival, Free for all, Teams, Capture the flag, Domination) for you to choose from. Personally, I believe the most interesting one is the survival mode, in which players team up against hordes of zombies and try to stay alive as long as possible. Those who love to play PvE definitely have to give this one a try :D

The game isn’t too difficult, but that doesn’t mean it will hold your hand and walk you through each steps. For those who are new, there are a couple missions available to complete, sort of like campaign missions in other similar fps games. By the end of each mission, there will be some rewards to collect and EXP to boost your rank with, so it’s not that hard to save enough money to collect your dream gears.

One thing that really caught my attention was the ability to create your own map. Players could buy selective objects (cubes, items, mechanisms and respawns) to add into a map to decorate it and save it afterwards to come back to it whenever possible.

It’s hard not to mention the clans feature in an online multiplayer game. After all, teaming up with a group of friends against enemies may guarantee fun times; it’s almost like the icing on a cake. Those who enjoy meeting new friends should join one of the available clans, it’s easier to learn the ropes while playing with your clan mates anyways. If none of the clans are to your liking, you could always make your own; it costs about 30 gold to create one. To each their own, I much rather to spend those gold on upgrading my character, just so I won’t die in 0.2 seconds in game.

Madness cubed is a zombie MMO with minecraft like graphics. It can be played offline or online. The game features micro-transactions. You can play PVP or co-op with other players to do your missions.

Graphics:

The graphics are standard block graphics similar to the numerous games like Minecraft, Guncraft etc. The framerate was good throughout my gameplay with this game.

Sound:

While the music is decent it can get repetitive as there is not much variety. The time i played only one track played the entire time. The sound of enemies, shooting, even zombie sounds are not that impressive.

Gameplay:

You can play solo or go online with friends to co-op, or join random servers. There are missions available which you can do. You will earn cash. You can also earn cash by converting coins to cash and guess what, these coins can be bought using real life money. This is a big no in my book and probably one of the main reasons I am not recommending this game. Another reason I won’t recommend this game is, it’s not entirely in English. Some stuff is in English while the rest is in Russian.

The missions are simple such as kill x number of zombies, collect x number of secret documents, last x number of minutes in waves of enemies. There’s a large variety of weapons available in the game. Your character levels up and weapons require you to be certain level. Overall, it got really boring for me fast and I wanted to move on and play other game. The game does have potential but a lot of things need improvement.

Conclusion:

The game has potential but in the current state I cannot recommend it. It has micro-transactions and it’s not even entirely in English. See screenshot below of me trying to upgrade a weapon and presented with Russian text and I have no clue what it says. These things really put me off and I didn’t play the game much due to that. The bits I played I did enjoy some parts, the weapons were fun to use and some of the missions can be fun but not recommended overall in it's current state.
