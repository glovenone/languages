Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“VR is such a new experience and game changer that in order to successfully complete my first VR game I need to solicit input from the 1st wave of VR gamers. DOTRE is playable and enjoyable in it's current state. My goal is to shape the game into something special for a final release.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“2-3 months time.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“The final version will have additional enemy types and a boss. The VR experience will be tweaked based on customer input to make it the most enjoyable and comfortable experience possible.

In the short term I'll be adding a new enemy type and tweaking how the scoring and progression system works.

Bugs found will be prioritized and bug fixes will be ongoing.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The game is currently playable and there are no known bugs at this time. The game runs on the recommend spec for HTC Vive @ 90 fps resulting in a smooth experience. Leaderboards are implemented and players can compete for best score.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“We plan to gradually raise the price as we ship new content and features.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“Starting a conversation and soliciting feedback on everything related to the game experience; whether that is the VR controls or aspects of the game.”

About This Game

Witness first hand the Dawn of the Robot Empire. Can you stop the invading robotic forces from another galaxy? Blast your way to victory in this retro sci-fi shooter. You'll need fast reflexes and an accurate aim to win.

Features:
- VR experience
- blaster
- staff / shield device
- physics based gameplay

System Requirements

    Minimum:
    • OS: Windows 8.1 or newer
    • Processor: Intel i5-4590 or better
    • Memory: 4 GB RAM
    • Graphics: NVIDIA GTX 970 / AMD 290 or better
    • DirectX: Version 11
    • Storage: 256 MB available space
