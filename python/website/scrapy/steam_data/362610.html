About This Game

High On Racing is an unique Retro Arcade Style Tactical Racing Game that will warp you into the sky to race against the time.

Find the hidden RED diamonds that will get you into the next stage by only sliding left and right. You have to be fast to check the ground plates that will let you turn, jump and just changes the direction you are flying.

Face 15 Maces with secrets, deadly traps and nearly impossible to master obstacles. And find the way through them to finally win the high score!

Another amateurish game on Steam. The real problem here comes from how little thought has been put on the actual design of the game. Here are two BIG design flaws that made me finally bother enough to write a review not recommending it:

- On each level you can work out a circular path where you will grab "extra time" power ups in addition to "point gems", so as long as you repeat this same path picking up the respawning items, you can rake in infinite points with no effort and never run out of time. This makes the whole point system irrelevant.

- To complete the levels you need to collect all the red gems in it. If at any time you are going to crash (die) you can simply press the Escape key and choose "restart level", this puts you on the starting point with all your previously collected red gems and NOT losing a live. So basically you lose nothing. This makes having a limited amount of lives to complete the level quite pointless.

This game would be okay as a free game, but not as a product with a price tag on Steam.
