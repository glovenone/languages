The Undying Plague
The Undying Plague is a top down shooter where your task is to fend off waves of attacking enemies and survive as long as possible. At your disposal will get a few weapons that we can improve for the money earned on the battlefield. Enter a world of pain, suffering and bloodthirsty zombies. Can you stop the plague?

About This Game



"The Undying Plague" has a simple task - to fight against waves of zombies attacking us. At your disposal, you have all sorts of weapons, each has different specifications. During the game you will meet several types of zombies that are different from each other including their speed, resistance and damage. Each type of zombie has its advantages and disadvantages, depending on the point of view. The Undying Plague is a fantastic combination of classic 8-bit and classic survival-horror games. Try to liberate from the shackles of death and defeat the plague! How long can you survive?

Probably the contendor for most unoriginal game of 2015.

As you have already noticed 'The Undying Plague' is a top down shooter in which you fend of waves of zombies by killing them with a variety of weapons.

Zombies drop either ammo or money, which you can spend on new weapons, although in my opinion some of the more expensive weapons are worse than the original guns.
You are also able to upgrade your skills 'attributes'; Strength (not sure what that does), Vitality, Toughness, Stamina & Swiftness. Each time you die you keep your attributes but lose the weapons.

The graphics look like a standard flash game, the sound is annoying & the music is OK but doesn't fit the game but these things wouldn't really matter that much to me if the game is fun to play, which it isn't.

The Undying Plague is as repetitive as it can get. The only thing you do is walk around and shoot the chasing zombies, which sounds like an easy task but then there are these other "Big" zombies which sometimes get stuck to a wall or stuck on each other and then suddenly storm at you, which kills you in 1 hit. As far as I know these are the only 2 types of enemies you encounter in this game but who knows you might encounter something else in wave 342.

Back in the day you would find these games for free on New Grounds or Kongregate and this would be a fine game if you found it there for free but I simply do not think it's worth the money here on Steam.

The Undying Plague doesn't do anything that hasn't been done better by about a thousand free flash games.

A short summary of the problems:

-Repetitive gameplay. Just walk backwards and fire.
-Hit detection. Hitting close targets is very difficult. In addition there's little way to tell you are damaging an enemy you can't kill in one hit.
-The game is dark and there are no brightness settings.
-The sprite work is ugly.
-The music is out of place. It sounds like some free public domain music just thrown into the game.
-Generic-looking HUD
-There's no visual way to tell if you are taking damage unless you are closely looking at the health meter in the corner.

Still, it's better than Zombie Zoeds.
