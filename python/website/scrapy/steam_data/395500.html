Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“We believe with community's feedbacks, we can make a better game. We are constantly testing the game with our friends and use their feedbacks to enhance the game and as of now it's a good time to reach to some people that haven't played our game and see what is their opinion.

Also, we are making this for people like us and with more people having access to our game, we believe we can make what people want.

Since core gameplay is done, there are couple of ways we can go from now and are very interested to know what people have to say with our updates and plans.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“Honestly it depends on Early Access's feedback and how it goes but our initial plan is about 6 months.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“More levels, more power ups and curses, more game modes, level editor (with Workshop), more characters and abilities per character.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“We have 4 game modes, one character, 10 levels, supporting only Xbox 360's controller, no curses or power downs.

We are planning to improve all of above with feedbacks.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“Price will not change when we leave early access and our supporters with Early Access will have access to special characters.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We plan to weekly update the game and briefly test our plans in 1-week iterations, then update the game and see how people react.

We also integrated analytics so we can get great amount of data on what people like or not.

Since this is our first Steam game and it was very hard to get past the greenlight process, we will do our best to not to let this opportunity wasted.”

About This Game

Rot Gut is an action packed platformer set in America's 1920's Prohibition era. You take control of an agent who, to uncover their mysteries, goes on a mission to fight the city's underground armed to the teeth organizations.

Game is %100 done and ready to be distributed.

Features:

- Gameplay: Several levels with different gameplay themes.

- Art: Graphics are based on 1920's American gangster's era with Noir style mixed with retro aspects of pixel art.

- Music: Each level has it's own uniquely composed music that is an exciting mixture of chiptune and 1920's Jazz.

- Details: In order to make the game feel more fun, strong attention was paid to the details in the game; from the small sound of shotgun shells bouncing on the ground to that kick that firing a shotgun should have.

- Cutscenes: Game features 5 different cutscenes with full sound effects that played various parts during the game to tell the story and also prevent repetition.

- and MORE! ...:

About the name: "Rot Gut" basically means the stomach ache that was caused by drinking bad or home made alcoholic drinks that were popular in 1920's due to it's prohibition.

If we get on steam, we'll give 10% to charity.

System Requirements

    Minimum:
    • OS: Windows XP, Vista or 7
    • Processor: 1 Ghz or faster processor
    • Memory: 512 MB RAM
    • Graphics: 500MB VRAM
    • Storage: 100 MB available space
