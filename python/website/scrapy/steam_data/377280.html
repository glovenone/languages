About This Game

A unique, dynamic, challenging, fully-voiced tower defense game for Windows, Mac OS X, and Linux. You begin at Veldamir Castle, a small outpost at the edge of the human lands, where Wynathean the Wizard walks you through your training. From there, you proceed to the elven, dwarven, and gnomish realms in an effort to save the lands of Alarameth from certain annihilation.

FEATURES

  • 15 highly varied levels with unique challenges, with a combination of "mazing" and "non-mazing" build areas.
  • 7 primary tower types (each with 3 levels), unlocked as you progress in the game.
  • 50 achievements, 7 trading cards.
  • Heavily story-driven, with each level voiced by at least 1 primary character.
  • Both 32-bit and 64-bit executables available*

*32-bit and 64-bit editions are only available for Windows/Linux. The Mac OS X edition is 64-bit only. The appropriate edition for your OS will be installed by Steam automatically.

It's unpolished, and could use a rebalancing as of launch day.

That said, I play TDs like most people eat and drink, and I can see the core of a solid tower defense game, here. The graphics are rough, and the strength of the mobs don't always feel right for their level - but I'm sure that'll improve with patches.

For the price - which even not on sale is pretty easy to part with - I'd say this is worth a try, if you're a tower defense junkie.

This probably shouldn't be your first tower defense, though: For that, I'd recommend either of the Defense Grid games, or Super Sanctum TD.

TLDR: At launch, this game is worth the $5-$7 (with or without sale) - though if you're someone who NEEDS slick graphics, this probably isn't a title for you.
