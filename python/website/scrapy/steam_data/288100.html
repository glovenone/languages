Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“Our Early Access version of the game is a near-final product and we're very proud of the quality level we have achieved so far. Before we consider the game a full launch product, however, we plan to add just a few key elements.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“We plan for the game to be in Early Access for about 2-4 months.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“Basically it's just these few things:
  • The boss fights in the sewer and industrial districts need just a little more time to cook, so they are not included yet.
  • Some balancing may still occur. Things like enemy health and weapon damage may change in future updates.
  • Various Bug fixes. These would be graphical glitches or minor AI problems. We're smoothing these out.
Other features may come online later.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The game is fully playable and players can progress through the game from beginning to end.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“The Early Access version of the game is offered at a discount. The price of the full game will be slightly higher.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We welcome all feedback during this Early Access period. Please let us know of ways that we can improve the overall experience of the game!”

About This Game

**Note to Linux Users: We'll be addressing the missing Linux executable shortly. Check back soon for an update!**

MechRunner is an endless action game that casts you as a powerful mech in stunning cinematic battles against a relentless robotic army.

Game Story

On June 30th, 1908 a mysterious explosion took place over the Tunguska River in Siberia, Russia that wiped out trees and wildlife for over 800 square miles. To this day it remains the largest âimpact eventâ in our planetâs recorded history.

In the years that followed, dozens of scientific expeditions to the site attempted to explain the disaster but failed to come to any conclusions. In the mid-1940s however, a team of scientists from the Russian secret service visiting the site stumbled upon the wreckage of an alien spacecraft containing technology that far exceeded our own. In the years that followed, the KGB set to work harnessing this mysterious new power source and created a new secret weapons program - The Division of Advanced Weaponry or "O.S.O."

They thought they could control the new power theyâd discovered, but it began to do more than run their machines. It began to drive the men working on it mad. The man in charge of the O.S.O., General Yuri Petrakoff, seemed particularly affected and on April 14th, 1949, Petrakoff launched a full scale assault on Europe.

One of Petrakoffâs lead engineers, Jesper Teterev, went AWOL and left the program early, wary of what was happening there. He went to live in secret with his sister, Isabel and niece, Allison. While in hiding however, Jesper (with the help of budding engineer and pilot Allison) was busy building his own mechanized war machine, called the XP-41, certain it may be needed one day to stave off the O.S.O.'s evil creations. He was right.

Gameplay

MechRunner is an endless-style action game mixed with fast and furious mode-changing mech action!

It is a "runner" in that the player is constantly moving forward while tracking down lethal O.S.O. forces but what makes it special is that youâre piloting a giant robot capable of some serious destruction! Other endless runners feature action elements, but none like this. Heat seeking missiles, heavy caliber bullets, laser cannons, and concussion bombs meet destructible buildings, debris littered streets, and tons of slice-able enemies to create a fast paced, visually stunning experience!

A typical game may take anywhere from a few seconds to several minutes to play. The higher your skill, the longer the game, the more rewards you will collect, and the more content you will unlock!

Features

  • Meticulously detailed, fast-paced action
  • Switch between Tank and Robot modes on the fly
  • Slice & Dice disabled enemies in cinematic fashion!
  • Extensive arsenal of weapons, customizations, and power-ups
  • 3 unique environments that blend seamlessly together - you decide when it's time to switch
  • Procedurally generated levels for endless variety
  • Numerous enemy types with different tactics
  • Epic boss battles!
  • Dynamic environmental obstacles
  • Unlockable skins, upgrades, and achievements
  • Rescue civilians and escort them to safety
  • Amazing Hollywood quality soundtrack
  • Optional controls - play with a mouse or controller

You have to play this game to appreciate it. Its a fun mashup of racing and shooter.
