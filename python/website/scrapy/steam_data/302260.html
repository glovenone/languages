About This Game

From the makers of Commando Jack, comes Truffle Saga!

Take Truffle on a cool adventure to rid the forest of angry Mushrooms in a fun addicting physics puzzler!

Truffle needs your help, and has acorns ready to throw at the pesky Mushrooms to destroy them! You have to do it in one shot, I bet you can't, though Truffle will be happy if you do!

Stop the Mushrooms taking over the forest now in this addictive saga!

- 60 levels to complete
- Complete each level 4 different ways to Crown it!
- Achievements!

For more info, visit www.colossalgames.eu

Truffle Saga is a mobile game that somehow made it's way onto steam. But will you want to play it here ?

We play as Truffle. We need to destroy the pesky mushrooms by throwing acorns at them. It's just as random as it sounds.

Gameplay is really simple. You throw down an acorn and try to destroy the mushrooms and you can also collect crystals. But you can only use one shot.

While the gameplay is really simple, controls are the reason this game is quite hard. It feels really clunky and it's really hard to aim. In later levels you can change the layout by flipping snakes, opening parts of level and use bees to bounce the acorn. But it's really tough with the clunky controls.

Graphics are probably the only good thing about this game. It looks really cute and the art style is really cool but that's not saying a lot.

The soundtrack is painful. It get's so tedious and it could make someones ears bleed. My suggestion if you do play the game is turn off the music as soon as possible.

The game is really easy. Even the harder levels are pretty easy and with enough patience you can beat any level within minutes.

The game is also incredibly short. Even though there's 60 levels, I finished the entire game in 1 hour. If you do want you can try and go back and collect all crystals and all crowns, but no one would attempt that. The developer promised new levels (not that I want any) but nothing has been announced.

The game isn't buggy, but the controls are a big problem. It's hard to aim when the line doesn't go where you want it to go.

Overall Truffle Saga is a horrible game and not worth your time and money. It's easy, short and not worth the money. I don't suggest getting the game even at a sale. I give Truffle Saga a 2/10
