About This Game

Heaven Island is an experiential game in which you'll explore wonderful environments and places.

On this Island, time and space are frozen and you'll be able to visit it and at the same time explore the inner of your soul.
All problems and worries of life will be left far behind so that you'll be able to rest your soul and mind during this experience.


The inspiration for the environments comes from the Renzo Piano designs and from the book "Origins of Architectural Pleasure" in which the player's welfare is of primary importance.

This game is a Massive Multiplayer Online experience in the sense that you'll be able to connect with all other gamers and will be able to view their essence in order to be able to create a "Connection".

All the details you see on the Island are made by hand, every rock you see is put there with a purpose.

We really hope you'll enjoy this wonderful experience.


Designed to be played with or without the Oculus VR, Gear VR and (soon) the Steam Vive VR




Warning: on some Apple Macbooks, the game crashes at start. We'll fix the problem as fast as we can!

Review about Heaven Island - The VR MMO

Heaven Island Is a "Chill Game"
It may sound stupid and impossible, but a game made on unity is actually ONLINE
Those souls rolling around are no NPC, They're players around the world playing with you, but it's just one of the problems that i'll list on the final of the review, but anyways

You're the spirit, a blue spirit, or maybe no, they say you can change your color
But i didn't found anything about it what so ever.

Your mission is...
NaN!
Yup, This game have no missions... Maybe.
This game have purpose to make you chill more, and explore around the big open-map island, Meeting other players, Collecting apples and shells, reading books and swiming with fishies, You make your own quest.

And now we're going to the main "Mission" of the game:Apples And Shells
Those are the only things that actually gives you something in the game (Meaning Achievements)
There aren't much achievements to get here, but collecting things are kinda painful, The easiest of them are apples, shells are kinda rarer, but not impossible.

And now for the best part:
The Graphics, Scenario And The Exploration.

This game have lots of it
The graphics itself are incredibely awesome.
The Scenario choice, execution and coloring scheme, Is Awesome.
And the exploration is also awesome
It's a big map to discover things, relax, hang out with friends or even take screenshots to use as wallpaper, Everything you stop with is new, So it's kinda of those games which you play on free time.

Conclusion
Heaven Island - VR MMO Is a awesome of a game
I really don't get those negative reviews, people can't play anything anymore without just having thei @sses on fire
But anyways
Here's the score of the game

    Pros
  • Charming Graphics
  • Calming Scenario
  • Multiplayer
  • Nice things to explore and read
  • An actually good beach walking simulator without annoying surfers to annoy you.
  • Backgrounds And Emotes are pretty good
  • Beautiful Coloring, Blur And Water Graphics
  • The water animals are actually cute

    Cons
  • Servers usually are abandoned, Empty, Not connecting or anybody to play with
  • Playing offline is boring
  • There are only spirits as players indicators, making everything confuse if you want to meet someone
  • Theres no in-game chat
  • No Indication how to change player color
  • No Customization
  • Can't change or even put a name on you
  • Lacks Configurations
  • Lacks Musics even at random
  • Lacks variety of sound effects
  • Water sound effects are missing, or even wave sounds.
  • Servers seems down
  • Takes time to load on slow computers
  • You only get the achievements after exiting the game
  • Kinda slowish gameplay

    So this is my final score for this game:

  • Gameplay - 6,5/10
  • Graphics - 10/10
  • Music - 2,5/10
  • Sound Effects - 2,0/10
  • Map And Scenario Choice - 10/10

    So
    This is actually a good game
    I recommend it to you if you're a casual player or if you can't chill
    And the creator's actually a pretty cool guy

    I hope this game updates and fix its cons later.

    Hope seeing more forward of this game!

    Goodbye!

    Have a nice trip...

    ~Victor J. StHang
