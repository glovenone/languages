Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“Dead Realm is 3BLACKDOT's first PC Multiplayer game and has been tested thoroughly to give users the best experience possible. We strongly believe that our game will give users a great experience, but we still welcome and encourage feedback. We need the community to tell us what they think about all aspects of the game and what they would like to see in it. We will do our best to listen carefully to what the community wants and make every effort to add new content as we grow Dead Realm. We want to make Dead Realm as much your game as it is ours!”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“Dead Realm will be updated periodically with new content until the final release of the game slated in early 2016.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“Dead Realm will have additional maps, ghost characters, and a variety of human characters at full release. We plan on releasing this content based on what the community wants as well as start building additional features and content that will be prioritized to what our communities wants to see.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“Early Access contains 2 Game Modes, 3 Maps, 2 Ghost Characters, 8 Human Characters.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“We currently do *not* have any plans on increasing the price.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“Our community will be the most important aspect of Dead Realm and its future. Our intentions have always been to work with the community to constantly develop new ghost & human characters, new maps, and new game types.

This early access release is just the beginning of what we hope to be an ever evolving project. We urge you to get involved and provide input into what types of new content you would like to see implemented. We encourage our community to post feedback, comments, questions and bugs on Steam Forum pages as well as using all social network channels to communicate with us on their experience as well as what they want to see in the game. Our team will closely monitor what our community wants and we will make every effort to respond as quickly as possible.”

About This Game

About This Game:

Dead Realm is a creepy, multiplayer action game with beautiful, immersive environments. You can play as either a Ghost or a Human character and work with your friends to run, hide, survive… or die.

Development Roadmap:
The original concept for Dead Realm was to create a few game types that were simple in nature, while also being addictively fun to play with friends. Our intentions have always been to work with the community to constantly develop new ghost & human characters, new maps, and new game types. This early access release is just the beginning of what we hope to be an ever evolving project. We urge you to get involved and provide input into what types of new content you would like to see implemented.

Lastly, we developed this game to not only be fun to play, but also fun to watch. That being said, we encourage you to capture and share your most terrifying gameplay moments.
