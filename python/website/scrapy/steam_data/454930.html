Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“We consider that at this stage game isn't finished yet and demands completions. We want to let out a qualitative product at the end!”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“We can't give exact dates because development goes unstably, but we can precisely tell that we will finish till summer.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“In the final version we will replace many models on more worked, we will add new objects and animals. We will improve the game world.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“Game is at a stage of a public beta in which the most standard opportunities from our plans are realized. 50%.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“Game won't change the cost.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We plan to listen to community and their requests. All will be able to express the opinion and to tell what they want to see in game.”
