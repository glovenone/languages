About This Software

MAGIX Fastcut contains various templates that can automatically edit your gameplay highlight footage to fit the beat of the music. Let Fastcut generate a creative trailer or montage for you, or you can manually edit your Let's Plays yourself, even if you're a complete beginner. Fastcut is the first automatic video editing software developed for fans of action.

Fastcut works with most standard video clips, such as those made with FRAPS, other screen capture programs, action cams, webcams, smartphones and tablets.

Automatic video editing: Â 
Video editing is now so easy anyone can do it. Fastcut automatically cuts your footage to fit the beat. Simply select an editing template with music, drag & drop to add effects, adjust colors and your montage is ready to go.

Direct uploadÂ 
You can upload your finished videos directly to Facebook, YouTube and vimeo, or share them via Whatsapp on your smartphone.

  • Spectacular trasitions and templates
