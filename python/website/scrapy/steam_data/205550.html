Now With Zombie Mode:

After having a fun and merry undescribed time in the woods, the legionnaires need a Helicopter ride home. They discover a creepy cabin in the woods(TM) and decide to call a helicopter. Whilst summoning the helicopter, they accidentally summoned the evil forces of hell as well! It wouldn't be a FL map without our signature humour so expect some unusual zombies, crazy environments, a ton of destructables, easter eggs and buckets of GORE. Who can get the highest body count?

About This Game

Foreign Legion: Multi Massacre is the (far too) long awaited follow up to Buckets of Blood. Now you can continue the cartoon-style third person battle online and battle your friends, enemies or even frenemies across 6 maps and various modes. Reduce the never-ending army of a non-descriptive country to a bloody, meaty pulp (with extra zest!) as legionnaires of an even less described country.

So what are you waiting for, get ready to make mince meat of other players, battle endless forces, determine the biologic equation, host a nice pick nick or swim in the ocean.

Key Features:

  • Battle waves of enemy AI with the 4 player co-op or go head to head up to 8 players in the team deathmatch or domination mode.
  • 6 map mix of classic BoB action and brand new environments.
  • Steam achievements like the assclown where all your friends can see that you spent time honking your clown nose 500 frigging times.
  • Own Foreign LegionBuckets of Blood? Sync your steam cloud save and import your progression into the game!
  • Using the steam friends system, you can easily invite your friends to a not so friendly game or use the open lobby system to insult and play with other players you will never meet.
  • DJ TRILT soundtrack featuring new and remixed songs of the original game.
  • PC and Mac can play together or fight to determine which is the better OS
  • 18+ game and we made sure to get that rating. So expect everything you might expect with a rating like that ;)

Basic Information
Title: Foreign Legion: Multi Massacre
Genre: Action, Indie, Casual
Developer: Sakari Indie & GriN
Publisher: Sakari Games
Release Date: 9 Jul, 2012

Basic Overview
This game is really fun to play, but of course with every game it has it's flaws that make it a bit less enjoyable than what people would've hoped for. Don't get me wrong, this game has some really nice graphics and interesting maps but sometimes can get a bit boring and repetitive if you play it longer sessions.

Game Play
Let's start off by talking about some of the good things about this game that really make it what it is. The game play is decent and the objective is simple enough to understand. Keep the generator on and wait for a helicopter to come and rescue you while you defend yourself against zombies. It has a nice way of keeping the game too easy or too hard with the threat level that you can choose from, Low, Medium or High. Starting off you will probably want to start with low just to get used to the game. But where this game falls apart is the controls. You start off without any indication of what each button does or what. I mean it took me a minute or two to figure out, but it would've been nice for even just a screen at the start of the game to show what buttons do what. There is also no story at all to why you are where you are and how you got in this situation in the first place. It would've been nice for at least a little story to go with the game. The community itself is dead, don't even bother trying to find people in a public server because I doubt you will anymore. I would suggest playing this game with friends who already own it or if you're really desperate then buy a 4-pack so that you can play this game with your other friends. The enemies on this game are zombies and ghosts. When I first started up this game it came up with kill the ghost or you will be cursed. I was confused as I hadn't even had time to adjust myself to what the controls were. I would've liked a explanation to what you had to do to kill the ghost at the start when it explains how to win (keep generator on until the helicopter arrives) which would've made it a little less stressful. The game can also get a little repetitive as this isn't much variation to each of the levels which there are only 6 of. The ranking system whoever is nicely presented and well made. You unlock guns by levelling up then use the money you have got form the missions to buy them. It works really well with this sort of game. It's a great way to pass the time either if you're playing alone or with some of your friends. Although I would've liked the choice of First-Person mode instead of over the should Third-Person mode. There might've been a way to change but I couldn't figure it out because of there being no control menu or screen telling me.
Score (Out Of 10): 6 - Gets repetitive, no tutorial, no story line and public community is dead.

Graphics/art style
This game has a smooth Frame even for not so good laptop like mine, but the walking animation for me is a bit weird. I would walk forward a step or two and the character would slide along and then do the walking animation half a second after. This really didn't look very good as the graphics in this game where really nicely presented and well polished. The map design was well implemented for the game play.
Score (Out Of 10): 8 - Nice graphics and map design, but the walking animation makes the game fall a bit

Music & Sounds
There isn't really that much to say about Music and Sound in this game because there isn't much to talk about. There's some nice music at the starting menu, but once you enter a level there is no background music. There are just some generic sounds that you would expect from a game like this and that's it. Nothing really that unique about the sounds or music in this game.
Score (Out Of 10): 7 - Generic sounds and no background music once your in a level

Conclusion
All in all a alright game, as long as you have some friends to play this game with then you will be okay. The public multilayer servers are dead, don't even try to find people to play with on this game. Graphics are nicely presented but walking animation is a bit weird at times. Nothing really unique or special about the sounds or music in this game. Would recommend to people who want to play this game with their friends.
