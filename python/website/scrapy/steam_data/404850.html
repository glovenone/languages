About This Game

DARTHY wakes up in the hospital and soon finds out he must release the souls of the robots imprisoned in the Gold Doubloons (Gold Coins) by the Giant Space Pirates. Help DARTHY Roll, Bounce, Crash, Fly, and Die to free the unfortunate souls.

DARTHY is a Simple Pixel Platformer based on old school mechanics.

Made with my kids Kaleb & Jeya play testing.



FEATURES

◉ 10+ Buildings w/ Old School Checkpoints
◉ Pixel Art
◉ Double Jump & Fireball
◉ Collect Coins to help the Robots
◉ Real-time physics
◉ Prepare to die. A lot!
◉ Security Ballz

DARTHY, a fun and challenging game

-This review is short because I have a very small amount of information to go on, will change in time-

DARTHY, a game created by 'Collin, Kaleb, & Jeya'. It's a nice change-up from the games I normally play, and I can definitely say I want to see more. A mobile port would be nice, too :)

GAMEPLAY:
The gameplay is fine, I have run into some problems whilst playing, but nothing game-breaking (as of yet). The level design is alright, it's not going to make you 'awe' in amazement, but it is fun enough to keep you tied to your chair for an hour or so (depending).

STORY:
DARTHY, who'd just woken up, has to save her 'friends', bound robots; each of these robots are trapped inside of a coin (golden doubloon) waiting to be saved, and it is up to DARTHY whether to save them or not. Will you ignore your friends plea's for help, or will you save them, allowing their souls to go to 'Robot Heaven'(?).

8.3/10

Although I don't have much to go on (not to mention I'm having 'writer's block'), I can say this game is pretty good at what it's intended for. Would be better off in the app store.
