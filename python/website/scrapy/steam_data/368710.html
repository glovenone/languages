About This Game

This game creatively asks you to control two people simultaneously. You should make them cooperate with each other to solve many puzzles.

这款游戏创造性的要求您同时控制两个角色，您需要让这两个角色互相配合以解决各种谜题。

Different from the requirements for controlling two people at different times, this game requires you to grasp the movement of two people, which tests computing ability of your brain and coordinate ability of your hands and eyes.

与其它一些分别控制两个角色的游戏不同，这款游戏要求您同时控制两个角色的走位，这考验您的计算能力和手眼协调能力。

This game consists of three chapters. Some new elements will appear in each new chapter, providing new challenges and pleasure for you in solving puzzles.

游戏一共有三个章节，每个章节都会出现一些新的元素，给您的解谜带来新的挑战和乐趣。

The levels become more and more difficult. At the beginning, some simple puzzles help you to be familiar with the game and all kinds of new elements. Then, the level will be difficult increasingly, especially the last few levels, which is a huge challenge for your brain.

游戏的关卡会逐渐变难，开始时，有一些简单的关卡帮助您熟悉游戏及其各种元素，之后，关卡难度逐渐提高，尤其是最后几关，对您的脑力将是个极大的挑战。

This is a perfect puzzle game for those who are willing to use their brains. You can experience great pleasure that other games can not give to you in this game.

对于喜欢动脑的朋友这是个非常好的解谜游戏，您将体会到其它游戏不曾给您带来的乐趣。

First impression: tough low-budget box-pusher with a twist

The twist is that you get two pushers, red and blue, and whenever red moves up, blue moves down and vice versa; left and right are also mirrored. There are colored obstacles that must be cleared by the correct pusher, and some require working together. This working together is the twist, and it's toughness showed just a few levels in when I was temporarily stuck in a position where the pushers would block themselves, and it required a small moment of inspiration to find a solution. Even earlier, I had to redo levels, since the mirror mechanic makes it difficult to move both avatars up to the same wall - and since I had destroyed the only aid I had for achieving this, I'd effectively gotten myself stuck. So it's challenging but fair, and requires quite a bit of thinking ahead.

The fact that the game is low-budget show itself in its low-budget look and some other annoyances. - I have to mention here that my previous review was negative because I was very annoyed with the fact that the exits were only revealed once you had basically solved the level; and having unbeknownst blocked the exit square earlier meant redoing the whole level. The developer took my criticism to heart and made it so that pressing Q now shows the exits, which makes the puzzles fair. Thank you! - I'd love to see fixes for the rest of my annoyances (did the characters gain some speed, or have I gotten used to it?), but for now they're acceptable: game runs with desktop resolution and (partially) cuts off the status bar on the left at 3:4 or 4:5 resolutions; controls are keyboard only and not reassignable; you can switch which character you "control", but there's no visual indication (maybe a glow or something?); and this game would really profit from multi-level undo.

The game may not look like much, but the puzzles are fair, tax my brain, and I can't remember having seen this mechanic before. Recommended.
