Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“The game is not 100% complete yet. I have an overall vision of what the game will be when it's complete and I haven't reached that yet. It's going to be bigger with more content and the game is at a state right now where it's working and got a lot of content in it.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“I've planned that the game will be there for hopefully not more than 4 months. Updates will be released every 2 weeks with some exceptions of course.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“The full version will be bigger and have a lot more content in it. Content like more quests, Zone expansion, more workstations, more endings etc.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“
  • The current version got 36 different quests with branching quest lines
  • 17 different endings
  • RPG elements
  • 8 Workstations
  • Random events that you encounter
  • Survival (not in the traditional sense but different)
  • Spend your cash on upgrades and consumables
  • Build your arsenal with the Rebels and get a huge army to fight for you
”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“Yes, as the game progresses throughout Early Access the games price will eventually be increased.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“People are allowed to express themselves with ideas and opinions however they wish. If you have any suggestions about quests, workstations or other ideas I would love to hear them out!”

About This Game

Introduction
Zone 22 is a first person game set in an alternative universe where the Nazis won against the race towards the Atom bomb and conquered the world during World War 2. You as the player is a part of the wonderful society of Zone 22, where you need to survive each and everyday by behaving, working and eating. Or will you rebel against this tyrannic foe?

Key Features
  • Earn your cash and experience within many of the different kinds of workstations

  • Spend your money on cigarettes, alcohol or upgrades to your apartment

  • 36 Different kinds of quests with branching events. Every quest is different and failure does not mean game over

  • Everyday is different, events will occur dynamically throughout the zone

  • Level up and spend your skill points to improve your character

  • 16 Different endings

  • Sabotage the zone to gain trust with the rebels and increases your chances of winning when you eventually decide, to fight back!

  • Black and White filter can be disabled...

How will you decide the ultimate fate of Zone 22? Will you work and stay indoors like a good citizen? Or will you fight back and lead your people to freedom?
