About This Game

Heroes of SoulCraft (HoS) is the best Arcade MOBA. Play a great MOBA in max 5 or 15 minutes on your PC, mobile phone or tablet against each other with mouse, gamepad or mouse/keyboard.

Heroes of SoulCraft (HoS) is an Arcade MOBA in which 2 teams fight against each other in fast-paced, tactical matches full of action and fun.
  • Angels vs. Demons team battles in quick, tactical games full of action and fun
  • If you like our previous games SoulCraft 1 & 2 you will love Heroes of SoulCraft
  • Play 5 or 15 minutes with instant matchmaking.
  • Need to leave the game early? No problem - we will join a new player into the running game.

Choose your hero
  • Select one of 12+ heroes: play the melee dwarf "furious axe" Grimnor, the range fighter "loose cannon" Keely, the mage supporter "flower princess" Dalia or many more.

Easy to learn
  • Heroes of SoulCraft makes it very easy to learn the basics within a minute. Over time you can choose to do more things manually like skilling or item-buying for the full MOBA experience.

Unleash your titan
  • Ever wanted to take a huge fire dragon or a big rock golem into the battle? Now you can! Choose your titan to accompany you and customize your Hero by pre-configuring your item-deck.

Heroes of SoulCraft is now available as a first Alpha Version. Download now for free on Android or Windows and help us improve the game by giving us feedback in the forums.

Heroes of Soulcraft is a free to play Arcade MOBA that is pure multiplayer PVP. It is a multi-platform game. The nice thing is your hero's items are automatically bought, games started quick so i guess they have enough players online, no in-game chat. It worked, looked ok, sounded annoying, played badly. The game has some different things from other MOBAs, like replacement players in running matches, titan player mode, controller support, low pc requirements.

Cons = There is no practise field or tutorials mode. There is videos, but they don't explain enough. Crashes, lags, and bugs out frequently. Lacks tooltips, and information in-game. Lack of content.

Try it, but don't Buy anything with money in it. There are BETTER MOBAs out there. Maybe ok for a starter MOBA for kids. Diehard MOBA players won't like it.

Not completly sure about recomend or not this game, so lets check its pros and cons:
+ Free and not pay-to-win.
+ Cross platform (Windows, Android, iOS). Actually I first played it on my android tablet (iPEGA-9700), so you can play it anywhere and anytime with a single account.
+ Matches can be quick. If you don't want to or don't have time for long matches you can play for 5min or 15 min matches.
+ Nice variety of heroes (not a lot). This can be good so you can find the ones that suits you faster.
+ Very accessible. If you're new to MOBAs or just want a lighter experience.
+ Best played with a Xbox360 controller.

- Terrible Mouse/Keyboard controls. Also I couldn't make x360ce work so just use an Xinput controller.
- Don't look for nice graphics or animations in here.
- Music and FX are not the best, but are not horrible.
- Unbalanced matches (sometimes). Sometime you're team will be full of low level players against really high level players making it totally unfair.
- Lag, sometimes. This is bad because the game doesn't seem to be complex so no idea why there's lag sometimes.

So for the big letdowns I'd say the terrbile MKB controls (considering this is a PC port), but probably having a proper configuration would be and advantage over the mobile gamers, so maybe that's the reason (or devs were just lazy).
But afterall is a nice ligth MOBA for guys like me that don't have too much time for big MOBAs like Dota2 or LOL and just want to kill some time and have a lightweight MOBA experience.

Just remember to USE an Xbox360 controller.
