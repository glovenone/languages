About This Game

Happy Birthday, Pep!

It's Pep's birthday and Putt-Putt® wants to surprise him with his first birthday party ever! Join Putt-Putt® as he travels through Cartown, helping various residents, reaping rewards, and gathering supplies for the party. With new friends to meet and new problems to solve, kids will love helping Putt-Putt® get everything ready in time for the big surprise--a birthday cake, presents, decorations, and friends!

  • Cartoon-style learning adventures where kids choose where the characters go.
  • Over 20 fun locations to explore.
  • The adventure changes each time the game is played.
  • Kids have so much fun they don't even realize they are learning.
  • Putt-Putt's® friendly and polite nature reinforces good social skills and manners.
  • Encourages critical thinking, creative problem solving, and teamwork.

It's weird writting a negative review for a point-and-click adventure game aimed at a younger audience. Not because my standards for them are low, but because usually they are very interesting and fun despite the fact I am now an adult. However, Putt-Putt: Pep's Birthday Surprise is a lazy cash-in to a franchise after the original company (Humongous) was absorbed by Atari. Backgrounds are blatantly reused from older games, there's something horribly wrong with the audio (as in I can hear the microphone Putt-Putt's actor used during all of his lines), the voice-acting is stiff, and when I tried to give this game a second chance, the cut scenes kept glitching on me, something I didn't expect from this kind of game. In a game where you're just supposed to have fun and figure out what to do, it's somehow completely devoid of anything resembling fun.

Putt-Putt was always my least favorite of the main Humongous Entertainment games, but they were good, with this one major exception. This game is terrible, I urge you to not cave in and try it for yourself.

I can already tell that this game is beautifully written and has one of the most intriguing plots of video game history. Putt-Putt®: Pep's Birthday Surprise shows the story of a post-apocalyptic wasteland where humans have mutated with vehicals and the struggles/importance of keeping up morale in a world where survival is easier said than done.
