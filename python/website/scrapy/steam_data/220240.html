About This Game

Far Cry 3 is an open world first-person shooter set on an island unlike any other. A place where heavily armed warlords traffic in slaves. Where outsiders are hunted for ransom. And as you embark on a desperate quest to rescue your friends, you realize that the only way to escape this darkness… is to embrace it

Key Features:

AN OPEN WORLD FIRST-PERSON SHOOTER

Create your own FPS adventure. Customize your weapons, your skills and your approach to each mission, whether you favor intense run-and-gun action, stealthy close-up takedowns or long-range sniping.

AN ISLAND OF DANGER AND DISCOVERY

Explore a diverse island playground, from mountain ranges and swampy grasslands to white sandy beaches. Discover relics, hunt exotic animals, play mini-games and travel quickly by land, sea or air. Fight your way through the island’s towns, temples, river ports and more!

UNCOVER A MEMORABLE STORY AND AN INSANE CAST OF CHARACTERS

Encounter an engaging and disturbed cast of characters as you take a gritty journey to the dark side of humanity, written by a Writers Guild Award winner.

PLAY WITH FRIENDS IN A FULL CO-OP CAMPAIGN

Play online and team up in a four-player campaign which challenges you to be your best and work together to prevail. Experience the island through the eyes of a wayward crew in their own quest to survive against the odds.

A DIFFERENT BREED OF MULTIPLAYER

Innovative multiplayer rewards skill and team play. To level up, players must support each other by boosting with battle cries, reviving teammates and calling in support weapons. After each match, players have the choice to punish or show mercy for their foes in fun and twisted ways, choreographed with interactive cutscenes.

USE THE MAP EDITOR TO CREATE AND ACCESS NEW CONTENT

The powerful and easy-to-use Far Cry® Map Editor is back, with new and improved features. A community of mapmakers ensures a constant stream of all-new content with the very best being showcased for the whole world to enjoy. Using the included tools, you too can make amazing maps, quickly and easily.

CUTTING-EDGE GRAPHICS AND GAMEPLAY

Far Cry® 3 is being developed by the world-class game designers who brought you such critically acclaimed titles as Assassin’s Creed® 2, Assassin’s Creed® Brotherhood, and World in Conflict®.

Far cry has changed a lot over the years. It has gone from a linear story to a more open world setting. Far Cry 3 set the bar for what an open world game should be. Beutiful enviroments with lots of exploration and challenges. The over the top story and set pieces don't take themselves too seriously and that makes for some of the best action in a video game ever.

The game has always been at the front of graphics technology. The original Far Cry pushed the limits of what PC's could handle and 3 is no exception. The huge view distances create a vast and detailed world. Jungles are beautifly rendered and are probably the best since Crysis. Running through dense foliage was one of my most memerable experiences. Getting lost and navigating the tropical island is half the fun. There are also a lot of detail settings in the options menu. Scaling the game to match your PC's preformance is easy but does provide enough options to keep even the hardcore crowd happy. There were also some interesting options that I had never encountered before and had to learn what they did.

The story doesnt take itself seriously, which is great. If it did, it would fall completely flat. Over the top set pieces and characters allow for huge action sequences. While not overly complex, the story did provide at least some interest, enough to keep me playing. It was interesting to think about what good I was realy doing for the Island. Was there any real point to trying to liberate the island from one dictotor just to become another? The love interests of the game try to play on your emtions but in the end it comes down to your own choices. At least half of the villans in the story are interesting. Crazier villians are always more intesting. Far Cry loves to play with your perceptions and make you question weather or not you are crazy. Mind over matter is not always cut and dry. It will make you question your own sanity. Also the jungles in Far Cry are amazing.

Far Cry 3 is a great game, but stumbles in a few key areas. The endgame is shallow and doesnt provide enough to do after the story is completed. I was able to conquer the whole island and was left with nobody to shoot at since all the missions were finished. The only thing there was left to do was some hunting missions which get repetative quickly. The second half of the game lacks much of the beautiful terrain of the first. It becomes more like a desert and a dense jungle. It feels more like this section of the game was rushed.

Overall Far Cry is a great game and has set the bar high for open world games. I would strongly recommend it to any far of FPS games or who loves exploration. It has a lot going for it and has provided me with some of my most memerable moments in gaming.

Solution to the Uplay issue: Disable the Uplay overlay

1) Go into Uplay settings
2) General settings tab
3) Untick the "enable in-game overlay for supported games" box
4) Start the game and click to confirm you want to start the game without the Uplay overlay on

The issue is Steam AND Uplay tries to enable their overlays to the game which cancel out and makes the game freeze and crash. By disabling the Uplay overlay, it allows Steam to take over and the game runs smoothly. I had this issue for about 5 months before figuring it out, sadly.
