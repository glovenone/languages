About This Game

The FIRST EVER and ONLY Top Trumps™ Turbo PC Game Available to Download NOW.


Top Trumps™ Turbo is a brand new, exclusive fun and exciting Top Trumps™ card game brought to you by Funbox Media. Use the cards and your skills to beat the computer and become the ultimate Top Trumps™ Turbo Champion! Play, win & enjoy this action-packed game with your favourite decks, regularly updated packs, stunning gameplay and a great atmosphere for the whole family to enjoy.

Spin the spinner to choose from 6 different mini games and fill your turbo bar with stars and enter into the Turbo final for a gripping head-to-head match. Collect more decks by downloading them and use your knowledge of the card statistics to win. Gain achievements by winning mini games and keep track of your stats to make sure you are the ultimate Top Trumps™ Turbo Champion!

You may well have only just discovered Top Trumps™ but believe it or not, the original Top Trumps™ card game is over 35 years old. The new look Top Trumps™ Turbo PC game will indulge you into a totally new experience where you will enjoy a variety of favourite decks available. Using the card statistics you have to beat the computer and become the Turbo champion! Chose your favourite deck, spin the spinner to choose from 6 different mini games and enjoy the Top Trumps™ challenge. Fill your Turbo bar with stars and enter into the Turbo final for a final head-to-head match. Enter the hall of fame with your achievements by winning mini games and keep track of your stats to make sure you are the ultimate Top Trumps Turbo Champion!

Test yourself – how well do you know your facts about Pop Stars, Cartoons, Pre Historic Animals, Geographical objects? Are you going to be lucky enough to get some free stars? Or perhaps you will have a chance to steal few stars from the opponent? Play, win and enjoy the fame of becoming the ultimate Top Trumps™ Turbo Champion.

Features

  • Players can build their own collection of decks.
  • Single Player mode vs. AI
  • Regular deck updates in the store.
  • Statistics and Achievements.
  • Individual Card and deck viewing options

I like playing Top Trumps cards. I do not like this game.

There is the obvious, very pink, elephant in the room. All of the textures were pink when I loaded up the game, it's easily fixed by changing launch options but I expected the developers to have fixed that by now. Graphically it isn't very impressive and looks like a much older game than something released in 2015. The music is kind of terrible too.

You aren't told what some of the statistics mean and whether it is 'better' to have a higher or lower value. I assumed that 'Hall of Fame' was how high in popularity they were in some ranking somewhere, with 1 obviously being the number 1 entry and therefore better. But it is the opposite.

There is no option to play classic TT either, with a whole deck being dealt and playing until one person has no cards. Currently it is more like really short (1-2 minutes at most) mini-games followed by a slightly longer round. I don't mind the mini-games but it would be nice to have the option to play with a full deck.

Luckily I got a free copy of the game in a giveaway because I certainly would not pay £15 for this. I doubt it would even be worth buying when it is on sale. If you want to spend that much just go out and buy a few packs of real Top Trumps cards and play with your friends and family (or strangers on the bus).

I did get the game to run (!) by right clicking the game in my Library, going to Properties, clicking Set Launch Options, and typing in "-force-opengl" (without quotes, of course). But on to the actual review -

Top Trumps Turbo is literally the dumbest board game I have ever played. And I've played some pretty dumb ones. It consists of comparing two user- or computer-selected (annoyingly, the game has no multiplayer with other humans) irrelevant statistics of the same type on cards. And, if you/the computer happen to reach the objective (spelling TOP TRUMPS TURBO at the bottom), you can totally lose it all if you don't make the cut during the showdown. Which is infuriating, because you spent all game building the objective up and now all of a sudden you lose.

It felt like the computer had total ESP at times, and the game felt like it was rigged when you got certain cards that you were absolutely bound to lose with. Also, the comparison didn't feel right - why would you choose which year is greater, wouldn't you want the item that's older to win?

TLDR - opened game, it was crap, right click -> delete local content

-- GRAPHICS --

90% of the in-game textures do not display and, instead, show a blank pink. All of the cards in the game display as pink rectangles. I tried to fix it - I really did. I tried verifying the game cache, and that didn't fix it, though it did find a file in error and redownloaded all 0 bytes of it. I tried changing the graphics quality, which did nothing except alter how laggy the opening video was. I tried uninstalling and reinstalling, and that did squat.

Due to this, I can definitely say it's an issue with the game files players are given, and not something caused by the player's computer or a Steam glitch. There's no way for anyone but the developers to fix it, and they have yet to acknowledge it as an issue.

I did see a couple of the menu screens, though, and they were...uh...decent, I guess.

-- SOUND --

It sounds like a generic party game. Was not able to hear any of the sounds past the menu due to not being able to play.

-- GAMEPLAY --

Due to the graphical issues, I wasn't able to play in the slightest. Was sort of laggy, even on the lowest graphical setting, though.

But I can say that for a party game, I am noticing a distinct lack of multiplayer features. Hmm.

-- SUMMARY --

Don't spend your money on this game if you want something to play. If you REALLY want to play a Top Trumps game, you have other options. Better options.

If you did spend your money on this game, get a refund. Immediately. I can't believe this was actually published by the same people who published the Guilty Gear games on PC. It's awful.
