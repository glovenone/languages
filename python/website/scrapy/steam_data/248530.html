About This Game

Depth Hunter 2 offers 25 exciting missions in 3 huge locations around the world. Players will hunt different fish species and face the difficulties of breath-holding spearfishing, an ancient fishing method.

Players will also have to find treasures and get the possibility to take underwater photos to capture the beauty of the simulated, detailed and lively underwater worlds theyâre exploring also in a exploration mode.

Spearfishing and Underwater Hunting are ancient fishing and hunting methods, used to catch fish by using a mechanical harpoon. Today, spearfishing is a sports activity in many countries around the world, especially in the UK, around the Mediterranean Sea and in South America. Spearfishers hunt while diving apnea, holding their breath, so have to coordinate their energy carefully while monitoring their prey, especially in tactically challenging hunting situations.

Depth Hunter 2: Deep Dive is a very calm and ambient spearfishing game. Its very straighforward, you get 5 areas to hunt (without dlc) with objectives (find and spear this species, find these treasures in this area, take a photograph of this species or location) and you scuba into the depths of the ocean that your in to complete the objectives. A very enjoyable game if your looking for a serene experience with small challenge. The controls are very tight, where you aim is where the spear goes and the swimming is surprisingly easy to control.

The visuals in the game are very pretty with the changing landscape, the different species of fish and the occasional wreckage here and there which adds greatly to the feeling that your actually in the ocean.

The soundtrack and the ambient noise in the game is incorporated very nicely that they complement each other, but if your in the mood to only hear the ocean you can change the preset. The sounds do immerse you into the atmosphere of the ocean, which is a good bonus for me.

Pros: Beautiful game, great atmosphere, different locations and challenges.

Cons: A little on the short side, but you can replay the levels and complete the achievements which adds to the total time played. DLC add more locations and species. If price is a turn off, you can complete the base game with 100% achievements and buy when its on sale.

9/10 If you've been scuba diving this will bring back memories of that experience. Great atmospheric game.

There is not that much to tell about this game: it's a very casual fishing/diving simulator, in which you spend all of your time underwater, swimming gently along some nice (sub-)tropical settings, mostly reefs. The gameplay consists of either catching the required fish, or of exploring the surroundings - either by taking "perfect" photographs of several fish, or by searching for lost treasures. There is a small element of advancing through the game, since you gather experience and money with every catch or find with which you can buy better equipment.

The main game consists of three diving locations, to which several can be added by buying the different dlc's. Every location can either be completed by fulfilling the different "mission goals" (which are mostly very basic), or can be just enjoyed swimming along in a free mode. This swimming, looking around and catching fish definitely has something very relaxing about it - this is the perfect game to play after a stressful day at work.

But the game does lack some features which would make it more attractive - I'd love to get some more information on the different fish swimming around me, and the diving-simulator part really is extremely basic. Everything is being handled automatically, which in a real-world dive surely would not be the case. Some element of action or even tension would have been incorporated here.

All in all, this is a game which offers just what it aims to do: a relaxing, nice experiencing, without any difficulty whatsoever. The Steam achievements are mostly easy to get, except for some overtly grinding ones (I don't expect too many people to spend 24 real-time hours underwater in this game).

Recommended to anyone looking for a casual, relaxing experience.
