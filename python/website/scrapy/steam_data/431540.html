About This Game

It's been a year since Semyon returned from "Sovyonok" and met the girls from the camp in the real world.
But it's New Year at hand! And it needs to be celebrated!
"Frosty Kiss" is a tiny piece of this holiday that will bring back the well-loved characters of the novel one more time, even if it lasts only for a few moments. But somewhere deep in your heart should always be a place for celebration and feelings left by a kiss in the most important night of the year.

It's a short and sweet story about new years.
As it is a free game, I was expecting it to be worse, but it's really worth your time. The art is a little bit odd at first, but you get used to it very quickly.
One of the best parts about it for me was the music. It's gorgeous. In most cases I simply turn off the music because it's so repetitive and boring, but that isn't the case here.

If you're a visual novel fan, just get it. It's free. There's nothing else I can say.
