About This Game

All Hell Has Broken Loose!!! The Haunted is a fast paced third person action horror game that focuses on delivering an intense multiplayer experience. Your goal is to liberate cursed places and survive the assault from the minions of Hell. The game features several multiplayer modes such as co-op survival, demons vs. humans and demonizer.

Key features:

  • Addictive cooperative online and offline gameplay for up to 4 players!
  • Multiplayer versus mode! Humans vs Demons in exhilarating and distinct game modes “Battle” and “Demonizer”.
  • 8 larger environments with unique level events such as meteor showers and lightning storms which affect the gameplay.
  • Over 30 upgradeable weapons and hidden special weapons and items.
  • Free future updates and DLC!
  • Custom level and modification support!
  • Powered by Unreal Engine 3.

System Requirements

    • OS: Windows XP, Windows Vista or Windows 7
    • Processor: Intel Pentium Core 2 Duo 2.4 GHz or AMD Athlon X2 2.8GHz
    • Memory: 2 GB RAM
    • Graphics: Shader Model 3.0 graphics card with 256MB of memory (NVIDIA GeForce 7900GS, ATI Radeon 2000 series or higher)
    • DirectX®: 9
    • Hard Drive: 4GB of free hard drive space
    • Sound: DirectX compatible sound card

Developed by KTX Software Development. KTX Software Development is a Division of Signo y Arte P.G., S.L., Spain. Distributed by Signo & Arte. All rights reserved.

It's literally just a Killing Floor ripoff, which isn't always a bad thing. Problem is killing floor has a player base, as well as mod support. This has 8 levels and is 100% dead. So this means playing by yourself against terrible AI bots. The levels are big, with good verticality. The movement is good, with jump/slide/melee and other components. Graphics and damage modeling are decent. Problem is the gameplay. You play 4 waves, each which take like 10 min and way too long. The ai just spawns guys right next to you that run straight at you, stand there, or get stuck on objects. Most of the guys look the same, and later guys are previous guys with more armor or an extra weapon. Each character uses the same gun, of which there are 3, and you slowly upgrade to a better version with no new firing. Ammo you just randomly get from guys when game decides to drop it, which can be right when youve run out of all weapon ammo. I did find like 3 extra weapons in 8 total levels so I don't know how that works but dont expect to pick any up. Theres random time things you destroy for no specific reason. Then you get to the final boss, who for some reason isn't explained how you hurt him and on beginner difficulty can kill you in 3 seconds of 1 attack. The game is just super boring, repetitive, terrible difficulty curves, and not much fun, especially by yourself. Even though there are multiple levels you'll be doing the same thing and there's no real reason to play more than 1.
5/10.

"Hidden Gem" is the only word I can describe this game.

The game itself seems to be a little dated, however, the fun is not.

Here's what I thought

Graphics and FXs
It is surprisingly good.

Characters are well-designed. There are several type of enemies as well as their variations. However, I feel that it could be better if they added some different models for the same enemy, to keep things interesting for the whole round.

Gore FX, imho, is better in certain situation compared to newer games with next-gen gore system. As it stands, it serves the game too well. (can be a little too much at times)

Maps are good, seriously, there are better than many newer games of similar theme. There are many neat details that will make you go "WOW", again, maps are good. ;D

Gameplay
Simple concepts, excellent executions. The game seems to design for soloing, but somehow, they make it so that you have to work together. A competitive co-op kind of gameplay, but encourages playing together.

This game describes the word 'challenging' very well. It is not hard, but challenging. You will be occupied for the whole round. It is intense, to the point that it might not be suitable for casual players.

Weapons are balanced, imho. They make it so that your starting gear can do something in late round. They are put into 4 categories, Melee / Pistol / Shotty / Machinegun. You get new weapons by upgrading, and each upgrade is really something, not just hit harder. Upgardes are performance-related imo.

As soon as you jump into the round, start shooting, meleeing. I am honest that you will start thinking of Resident Evil. (especially 4, and the laters) I personally think that ___COM should make their several latest REs' controls similar to this, rather than the awkward controls as they tried to maintain stop and aim from older tittles.

Anyway, actual gameplay can explain better than words:
http://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=546460819

Overall
Good game for a cheaper price, if you have some friends to play with, it is worth to try (solo is fine too, though coop is better imo)
