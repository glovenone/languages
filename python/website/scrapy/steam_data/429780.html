Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“Early Access is a way for us to develop and improve the game with the help of you!
Nobody know's better what the game might be missing than the people actually playing it!
As a developer, you often get stuck in a specific point of view. Doing early access gives us very valuable feedback that will help us to improve many aspects of the game!”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“Very hard to say. Once we feel like the game is polished and contains enough content it'll be ready for a full release! We plan to keep the game in Early Access for as long as we feel it can benefit from more iterations and feedback by the community.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“It all depends on what kind of feedback we receive from the community.
There will be more content like maps, gamemodes, etc in the full version of the game.
Sounds and music is another thing we are currently working on and are currently missing.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The game is fully playable and already provides lots of replayability!
There are currently two gamemodes. Bunny hopping and sliding with a couple of maps each. We will add new maps as frequently as possible in order to make the game move forward! All maps are playable from start to end!
Only basic sound is currently implemented.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“The Early Access is launched at a discount to show our appreciation to early supporters that wish to help us make the game better. The final release price may differ from the early access price.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“The community is essential to Bunny Hop League's success. The whole point of Early Access is to work with the community in order to make the game better.
We will need feedback on things like bugs, general "feel" of the game, how levels are built, design input and of course your overall thoughts regarding the game.
If you think that something needs changing or rethinking, we want to hear it!”

About This Game

Inspired by old school first person shooters, Bunny Hop League takes you right back to the golden age of gaming, when hopping, strafing and sliding were all the rage.

In most games, these techniques are used to make yourself a harder target or move faster. However, in Bunny Hop League, they are core mechanics, used to get from one place to another as quickly and efficiently as possible.

Bunny Hop League is an arcade-oriented game that's about challenging yourself and reaching your goal as fast as possible. While it can be hard to master, it's extremely rewarding when you finally do!
