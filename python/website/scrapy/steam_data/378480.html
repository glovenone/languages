About This Game

It’s the first time developers have taken a risk to combine magical fantasy style and pragmatic steampunk. Players try the role of merciless warriors who destroy enemies using armored wheeled monsters. Each machine is a result of ingenious military engineering. In addition to tens of weapon and armor types all the machines are equipped with exclusive bonus modules.

Battles full of adrenaline take place on earth and in the air, under the blinding sun and in bitter frost. Interactive maps and well-detailed physics considering landscapes and peculiarities of all mechanisms supplement rich gameplay.

*For Mac-players : if you have an issue with the game (black screen) - please turn off Steam overlay.

Pros:
+ Free to play
+ Has multiple vehicles to start with: 2 ground vehicles, and 2 aircrafts. More can be bought when you play matches and increase your account level. Amount of experience which you gain after each battle for your account depends on: did your team win or lose the battle, how many enemies you killed, how many flags you captured.
+ Everything can be obtained just by playing the game, so pay-to-win is not necessary.

Cons:
- Only few maps and 1 gamemode (Conquest)
- No tutorial whatsoever, but if you have played games with vehicular combat which are similar to this one (Unreal Tournament 2004/UT3 for example) You can get the hang of things quite quickly. Just remember to check land and air vehicle controls.
- Teamwork can be difficult / Very little ingame chat communication with team members. If you have nice group of steam friends to play this game with, i'm sure that things work out better (You can create a clan in this game.)
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
I don't know where to put this, to Pros or to Cons...but each of your vehicle has progress bar above the vehicle icon, which means you can level up your vehicles when you play battles, and you get certain amount of experience for that vehicle which you just played the battle with. Gained experience for vehicle depends on: did your team win or lose the battle, how many enemies you killed, how many flags you captured. Leveling up a vehicle increases it's armor, speed, handling, ramming damage, different damage resistances. Also, each vehicle has different stats.
