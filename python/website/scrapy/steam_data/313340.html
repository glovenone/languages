I got this game with the recent Humble Bundle and thought I would give it a try. Several minutes in, I realized this game was not for me. If you are looking at this game, and you didn't read anything about it yet... there are no controls to this game. You can zoom in, you can zoom out. You can use the some of the keys on your keyboard to type out a bit of a song for the mountain, or for yourself, perhaps because of how bored you are.

I couldn't stand more than several minutes of this game, and I think the only reason I made it that long was because I was temporarily amused by the faux-piano.

If you get bored easily, this game is not for you.
If you like having some sort of control over your games, this game is not for you.

If you want to read what the moutain is thinking, and feel some sort of sad emotions or something along those lines, or you enjoy using your keyboard as a musical keyboard, perhaps this game is for you.

Personally, for people like me, I wouldn't recommend it.

"Mountain" is not a game. It should have been produced as a screensaver, because that is what this is. It shows a mountain on the screen, which slowly rotates, while sun comes up and goes down and sometimes weather changes. You may type things on the keyboard, but the only effect is that after you typed a period, a random sentence is shown at the top of the screen. You may use the keyboard to play the piano, which is the best feature of this program.

I have heard some people rave about "Mountain". I have no idea why. It is less interactive than a Tamagotchi. I let the program run for over an hour, and absolutely nothing happened. Controls there are none.

Do I recommend "Mountain"? Here's the thing: it is excellent for what it tries to be, so if what you are looking for is a screensaver that you can watch and contemplate, then "Mountain" is a great choice. But if you want to play a game, then by all that is holy give it a miss. It isn't worth the 99ct asking price,

I do understand that if you wait really, really long, you might see a random object impact your mountain. But that did not happen within an hour, so I killed the mountain by launching a meteorite. At least something happened.
