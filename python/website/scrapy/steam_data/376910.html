Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“We decided for Early Access so the players can provide a much valuable feedback to us.
We believe that with community’s help we can make even better game than we would make it by limiting it to only our ideas.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“The plan is to release a full version in 2016.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“Hot Guns will feature a single player campaign, local deathmatch and 1-4 players separate co-op missions.
Lots of messy guns, bosses, shop and a big hangar to keep all of the collected stuff there.
Steam features like Achievements, Leaderboards, Cards are also on the list.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The game currently has the first 5 levels (out of 7) of main campaign, 2-4 players deathmatch arenas and 1 Co-Op mission”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“The current Early Access price may be adjusted during or after development is completed.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“At this point we really hope community will get involved by providing a honest and valuable feedback, so the game will improve and evolve during the development. The builds will be delivered 1-2 times a month.”

About This Game

When serious problems arise a quick and simple solution's their only choice - so they call for you.

Your conventional methods of operation are "shoot first then shoot again!", its never failed yet! The mission is to eliminate the guy who is in charge of rather predictable but well armed organisation.
You'll get coordinates, a pager, powerful guns and access to unlimited ca$h! You work on the basis of what you find is yours so fill your boots!

Completed game will feature:

  • Full-action Single Player story
  • Up to 4 Players in the Local Co-op missions
  • Local Deathmatch arenas
  • An awful a lot of justified violence
  • Powerfully messy guns
  • Milions of fake currency to collect (it can be spend to buy even more powerful guns !)
  • Gadgets which will make running and shooting even more pleasant
  • Steam Cards, Achievements and Leaderboards
  • And who knows what else !

Once purchased you will get Prison Run and Gun game automatically for free

http://store.steampowered.com/app/436390/

Divertiti a distruggere tutto ciò che incontri usando tattiche diverse in un action-platform fuori di testa!

Features:

- Storia Interamente in Single Player
- Fino a 4 giocatori nella modalità Co-op Locale
- Arene Deathmatch
- Un sacco di violenza.... "giustificata"
- Tante armi da utilizzare
- Acquisisci più monete possibili per comprare tutto l'arsenale che vuoi!
- Carte Steam e Classifiche online!
- Previsti aggiornamenti futuri, con tanti altri extra!


Parere personale:


Hot Guns, è un action dallo stile grafico semplice, ma dal gameplay abbastanza difficile!
Spesso bisogna capire bene come affrontare un livello, poiché non è sempre facile!
Lo stile retro è molto simpatico, e le armi sono molto divertenti da utilizzare.
L'unica pecca ad oggi è il prezzo del gioco, per il resto è un buon gioco che riesce a divertire ancora di più in multiplayer
