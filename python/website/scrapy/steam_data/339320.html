About This Game

Snake Blocks is a 3D puzzle game about snakes! You must weave a group of colorful snakes through over 50 hand-crafted levels, until you've found a way to bring them all to their respective destinations. With Snake Blocks' four game modes of ever-increasing difficulty, you'll find yourself snaking for hours!

But wait, there's more! Snake Blocks comes with a built-in level designer, so you can create your own puzzles. Steam workshop support coming soon!

Snake Blocks is playable on PC, Mac, and is coming soon to Linux!

Your snake will often go in unintended directions because the tolerance between them is wonky, and then when you try to back it up it'll go loop around some more.

Before I start off I like to state that I've been waiting for a sale for this game since day 1. Here it is and now I get to play it! How was it? at first impression, not great.. BUT not bad :/



It's a good idea. I'll say that, but it has a few problems (which will hopefully be fixed by the time you're reading this). Starting off, mouse acceleration. This is a terrible thing for a puzzle game like this. And even if technically there is no actual mouse acc in this game, then the sensitivity or something, is screwed. The next flaw is MAJOR. From what I know (which is nothing) the levels you can create can be only played by you. That means the level editor is pretty much non-existant!!! However I'm not sure on this one and am waiting for a response from the dev about how the level editor works. However this game isn't ALL bad, I mean the gameplay is actually quite unique and it is actually pretty damn fun. I also like the easy access of the level editor and the unique visuals of the game. Oh, and yes. you will scratch your head numerous times while playing this game!

6/10 Fun gameplay but not without some flaws
