Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“The game is currently undergoing level developing. And new mechanics are done underway as the game progresses. This game is made available as early access for the developer to get support for the game. Giving him less time finding a job or getting studies done and more into developing the game all day everyday. Which is a dream job.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“This game will be in early access for a few months, depending on the number of games sold. low number of sale means I could not support myself to fully develop the game. High number of sales means I could care less about paying the bills and have them paid for without a worry about supporting myself.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“Full version will be completed with all chapters available with at zero to minimum bugs. At full version the game will have all available chapters with story finished. I may have a goal to add more challenge levels along the way.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“Current state of the early access version is near stable. Which should have certains levels available for play.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“I plan to gradually raise the price as the game devlops as to add levels and features along the way.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“I plan to ask what the community would like to say about the game which may cause it to add or reduce features, difficulty and reduce bugs.”

22 June

Switch Mode, Level Editor, and Summer sale

Good day dreamers,

I got a heavy update for Eclipsed, First off, after careful consideration, I have added a mode where you can switch with the girl. Me and a few that I know are able to call the girl easily and direct her, which was one of the main feature of the game. Since its actually hard for most. I added a feature where the player can control her on certain levels. It makes it easier for them and it pokes a hole to my AI system but, its worth it for a greater cause.

The second thing, Level editing. Lucid dreaming is active for level editor. It is more of a teaser, but you can make and play some levels. Yeah, seems weird to have it available and just show pieces of it, but that's how Early Access is. On another note, there are a LOT of variables that are needed to be thought about when making a level, I am insuring that nothing goes wrong when updates happen. And I am planning to give incentive to play more. Which you have to continue in the story line to get access to some items that you can use in the level editor. The level editor has more features that it would currently show.

Summer sale is coming, so I am giving a huge discount for the game for people who haven't tried it yet. I have no matter to the amount I am getting but I got rent to pay too. Either way, I just want more people to try my game.

I am hoping to see more players have the game.

I am looking forward to adding more achievements and more level editor features for you guys. So please, Bare with me O3O

About This Game

Eclipsed is a monochromatic adventure platform puzzle game. This is a story about a boy who wakes into a world of darkness. He ventures deep into the world ever changing. He finds beauty and mystery in the dark and strange world as well as a girl who seems to be as unique as him. Together they venture forth to rid of the world they are in.

The game follows the story of the Major Arcana of a Tarot deck. The game has numerous features such as a variety of puzzles some unique to the game with combinations of it; Semi rogue-like levels that give each computer a set of levels that is unique for each one; A smart AI that you will have to see; Challenge modes and YOLO mode that can give a more rogue-like experience with a cost of a life; and more to follow.

There is more to the game, as an early access it is still under construction. So please, bare with me as I make more levels and features. Only one person is working on the game with only his free time to work with. Which I am hoping to change.

A Story Driven Puzzle / Platformer
https://youtu.be/Fbab3QAORA0
+ Unique level changing mechanics (light dictactes whether platforms exist or not, and the girl companion can see new parts of the level if she's close by)
+ A nice moody / mysterious soundtrack and story
+ The levels are interesting
+ I found it surprising that even though the girl is about a second or two behind you while following she manages to make most of the jumps and follows you quite well.

This game is in EARLY ACCESS so don't take these negatives too seriously
- The movement controls were not favorable to me, I did not like how you kept moving for a few seconds even after you let go of the arrow keys, and I also did not like how your jump height seemed to be lowered if you are running
- It was sometimes hard to read the story text as you were focused on the stage itself
- I felt like visually the stages could improve a bit, perhaps some more things in the background or perhaps adding a rain effect would add to the mood.

~ I felt like it could have benefited greatly if there was a narrator in the game
