About This Game

Caveman Craig is a unique strategy/survival game set in a vicious prehistoric world of cavemen, neanderthals, sasquatches, and freakin' dinosaurs!

Build up your clan and conquer the 5 tribes of boggdrop. You decide the fate of man as you hunt, gather, and prepare your way to victory! Demonstrate new skills to your cavemen so they can contribute to the survival of your people. Gather your brave warriors to fend off an onslaught of dinosaurs, and use special tools and equipment to outsmart the rival tribes.

Featuring..
  • Hilariously brutal battles of rock, spear, and claw! Rawr!
  • Grokkloads of secret unlocks to help you along the way, including some really stupid cheats.
  • All the dinosaurs we love to fear! T-rexes, velociraptors, walruses.. wait what?
  • Lots of extra game-types, including custom games and survival mode.
  • Share the experience with your mates - compare your stats with Steam leaderboards, or grab a controller and play split screen co-op!
  • An epic soundtrack by platinum selling composer, Ricky Garcia (LaFee, Jordan Rudess).

Caveman Craig; The Tribes of Boggdrop is a unique strategy/resource management game set in the distant past where cavemen and dinosaurs roamed the earth. The platform sidescroller style paired with pleasant graphics and gripping music is well-suited to the goal; survive!

With a variety of in-game features including 'powerup' mushrooms that give you a variety of good and bad effects, XP Rewards that you can use to help you conquer the other tribes or just grow your own, as well as unlockable minigames starring the games' (very difficult to pronounce) dinosaurs, Caveman Craig has something for everyone.

Whether you are a survivalist, keeping yourself and your tribe alive against the hordes of dinosaurs or a co-op helper trying to build the biggest tribe you want with your friends, everyone can come and enjoy watching the Hunter cavemen destroy the dinos, the Gatherer cavemen grab the bodies and drag them back to the cave where you and your Preparer cavemen can chop up the meat and squish the berries, preparing for the new recruits!

It's a wonderful game you could spend hours on, but keep an eye out - there's a giant lurking just around the corner!

As a player of the original Caveman Craig years ago (back in 2008!), I was delighted seeing that it has been expanded upon and released on Steam.

The review

I lost hours when I was in middle school playing this game, and now out of college, I have to restrain myself from pulling it up during work hours. The game provides such an easy gateway out of reality and into the world of Craig and his cavemen. The original game alone had enough content to keep you occupied all day, training your cavemen to gather, hunt, and prepare food. The game features not only the five levels, but new game modes that bring new energy to the game, and challenge players in new ways. This game will forever hold a special place in my heart.
