Akuatica:
~~~~~~~

I do not know about you but I always love to sit at the beach, look at the beautiful sparkling water and every now and then I wonder…. Is life under water peaceful? Would it be exciting to discover life underneath these beautiful waves? And then I came across Akuatica….! You have to play it to believe what a hard life it is to be a fish!!

Akuatica is an action/adventure/puzzle game where you play a little fish with 5 red streaks on his back who will encounter interesting situations during his journey. The streaks on his back are the health bar of our joyful little fellow and he is armed with electric bolt (It’s true..! a bit like an Eel) to stun or to send your enemies to the abyss. However, a boost of speed is very important in this game; as you will really quickly find out, it is quite a challenge to destroy your foe. So, sometimes you will need this extra boost to avoid a terrible misfortune…. And you do not want this to happen to our fish! An important point, it will take about 5 seconds to regenerate your electric bolt and your speed boost.

The game has 27 levels to beat and offers 2 types of difficulty, normal and hard (thank god for that, I got stuck on the piranha level for a while). Writing this, the clues are obvious and sometimes they are so clear-cut that you will miss them and get stuck, just like me! That is where frustration kicks in! LOL! Great foe to battle against: sharks, turtles, bloody piranha (I occasionally swear, but this ugly fish got a gut full from me!), pelicans, whales and others from the river to the open sea.

Little green fish will pop up here and there; give them a good electric shock and you might get a bottle of health!

The artwork is superb and I really like the feel - it is a journey you are playing, great concept. The audio is good too and will change when you move to and from different locations (sea, rivers). Control-wise, as you use the mouse to move your fish it can be a bit off a challenge and can be frustrating, especially when you use your boost of speed.

Positives:
~~~~~~~

- Great artwork!
- Good puzzles & fast pace action
- Challenging
- Achievements & trading cards

Negatives:
~~~~~~~~

- Can be frustrating

Akuatica is very well presented game with challenging puzzles and epic fish battles (Grr..)!

9/10
