Don't like the arcade fest that is DiRT Showdown? DiRT Rally is too hard for you? Then DiRT 3 is the game you are looking for!

The main purpose of the game is to complete all kinds of races across 4 seasons, while additional championships are unlocked by winning in a specific category. Speaking of categories, there are 6 in total:

-Rally is what the name suggests : off-road courses from point A to point B. Some of the stages are short, but it's fine.
-Rallycross events are pretty much circuit races using rally cars. You do a set number of laps and that's it.
-Trailblazer is like Rally, but focuses on speed and puts the player in hillclimb-spec cars. Really interesting, considering you don't have a co-driver here.
-Land Rush are offroad races with stadium trucks and buggies. Same as Rallycross.
-Head 2 Head is a round based duel race between 2 drivers with changing routes. It's ok, but takes too much time to even unlock them.
-Gymkhana is a freestyle trick competition. It's skateboarding, but with cars. I don't like it too much, especially since it's somewhat forced in the career mode.

Other remarks:
-Overall physics are well done and hit a nice spot between simulation and arcade.
-Keyboard controls are easy to use.
-Unskippable voice commentaries >:(
-Want to do a time trial record? Good luck, top 100 is filled with cheaters.

Final rating : 8/10

DiRT 3 is one of, if not THE best racing game currently on Steam.

The graphics are great, the UI is beautiful and the maps are detailed and gorgeous.

DiRT 3 offers a wide variety of difficulties, shying away from the norm and is accepting of more casual players who aren't interested in complex controls. That said, the controls are great - both on controller and mouse/keyboard.

There are almost too many maps to choose from, and they all are of excellent quality. The game-modes seem to go on past the horizon as well, although each and every one still manages to be unique and fun.

I personally recommend the 'intermediate' difficulty for new players, and to turn off ABS as soon as possible. The game has the just the right balance of challenging but not so challenging that it isn't fun.

DiRT 3 is what to buy if you need a fun racing game. It's not particularly realistic in terms of physics, but if it was, it would be a worse game. It's well priced, and THROUGHLY enjoyable.
