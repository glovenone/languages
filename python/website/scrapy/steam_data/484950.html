About This Game

Real-time Action + RPG+ MAGIC Tower Strategy = PUZZLE ARPG

The game starts in a dark underground maze.
A female officer was confined in a prison
She wants to get out of this nightmare maze !
Would you like to help her?

There are plenty of hidden elements in the game! ~
You need to improve your attack and defense to defeat the monster!
At the same time, you also want to ensure that you collect enough keys to open various types of doors!

IN MY GAME:
Various types of small puzzle game is waiting for you ~
10 elaborate levels
8 powerful boss waiting for you to challenge!!!

4 types of language: Japanese, traditional Chinese, English, simple Chinese

Game support WIN XP Vista 7,8,10 system!

Product received for free
It's an interesting game, similar to gauntlet in a way. You wail on enemies as ♥♥♥♥ Mcgee AKA Alma, search for secrets to get OPed items, and progress in the maze using magical keys. You need to think carefully how you use your keys though, as you can end up screwing yourself over later on. As for the story, there's not much going for it, it almost feels like an old dungeon arcade game with RPG elements.

Summary
+ Fun gameplay
+ Plenty of little secrets to find
- English is atrocious ( See suggestion 1 )
- Lacking overall challenge ( See suggestion 2 )
- Controls are wonky, but I adjusted. ( I couldn't find how to remap stuff ) I suggest playing with a gamepad.


Suggestions
  1. I don't think the developer's native tongue is English, as such I think it would be a good idea to request or take help from the community to improve the English ( and other langauages ) translation. Maybe open a topic in discussions for this ? Making your game understandable would help improve its quality.

  2. The game is challenging in its own way, but I think it could be more brutal. For the most part a LOT of the enemies are static ; they just sit there and take it. Making a majority of the enemies move and pursue the player would give a REAL challenge and make the game feel more alive.


Overall I'd suggest it if you like old timey dungeon crawlers, with a miniscule slice of anime. In it's current state though, I wouldn't recommend it at full price. Don't get me wrong, I had fun going through the game and finding all the little secrets. It's just that this game requires a lot more work to be of suitable quality. It has potential, so keep it up.
