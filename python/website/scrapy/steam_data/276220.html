About This Game

My Best Friends – Cats & Dogs: Can this be real? Sure, because this is a pet care game where puppies and kittens can be taken care of and be given a lot of training as well. You are responsible for the sensitive upbringing of your pet: You decide on the food, the composition of training courses, the grooming and decorating.

If the pet is given optimal food, grooming and heaps of love it will perform well in both agility and beauty competitions. Winning these, the owner will be able to buy even better pet care products and costumes for the loved one. Detailed and realistic animations of the cute Huskies, Dalmatians, Retrievers, German Shepherds, Maine Coons, British Blue, Siamese and European house cats provide extra fun and motivation.

Features

  • Player takes care of extra-cute puppies and kittens.
  • Highly detailed graphics on pets, objects, setting and movements.
  • Players can design their own jewelry for the pets.

Okay, all kinds of animal caring games are my guilty pleasure. I've played a lot of bad animal games that were enjoyable. But this one... It's really bad, even for kids. Hell, it's kind of upsetting how some people think that you can put little to no effort in child's game and still feel fine about it.

The backgrounds are ugly and motionless. Dogs and cats sometimes just freeze after an animation and stare into the abyss with dead eyes. Gameplay is repetetive and BORING AS HELL. You can unlock some new features, like tricks or places to go to, but they're all not that interesting and they don't change almost anything. For example, you can unlock the forest area and take your pet for a walk, but there's absolutely no point in doing that. There's nothing to do there, nothing to explore or just look at, and your character is painfully slow.

To sum up, this game is pretty harmless, so if you really want to spend your money on something silly, you can. But you'll be better off saving it for same better animal games. Or, I don't know, a cupcake. Cupcakes are always good.
