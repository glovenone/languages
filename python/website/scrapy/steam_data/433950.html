About This Game

A throwback to the classic quarter eating arcade games of the 80's. Bit Blaster XL is a retro styled, addictive, and fast paced arcade shoot 'em up! (shmup).

Description of Gameplay:
Your ship can not stop moving; so turn it left and right and use your boost/brake to keep from running into an ever increasing amount of enemies.
Your ship is always shooting, so make sure to pick up more ammo and the variety of awesome power ups to help you survive longer.
Get the highest score you can while collecting coins to buy more ships that are faster, shoot faster, or have more shields.

Simple, but fun and addictive. Perfect for 5-10 minutes breaks.

In this game you fly around and shoot increasingly more challenging enemies while trying to avoid colliding with them, the game area walls, and enemy bullets. Simple as it is, it's very fun. There are some pickups that give you different kind of weapons - some more, some less useful. You have a bunch of ships to unlock by collecting coins dropped occasionally by enemies - the ships differ in their speed/turn rate, size, amount of shield they start with, and fire rate. That's about it, but coupled with cute pixel graphics and a very nice soundtrack, it's enough to make a fun game I keep returning to whenever I have spare 10 minutes.

Bought this on the Steam sale a couple days ago, and it's already taken a place, alongside Downwell, in the category of games that I play 5 to 20 minutes a day. The game has a very simple but effective design, and the variety of power-ups puts this game above many of its classic counterparts. Like games such as Downwell, and the great arcade games of the early-mid 80s, this game has an incredible ability to put you "in the zone". My first two runs frequently end in the first minute. Then something clicks, I shift into gear, ignore the distractions around me, and I have a...blast.
