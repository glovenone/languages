What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“We want to let you play our game as soon as possible, that is the main reason why we are going for Early Access.
We plan to implement multiple game modes and add massive amount of content to our game. We hope that with your help we will be able to increase quality of our game and achieve interesting and balanced gameplay.
We hope that cooperation with Steam Community will help us to make interesting and hottest product.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“6 - 12 months aprox.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“More content, higher quality and overall stability of our game”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“Fully playable and operational game”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“No, there will be NO difference. Our game will always be F2P (free to play)”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We actively monitor comments and posts about our game in different groups. We also plan to let players to create their own content for our game.”

About This Game

Free to play shooter about the confrontation of two irreconcilable sides, represented by the government military housings and the armed hirelings. Try your hand at virtual battles with off-scale dynamics and hurricane gameplay in the spirit of the classical shooters.
In the game are available a wide range of maps and several modes. Level system allows player to open achievements, weapons, stickers and more.

System Requirements

    Minimum:
    • OS: Windows XP
    • Processor: x86
    • Memory: 1024 MB RAM
    • DirectX: Version 9.0
    • Network: Broadband Internet connection
    • Storage: 512 MB available space
