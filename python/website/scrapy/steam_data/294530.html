About This Game

So who is haunting the schoolhouse, taking the toys and practically scaring the scales off all the guppies?

Good thing super-sleuths Freddi Fish and Luther are on the case. Their plan: build a trap to capture and expose the phony ghost. To reach their goal the two friends need your child's help to find a few things-and the list of items changes every time they play. Kids will dive right in to direct this undersea mystery filled with old friends, new faces and a festive sing-along soundtrack.

  • Like the real world, Freddi Fish's world changes every time you play.
  • Search through fascinating places like a submarine, a ship or Atlantis.
  • Out of time? You can save the game and finish it later.
  • Take breaks with Freddi Fish: play games like Crab Invaders, solve puzzles, draw pictures or just sing songs.

Freddi Fish 2 is one of the very first PC games I ever played. I played it off a CD I got in a cereal box on my dad's ancient Windows 95 computer, which I had for about 10 years.

This is a game I have this strange nostalgic fondness for despite fundamentally being a simple kids game. The plot centers on the two characters, Freddi and Luther, who have to discover the identity of a mysterious ghost who steals the toys of all of the other children. And they have to gather various objects inorder to build a trap to capture the ghost. Like I said it is fundamentally a children's game so the humor is a bit predictable, the puzzles are very simple and you're frequently told what to do and what to get. (IE: Freddi says you need scissors to cut some kelp, you need a wrench to remove a wheel, you need to ask a character for a combination to a lock. Etc.)

Something the game does have much like other Humungous games is random elements. When you start the game up, certain elements are removed wheras others appear. For instance, all of the trap items you need to locate are randomized during every playthrough. While this isn't that big of a difference it does make the second or third playthrough feel different. When I was a kid this blew me away at the time but nowadays it just feels like a way to pad out the game time. Since it is a very short game.

Going through it on the first try you can beat the game in maybe 30 minutes. This is a common aspect of Humungous games compared to other SCUMM games like the ones by Lucasarts. So it might not hold your attention as an adult, but it will return you back to a day when you were a kid, and every game was a treasure to behold.
