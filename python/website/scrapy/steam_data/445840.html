If this game ripped off both the name and the gameplay of Popcap's Plants vs Zombies, I'd almost be willing for forgive itâAfter all, the tower defense gameplay in those games is fun and frantic. However, Undead vs. Plants is not that. Rather the opposite I'm afraid.

You are a zombie who looks just distinct enough from Arnold Schwarzenegger not to get sued. Your goal is to kill as many plants as possible by bouncing three bullets off walls and obsticles into them. If you've played Angry Birds, Where's my Water?, Cut the Rope, or any of the other hundreds of mobile games based around getting three stars by doing one task over and over, then you'll understand the gameplay loop here.

Unlike those listed mobile games though, this is in no way fun. There's essentially no animation, terrible music, muddy, blurry art and physics-defying enviroments.

Stay away from this. If you've got a hankering for Plants and Zombies in the same game, I've got three or four I'd much rather reccomend.
