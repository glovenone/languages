About This Game

Weapons Genius is a Simulator of building Weapons!

In Weapons Genius you can build your own Weapon! You can build A big and powerfull gun, or you can build a small but rapid gun, or you can build a gun with high accuracy!

In practice mode, you can earn money for another guns to build!

Graphics and Sound in game is realistic!

System Requirements

    Minimum:
    • OS: Windows 7
    • Processor: Intel Celeron, Pentium 4
    • Memory: 1024 MB RAM
    • Graphics: Intel HD Graphics, AMD 760G, Shader Model 3.0+
    • DirectX: Version 9.0
    • Storage: 200 MB available space
    • Sound Card: DirectX 9.0 support
    Recommended:
    • OS: Windows 7, Windows 8.1

Decent sales video

Cons:
Maybe 2 hours of game play
1 of the three barrels does not work at all as the sight blocks the center dot from the scope.
Even though they have them there is no open sight shooting as there is a dot super imposed.
There are only 3 barrels, 3 magazines, 3 rifle butts, 4 scopes, and 1 silencer. (Of these they all do something different depending on the cost.)
Glitchy does not begin to cover this game, I was forced to restart 4 times due to lost guns or getting stuck somehow.
Once you purchase the best weapon parts there is nothing left to do in the game.
The music is loud and obnoxious.
Only gives 2 locations to shoot at
At the gun range the long range shot is only a head shot.
Only 4 things to do in the apartment test the gun, order parts, weld on parts, and go to the shooting range
At the range it is always scored and always the same targets.
I am sure there are more but that is all I can think of.
