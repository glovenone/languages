About This Game

It’s the hottest summer on record, and all across Europe, valuable objects are disappearing.

Museum curators unlock cabinets and find precious artefacts stolen. Wealthy mansion owners wake up to see their priceless paintings have vanished from the walls.

One thing’s clear: a master thief is touring the continent and the police are left scratching their heads.

In this 15 minute game by Crows Crows Crows, a team led by William Pugh (The Stanley Parable), slip into the soft-soled shoes of the mastermind responsible…

… silently cross the darkened lawn of the mansion…

… hold tight to the tranquiliser gun in your pocket, and commit the most audacious heis--

oh god I can’t do this any more, i’m joining the strike. i didn’t want to - i honestly didn’t want to, but it’s gone too far

i’m supposed to be writing the steam store description and that’s it, that’s my job, but because of the understaffing i’ve been told i need to work in the lighting department too and it’s only a matter of time before i’m re-assigned to wildlife preparation

and everybody knows that working for wildlife preparation is mostly about trying very hard not to be eaten by the tiger

so that’s it, i’m joining the picket. if they want somebody else to finish the description, that’s their problem

i’m out.


- tina

As depressing as this game was, I couldn't help but chuckle at the morbid deaths of those who defy your thieving enterprise. However, I couldn't seem to get past the 15-minute mark, when you first enter the Orangery. The guards are too perceptive, and once you even enter their line of sight from behind, you're found and detained immediately. The trap where the oranges fall over backfired on me several times, until i cheated myself to the next area. since the tranq gun had multiple rounds in it, i felt free to use all of them except the last, for the tiger. As i expected, as soon as i entered the room (wearing my sneaker-shoes and using the silent gloves), the tiger woke up. however, the tranq gun needed at least 2 darts to knock out the tiger, and thus i was killed. fixing my mistake and leaving at least 2 tranq darts, I tried again and succeeded. but what i didn't know was that i needed to conserve as many tranq darts as possible, because the guards that held the 3 codes for the vault were on the roof, the bathroom and the doctor's lab. this required more time than i'd like to admit, but eventually i used every tranq dart and aquired the 3 codes. at this point, however, i was notified that one of the guards woke up and contacted the police.in my desperation, i used my pretzel distraction device to lure the guard away from the exit, and bolted. but it was too late. the timer gives only 2 minutes to escape the premises, and on every try it would have taken me 5. every exit but the roof was blocked.
I hate this game.

That being said, everything i just said was a lie.
10/10

Overview:
Dr. Langeskov, The tiger, and The Terribly Cursed Emerald: A Whirlwind Heist is an amazing short indie game. It takes you on a small adventure, behind the scenes of a "game". As you would expect from a William Pugh directed game, it had a comical script, which accompanied the amazing atmosphere quite pleasantly. The achievements provide a bit more replayability, with challenges such as collecting all the pretzels or turning off all the lights.

═══════════════════════════════════
Positives:
☆Comical script.
☆Amazing Atmosphere.
☆Heap of achievements.

Negatives:
☆None.

═══════════════════════════════════
Summary:
Overall, I had no regrets playing this amazing game and would gladly purchase a longer form of this game or simply donate. My time well spent with a humorous ending!
