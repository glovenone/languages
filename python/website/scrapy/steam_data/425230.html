Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“Early access will allow me to better use the fanbase that I've grown through my Twitch stream to further develop Sword 'N' Board. I've been doing a development stream for Sword 'N' Board daily, Monday - Friday at 10am PST which you can see at:

http://www.twitch.tv/stuffedcastle/

Many of my twitch viewers have expressed a desire to purchase the game to further help the development for the game, as well as keep the stream going, as I do instructional streams for both Unity, and C# development which people seem to enjoy.

My focus is to use a combination of Early Access and Twitch to promote the game, and it's development, as well as promoting the twitch stream at the same time. I'm also hoping that the money gained through early access will make it easier to raise the funding needed to attend local shows such as Pax (since I am a Seattle native) and better promote the game overall.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“I expect the game to be in early access no longer than a year. I have a clear roadmap of the development for the game, which sets specific milestones for development month to month. Come the end of February I plan on having the entire "Overworld" of Sword 'N' Board finished, along with all of its subsequent puzzles.
Come the end of March I also hope to have 3 of the 8 dungeons finished, complete with its puzzles and progression taken care of. And each of the following months are already planned in explicit detail.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“A lot of my fanbase over at Twitch have expressed a huge interest in a small "arena" style brawler within the game that people could use to fight eachother using the various item combinations within the game.
While the full game itself will have access to all of the dungeons and item combinations needed to progress through the world, the "Arena" that I would like to add would include item combinations not needed within the main campaign of the game, which would be specifically for the "Arena" style brawler.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The Early Access version consists of quite a bit currently! There is a large Overworld area for the player to explore, as well as many hidden areas which are at times complicated and difficult to find, but are rewarding! You as the player will have to use your bombs to discover some of the secret areas, as well as your item combinations to solve some of the current environmental puzzles.

Not only that, but there are currently 10 different item combinations which you can use immediately, with the 4 items that you have in your possession (though you don't start with bombs immediately, you can find them from defeating enemies).

There is also a shop where you can purchase items, such as bombs, tape (to repair your shield) and a Blacksmith which you can take all of your found items to - and then utilize to forge items and upgrade your stats!
Upgrade your stats also currently works in its current iteration. Should you upgrade Sidd's Attack, he will hit enemies harder. If you upgrade his Defense, he'll be allowed to take more damage before he is defeated. If his Agility, Stamina or Luck are upgraded, he will move faster, shoot faster, be able to carry more and more items and as his luck increases he'll find items easier and more plentiful which he can then use to upgrade further.

The Game also offers 3 different save slots, which a user could use, to return to their previous game at any time, while also allowing a friend of theirs to experience the game without disturbing their own.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“The game will be priced at just a few dollars to start off with and gradually increase in price as more content is added to the game!”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“The community is largely already part of the development process when it comes to my Twitch stream. I've taken many suggestions from players of the Beta currently, as I also do Beta key giveaways on my Twitch stream so I can aquire feedback from people playing the game. I also really enjoy people finding bugs, which I can then fix and better polish and refine the game, which has lead to the game running quite well in it's current state! Also, allowing people to post to Steam discussions, and be aware of the twitch stream will only further help development of Sword 'N' Board. I really want to focus on the relationship that my Steam Early Access and my Twitch stream will have together, to both further fund development of Sword 'N' Board, and cross promote each other while allowing me to acquire feedback from players!”

18 June

New features Incoming!

I know that I've been lax on updates here recently, as I've basically and my nose to the grind stone trying to refine some things and add a few new features.

Steam Trading Cards:
This will probably be the thing that makes its way into the game the soonest, as I've already been working on designs for some time, and is also one of the easiest things to implement. I know people love being able to collect and trade cards, so that's definitely something I want to have in the game.

The Narrator:
The narrator will be a main part of the game, and will follow Sidd on his journey. The Narrator will obviously be a part of Sidd's imagination and so will not only give a look into the mind of Sidd himself, but will also provide some more comedic moments while also helping flesh out more of the story. There will be an option within one of the main menus to turn him off however, if you'd rather not have him around.

Key rebinding:
One of the main issues with the game right now is the lack of being able to customize your controls within the game itself. While not much will change with the controls when it comes to using an Xbox controller, I want to give those of you that are using different language keyboards the ability to make the controls much more comfortable. While the controls are currently programmed with a Qwerty keyboard in mind, this causes a lot of issues for people playing on keyboards without a QWERTY configuration.

Dungeons:
As always, the Dungeons are constantly being worked on. While the 2nd Dungeon is almost near completion, a few things are having to be redone before that can go live. Dungeons are always an on going task and more and more areas will open up within the game as those become available.

Mini/Overworld Map:
Right now within the world of Sword 'N' Board there is no map available to see where you have, and haven't been, or how to get back to important areas. I'm currently working on both a Mini map (that will change based on the area that you're currently in) and a world Map that you can use to see your overall location within the world of Sword 'N' Board.

More randomization:
I've already started adding randomized items within the world that you can use your bombs on to receive more crafting materials and other needed things for your journey. These things will randomize and be different each time you play. Not only that, there will be randomized puzzles as well, to help add some replay value to the game, which will help it during its time in Early Access!

About This Game

In Sword 'N' Board, you play as Sidd a young kid going on a pretend adventure in his backyard in search of his missing video game console! You'll fight through imaginary monsters, find secrets and solve puzzles using the items you find on your adventure. Each of your items can be combined with other items in your inventory to enhance their uses! All of your items and their item combinations will be able to be used to fight monsters and solve puzzles during your adventure! As you find more and more items and unlock additional item combinations the puzzles will only become more complicated and difficult!

  • Blacksmith to craft forged items and upgrade stats!
  • Unique item combinations.
  • Unique blend of hand drawn assets and pixel art!
  • Multiple save slots
  • A constantly growing Overworld to explore
  • Hard to find secrets! (No hand holding here!)

Sword 'N Board really offers that retro feel that I crave from older games that I played when I was younger. A lot of devs try to pull this off, from downgraded graphics and silly music, but this dev does it right in all aspects. The graphics, for one, is what first had me drawn to this game - the artstyle is incredible and a lot goes into the detail. The gameplay is so fun. As a beginner, you may feel as though the game may be too punishing with those first mistakes, because you start off with low stats. At this point, dungeons have started to be introduced in the game. From completing the first one, I cannot wait to play more. As a beginner dungeon it was fun to find all the puzzles hidden within, and offered puzzles that stumped me at first. It alone offered a good deal of content to explore. With it being the smallest dungeon, I know this game will continue to get bigger and better, and with it I will be investing even more time into it.

Oh, and item combos? AWESOME. I have never had so much fun with item combos in a game before, lol. Currently, there are just two that I can unlock I believe, and I am just waiting on the edge of my seat to get my hands on more items I can use!

Overall, great gameplay, great artstyle, great music, great combos. And most importantly, a great dev that I have come to respect and support 100%. Keep up the great work- I cannot wait for what's to come.

6.4/10 - The Next Generation of RPG Maker; Promising Early Access

This is an Early Access game which shows some real promise. I'm excited to report that there are constant updates and transparency from the developer. I really appreciate this because it doesn't take long to see the progress of the game and where things are headed.

The game has the format of an RPG maker; but the similarities end there! This game has some beautiful, custom maps, live battles and an innovative forge where you can create items. The story doesn't have the most thrilling plot; a boy in search of their video game console.

A few things that didn't do it for me: 1) the monsters don't have the same artistic brilliance as the rest of the game. Worth A few bucks! Once some polish is added it will be worth the full $5. Keep an eye on this one!

Usually not a fan of Early Access games, but for this developer I make an exception!

The daily streams on twitch are a great way to see progress being made and provide the peace of mind that the project isn't going to have a year long hiatus without any news or updates.

The game itself:

Yes, it is early access, so there are still some bugs here and there, but it's a very playable game right now and I haven't run into any game breaking issues yet which is pretty impressive for a game that was just launched.
