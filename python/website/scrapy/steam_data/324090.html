9 June

Football Club Simulator - FCS 4.0.2.1 = Focused on stability. Update now available!!!

The FCS 4.0.2.1 version of Football Club Simulator is focused on getting the best stability of the videogame and the competitions included.

To do this we have counted with the invaluable help of thousands of players from the International Community of FCS.

Players from Brazil to Germany, from England to Russia and from Spain to all countries of Latin America.

Without your reports, saved games, DxDiag and dump files it wouldn't have been possible to achieve such an improvement in so little time.

Many thanks to all.

Nataly Stefano - http:// https://www.facebook.com/nataly.stefano
Worldwide CM - The Football Club Simulator Studio


STABILITY

General:

1.Fixed the "marking" by default of the country in the drop-down list of the player selection window.

2. Fixed the automatic alignment which now, if required, chooses youth players in replacement of injured and/or cautioned players.

3. Fixed the message when renewing your own player.

4. Limited to three the number of goalkeepers that a computer controlled team can handle.

5. Fixed the error at the end of the first season when playing in Pro-Manager mode.

6. Fixed the error when selecting a team at the end of season in Leagues of countries with "Cup 2": England, France, Mexico and Argentina.

7. Fixed the error when displaying the offers tab in the transfers window.


European competitions:

1. Fixed error when performing the group draw of the European Cup.

2. Fixed the error when ordering the groups of the Europa League.

3. Fixed the error when performing the draw of the round of 16 of the Europa League.

4. Fixed the error when getting the number of available places for non EU-Players.

5. Fixed error in the schedule of the english league so two matches aren't played on the same day.

6. Fixed error in the spanish Cup so the away and home games of the round of 16 aren't played both in the same stadium.

7. Fixed the error in the day 33 of the League of Italy that allowed to play two games in the same day.

8. Fixed the error of positions in the classification of the League of France concerning the Europa Cup and the Europa League.

9. Fixed the error in the name of the player "Rena" of the team "Paris" who appeared with incorrect characters.


American competitions:

1. Fixed the error in the schedule of the League of Mexico at the end of the regular season in the Apertura and Clausura tournament which allowed two games on the same day to be played in the promotion and relegation "Play-Offs"

2. Fixed the line-up in the trophies of the 1st and 2nd division of Mexico.

3. Fixed the qualifying of the best second team to the round of quarterfinals of the Mexican Cup.

4. Fixed the name of the player "Oliver" of the team "Mexico City" with incorrect characters.

5. Fixed the line-up in the Trophy of the 1st. division of Argentina.

IMPROVEMENTS

1. Counter-Offers to players in last year contract. Emotion in the signing until last minute

A new algorithm for the signing of players in the last year of contract manages and takes into account up to six variable for the calculation of a possible counter-offer and retention of the player by his current team:

The variables are the following:
1. Signing offer
2. Clause offer.
3. Age of the player
4. Quality of the player
5. Average quality of the current team.
6. Average quality of your team.

Note: Try it out and give us your opinion to adjust it and improve it.
Write to Nataly Stefano: mail@footballclubsimulator.com

2. All or nothing. The promotion and relegation Play-Offs are here for Mexico and Argentina

Now we are risking the season in the field. And there is no need to rely on a "confused" calculation of averages of classification in the last 4 years.

The "Play-Offs" in real time are finally here, where the emotions get high when gambling the promotion and/or the relegation in the leagues of Argentina and Mexico. Get the victory in the field and not through bureaucracy.

In addition, we have updated the names of the groups to "Apertura", "Clausura" and "Final" so that everythign is clearer. And also the name of the competition "South Cup" which created a lot of questions, for the "Liberty Cup" one.

3. Get the maximum performance of your team. Manage foreigners and non-EU players up to the limit allowed

FCS already manages the official boundaries of foreigners in Argentina and Mexico and the non-EU players in Europe. Both the total in team as the total in the field.

This way, you will have clear how to do the line-up and the signings throughout the season in order to have the most competitive team.

Note: There are still some errors to find in player with double nationality. If you find any player in this situation, send us a "screenshot" and we will fix it right away.
Write to Nataly Stefano: mail@footballclubsimulator.com
