About This Game

  • It's FREE!
  • Fight in turn-based multiplayer battles up to 9v9 with the old school graphics you've been missing!
  • Enter battle as a Warrior, Mage, Rogue, or Healer of either gender, and use a card to transform into any of 16 characters, combining their stats and abilties with your class's!
  • Elemental properties for every character; collect gems that let you choose yours, and cast its magic!
  • Gain Charge during battle to do more damage, boost specific stats, and more-- or just save your turns for later and pummel an enemy all at once!
  • A rockingly awesome soundtrack!
  • Journey through eight intense Trials to gain cards and gems for use in the Arena!
  • Enjoy the 2-player tactical-RPG-style Card Game-- a great additional game on its own, it lets you earn cards and gems too!
  • In the mood to just chat for a bit? Hang out in the lobbies, the Card Game, or even the audience of the Arena!
  • Wins in large battles are recorded to the server and periodically posted, so people can see where you rank!
  • Updated just before the Steam release with new backgrounds and other improvements!
  • Play as a Champion (boss) and take on 9 enemies at once!

"Whoever making these positive comments are either bots or liars."
Woah there... just because you don't like a game, doesn't make someone else a liar.

Ehem, anyways, here is my review:

This is a F2P turn based strategy multiplayer game w/o microtransactions. Sure, this game looks like it was made by a couple, maybe a few people at most. The art and gameplay work well, even the music (as cheezy as it is, and I like cheese) is somewhat addictive... though it is as fast as any online browser point-and-click game from the late 90's to early 2000, which isn't fast at all. Unless you are waiting to join a game, or interested to see how someone fairs during their match, you will probably be bored sitting in the audience, however, you can cheer on a team that you want to win once, which actually gives a minor helpful boost (That is pretty awesome, minor, but still awesome.) First day, devs were here to help players learn how to play, and it was fun seeing Psycrow get knocked down a few times. (still cool in my book ;) ) Detailed help page pretty much helps you learn how to do battle in the arena. The trials were ok. They probably could have done much more with them. I still have trouble with the card game, but I will still come back for more arena, because that is where the fun is, and of course , what the game is mostly about. If I had to rate this game currently, I'd say 7/10 because it's not the most baddest game ever, however, there is excellent replay value, and for the type of game this is, everything works out. They could improve on this so much more. I will be playing more often to pass the time.

Message to Devs:
UPDATE 3-28-15 I noticed that you removed the charge button today. WHY?! it totally ruins the fun out of the game. I am tempted to NOT recommend this game now. It's sad, however, the truth hurts.
UPDATE 3-30-15 I am now changing my reccomendation to NO. Dev explanation is that charge should be considered luck for it to be a level playing field... that's not level at all... if it's nerfed to only chance now, then there is really no skill involved anymore. Such dissapoint.
