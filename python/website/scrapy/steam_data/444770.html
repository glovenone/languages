About This Game

Mimpi is a lazy doggy who becomes the “Superdog” in his dreams. Help him to save his friends by manipulating the environment of 6 original illustrated worlds in this charming mix of adventure, puzzle, and platformer. Find out what dreams dogs have and download the sequel to the award-winning Mimpi 1! Mimpi Dreams is a casual game, do not expect strong challenges, we want you to have fun and finish it. The game was designed as multiplatform, so you can play it on iOS or Android devices, but it is played best on a PC!

Features
● Scientifically accurate super-realistic simulator of dog dreams :)
● Casual gameplay combining platformer, puzzles and adventure
● 6 different worlds to explore: stoneface forest, tesla landscape, bottle seaside, fairytale, medieval castle and factory!
● Soundtrack by award-winning​ composers Jaroslav Vyhnička and Alexandre Guiraud
● Sequel to the award winning original - yes, there is Mimpi 1

PC Features
● Proper keyboard and mouse controls designed for PC (you can also use the gamepad).
● Different camera zoom and more parallax backgrounds.
● Steamworks features: Cards, badges and Steam achievements!

About Mimpi
Mimpi is a lazy doggy who likes to sleep a lot. In his dreams, he becomes the dog hero, who rescues and saves whoever is in need of rescuing or saving! Be it a lost pirate ship, a lab rat who wants to be free or a princess held by a dragon - Mimpi hero is here to save the day. Of course, Mimpi can not do it without his sidekick and that is YOU!

Mimpi and you are the makers of his dreams. Help Mimpi be the hero by manipulating his dreams - move obstacles to allow Mimpi to get through levels, solve puzzles and help Mimpi communicate with the creatures in his dreams.

A relaxing, cute and imaginative experience is what you get with Mimpi Dreams. At its core it's a mild puzzle-platformer, in which you can directly control the titular doggy through varying platforming challenges. Along the way, you get to control elements in the environments via the cursor, which involves some mild puzzle solving.

Both platforming and puzzles are very easy and for those who require it, there are lightbulbs to collect which can be exchanged for tips at the varying puzzles you’ll encounter. The game is less about actual challenge and more about drawing you in with its aesthetics and this it does well. Every level represents a dream and the vector based, layered graphics really supplement this well.

The biggest problem the game has, is that it’s really short. At this moment it features 5 levels, with the promise of more (free) levels in future updates. These initial five levels can be played through within an hour. This one hour offers an amazing experience however and the longing for more in a way, is a huge compliment to the actual quality of the game and in particular how well it manages to draw the player into its different settings.

At the full price point, it’s a bit of a hard recommendation to make for the amount of content you’re actually getting at the moment. However, if you do end up picking it up, you’ll find a fun little game to go through while it lasts and hopefully we’ll see more levels down the road to showcase more of the creativity from this team. I’d especially recommend this game to those with younger children, people fairly new to video games or just those who enjoy experiencing different artistic expressions, who are less concerned with finding actual challenge in games. For those people, this is a must, at least during sales.

Mimpi Dreams is a casual platformer with some minor puzzle aspects, about the heroic adventures of a dreaming dog.

Check out my Quick Review: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kreGx6tlxEM
Each of the 6 levels acts as its own story illustrated by speech/thought bubbles. The underlying story for each level is Mimpi showing up and saving the day. The game is very casual. You bark at, and interact with objects in the background to help you progress through the levels.

Pros:
  • the controls are customizable, and the game has full controller support
  • cute graphics - the 2D animation is simple, yet colourfully done
  • casual/kid friendly game, with simple gameplay and hints

Cons:
  • some of the whistling sounds are a bit annoying (a volume control setting for music and sound effects independently would have been helpful instead of just an on/off toggle)
  • very short game

There are hidden secrets that you can discover, as well as other, more easily found collectibles to earn achievements. Once you find everything, there is little to no replayability.

The entire game took me a bit over an hour to complete all 6 levels. The price on Steam is too high to pay for the game’s current content, so I would definitely wait for a sale. Currently, the game is also available for Android and iOS. If you’re looking for something short, casual, or kid-friendly, Mimpi Dreams is a cute game, and it was fun to play while it lasted.
