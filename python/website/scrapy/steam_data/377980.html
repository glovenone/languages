UNDEADS
UNDEADS is a FPS video game set in near future where the human race is dying after the arrival of the undead army, the hero has to fight and destroy completely all the undeads in various maps or places that are in different setting/scenarios to restore the dead city and complete the goal.

Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“I think my project can be developed and improved . UNDEADS is a FPS video game I made, I been working on it alone with beta testers, I read comments from players who wrote on the official page of the game, their suggestions, ideas and opinions. I have my own many ideas too and still want to develop new goals, maps, scenarios and unique enemies for the game. The possibilities are endless and I want to improve it to be unique and same time fun to play by single or multi player. There still a lot of things I wanted to add with the help of the community.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“Images made were small , fps old style is becoming a real game with the enemy classes, weapons and aims to discover the full potential of the game. The ideas that comes out during development, lead me to believe that the merits of the game I owe it from community of steam, but its only possible with the help of players . Hopefully three or four months should be enough to collect ideas from the community and add those to the game.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“The game is almost finished on its first level, and I know still not enough, it needed more level in addition to the first one. I observed that working closely with the community I could achieve a better end product and continually improve it with new game mode and the full game version can be accessible to satisfy more avid players .”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“As of now the game mechanics are complete and 90 percent of the great dead city map is in full function for single player, with various goals to complete, weapons upgrade is up to 4th level, enemies were completed. I want to hear the opinion from the community, I am open to all criticsm, either constructive or destructive and I will listen to their good ideas and make them my guide and inspiration in the development of the project.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“In future I will gradually increase the price of the game but I will make sure I have added new content and functionality to satisfy buyers/players.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“I'm trying to collect tips or ideas to add to my project and during development I always considered critical approvals and community.”

Edit: Review update after 6 months (08.05.2016).
This game had some rough time from players, but it is nice to see, that developer just goes on and is still busy experimenting, updating the game (last update 01.05.2016).

In meantime I have also tried some other builds from the game other than first two builds and I thought, that game was on the right track. But trying to play the latest build I'm not sure what to think anymore. Sure carater animations & some special effects are nice, but all the other environments, singleplayer play styles are gone, what I have seen in the game so far, only one (empty) city map remains (for now). In single player are some impossible accurate dudes, which is not fun at all. Multiplayer is same map & you need a friend to test it out because server is empty. Game also needs lot of optimations, struggling to keep the constant framerate. Sure, it is hard to review work in progress, but right now I don't recomand this game.


Now when I had chance to play latest version (v1.7) I can see where the game wants to go or what to be. It have goals, powerups and enemies to stop you. But right now there is very long way to go. There is only one map (other 3 are missing) and one character to select (other 2 are locked), poor enemies AI, some missing textures, no multiplayer, no way to change controls, graphics settings (only resolution and Vsync) etc.

Edit: You can change graphics settings in the menu Fastest, Fast, Simple, Good, Beautiful & Fantastic.

There is definitely something unique about the game. I don't think it will be great game like Killing Floor or Left for Dead are, but there is a change it will be very cool game anyway, will see I may change my review to positive in the future.


Edit: 01.11.2015
Developer have fixed the issue.

Sadly I can't say much for the game, because I only have early DRM-Free release of the game (v1.4), but it looks promising. Right now it is not possible to install game from steam. No size is shown during the install process and after "successful" install there are only empty folder in steam game directory. Verifying the game files has no effect.
