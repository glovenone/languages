About This Game

Promised: You will love this addictive game!

Don't make these monsters angry! Fire your bombs at those lousy creatures and get buildings to collapse on them. With lovely HD graphics and awesome physics!

"Bomb the Monsters!" features the story of a highly talented painter who is living a perfect life with his beloved wife. But one day things are changing when a huge horde of ugly monsters invade planet earth, capture the painter's wife, and exhaust and exploit the whole planet. Now it's up to the player to stop this horrible invasion and rescue the poor hostage - easier said than done!

The very well-known gameplay offers an easy entry into the game: In every level the player needs to place a limited amount of bombs next to the monsters for getting surroundings and buildings collapse on them to drive the monsters out. But the monsters are coming in various sizes and shapes and are fat, imbecile and extremely sticky. Because of the physical structure of their bodies, these stupid creatures stick to almost everything what makes it sometimes tricky to bomb them away.

"Bomb the Monsters!" offers over 60 levels in beautiful HD graphics; each level comes with non-linear solutions to eliminate the ugly monsters. Great music and sounds plus a motivating achievement system are part of this addictive mobile game as well and offering fun for the whole family.

FEATURES:

* Easy to learn, hard to master!
* Addictive and very famous game play
* 60 levels, divided into 6 different worlds
* Realistic physics. Everything in the game uses physics attributes such as mass, force, friction and gravity!
* Non-linear solutions for each and every levels
* Breath-taking animations
* Great music and awesome sound effects.
* Motivating achievement system with more than 10 unlockable items.
* Multilingual

What is this game? Think Angry birds without flinging the object at the targets. You place the "bombs" next to the target and detonate...

The physics of this game is messed up; the blast will go through some objects, but not all, despite the objects it goes through being less likely to allow it than the ones it prevents it from going through ( this may simply be a bug / glitch ).

Placement of the bomb is annoying, and if you place it too close to something it teleports far away.

Physics of objects affected by blast don't behave in ways you'd expect.

No options ( Sound and music are combined into one setting without a slider to designate volume; in other words: mute all or have incredibly loud audio bombard your headphones ). This game was clearly developed with mobile in mind; not all mobile games are bad but this one has no options and that is a major downside for me.

A different type of physics game. I saw the screenshots and thought, "Oh, great, another Angry Birds clone." But I was wrong. Although it does use the physics of explosions causing the bad guys to be propelled off the platforms, you place bombs and detonate them instead of launching a projectile as in Angry Birds. You have to get all the monsters off the platforms to win. You get a gold trophy if you do it in a certain number of moves or less. Later levels can only be unlocked if you have a certain number of gold trophies.

Later levels introduce the brainless, which must be protected from falling off. So that makes it more difficult when you have to get rid of monsters without also getting rid of the brainless. I wonder what they have in store for us in later levels? I can't wait to find out.

The graphics are so cute. I hope this game becomes as popular as Angry Birds because I want a plushy of the monsters. They're so cute!

Music is whimsical and fits the game.

Control is done by the mouse.
