Click here if you want to watch playthrough https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wcmAj1Cj7WI&feature=youtu.be just put up here for those who hate reading loads.

Cons
-There is no tutorial meaning you just end up flying in space with no real way of knowing what to do.
-To my knowledge you can't refuel your ship or regain health.
-In your inventory there is something that seems to be a crafting space but there is no way of knowing and there is no crafting recipes either...
-None of the items have a name.
-I don't even know if you can actually attack enemy ships.
-Your character uses the same HP and Fuel as your ship does.
-Items don't really do anything to my knowledge.
-The music is extremely repetitive, this doesn't bother me but I can imagine this would bother a lot of people.
-There is a very small amount of options and you will learn to hate the options too as there is no fullscreen option and also no way to turn down the game sound unless you turn down your master volume.
-Music is way too loud.
-Not that many character colours

Pros
-The game is sort of fun untill you find out that you just go around and kill things and mine for no reason.
-The music at first sounds nice.
-Enemies look funny

Overall I didn't really like the game all that much.

If you the developer are reading this, im not hating on your game or anything, im just giving you my oppinion and experience with the game, I would have played for longer but I didn't know what I was doing and found it fustrating over time, please make an introduction to the game and explain how to play the game as its really unclear to me as to how you play your game, and if you could ac least take some of the things I said into consideration like the screen size and such, then I would find the game a much enjoyable experience.
