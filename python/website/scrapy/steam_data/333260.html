About This Game

Marvin's Mittens is a 2D platformer with a focus on exploration and collection. With gorgeous, hand drawn art, Marvin presents a soothing world to explore, free from enemies and danger. Collect magic snowflakes to increase your jump height and race your sled across the landscape to reach new areas before your mother calls you back home. Learn the secrets of snow elf magic and traverse the world beyond Marvin's backyard to solve the mystery of the missing mitten. Immerse yourself in this charming indie title!

Key Features:

  • Beautiful winter landscapes and charming characters
  • Carefully placed collectibles encourage exploration and revisiting of levels
  • Ever-increasing jump allows access to new parts of the world
  • Coast down hills on your sled, find jumps to reach new areas
  • Nearly an hour of beautiful original music
  • Find shortcuts to venture further before Marvin is called back home by his mom
  • Sneak up on wildlife to add animals to Marvin's sketchbook

Also on offer is the Marvin's Mittens Original Soundtrack that includes over 35 tracks from the game along with bonus tracks you've never heard. The soundtrack is available separate or in a combined bundle with the game.

Go for an adventure!

One of the most amazing things about Marvin's Mittens is how perfectly it captures the magic of playing outside in the snow in deep winter. It hit me almost immediately: The colours, the sounds, even the isolation. But it's not just for children, and in fact I'd say the incredible sense of nostalgia it engenders makes it more appropriate, and almost certainly more effective, for adults.

"Charming" is probably an overused adjective in game reviews (or at least on the backs of boxes), but if any game deserves to wave that flag, Marvin's Mittens is it. It's not a difficult game, nor is it particularly long, and it's not going to put your platforming skills to the test. But it is an immensely satisfying experience that recreates a childlike sense of wonder and exploration like nothing else I've ever played.
