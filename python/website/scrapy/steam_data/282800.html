About This Game

100% Orange Juice is a digital multiplayer board game populated by developer Orange Juice's all-star cast. Characters from Flying Red Barrel, QP Shooting, Suguri and Sora come together with all-new characters to duke it out... with dice.

This is a world where dogs, people and machines fly through the air. In that world, a small patch of darkness was born. Infinitesimal at first, it gradually began to envelop everything...

A youth named Kai, led by the mysterious life form known as Marie Poppo, begins a journey that will span worlds. The black, bottomless darkness of desire slowly eats away at both Kai and the world itself...

Let Marie Poppo lead you into a mysterious world to play. Grab your deck of cards and let the games begin!

Features:

  • Dice, cards, stars, battles and pudding
  • 4 Single-player campaigns
  • Playable by up to 4 players online
  • Countless battle combinations
  • Utilize each character's unique combat attributes
  • More than 50 different cards to collect
  • Character abilities in the form of Hyper Cards

New Steam Features:

  • Full HD support
  • Redesigned multiplayer experience using Steam's lobby system
  • Spectator mode
  • Avatar customization
  • New character skins to unlock
  • VAC enabled

System Requirements

    Minimum:
    • OS: Windows XP/Vista/7/8/10
    • Processor: Intel Pentium 2.0GHz or higher
    • Memory: 512 MB RAM
    • Graphics: Direct3D compatible video card with 128MB memory or more
    • DirectX: Version 8.0
    • Network: Broadband Internet connection
    • Storage: 500 MB available space
    • Sound Card: DirectSound compatible sound card

100% Orange Juice is a board game developed by Japanese indie developer, Orange Juice, and published by Fruitbat Factory. this game may looks cute, but don't be fooled, guys. This game can be really evil and frustrating, (Because of RNG stuff) but that doesn't change the fact that this game is fun. The objective in this game is to, fulfill your norma, either by killing your enemies, or by gathering stars (which can be collected through star panel, effect, etc). There's also a deck building system that can help you a lot !

So on to the review !

(+) Pros :
- You could get a lot of playtime for a cheap price.
- There's a lot of character to choose from. Each with different playstyles and quite balanced !
- Your action matters, while this game rely heavily on RNG, your decision still matters and could bring you to victory.
- Multiplayer, Up to 4 players online ! Gather up your friends, mate.
- The online lobby is usually packed with players. Also, most people i met here is really fun and kind !
- Catchy music to accompany you playing.
- And the most important thing is, Fruitbat Factory is really dedicated to this game, that's what keeping this game fresh and alive after such a long time. Thanks Fruitbat !
- Also Poppo. Poppo is love, Poppo is life.

(-) Cons :
- RNG, while that's also one of the fun aspect of this game. sometimes, that RNG itself could leave you in frustration. Also, some people don't like luck-based game.
- The artstyle of this game is anime-ish, and that might not be suited for some people.
- Lack of in-game tutorial, This game is pretty much easy to learn, but.. it's kinda important to give the players insight about the game.

"So, is this game worth it ?"
Yes, totally, my friend. It's pretty much a steal for it's price. Get it~
