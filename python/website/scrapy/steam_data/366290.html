Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“Early Access allows you to help shape the creation of Carp Fishing Simulator. You get to play the game early and add your input on what you do or don't like about it, this in turn helps us deliver a better experience. The more information you can provide, the more help it will be.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“We aim to release the final version in early 2016.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“We plan to include more species of fish, better physics, more venues to fish on, more fishing tackle, different fishing methods, online play & full character creation.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“At the moment you can fish at 1 big lake that has 25 pegs to fish from, you can use all your tackle to catch the Mirror Carp, Common Carp, Grass Carp, Tench, Bream, Catfish & Perch. There is a realtime day/night cycle and dynamic weather system in place, also seasonal changes. There's a huge selection of baits to choose from, you can even create your own bait using our 'Create A Bait' software.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“Yes. The price will increase once the full version is released to reflect the products value.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“Players are welcome to post on the forums or Facebook group with any suggestions they have. Update progress will be provided via Steam & social media. We hope to have competitions etc too, hopefully to help get the community to take part.”

About This Game

Carp Fishing Simulator is the most advanced, authentic fishing experience on the market.

Are you tired of not being able to cast where you want?
Tired of having a time limit?
Tired of being told by the announcer to lower the line tension?
Tired of information that you wouldn't know in real life given to you on the user interface?
Tired of catching fish after fish, no matter what you do?

Carp Fishing Simulator is for you!

  • Fish from over 25 pegs around a lake containing Mirror Carp, Common Carp, Grass Carp, Tench, Bream, Catfish, Perch & Pike.
  • Use up to 3 rods, a spod rod, marker rod and baitboat.
  • Make & share your own baits with the 'create a bait' software.
  • Real Time of day - Real accurate Time of day or night.
  • Dynamic weather - Changeable weather conditions.
  • Seasons - Depending on the month you fish, you'll see seasonal changes which also affect the water/air temperature.
  • Full character customisation (COMING SOON) - Don't just choose a character, make your own!
  • Real AI - Fish are all completely controlled by complex AI, They are released in to the lake and do their own thing. No behaviour is pre-programmed.

And the list goes on...

Early Access Review
Posted: 10 May, 2015
Carp Fishing Simulator mentions it's the most advanced authentic fishing sim on the market. They were not wrong. After playing recent games like Dovetail Fishing which seemed very arcade focused, I was pleasantly surprised at just how much of a simulation this game is. Time of day, weather and peg selection all play a huge role. Don't expect to catch a fish every time you cast, patience is required and if you want to hook the big ones some knowledge of bait, line and rig setups is huge plus.
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4iGe-t1wyRE
With that said you don't need to be a professional either. Amateurs can randomly select bait, have a little patience and enjoy a relaxing day catching fish. I'm the first to admit many of the options went well over my own head as we catch mostly bream, barra and coastal fish in australia. Was still able to land a few 25 odd kilo carp, sizable catfish and plenty of smaller stuff which brought a smile to the dial. Far more then just carp fishing as well. At the moment the game contains several species of carp, catfish, bream, perch and tench with plans to add more species in future. Fish AI was a real highlight, all placed into the lake with no pre-scripted movements or behaviors. Releasing fish could use some tweaking but the catching part itself feels quite good.

Graphically speaking it's reasonable for a sim. Setting up your tent and lines around the nearest snag point on the lake while watching the sun slowly drop behind the hills can really help on those stressful days. Would like to see more detail added to the surrounds but it's passable. Fish all seem to be the one size, reeling animations and a few other things need work but nothing missing that's gamebreaking. One nice feature is the leaderboard which shows all the catches around the world and how you match up, in recent patches it now stores all these for future bragging rights to friends.

Other plans that I'm aware of is adding online multiplayer, different ways of fishing, new locations and character creation, along with tweaking and refining the whole experience of course. Carp Fishing Simulator can only get better from here and it's a game I can highly recommend keeping your eye on to any fishing or simulator enthusiast. Cheers folks and happy gaming/fishing. :)

Carp Fishing Simulator in my opinion is not only the best out there but what us Anglers have been missing for years!

I have had many fishing games like Fish Sim 1 & 2 and also Dovetails Fishing (Which just feels like Sega Bass)
None of them come close to this.

Early access so there is loads more to come and with what I have seen so far, Its a winner to the point that I now have this not only on Steam but also on IOS.

This is exactly what it says, A Simulator. And i will stress on that because if you want to cast in and catch loads. You wont do well, and could possibly tarnish your thoughts on this Simulator.

Everything you do from choosing the peg you want, tackle you want and Bait, affects your chances. Just like real Life!

With the news of added features which Ive seen via youtube, It is going to be simply - The only Fishing Simulator we will ever need.

I love this game.

There are little things not ready or completed, but we all get that with Early Access.

I will also add, The way you can customise what tackle and rigs you use is quite good.
The bait selection is good, and you can also download the Bait Creator so giving you even more rewards when you catch. I feel great when I catch a Fish on a Bait I created.
The 25 Pegs is more than enough to start with.

The Graphics are Beautiful. I run this game in 1080 on Fantastic, I find I take more screenshots of the scenery than the fish I land lol

The 3 Bait Alarms work well, and are easy to tell which is going, either by the different tones or the different colors.

Im enjoying my time on this Simulator, and I now know I will get in trouble from the wife for staying up all night !!

All in all, I think it is really good value so far, We will have to wait for the addition updates, Progress etc, but if we are going off by whats already here... Its going to be fantastic!

If you love fishing, you will fall in love with this.

A must buy for any Angler!
