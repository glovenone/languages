Claw your way through the abyss of the mind and survive the horrors that stalk you as you attempt to survive seven days as... an Indie Developer.

Product received for free
My playtime: 26m (based on steam)

Intro
Make it Indie! is a game about a man (you) who wants to make an indie game in a week. You'll plan on what the man will do in the 7 days and your choice will affect the result. During game development, there will be a mini game to "simulate" how the making of indie game works. There are 2 endings in this game; all depends on your choice. This game mostly consists of videos which i think make the game size to be quite large, around 1 GB.

Graphics
The graphics basically is just a text which is placed on several places with an image to fill the screen with (and no, i don't think it looks good except the image in the scene where you have to choose what to do during the day). During the mini game, there will be several images changed as a background which will disturb your concentration. If you choose to not working on the game during the day, you'll be presented with a video which is.. terrible. It seems that the video is skipped every few seconds and my head hurts because of it. I ended up skipping the entire video after i watch 2 videos.

Music
The ending music sounds pleasant after hearing all those terrible sounds in the game (especially in the videos). Other than that, there is nothing to say.

Mini game
You'll be prompted to do a mini game if you choose to work on your game. The game will provide you with circles which has been cut in 4. Some parts in the circle will be missing and you have to find the part which is on the circles provided. You'll receive good ending if you find the right parts 77 times during the whole week (and you can't track how many you did it right). However, the background can make you crazy. It keeps on changing every few seconds and some has a really bright color.

Pros:
- 2 endings

Cons:
- Terrible videos

Conclusion
This game mostly consists of videos with a mini game on it.

Should you buy this game?
No.
This game can be finished in less than an hour (it can be finished in 5 minutes according to a guide if you choose to skip all videos. Fyi, this game is around 1 GB, and the videos are terrible.
