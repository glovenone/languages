About This Game

Awakening of Solutio is a 2D platform game placed in a fairytale, full of dangers the forest. Traveling as a title Solutio accompany by the faithful friend of the forest ghost Ali.

Your goal is to free the remaining forest ghosts, which are the energy of the forest. On your way you will meet many of the traps that you have to overcome to get the keys, without which you fail to save the forest.

Get ready for an unforgettable adventure, explore the innermost recesses of the forest, lots of fun, and at the same time a lot of challenges.
Dont forget, future of the forest depends on you.
