An Arcade Driving Platformer
https://youtu.be/7AtscKXvkSs
+ Interesting level Design
+ Good soundtrack
+ A fun concept although the driving mechanics need some improvement

This game is in Early Access, so don't take these too seriously
- Awful car control in every aspect, slight turns cause you to spin out, breaking causes you to slide forward or off the edge, reversing takes ages to turn around, small jumps may cause you to lose all control and roll.
- Repetitive / Grindy, you will mainly be grinding for credits to buy upgrades without making much progress
- Due to the poor car mechanics the game is overly challenging to the point where it's more frusterating than fun.
- No Controller Support

In its current state I can not recommend this game, although it does have potential to improve.
