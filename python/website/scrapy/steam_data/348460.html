About This Game

You are dead. What happens next? Drinking and fighting, of course! As a reward for your brave deeds in Midgard (or, in the case of the Drunkard, his extreme alcohol tolerance), the gods have let you ascend to Brewhalla, where you get to do your favorite things for eternity: boozing and brawling. Have a brew, grab a weapon, and crush, kill, destroy, DRINK!

Set in a vivid, stylised, Norse afterlife, Bierzerkers is a multiplayer melee arena combat for all comers. While quick to pick up and get in some killing, the game has a great deal of depth, both individually and as a team.

Crush your enemies, see them driven before you, and hear the lamentations of their sheep!

Key Features



  • Team-based, 5v5 online melee combat
  • Fast, arcade-style gameplay
  • Quick matches
  • 5 character classes with unique abilities (and more to come!)
  • Easy to pick up, but hundreds of different class, ability, and environmental interactions make mastery elusive
  • Gorgeous mythical environments full of color and majesty
  • Join Early Access to shape the development and future of the game

Bierzerkers is a sort of TF2 meets Monday Night Combat meets Chivalry kind of thing.
The visual style is pretty nice and I like the viking theme, it's not something you see very often.

However, the gameplay can be a bit frustrating.
  • First off, it feels a little clunky at times and actually made me think that it might be in late beta or something, but it isn't super bad.
  • Second, the fact that you are given only two playable characters for free and are otherwise required to spend six dollars on any other characters is utterly insane. To the best of my knowledge, there is no in-game currency for buying these characters.
  • Third, matches are all extremely one-sided. I didn't play a single one that wasn't scored something like forty or more points to zero. This leaves them feeling unfulfilling from either side.

    Some other notable things I found distasteful:
  • Valkyrie, one of the free characters, is too strong. She has two shields that prevent enemy projectiles or players from passing through and has an easy setup for grabbing the sheep (the flag) at the start of each round. In case devs read this, I'll describe it here: Valkyrie can springboard from the starting area and while in mid-air tap right click once for a flip and then again for another and then press shift to fly up to the sheep spawn. Then, if timed well, the sheep will spawn and Valkyrie can press shift again to plummet down to the other side of the arena with sheep in hand.
  • Say you get lucky and manage to find yourself on the winning team, nice. You may just win this match. But beware of autobalance! After your team has racked up sixty-something points to the enemy's zero, you'll suddenly find yourself on the losing team with no notification whatsoever. What's worse is that this can happen to anyone, I started scoring points for my team and in the last minute of the match I was flipped to the other team. So great!
  • I already mentioned this but it's so bad it's worth mentioning again... SIX DOLLARS for a character! I know this game is free-to-play but come on now, that's totally unreasonable.

    But anyway, when you aren't playing against four Valkyries and both teams are relatively even and you aren't autobalanced to the team with zero points, it's fun. At it's core, this game is nice and enjoyable, but not in its current state. There are some neat ideas and it has nice theming but I just can't see myself investing any time into it.
