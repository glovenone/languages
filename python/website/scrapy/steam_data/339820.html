Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“The structure is done. Early Access so we can create interesting missions for the players and ask them about the general feeling they get from the game - should it be darker? Scarier? Is it too easy or too hard?”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“Approximately for two months.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“Space exploration will embrace five different solar systems, not just our own. More creatures will be added to the game and new missions on the main storyline.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The structure and playability is ready, death system, survival system, crafting system and combat system. It has however just the first ten missions and one solar system to be explored.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“No.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“The players will fully participate in creation of the ambiance and missions for the game. Help with ideas for new weapons, creatures and ships.”

About This Game

"O you hapless creatures, who feel it but who are loath to speak of what defines man" - Friedrich Nietzsche

The world is over. When the aliens came, their ships were dark and peaceful, but then it started. The aliens destroyed all the major cities and moved on to annihilate the entire planet Earth. Nothing was left standing. Now they hunt for survivors and you're one of them. You have always lived in a small town, now devastated by the alien invasion and you need to survive.

Find weapons, find food, find water and hopefully, you'll find a way to escape planet Earth and maybe there is hope out there.

Not a complete game. At all. There's no guide, it's too dark in the beginning. Buildings disappear, you can't tell if you're inside or outside sometimes. It sounds like it has a lot of potential, but releasing this game for $10 on Steam was irresponsible and borderline unethical. It is in alpha, at the best. Maybe even pre-Alpha development.

Early Access Review
Posted: 28 December, 2014
Alright, I've played and liked a bunch of Early Access games in my day. However, this is the most unfinished one I've ever laid eyes on.

First, you get given very vague instructions, there is no map, there is no objective indicator and the enemies can literally sneak up on you with no sound or even effect noise for their attack. I just happened to look at my health at one point and realized something was trying to eat my face.

The enemies can't be looted, and the initial map (crash site) is huge, but since you don't know where and what you're supposed to be collecting/doing, you just sorta run in circles hoping you get the right item by accidentally running over it.

I think the premise is nice, I think the game will MAYBE one day be playable and worth it. But as of now, I'm feeling super cheated as it's the bare skeleton of a game and not anywhere close to what they think it will become.

I'll keep hoping that I see it's updated in my steam window in the future and give it another try, but for now, it's just going to haunt my harddrive as the little game that maybe could one day be worth playing.
