About This Game

From the creators of the #1 selling-PC adventure games comes a new Nancy Drew story-driven casual series that is Fast Fun for Busy DetectivesTM. Nancy Drew Dossier hidden-object games immerse you in a suspense-filled hunt to piece together concealed clues as you search and seek to crack the case. A bomber plagues the Redondo Spa! Can you defuse the situation? The annoying explosives are pranks, but it could drive away the Redondo’s clients and celebrities. Nab the culprit or you’re out of business!

  • Six ways to solve the case
  • Replay exciting mini-games in Arcade Mode
  • Earn bonus points to unlock extra content
  • Combine clues to solve puzzles!

Posted: 18 April, 2015
The Nancy Drew Dosier games take a slightly different twist on the usual hidden object genre, there is no list of object you need to find on the screen, simply a counter that tells you how many hidden objects there are. Its up to you to find them and then figure out which ones match to solve the puzzles on the screen. A fan with a broken power cord? Match it with the sticky tape. A piece of paper stuck the the ceiling? Match it with the fixed fan to blow it down. It's a enough of a twist that it gives the dosier games a unique feel. The voice actor who plays Nancy in Her Interactives main Nancy Drew series is back for the dosier games and gives a good performance, in fact all the voice acting is good as is the music and general presentation. The plots are suprisingly good for this kind of game too and I found myself quite caught up as the mystery deepened. Reccomended for fans of Hidden Object games or the main Nancy Drew series who are looking for something a little different

The Nancy Drew Dossier is a fairly good puzzle game , it's not too hard . Some might even call it a tad too easy .
It has some nice voice acting and funny dialogues . I enjoyed playing it , I wouldn't call it a hidden object game , as you ain't got no hidden objects scenes like in the other rather typical hidden object games. It's more of an adventure puzzle game , as you drag and drop the hidden object and combine or manipulate them to get new more helpful items .
The story was okay and funny . Not very deep touching , but okay for a nice casual game . And I really liked the various mini games , like e.g. rotating chemical ingredients to match colors , or combine various torn letters tetris-style to get a full letter ..and so on .

It supports Steam Cloud and is best played with a mouse .

Recommended to all casual gamers .
