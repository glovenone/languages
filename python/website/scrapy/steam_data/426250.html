Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“Early access helps us test the game with a wider audience in order to get early feedback on bugs the game may have and which sport types the community wants to get.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“Early access status will last for about 3 months, since we need approx. one month per sport type we'd like to implement properly and there are 3 sport types missing for the final release.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“The full version will include all 4 sport types that are available for launch, including all the bugfixes we make during early access. Sport types and the way they are presented are subject to change depending on the community feedback.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“We have one sport type done (Bowling) and the menu and Baseball are work in progress.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“There will be no price difference between early access and the release version.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“Depending on the feedback we get the sport types and where they are held are subject to change. Also the whole UI will be based on user feedback.”

About This Game

This game was build from the ground up for Virtual Reality and Motion Controllers. Compete against your friends in several fun and thrilling sport events like Bowling underwater or Archery on top of a mountain. Use your real life skills in order to achieve the best scores and enjoy some beautiful landscapes!
Periodonica is a hotseat-style multiplayer party game, like Wii Sports, where you get together with some friends and try to beat each other’s score.
For the initial release four sport types will be available: Archery, Baseball, Bowling and Frisbee. Shortly after release a free “Olympia-DLC” pack containing Curling, Golf, Handball and Tennis will be available to everyone that bought the game.
We will add new sports over time and also implement multiplayer features so you can even play with your friends via a network connection.

Note: Currently only the HTC Vive is supported since the game requires Motion Controllers.


--Release content--

Archery
Up high on a mountain, surrounded by a beatiful landscape you will compete on whom gets the highest score in this thrilling archery game. Aim precisely and keep a steady hand when trying to hit the targets or else you’ll lose!

Baseball
Are you afraid of heights? Let’s hope not, because this isn’t your regular baseball field! Hit the ball as good as you can to score points and hopefully your ball will make it to the other side of the pit

Bowling
Get your squeaky shoes ready and bowl several miles under the sea. While you try to hit all pins watch out for sharks and other marine creatures!

Frisbee
Enjoy a beatiful day at the beach and play some frisbee with your friends. But watch out for the wind as it might ruin your shot!


--Free DLC content--

Curling
You are trapped on an ice floe in the middle of the sea - so why not spend the time nicely and play some curling with your friends? And if are lucky a penguin may give you a visit - but watch out for polar bears!

Golf
Playing golf on the green is boring, so why not play it on the moon?
Enjoy your view towards earth while you try to have the lowest number of strokes, but be aware that playing on the moon sometimes has it’s own rules.

Handball
Do you have what it takes to hit the targets popping up by throwing a ball? Find out by playing some handball and show your friends who’s boss!

Tennis
Better get your windbreaker ready for playing tennis in the sky. While standing on the clouds serve your opponent and try not to fall down...

The envrionments are very pretty for a VR-game and the actual sports are very intuitive. I guess what most reviewers don't see, is that this game isn't a singleplayer, "I stand alone in my room"-type of game. It's a party game made for you and your friends to enjoy.

Until Updates are made this game in it's current state should be free or much cheaper ($0.99 at most). The bowling game is not even similar to a real bowling experience as you bowl 3 rounds with 3 balls and no score is kept. Just abysmal.
