About This Game

The Skies is a post-apocalyptic MMORPG with a non-linear storyline, in which you follow your own path or join other players to conquer the wasteland.
In this innovative sandbox you create your own adventure in a player driven economy your actions will result in the development of both the world and your character.
The game story takes place in the early 21st century, when solar activity increased dramatically. Within two weeks civilization collapsed, wiping out power sources, government and infrastructure. Humanity descended into darkness.
In 2150-2170, dust began to settle on the surface of the planet and the sunrays reappeared after 150 years of darkness.

Is mankind strong enough to restore what they've destroyed over the last century? What is life going to be like now?

Ok, remember, its still in early Alpha development, dont forget that, because once you get into it, and bugs happen, it can get a modern gamer annoyed. The folks developing this game are doing so on limited staffing and budget. So a few things you should know. At the moment there are only a limited amount of servers, and if you create a new toon on one server, it doesnt port over to another server (yet). Do the tutorial at your relitives farm, make sure you pick up the bump gun in your room, put the clothes on that are in the waldrobe, and take the first quests, hold off taking the pie, which activates a port to Kimary, the starter town. go exploring around the farm, shoot a bunch of wounded dogs, you can easily level up to 2-3 doing this, and you can stock up on supplies around the area. Stones are ammo for the bump gun, which is effective for the start level. Look at your crafting recipies under you Inv (I on keyboard) and how the weapons relate to character (C on keyboard). You have primary and secondary points. Tip: increasing Inteligence will give you bonus points to spend on your secondary tallents, but your secondary talents are limited by your primary levels. for example, your rifle is under dexterity, 1 lvl of Dexterity, allows you to raise your rifle 5 points. Other fast keys to remember F - Flashlite, G puts your weapon away/out, T frees your mouse pointer so you can click on chat, except trade or team invites, etc. Kimary is the starter town, have a look around, on your map, a question mark above characters is a mission, some missions are time sensitive, but many of them will give you a weapon. points are limited so stick to one weapon type, like light pistol, or rifle and heavy, or automatic, etc. You can store stuff at the warehouse (look for the Mayor, he has access), watch for overloading your toon, over 100-110% load, you cant run, 110%- you barely walk, hard to run from the enemy. sometimes it gets buggy, and thinks you are overloaded, its easily fixed by exiting the game, then re entering it. I often get this bug after 4-5 hours continous play. Remember, its in ALpha development.
There are vehicles available, they cost bucket loads, you want armour and weapons first, remember you need to improve your heavy armour skill to be able to use it successfully. Some earlier armours you can craft yourself, others its hard to find those craft mat's, so its easier to save and buy them, though at first, it will seem hard to get, grinding low level monsters like scarabs and dogs, will give you an understanding of your weapons and dealing with swarm respawning. I also found hording mats is a good idea, every time you harvest something you get experience, it all adds up to get those levels, and 50 or a 100 grain can be sold for quite a bit. its a good idea to get lvl 5 in tech as soon as posible to craft, and at least lvl 20 in traveling to harvest copper and sulphur, higher for other nodes. Lvl 5 lets you make a lot of the armour, food, and crafting materials, so you can focus on other parts of your charactor.

I like the concept of the game, and everyone needs a riding pig at least once in their lives, and the beetle bug car looks interesting, though thats a lot of gaming to earn that money. I hope i see you out there (dont get too upset with the buggs, which are being worked out).
