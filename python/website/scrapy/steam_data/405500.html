3 July

New Update Coming Soon

In the first update we added Mouse and Keyboard support and fixed some bugs that were affecting our players. Coming soon is another update fixing the biggest issues players have raised with us.

We're bringing Fast Restarts to all, not just to those with the fastest machines.
We've added a Tutorial and we've designed some simpler controls for those struggling with how to master the SmashBreaker and Drop to Putt controls.
We're also adding a prompt on screen when in range of a Bucket to drop into, another requested feature.
Other improvements include better Cameras, a follow ball cam for Putting and a new feature called the SmashWave for clearing debris around the Ball.
Finally - we are improving the holes people like the least so contact us for your chance to have your say on which holes that should be.

Watch this space but we estimate the changes will be in Beta within the week.

Thanks for all your support. We are a 100% Player Supported company. Which means every penny we get from sales of Dangerous Golf go directly to paying salaries and equipment enabling us to keep making games.

13 June

Dangerous Golf Updated

We have now pushed our first patch 1.2 LIVE.

This patch includes:

  • Mouse and keyboard support (see caveats below).
  • Fix for audio corruption issue.
  • Fix for no audio issue.
  • Settings and How to Play have been moved to the Main Menu as players were having issues finding them.
  • All Achievements now functioning correctly.
  • Removed look restriction on all holes and removed hold on tee so camera will move to the ball quicker after teeing off.
  • Language fixes.
Thanks to everyone who took the beta branch and helped us confirm that our fixes are working.

The mouse and keyboard support comes with the following caveats:
  • There is no support as yet for remapping your controls. This will come as soon as we can get to it.
  • There is no support as yet for localized keyboards. This will come as soon as we can get to it.
If you do experience any issues other than these please mail us at pchelp@threefieldsentertainment.com

About This Game

Mixing arcade style high score gameplay with humorous irreverence, Dangerous Golf isn't about Par or Birdie. The aim of the game is to cause maximum damage over 100 holes and four unusual locations.

Smash up the toilets, make a big mess in the Kitchen, destroy priceless antiques in the Palace. This is golf played indoors where the more expensive the object the higher its game value. Humorous destruction and over the top trickshots are the way to score big.

Destroy enough things off the Tee and youâll earn a SmashBreaker. This turns your Ball into a Fireball that you can steer around the level causing more destruction and chaos. The bigger the mess, the bigger the Score.

Long shots, Ricochets and yet more Destruction are the name of the game when Putting. Hitting your ball straight into the hole is NOT what Dangerous Golf is about and whilst you can do that it wonât earn you the big medals or help you beat your Friends to the top of the Leaderboards.
