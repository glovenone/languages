About This Game

Mater is holding a national race festival allowing racers from all around the world to challenge Lightning McQueen. Along with racing against the new characters, players can explore the world of Radiator Springs and Lightning McQueen's racing headquarters. There are also more than 20 mini-games to tackle, including Ramone's Rhythmic Rumble and Fillmore's Fuel Frenzy. Mike and Sulley from Monsters, Inc. make an appearance as well.

System Requirements

    Minimum:
    • OS: Windows 2000/XP/Vista
    • Processor: Pentium 4 or Athlon XP 1.6GHz Processor
    • Memory: 512 MB RAM
    • Graphics: 64MB DirectX 9.0c Compatible Video Card
    • DirectX: Version 9.0c
    • Storage: 2 GB available space
    • Sound Card: 16-Bit DirectX 9.0c Compatible Sound Card

Better then the first game, and the best pc version of any Cars.
Lets get to the good. It runs at 60fps(unlike the orginal), and it features much higher quality textures. The racing is fun and the open hub world is great to have. This is like an expansion on the first game that makes the original almost useless. Then there is modding, but I will get to that. That gets me to the problems that do not make sense...
First it does not support 1080p(The 1st did) on my screen, however my laptop runs at 1080 fine,and the 360 rotation is terribly touchy. As for the graphics options there are not any really. Typical Disney port. Though you would really need a paper weight to struggle running this. Then theres a little known glitch where if you're running it off of fast HDD hybrid(32+GB cache) the sound will glitch and the game will freeze for a half second to catch up. So run this off of a slower hybird, normal HDD, or SSD. I am running it on an SSD and it runs great. On top of that Chick Hicks is gone, among others from the movie like DJ and King. That gets me to best thing about this game.
A mod called High Octaine. It puts all the big characters in this and adds many new tracks. You can pick Snot Rod, Cartrip, Wingo, Lizzy,and Fred. Freaking Fred! And many more. It even adds McQueen and Matrer from Matters Tall Tales as well as Mia and Tia wearing different costumes from Tall Tales. I have yet to get a crash and everythings buttery smooth even though it is not widescreen. If you go to your graphic control pannel you can increase and enhance everything; so don't be afraid of messing around with things like texture qulity and set AF x16. Like wise Sweet Fx works great enhancing the draw distance, lighting and colors. Go to Nexus and download Sweet Fx mod for Assassins Creed Rouge and drop it in with the exe. Its a great preset and doesn't require any tweeking if you don't want to. With all that in place it is the most visually impressive Cars game anywhere with the most content.
If your kids like Cars, then this is the version to get on PC hands down. None of the mods take much work and the game runs very well.

Graphics: Very Good, but odd. For example: High Poly models, but low reseloution textures.

Music: Also from the first game. That menu music is still "driving" me crazy. Some songs are from the film, such as "Life Is A Highway."

Score: 8/10

Great for the kids, or for teenagers and adults looking for a nostaglia trip. Just don't expect to get very far using modern systems and keyboard controls. But that is just my opinon.
