About This Game

Match 3 Revolution” is an original physics Match 3/ Marble Popper game. Its main features are:

- Create sets of at least 3 touching balls of the same color.
- 100 Levels of Unique Gameplay.
- Original game modes: click a ball, draw a line, drop a ball and play pinball.
- Supercharge with combos.
- A lot of power-ups (electroball, fireball, megabomb, fusionball, missiles,…).
- Top 50 Online World Ranking in every level.
- Original music.

System Requirements

5min of gameplay
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9_D3pwbr_Js

If you don't know this is a match 3 of a like color game.
Balls or squares are tossed to the center of the board and you try to match 3 or more like colors.
Some levels you can just click to destroy matches but most will need you to draw lines over them.
There are also pinball (boss?) levels where you need to get 3 balls in holes on the left and right sides.
This game also has power ups.
On the right you have an sword, axe and 2 rockets and after selecting them you can throw them to destroy balls.
On the right are balls you can drop in, fire (burns balls it touches), fusion (turns balls into like colors it touches), electroball (shoots out lightning to destroy balls), bomb (explodes after a bit removing balls)
The levels do change thing up with the way balls are drops or placed so if your into match 3 games this is definitely feels different though less refined.
