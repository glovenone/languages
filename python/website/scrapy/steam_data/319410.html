About This Game

Hunt, race and prank to become a Robertson!

Take on the role of Willie’s son John Luke as he learns how to become one of the Robertson men! Interact with Willie, Uncle Si, Jase and the rest of the family from the hit TV show Duck Dynasty®. Go duck hunting, varmint shooting, off-roading and play office pranks in the Robertson’s hometown of West Monroe, LA. Will you learn the way of the beards?

  • Call in ducks to your blind using a variety of calls and then fire away.
  • Hunt Louisiana wildlife including squirrels, frogs and fish for Miss Kay to cook her famous dishes.
  • Join Jase in duck call room antics as you sneak around the warehouse to trick Willie.
  • Race against Willie in an airboat as you drive across the Robertson family swampland.
  • Uncover Uncle Si’s bizarre “treasures” with a metal detector.
  • Track down pesky beavers with Phil to stop their damns from ruining the duck ponds.
  • Play through 3 game modes including the main story, duck shooting galleries and airboat racing.

Oh Duck Dynasty, where did you go wrong?

Was it your mandatory shallow minigame-esque activities? The overly-vast wastelends of the Louisiana Bayou which you will spend hours traveling through? Was it your horrible, unresponsive controls? How about that even thinking about right clicking your mouse will minimize the game window?

No, it's not those. Even those sins could be forgiven. Where Duck Dynasty has gone wrong is in its very conception—in the idea that a game based on a reality television show centering around the manufacture of duck calls could provide any modicum of pleasure. The idea that a game in which your goal is to become less educated, less competent and less civilized such that you—a teenage boy—can fit in with a group of 40-something-year-old men. The idea that making digitzed versions of these very rednecks and voicing them with poor quality recordings, while occasionally cutting in television clips ever-so-loosely related to the plot at hand.

That's where this game went wrong. That's the concept which above any other has offended me. And that, along with everything, is why you should not play this game.
