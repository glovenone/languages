Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“We are ready to ship a fully formed game and first wave of levels, however since VR is so new, we would like to engage the community to see what types of levels and puzzles they want to see with our mechanic and game play. Or, if we should tweak some of the physics, timing, etc.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“We should have our final version by mid-summer. Additional levels and other DLC after that.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“We will be rolling out updates over the next few months that will include many additional levels, modes, environments, achievements and challenges, trick shots, bonus worlds, physics modes and you'll be able to select different types of projectiles.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“This is a fully working game with 60 levels.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“We might raise the price when the final version arrives, but if you're with us at the beginning we'll give you those updates.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We'd love to hear your ideas for levels, power-ups, challenges. We are all ears. We'd like to make this an must have casual game for VR.”

About This Game

VReakout is a modern re-imagining of the classic brick breaker genre with a virtual reality twist and designed for the HTC Vive VR System.

Your mission is in deep space where you clear the areas before getting sucked into the Black Hole. Don't look back.

Break through the levels within the time limit. How many balls will it take you?

Work around the ice cube barriers! Perfect your timing to defeat the pulsating cubes.

Bonus points for speed!

The challenge continues with a steady release of updates containing power-ups, crazy awesome levels and environments, physics manipulation combined with an action packed frenetic pace and music that's on point.

Early Access Review
Posted: 29 April
If you ever wanted to feel what it's like to be inside a classic arcade game, this VR experience is for you. It's an atmospheric, mesmerizing take on the classic block breaker. I had a lot of fun just looking around and enjoying the environment and vibing to the music. The game play involves throwing balls at a series of block puzzles. It's simple at first, but grows more challenging as you must find a strategy to work the angles and throw around obstacles. You'll do a lot of damage throwing balls and work hard enough to get an arm workout. Along the way you'll discover a few zen moments of destruction toppling down massive block walls, and satisfaction when balls lobbed around a corner connect with the intended target. It's a lot of fun to be able to throw and break things in VR, and just like a classic arcade game you can expect a frenzied escape from the ordinary.

The game mechanic in it's current form is kind of simplistic, but again, early access so hopefully thats also on the way.

In either case, $5 is cheaper than lunch and this game will help you burn that off, have a good one!
