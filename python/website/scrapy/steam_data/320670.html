About This Game


Enter a magical fairy-tale world and overcome the evil Crow Witch! After the Crow Witch captures the Months, the world may never see a New Year again! Help her desperate captives escape using your Match 3 talents! Master minigames and find helpful items in Hidden Object scenes in Season Match 3: Curse of the Witch Crow. Free the Months and save the day!


  • Multiple minigames
  • Over 250 missions
  • Taro card fantastic characters
  • Hidden-object scenes
  • Learn-as-you-play tutorial

The third game in the Season Match series offers more of the same match-3 style play from the first two games. This time there is a bit more content, it took me a little longer to beat this one than it did the first two games combined. There's a story mode, where you have to free Month-themed characters from the evil clutches of the Witch Crow by matching gems until certain treasures are unlocked. In between the match-3 rounds, there are little mini-games which require finding objects, sorting out puzzle pieces, or comparing two pictures to spot the differences. I found the mini-games in Season Match 3 to be more consistent, and more in-line with the overall game itself than the ones in Season Match 2.

Unfortunately the issue with some of the small, oddly-shaped boards where gems are prevented from falling depending on the blocks that remain is still an issue, and the crystal shards that lock up more blocks makes the problem quite a bit worse on some maps, preventing any gems from falling to refill at all. Some powerups can get around this, but not all powerups are available on each board so sometimes the only solution was to restart. And restart again.

Still, Season Match 3 - Curse of the Witch Crow is a nice match-3 game that's good for killing some time or relieving some stress. Or just adding some achievements, if that's your thing, since the series has great steam integration with trading cards, achievements, and nice items.
