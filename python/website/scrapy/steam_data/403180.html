About This Game

Grab your pencil and get ready for the most creative DRAW A STICKMAN adventure yet!!

Imagination is the key, as you enter a magical storybook land full of mystery and wonder, unusual creatures, and enigmatic puzzles! Create your own original stickman, and then watch it come to life in DRAW A STICKMAN: EPIC 2! Challenge yourself to unlock every secret, collect every drawing, and share your creativity with the world!

A NEW STORY!
Embark on a perilous journey through time! Draw your Stickman, and create a friend!
But be warned… disaster falls upon your ally! It’s up to you to become the hero!
Maneuver your way through the magical world of EPIC 2 in a quest to save your partner!

YOUR DRAWINGS MATTER!
Create and save unlimited drawings in your sketchbook! Use them throughout your adventure!

SHARE WITH FRIENDS!
With the new share feature, you can now send your creations to your friends! They will then be able to use your drawings in their own epic adventures!

FIGHT EPIC BATTLES!
Face off against inked-out goblins, tongue-lashing frogs, fire-breathing dragons, and BIG bosses! You must use your own strategies to solve intriguing challenges and puzzles, while battling all new villains!

Jump in to this action-packed adventure that allows for endless creativity! As you explore each colorful environment, choose from an assortment of drawing pencils and tools to help you successfully overcome every obstacle along the way. New features and enhancements allow you to choose from a larger color palette, along with a variety of pencil sizes. With the new share feature, send your drawings to your friends! Unlock hidden Color Buddies, locate puzzle pieces, and enjoy new drawing pencils for Wires, Eggs, and Ice! This is an experience like no other, personalized by you!

DRAW A STICKMAN: EPIC 2 is the sequel you’ve been waiting for, and is sure to offer hours of entertainment to avid gamers and creative minds alike!

First off, you move by clicking. I kind of hate that but that's personal taste.

Secondly, you don't have to make a stick man for your character which is pretty great because I got to make a mudkip. ♥♥♥♥. Yes. The second character you make is more or less your enemy but I think you're trying to save him or something?

The gameplay on the first level is alright. You pick up this green pencil thing and all of the trees that are missing leaves you kind of scribble on to add leaves to them. This then allows you to move some of the deadly sludge out of the way so you can progress or kill some enemies. That's right, no weapons on the first level unfortunately. However after you beat the first level if you don't like how your character looks or walks with animation you pick up a sketchbook which allows you to draw more characters or enemies. So don't fret to much if it looks clunky at first.

Also, I really made this because I was trying to quit out of the game while I go smoke (so I wouldn't go over the two hours in case I ran into some reason to get a refund) and I could not find a quit button. I might be an idiot (hopefully) but i had to open up my task manager to quit the game which is a big no-no.

Overall, this game being on sell for $3 seems well worth it unless I run into something awful, which I will immediately update this review with.

Update:

Okay. I had no clue this was a puzzle game. It might have been in the tags but I guess I missed it or something. The game is actually pretty thought provoking and continuously builds upon the abilities and mechanics you learn. You actually have to use your brain for some of the puzzles and there is some light comedic moments in this game as well.

So far it seems like you will have to back track to get some of the unlockable items, which I despise being in games but whatever. And yes, there is no quit button, you're going to have to task manager to quit this gem. Also, you might want to mute the game while you play because the human-people-npc-guys make this squeaky noise that sounds absolutely awful...

Overall I'd give it a 9/10 because it feels pretty unique in it's ideas and it's actually a good puzzle game.

Update 2 that's not necessary:

Well. It only took a little under 3 hours to beat the game 100%, which is a shame honestly. There's only eight levels apparently and none of them are particularly long if you have a good head on your shoulders. I believe this was a mobile port as well since it has a pop up window asking you to rate it, and then it has a big ♥♥♥ button on the home menu that brings you to their website and reads "play more games."

I'd say buy it for the $3 but the full asking price is kind of pushing it. This is steam, not a phone or tablet. If you think you'd let your little sister or brother play this game after you beat it and you know how to erase all of your stuff so as they can start out fresh, then I'd say get it at the full price but $3 is honestly more than enough for this game, regardless of how much I love being a mudkip with a lightsaber.
