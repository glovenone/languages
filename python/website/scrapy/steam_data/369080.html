Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“We would like to take the opportunity to release Age of Survival on through Early Access here on steam for a number of reasons. It will firstly let players into the game at an earlier date than would previously be possible if the game were to be released in a fully finished state.

This will allow for greater feedback from the community, in specific things they want to see changed about the project, new features they might want to see added, and so on. The greatest benefit of Early Access is to be able to communicate and work with the community on creating something great.

Early Access will also allow us to grow in stability as far as funds and business is concerned, keep our server costs and employees paid while we continue development on the project.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“Our goal is to have the project in Early Access for no longer than 1 year.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“The full version of Age of Survival will be vastly different from the project in it's current stages. We are working on tons of new features like new animals and weapons to be added, tons of new survival features we have started, the addition of vehicles, character customization and more.

The final product will be a more stable gaming experience with a full set of features.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“In it's current state, the user is able to see a list of servers (via bolt, and zeus) and connect to any server. The player is able to see other players on the map, combat is enabled. The player also has a fully functional and networked inventory / crafting system / building system. Swimming is functional, and the player is able to explore the ocean. Animal AI is functional and networked.

The core functionality of the game is largely in place and functional, and through Early Access we will be refining the current features as well as adding tons of great things.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“We are planning no price increases after leaving Early Access at this time.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We welcome any and all feedback from the community. We read the comments, as well as have a steam group open for the project, as well as keeping current on other sites such as social media, reddit, etc.

If the community largely sees something that needs to be changed or added, it is definitely our goal to see that happen.

As well as being developers, we all play games, so we want to have as much of a game player's point of view towards this project as we do a developer's point of view.

In a short way of saying it : We want the community involved in this project, and be a deciding factor in Age of Survival's development.”

About This Game

Survive, explore, craft, build, fight, die! Welcome to Age of Survival.

Age of Survival is survival simulation crafting game from Seattletek. Your player has been stranded on an island not far off of the coast of Africa where you will encounter creatures like boar, elephant, lions, hippos, crocs, rhinos, and tons of sea life.

Explore a beautiful environment based on real life heightmap and satellite image data. Interact with trees, ores, creatures, structures, explore the ocean, and travel to new environments. Your player is also able to build structures anywhere on the map, make sure to protect your valuable resources! Craft items like you never have before : craft firearms, weapons, and your tools in a more in depth manner by having to construct individual components, and combining them to create a final outcome. There are currently ten types of crafting in the game, from your basic crafting available on the character anytime, to things like water de-salination and distillation, tailoring, reloading, toolmaking, different types of cooking and more!

Truly test your survival skills!

Age of Survival is a truly immersive survival experience, which brings you to a new level of survivalism and creates a rich experience unlike any other!

We truly care about the community, and want everyone involved in our development throughout it's process. With great features already, and huge plans for the future, Age of Survival is setting itself in motion to be a contender in the survival game arena.
