About This Game

Experience the passion of rugby in the official 2015 Rugby World Cup video game.

  • Choose among the 20 teams in the World Cup and take part in the most prestigious competition in rugby.
  • Rewrite history with your favourite team by taking it from the pool stage all the way to the final, or by creating your own customized tournament.
  • Experience the World Cup as if you were there with commentary from Miles Harrison and Stuart Barnes.
  • Make all the rugby moves with accessible controls: hand offs, dummy passes, drop goals, tackles, pop passes, conversions...
  • Find the ideal tactics for lifting the Webb Ellis Cup: switch players around in the heat of the action, move your line of defence in real time, substitute tired players at the right timeâ¦

Posted: 22 December, 2015
Thoroughly enjoying the game. Using a gamepad, which is working really well. The graphics are decent; the gameplay is fast-paced and enjoyable; and the controls work really well. It is easily the best rugby game I have played, despite the many negative reviews. Compared to other rugby games, this seems to give much more freedom to actually play rugby properly and to execute a fast-paced running game. The controls for rucking are another highlight, I believe, actually allowing for slight errors to result in penalties (as opposed to just deliberate actions).
One or two people have suggested that you may need a decent working knowledge of the sport to enjoy this game, and I can see how that could be true. Even so, I would still strongly recommend this game.
