About This Game

Find Out - this is an adventure game in which you will have to escape from an unknown location, surrounded by a large maze. The protagonist finds himself in the middle of a small forest area, which is a part of the experimental complex. This complex is surrounded by a complicated and confusing maze. You have a limited amount of time, and if you won't get out of the maze before nightfall, you will face all the horrors lurking in the maze.

Game features:
-day-night cycle
-dynamic environment
-spectacular visual effects
-interraction with different mechanisms
-unique atmosphere

I'm kind of neutral about this game. I named the character Paul because after pressing random buttons on the keyboard I discovered you can view detailed information about the world including the character being titled as "Dude". So I named him Paul. Paul seems to be stuck in some weird forest surrounded by a small maze where you must find 8 tiles to escape the maze. It sounds easy but it is designed to be time consuming and rather dull in my opinion. The graphics were nice and it runs as great as any other Cryengine game but it is simply lacking content. That being said, most of my fun in this game came from enabling no clip and third person camera so I could keep flying Paul up in the sky and watching him crash down. So that was exciting. I also had some fun exploring the forest area and looking around. I'm sure the average person could probably get 30 minutes to an hour of fun out of this game so I can't recommend the game at the price. It does have trading cards though. Anyways, Find Out is a decent little tech demo where Paul tries to find escape but only meets death as I keep dropping him from 100 feet in the air onto solid rock. It could be fun.

6/10

Ok, If you are thinking of buying this "Game", Go outside, find the first stick you see. That will be more fun to play with than this game...

This is not a game, it is a cryengine map.

The only plus of it, is that cryengine allows the forest in the middle to look somewhat nice if you ignore the floating grass that is around.

All the game consists of, is walking around a dull maze, finding 8 pressure plates, and then finding the exit.

You are even able to just fly around the map if you press f4 to go into flymode (Intended for developer use)

I bought this game because i enjoyed maze runner, and thought i'd give it a shot.

But seriously, dont buy this game in its current state. It is a waste of both money and hard drive space. (4.5 Gig for this? Wow)
