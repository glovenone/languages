What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“It is really important for me to make cool and stable game. Of Course i need feedback, to make game better. And i need time to fix all bugs, add new maps, levels and other cool thing in the game.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“Up to middle of 2016 - in bad case.
Up to end of 2015 - in good case.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“More maps, more characters, improved game logic and no bugs.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“Main part of the game is done. Most changes it is - new maps, characters, weapons. But new things in game must be placed in code too.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“Price will not change.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“I try to be close to community to understand needs, expectations about the game. Bug reporting as well.”

About This Game

Pixel Fodder - another one platformer where you must crush the bad guys and save the world!

It the Pixel Fodder you will fight against robots, trains, helicopters, evil corporations, soviet zombies, parodies for game heroes and tons of bad guys. And more you can destroy all map up to the last pixel if you wish OR create your own map in editor and destroy it then. Anyway you will not craft or survive - just smashing up everything!

In your squad: Pixelshtein, Mars, Monk, Comяaд Ivaиov, Anonymous, C.O., Slam Dunk jr., Leonerdo, old man, Comendante, Hugo, Heel of rage and other crazy guys. Think that they not so crazy as you wish? Create your own hero! Huge choise of wepon, armor and ... shoes.

Its all about the Pixel Fodder.

Have any crazy ideas how to improve the game? Feel free to give me know them. Game in development.

As promised I returned to review the game at a later date and am happy to say that it has shown a ton of improvements. Old review below

Why would I say 90% nice things about a game that I do NOT recommend?

TLDR: This game could be a perfect replacement for Expendabros and I mean that in a complimentary manner. It has tons of characters to choose from, although I find the current differences to be less than thrilling, it's a lot for an early access game to have so many choices already. Same thing with the world choices. All of this sounds good still, so why not recommend it?

It's all content and they have yet to fix basic function. It's really frustrating, which isn't new to EA games, but it's frustrating for an entirely new reason. The menu functions are awful and you have to switch back and forth between keyboard, mouse and gamepad. The physics engine in the game needs a lot of tweaking making basic controls a little wonky. Again, these are things that EVERY developer fixes before exposing the game to the public. There are a few other basic issues like this that make me nervous. With so many of the budget priced developers abandoning their games right after cashing in with a launch I HAVE to tentatively say wait to buy. A competent developer will get this under control in a week or two at most. If I don't ammend this to a positive recommendation by Sept 15, I never will. To be fair to the Dev, I have set a Google calendar reminder to check in on the game and ammend my review. I wish them luck and REALLY hope they pull it off.

Early Access Review
Posted: 1 August, 2015
Pixel Fodder is a game where you run around as 3 different people, in different gamemodes and levels to kill either bad guys or just restore justice in the pixel meat world.

The game feels awful in it's controls, and give the feeling of a delay while running around, jumping, climping etc. You die really quickly, and sadly i lost interest within minutes after trying all modes. The design of characters and overall map is really good for a pixel game, but the game it self punches you in the stomach of it's hard diffuclty and design.

When you die, you start in the beginning and the map stays as you left it. The walls and death people will stay destroyed. Every wall and ground can be destroyed so far. The only thing i don't understand is why a pixel game should have football practice map for a shooter game, in it's tutorial.

The game was not my cup of tea, and i lost interest really quickly, but the game works fine without problems, which is why i recommend this for people who enjoy pixel games. The game offers alot of different content and gamemodes to play in. - I'm sure that people who enjoy this kind of games will find many hours of fun as the games offers many different modes.
