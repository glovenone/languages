About This Game

I believe that gaming should not be anything complicated or diffcult,
but a relaxing and pleasure experience.

Therefore, I designed this ARPG

battle system which requires no attack button to play.
The simplicity brings a fluent and interesting gaming experience.

Eternal Senia is the realization of this concept.

I spent one year developing this game.
It is a game that can be easily enjoyed by any player.

Definitely a game that's worth sinking a couple of hours into.

The battle system is pretty basic but the game provides a steady pace at introducing new elements as you go along, keeping it fresh.

The storyline is easy to follow and understand, albeit with some grammar and spelling (taken into account this was originally in Chinese). The visuals also really help the storyline, and allows you to feel for the characters.

I find the music to be the highlight of this game. I really enjoyed the somber and sad pieces that are quite approriate for the theme, as well as the battle music that pumps you up when fighting.

I'd give this game an 8/10 for good gameplay and music, and a decent storyline. Considering that it is also free, I highly recommend anybody that's into Action RPGs or have a nostalgia for Ragnarok Online to play this game.
