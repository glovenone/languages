About This Game



Cyborg Rage is a multiplayer coop space shooter that is all about cooperative online action: Work with your brave wingman to destroy all enemy starships or be destroyed! Master the advantages of your ship class to withstand your enemies!

Cyborg Rage is your source for fast-paced action, massive explosions and a cooperative gameplay core for two pilots that demands good reflexes and fast strategic decisions.

Face the future


The year 3226: The future. Battle Cyborgs were never designed to be smart or develop any kind of self-awareness. They had been produced in masses as expendable military units. It was a time when security was more valuable than freedom. We sent them to the frontlines.

Today, they return.

Choose your ship


Combine your ship technologies with those of your wingman for a maximum of cooperation!

Depending on your chosen ship, your arsenal may include a destructive EMP missile, a missile barrage, instant shield rechargers, an electric field, a nano repair impulse, a massive shield, the devouring nuclear missile... and more technologies that will leave your enemies without doubt who is dominating this sector in space.

Pilots unite


Make one of your friends your trusted wingman or let the matchmaking find a player of your skill level to face the challenge together! Just remember: For the moment, you two rely on each other!

Mitchfynde

Posted: 28 February
This game was given to me by Black Shell Media to provide them with private feedback. Keep that in mind while reading this review. Just to be clear, I was not given the game in exchange for a positive Steam review.

Space shooters or shoot em ups are not exactly my favorite genre. It takes a lot to impress me in this genre. This game didn't impress me, but I was pleasantly surprised that the game was actually good. I was initially very turned off by the visuals, the menus, and the UI... but the game is fun. When it comes down to it, a fun game is better than a pretty game.

The game is very simple to learn and easy to control. While the controls could be tightened a bit, there isn't too much to complain about there. The core gameplay is very fun. The autofire is a nice feature for those of us who don't want carpal tunnel injuries! Something other games in the genre could learn from.

The way the entire game is played on the mouse just makes sense in this game. You steer with the mouse, you use skills with the mouse. It just works. The skills are one thing I really loved about the game. The combo of offensive and defensive skills made the game feel strategic, which was unexpected.

The game could use a visual upgrade. As it is, it really just isn't much to look at. There's no character to it visually. It just looks bland and generic. Pretty much the same thing can be said for the music. Although, the music is not too bad. That being said, I actually liked the voice acting. The voice acting in these sort of games is normally non-existent or absolutely terrible. The voice acting in this game is actually pretty decent.

One particularly nasty thing about this game is the fact that the multiplayer is totally dead. That's not really a fault of the developers. The multiplayer is only dead because nobody is playing it. That's likely due to the game being fairly unknown, not because the game is bad.

My last complaint is that the game feels lacking in content. I'm not exactly sure what I would change as far as that goes, but it's just something I feel is worth pointing out. Maybe some more unlocks and just more visuals in general.

I know I've listed a lot of complaints, but the game is really pretty decent. I'm giving it a recommendation. The amount of content in the game is low, but if you like these sort of games you will likely enjoy this one.

Posted: 20 February
I usually play shoot 'em up space games where you move with WASD and aim with your mouse, Cyborg Rage was made to work only with your mouse. You pilot your spaceship moving your mouse and use your abilities with right and left mouse buttons.

The coop can be played with real persons or AI, the AI is quite good so you shouldn't have any issues playing with it.
You have campaing and match modes, they looks the same except match mode is shorter than campaing, you kill a single boss and get your points.

With points you can buy new spaceships, the more you play and get points and advance in game the more ships will be avaiable to buy. Each spaceship has a different ability, one has a shield and the other has a missiles and so on. There is a minor customization option, you can change the color and it will affect your ship, your cannon blasts and your thrusters.

Your objective is simple, you have to destroy enemy ships and avoid their shots until you reach the boss so you have to defeat him. If you or your partner die then you have to wait 8 seconds to respawn and the other can't die before the 7 seconds, otherwise you lose.

Great game, the perfect choice to play with a friend.
