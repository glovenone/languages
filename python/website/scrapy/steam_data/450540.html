Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“Not every game is a good fit for early access but we think Hot Dogs, Horseshoes & Hand Grenades is a perfect fit. Itâs a sandbox game, it is already a blast to play, and since VR is still developing at such a rapid pace we think this is the best time to iterate on a live audience, and get tons of feedback from doing our developing out in the public.

Plus a lot of you have expressed that you might explode if you donât get to play it soon.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“We are planning on Leaving Early Access this summer.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“Hot Dogs, Horseshoes & Hand Grenades will continue to grow through a series of experiments, environment mock-ups and prototypes. We're not set in stone on the exact scope or breadthe of the experience, as we'd like to respond to what the player-base is really enjoying. You should expect the following categories of content to be filled out over the game's development:

- More Weapons/Virtual Objects: We've already got about a dozen weapons in various stages of pre-production. The goal is to have these added at a rate of about one a week, possibly faster, possibly slower. Prioritization on what types will be added will be based on providing new interaction types for the player (new and different types of reloading, cycling, aiming).

- Game-play Prototypes: Hand tracked VR plus mechanically simulated objects make for an entirely new type of interaction rhythm for gaming gun-play. We'll be exploring various genre/mechanic contexts for our virtual weapon systems, and pushing them into the game's build at very early stages to see how they play, and get feedback. The best that rise to the top will get further love and refinement.

- Chill out Spaces: For as much as games focus on the action-ee side of guns, anyone who's gone shooting in the real world knows its an activity that's much more akin to the pace of fishing. It's a relaxing experience. So we're going to continue to expand an indoor and outdoor shooting range experience focused on all that futzy simulatory joy including manual reloading, zero-ing of sights, and getting a stabilized long-range shooting system working smoothly.

- Anything else our nutty minds come up with and decide to slam in for the joy of it!”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“Right now Hot Dogs, Horseshoes & Hand Grenades consists of three environments:

The Warehouse: A large indoor environment will the entire suite of weapons and interactive toys we've been working on. There's a dynamic target sequencer to play with as well.

The Indoor Range: A small, realist indoor shooting range with paper target system that can be sent to various distances with scoring system.

Arcade Prototype #1: A more abstract/sci-fi arcade-style prototype with 30 waves of intense geometric enemy action!

This section will continue to be updated as the game's content expands.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“There will be a small price increase when we leave early access.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“Our goal is to use Early Access as an extended play-test period. We are really looking forward to getting Hot Dogs, Horseshoes & Hand Grenades in the hands of the community and to start getting feedback. As we work on adding more toys to the sandbox, we want to see all the crazy uses of those toys you folks come up with. While this user feedback will no doubt help us catch some bugs and glitches, we are most interested in seeing what is really working well so we can push those features harder.

While we canât include every bit of feedback, if you are really enjoying one of our experiment scenes, Hit the community page and let us know!”

4th of July Hotfix is Live!

Hi everyone! I know I said I was taking a break, but a couple small things were bothering me, and I wanted them fixed before you all might be celebrating the 4th, having family jump into your Vive, etc. So without further ado:

  • Roman Candle charges launching cause a haptic buzz in the hand holding the candle now.

Fixes:
  • AKM Fire selector is now in correct order (Safe, Full, Single)
  • Bolt Action Rifle breach must be closed to fire, and a couple other misc. guards added to make it harder to 'break'.
  • Grenade explosion force regression in Grenade Skeeball has been fixed.

Hope you're all having a wonderful weekend!
