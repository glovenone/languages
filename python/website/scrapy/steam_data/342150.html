Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“IMPORTANT NOTICE: Games of Glory is not your standard MOBA - controls and weapons are different. We have not yet done a full tutorial, please be patient with us. Try the two maps with bots first!

We depend on our community to help us develop Games of Glory and Early Access gives us the chance to involve gamers and fans early.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“The game is already quite advanced, but there remains important features to finish. We hope to leave Early Access within 6 months”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“Important additions are a spectating client, more Clones (Player characters), additional game modes, plus tournament organization features.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“Games of Glory is really fun to play! It has two good maps, a training room, many characters implemented. There are still bugs, but game play generally works well. Servers are as yet untested for high loads.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“The game is and will be free to play. We believe current DLC packages are cheap and will likely be more expensive in the future.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We interact daily with the community on our forums, and we implement many of their ideas. We also have votes about graphics and features. The game will also include a game mode that gives our players a direct impact on the future of the universe.”

About This Game

Immerse yourself in the atmosphere of a true sports arena!

The Games of Glory, gladiator games put in place by the Synarch to insidiously entertain the inhabitants of the intergalactic universe, are THE sport of the future!

Engage in intense arena battles by incarnating the fiercest Clones to win fame and glory, and soon, influence the future of the universe.

  • Aim all of your shots with pure skill (no auto-attack)
  • Wide selection and instant switching of weapons (weapons not Clone specific)
  • Run & gun mechanics
  • Two unique game maps and modes: Svandia "Superstar" (Protect the VIP) and Arkashan âConquest" (Capture the points)

  • Playable with mouse or keyboard (WASD) controls

Games of Glory isn't a simple MOBA, for having played at every major games of this kind (DoTA, DoTa II, LoL, HoN, SMITE, HoTS...) I can tell the only game I found such original, deep and crazy experience is GoG.


Gameplay: In a MOBA gameplay is kinda straightforward, or it is?

Usually you have to use your mouse, right clicking on the place to go, right clicking on the ennemy to auto attack... well not that much exciting isn't it?
In GoG there is no such thing as an Auto Attack and you can move via mouse click or with WASD (yup this looked strange too me also, but this gives trully new gaming perspectives).

Let's talk about maps, here we have 2 different maps with two TOTALLY different game modes and ways to play, one "classic" defend base -but with some new shiny ideas such as capture the point income and victory point- and one "protect the VIP" objective.
This gives more deepness to the game since you have to rethink how to use your clone (champion) and build!

Since we're on the build...Dion't you find strange to give a sword to a hero whom is using an bow and not even seeing its attack (animation, or way to attack) change ? Let's say for LoL : giving Infinity Edge (a mothafreaking sword) to an Ashe and that Ashe still using a bow you didn't even bought?
Here every weap you take (riffles, guns, knifes, warhammer, rocket launchers and even A FLAMETHROWEEEER) is really on your clone and you'll see him using it to deliver DEATH on battlefield.

I could keep on chatting on the game, but why not just trying the game by yourselves? ;)

I'll finish by one thing : for me GoG could easily stand for "Great Original Gameplay" ;)

A TL;DR can be found at the bottom of this review.

Pros:
- Good, unique concept. The gamemode could use a little more defining here and there in explaining what everything does, but after a few matches you can get the hang of it and comprehend it well. The gamemode calls for close matches between both teams.
- AI can be challenging, it takes a little effort to play better than them, personally I feel like they're the perfect difficulty to play against before you can play against actual players.
- Heroes are cool and unique, and their classes are well-defined.
- You can make groups to play with your friends.
- Level progression is slow but not unbareable.
- Varied free hero rotation.

Cons:
- Video-based tutorials are really the only emphasized form of education on the game.
- I have a high ping on the servers (I live in Washington state in the United States of America) at about 160, makes gameplay a little less enjoyable than it could be.
- You don't receive very much currency for each match, and matches last about 20 minutes on average, makes matches feel less worth playing than they could.
- Matches can feel long and dragged on with rogue teammates.
- More optimization options would be great.
- Low playerbase, so install it and play :D

TL;DR:
Overall, it's a good MOBA. It's hardly a copy of any other MOBA I've played, and it can be a lot of fun. However, matches can feel long depending on your team, and can lack enjoyability because of the naturally high ping servers and because of the short rewards with each match. I'd definitely try it, I know I'm for sure going to continue playing and following development.

I have to be honest - right now, it's a Very Early Access game. Forget the bundles, the game is not worth the money right now. The main issue is imbalance and overall unappealing interface and gameplay. It's a typical MOBA that doesn't bring anything new into the genre, nothing fresh or amazing.

I do realize that there is a core or a structure to these types of games, however the only mode available right now is a downright clone of League of Legend's Dominion mode with a few twists. When Dominion first introduced, it had a clear objective for the player. Even after playing the GoG tutorial I couldn't understand what was going on the map for a few games. It's a Team Deathmatch with a few spots that force the players to meet in that certain spot and spam whatever they've got in their hands, with little fun, smoothness, tactics or appeal.

Assault rifles are clearly the best weapons in the game, especially if you're using the character Pinto. Speed and damage plus his ability to instantly reload his weapon makes him so overpowered that I've been cutting through enemies like a hot knife through butter. And even then, the enemy team would simply use the tank to take on the towers and get to my team's core, destroying it within a single raid. Absolutely no balance, no map modifiers (apart from spots that generate money faster for your team), no creep involvement in leveling up - all that has to be completely redone for GoG to be a fun game.

Visually, bland and uninteresting - I literally have nothing else to write about it. It looks Free to Play. The soundtrack is nonexistent but I can't complain, I don't want it in a MOBA anyway (maybe apart from the menu screen).
