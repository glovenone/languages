About This Game


Set 50 years after the climactic events of the original Red Faction, Red Faction: Guerrilla allows players to take the role of an insurgent fighter with the newly re-established Red Faction movement as they battle for liberation from the oppressive Earth Defense Force. Red Faction: Guerrilla re-defines the limits of destruction-based game-play with a huge open-world, fast-paced guerrilla-style combat, and true physics-based destruction.

Key features


  • Open World Guerrilla Warfare Â You decide who, when, where and how to battle. Utilize guerrilla tactics, improvised weaponry, and modified vehicles to lead insurgent attacks on EDF targets. Launch attacks based on your own gameplay style, take on missions in any order you choose, or engage in destructive activities to weaken the EDF's grip on Mars.
  • Strategic Destruction Â Use destruction to your tactical advantage, setting ambushes or chain reaction explosions to attack enemy strongholds and permanently modify the game environment. Leverage fully-dynamic physics-based destruction to improvise on the fly: blow holes in a wall or floor to set an ambush or escape, take out a staircase to stop your pursuers, or drive vehicles through blown out walls.
  • Evolving & Emergent Gameplay Â Carve your path through an ever changing landscape as you improvise your combat tactics - mixing gameplay styles, vehicles, weapons and explosives to defeat the EDF.
  • Epic Sci-Fi Setting Â Explore the huge, unforgiving Martian landscape, from the desolate mining outpost of Parker to the gleaming EDF capital city of Eos; then tear through the fully destructible open-world environments swarming with EDF forces, Red Faction resistance fighters, and the downtrodden settlers caught in the cross-fire.
  • Multiplayer Combat Â There is no place to hide when you put your guerrilla warfare skills to the test in a variety of highly destructive multiplayer combat modes.

I've already bought 8 copies of the game for my friends. It is pure fun for LAN parties with its crazy multiplayer modes and THE SLEDGEHAMMER of course!

Singleplayer campaign is solid and rich. I didn't expect it at the first time played. AI adapts to destroyed walls, they can even crush a building occasionally to get you out. Lots of explosives and powerful vehicles create plenty of "omg wtf lol!" moments all the times. There is even a stealth system! And cover-sticking if you wish! And did i mention explosives? Remote charges, landmines, barrels, black-hole generators, rockets, HUGE ROCKETS... They all fit the game and it's fun to combine stuff.

Community support is nice to tears. You can download a load of mods and user-friendly mod manager (google for FactionFiles). In the winter of 2016 i thought that people squeezed every bit of modability and hackability from the game, but new modders and their work apperaed since then!

Multiplayer is beautifully crazy, but it is not easy to find people playing since the community is not that big. Someone made a steam bot "buddy" which can alert you when someone creates a game. Isn't it awesome?

I only wish that something will make developers release an SDK / map editor. However, even without that, there are A LOT of maps for multiplayer already in-game, a bunch of maps for demolition mode and a big world to explore in campaign. All of these destructible.
