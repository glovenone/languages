Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“VR: Vacate the Room is really just a short game. It is intended to stay that way. But i really hope to get some inspirational feedback from the Steam community to enhance the experience.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“The plan is to leave Early Access as soon as possible. But if there are a lot of good ideas from the Steam community, it will stay as long as implementation (or even discussing them) requires.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“In the final version of VR: Vacate the Room some changes might appear. That depends on the feedback from the Steam community.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“In the current state VR: Vacate the Room is completely playable. Some features might be added and maybe some gameplay changes might occur.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“While in Early Access the price of the game will be reduced.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“I'm looking forward to constructive criticism, discussions and good ideas. It is also okay to just record your playthrough, comment it and share it with me.”

About This Game

VR: Vacate the Room is a short Escape the Room game inspired by such classics as Crimson Room.

It is a VR Experience where you need to find clues to solve puzzles and find your way out. There are some items that can be grabbed and carried around. With some items you can interact (such as switches and numpads).

It currently only supports the HTC Vive. Medium room size is recommended, but small size should be alright as well.

System Requirements

    Minimum:
    • OS: Windows 7 SP1, Windows 8.1 or Windows 10
    • Processor: Intel® i5-4590, AMD FX 8350 equivalent or better
    • Memory: 4 GB RAM
    • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce® GTX 970, AMD Radeon™ R9 290 equivalent or better, Video Output: HDMI 1.4, DisplayPort 1.2 or newer
    • Storage: 150 MB available space
    • Additional Notes: 1x USB 2.0 or better port also required

Mind your surroundings
VR is a tricky thing! Make sure you have enough room.

The developer can't be held responsible for any damage you do to yourself or your surroundings.
