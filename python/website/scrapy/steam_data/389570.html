About This Game

Eat cells or die trying!

Warning: Incredibly addicting Multiplayer Game !

** LAG: If you experience high latency, please open the Settings Menu and change your server location **

You are a cell, wandering around looking for smaller cells to absorb and grow. Larger cells, likewise, are seeking for smaller cells like you to absorb. Smaller cells move fast, larger cells are very slow! You can split yourself to increase your speed but this will increase the risk of being eaten. Finally, beware of the viruses!

Mitosis allows you to:

- Four Game Modes: Free to Play, Random Team, Capture The Flag and Guilds War
- Guild system with chatroom
- Equipment system
- Custom skins
- Eat smaller cells to gain mass and become larger
- Avoid larger cells because they can absorb you
- Separate yourself into 2 cells to gain speed
- Use viruses to hide yourself or hit others
- Expel mass to decrease in size
- Have a lot of fun!

Many people say that Mitos.is is a shameless rip-off of Agair.io, and as a result say many negative things about it.

Well yes. It is a rip-off, but it's a rip-off worth playing.
Consider this about Mitos.is:

• Music and Sounds come included by default
• There's a larger library of skins to use + custom skins
• There's (more) equipable items that can boost up gameplay, thus making it more appealing
• Different gamemodes are available to play besides the regular (FFA, Teams, Party) that again make you more interested
• In-game chat is a thing
• It lags alot less
• You can invite your friends to play in any server if they also have Mitos.is
• The micro-transactions that everybody that bashes on when hating on the game are completely optional; they only serve a purpose to buy in-game coins (as far as I know anyways). Coins can be found during gameplay and are collected by players that have an in-game account as they are seen across the map, or if they earn an achivement or reach a goal they get coins to buy what they wish

However, there's a something that I need to get across in terms of that last point: People who bash on this game just because you have the option to pay think that the game is immediately trying to cash in on Agar.io's success. To those who think so I have a message for you: PLEASE do your research before you hate on this game. Agar.io has the EXACT same system in which you can pay for coins, so don't act like the whole micro-transaction deal is a shock to you and a valid point to say this game is bad, or a "Pay to Play" when the game you came from does that too.

Overall if you like Agar.io, I'd recommend Mitos.is to you.

Sure. Mitos.is is a rip-off of Agar.io, but it's a rip-off that goes an extra mile to make it better.

Y'all take care now (ᴗ ͜ʖᴗ)
