About This Game

The dark forces have taken control over the realm!
Lead the epic heroes to defeat the Dark Lord and clear the darkness from the world!


SIMPLE & INTUITIVE MECHANICS
• Tap to control your heroes and go towards an enemy to attack!
• Activate deadly skills to clear the dark forces from the realm!

REAL TIME BOSS RAID
• Team up with four other players in real time to defeat raid bosses!
• Fight together against the Dragon or challenge the Dark Knight!

INFINITE TOWER
• Test your limits by progressing through the Infinite Tower!
• The higher the floor you reach, the more rewards you will get!

LEADERBOARDS
• Try to rank the highest on the leaderboards for amazing rewards!
• Become the leader in various leaderboards!

SOCIALIZE
• Make friends at Skyrock through the global chat!
• Add friends and hire each other as mercenaries!

EQUIPMENT UPGRADES
• Collect gems to upgrade your gear!
• Enhance your equipment to make your character even stronger!

*A network connection is required to play.
*Tablets supported! Want to enjoy Elements: Epic Heroes with a big screen? No problem! Download Elements: Epic Heroes on your tablet now!

I'll keep this simple.

-The game is cheap.
-The gameplay is cheap.
-Currently level 7, the whole time i've just held down left click and used my skills maybe 4 times in total.
-Double clicking makes you roll/dodge which consumes a tiny bit of mana so don't spam click.
-The quests starting out are too simple. Like most MMOs you are to kill X amount of this and return.
-There is no open world, there might be later on? Each map/stage you click to join and offers maybe 5 minutes of simple game play till you get to the end and teleport to town or the next map.
-After you complete the simple maps you can be prompted to buy ingame items to make yourself stronger for $4.99, I clicked buy to see what happens and it tells me "This is not available in beta"
-There is ingame currency gold, crystals and gems, each you can spend real money to obtain or very slowly by playing of course.

I don't expect the game to get much better. There are plenty of F2P games out there that you could find more enjoyment with.
