Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“Teddy Terror started as a product of the IndiesVsPewDiePie Gamejam in Dec 2014.
In the past months we developed the game more and the Core gameplay has been set.( randomized levels, bosses, enemies, items, with a playable character)
We are polishing this game into a full product as we are currrently still undergoing changes and with Early access we want to involve the community during our development process and finalize Teddy Terror into a finished game powered by you.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“Teddy Terror will be in Early Access until it's finished.
We don't want to limit it to a time frame. We hope to create a great game together with the community rather than rushing it.
The game will initially be launched on PC, as we are able to get more resources we plan to launch it on other platforms as well.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“We are planning to keep adding content to the game and release the game when we reach a certain milestone. The released version we have in mind will have at least 100 different enemies, 100 different items, 30 different toys, 8 different areas with a total of 30 different Epic Bosses.( These numbers may be higher based on the resources we get)
You will also be able to compete against the world with achievements and ranking.
Together with amazingly written music we hope to give you a great gaming experience.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The game is a playable build, but may contain several bugs or glitches and errors, which we are very eager to fix as soon as possible!
Every two weeks we will update the game with more content or changes based on the feedback we get. So keep that in mind before purchasing! This version is an unfinished product and will undergo some updates/changes based on the feedback we get from the community.
We will open a forum soon where you can post any bugs or errors which we will try to fix. Until then feel free to send us a mail through our contact form on our website.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“After we finished developing Teddy Terror it will be available for about the same price range.
The price range is currently set for you to be able to be able to play the game while being part of the development progress. The game will still undergo several changes, so keep that in mind.
But if you want to be part of the development process and see how this game will grow, now is your chance to do so.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“Soon we are launching a forum where the community can discuss about Teddy Terror.
We are really interested in the feedback provided by the community and we will take them into consideration during our development process. Therefore everyone is free to mail feedback, suggestions, bug reports to us with our contact form on teddyterror.com until we open our forums.”

27 June

Patch v.1.10 Early Access: Invasion of the Sins!

Teddy Terror Early Access Update v.1.10 : Invasion of the Sins!

Developer's Note:
It has been a while since I launched an update for Teddy Terror and after a long period in a semi hiatus state I am proud to announce the launch of Teddy Terror's latest update during the Summer Sale with big changes!
The reason for me to release Teddy Terror on Early Access was to involve the community with the development of the game.
That being said I am trying hard to give you all a smooth gaming experience, and remove as much errors/bugs I can find. However this doesn't mean that the game is bug-free and I have really appreciated the feedback I have gotten so far to improve Teddy Terror and would love to hear your feedback on this update! I will try to fix any issue in the game as soon as I can.

Patch Notes:
In this update there are several big changes that I hope will improve the player's experience.

How skilled are you really?
Test your skills in the new challenging Game mode: Invasion Mode!
Synopsis: Your conscious needs your help to defeat the Sins!

Rules:
  • Clear waves of enemies
  • Dodge roll!
  • Destroy the shells containing the wave boss
  • Defeat the Sins
  • Each boss drops tokens and candies which you can use in the shop
  • Only activatable traps
  • No toys allowed
  • Perma-death without character levels
  • New score system: gain score based on performance
  • Each monster kill is worth 5 points
  • Each boss kill is worth 50 points
  • Each wave time out reduces 10 points
  • Each damage received reduces 2 points

ADDITIONS:
  • NEW Score system: Currently applies to Invasion mode. Story mode will receive it's own scoring system in the future. For now the timer in Story mode has been removed.
  • NEW: Added Damage system: now traps and weapons deal damage to monsters and bosses
  • NEW: Monster HP, every monster has his own health bar.
  • NEW Stats: Critical hit chance, Item Find. Critical hits deal 4x your damage. Item Find is the percentage to find drops like candy, hearts, keys.
  • NEW Pickup item: Keys: collect keys from monster drops to open locked walls for special items in Story Mode!
  • NEW Pickup: Souls - collect Souls to get experience in Story Mode.
  • NEW Pickup item: Invasion Token : exchange invasion tokens for extra damage or full health in the Invasion mode shop.
  • NEW Character Selection screen with character art!
  • NEW Character: G-Chan - unlock this Funtastic character by collecting souls!
    NEW Monsters: Several creepy monsters have been added to the game, with different skillsets.
    NEW Bosses: Invasion mode brings a total of 8 unique bosses( 7 sins and their shell) with each their own skillset!

    CHANGES:
  • Boss health bar: a new visual style for the Boss health bar
  • Boss room: added two small rooms where players can exchange candy or keys for additional items!
  • Graphics: Improved visual style of the game.
  • Controls: Pressing ESC twice in a row won't bring you back to the Menu. Instead pressing ESC pauses and unpauses the game. To Exit the game you either press E or END when paused.

    KNOWN Issues:
  • Music sometimes tend to mute after pausing the game. Currently working to resolve this.

    FIXED Issues:
  • Fixed the sound issue at Green Ogre boss and several others audio related issues.
  • Fixed several visual glitches.

    UNDER Development:
    Currently I am working on several new functionalities:
    • Steam Leaderboard Ranking system
    • Controller Support
    • Save/ Load System
    • Additions to the Story mode
    So stay tuned for the next update!

Please feel free to let me know if you find any bugs/errors, glitches or just annoyances with the game! Every feedback is very much appreciated! you can contact your reports to contact@ragemonstergames.com

Teddy Terror on hold

Hello everyone,

First of all from the bottom of my heart I would like to thank everyone who has purchased and supported us with Teddy Terror.
Without you as the community Teddy Terror would have never come this far.
Thank you.

As you have noticed Teddy Terror has not been updated for several months now and It's kind of unfortunate to announce that we are currently on hold with the development of the game due to the lack of funds.

Until now I have self funded the development and marketing of Teddy Terror and sadly our funds and income are not sufficient enough to work fulltime on the development of the game.

That being said, I am looking for ways to fund the development of Teddy Terror and I do not intend to stop the development.

Teddy Terror will eventually be released, that is my goal and passion, it will only take a bit longer than anticipated.

I hope you can bear with me and still be able to support us during our development.

I have many ideas for the game that have yet to be realized.
New game modes, Rewards, Boss mechanics etc.

Thank you for taking the time and effort in supporting us.
We hope to release the next update soon.

Kind regards,

Gianni Knight
CEO Rage Monster Games

About This Game

Teddy Terror is a 2D horror survival with a challenging passive-aggressive gameplay where using your environment is crucial for survival

In Teddy Terror you take on the role of a little boy with his Teddybear who wakes up in a nightmare.

With your surroundings as your weapons your goal is to Survive the nightmarish world and escape from tongue spitting eyeball creatures, horrifiying bosses and hordes of unimaginable abominations!

Unique Gameplay

  • Passive aggressive playstyle - be swift and escape from the clutches of evil or wait for the right moment to strike and eliminate insidious creatures with deadly traps.

  • Diverse killing possibilities - Lure hellish creatures in to traps, activate traps by throwing your teddybear or find powerful toys to eradicate every monstrosity that may lurk in the depths of the darkness.

  • Roguelike features - permadeath, randomized loot(items and toys), randomized deadly levels with gruesome enemies and gigantic bosses with endless replayability.

  • Level up, find items, complete achievements,empower yourself and grow from a helpless child into a killing machine


Can you persevere his unending world of torment and terror?

Support us and find out!

Rage Monster Games is a dutch Indie game developing company managed by Gianni Knight.

Now several months in development we made huge changes with an all new experience.

We are working hard to deliver a great gaming experience and to bring Teddy Terror to life.

Therefore we hope we can share our excitement with you.

Early Access Review
Posted: 21 May, 2015
A really unique rouge-like. You must clear each room by destroying all enemies, however you get no weapons. Instead you rely on traps around the map to vanquish your nightmarish foes. You do have your trusty teddy bear though to use to activate switch based traps and to slow enemies to get them in the right position at the right time. The game looks similar in style to Nuclear Throne, but has little else in common other than its 2-D pixel-art aesthetic.

There is already a short tutorial in place to show you the basics of the game, but even though I played through it I felt I personally didn't need it. The game is quite simple but fun and includes a static upgrade system so eventually even if you keep dying you will improve your character. There are also many random items which will help you along the way, such as a toy gun (which actually isn't a toy at all) and other defensive and offensive items. I think the price is somewhat low for this title so I can easily recommend it.
