About This Game

Best In Farming!

An idyllic farming environment surrounded by inviting & untilled mountain panoramas set in the beautiful landscapes of Tuscany, The Alps and USA await all fans of the Agricultural Simulator series.

At their own farm hobby-farmers undertake the daily tasks and challenges of a professional: The production of food, sustainable resources and delivery of eco products all have to be managed.

Fields have to be worked and farm animals cared for. The dynamic ground makes the whole game more realistic! Future farmers have to invest tactically and with foresight â the rapid spend of budgets by running costs and seed bills must not be underestimated. To realize a profit takes financial and tactical skill.

Key Features


  • Including Interactive Tutorial
  • Includes 4 Maps
  • Includes more than 100 machines
  • Free placeable building including:
    • Solar Energy Plant
    • Greenhouse
    • Windmills
    • Dung Tank
  • Extensive Help System, including Transport tasks
  • 6 different animal species

bought it cheap on bundlestars and got bored today, so i gave it a try.

good things first:
the tutorial is easy to understand, the controls and menu are simple as well.
the game has a few different scenarios to play, with a few nice design ideas.
you can use different vehicles and interact like on a real farm, yes, so far to the point of a simulator...

the bad things:
the graphic engine has an incredibly poor performance, everything lags, textures take ages to load, the vehicles and buldings look "ok", everything else is just outstanding ugly, especially the water.
(dont get fooled by the screenshots on the store page!)

the physics in the game are completly broken, driving with 5 km/h can flip your machine over, if you dont pay attention, your trailer can get stuck in the front of your tractor,...or else.
the sounds of the environment and animals, the animations... just terrible.

after i remembered the fact that i basically paid nothing for it, i just took it with humour and tried to break it even more.
it can be quite hilarious when you see how many things can go wrong in a single game.

sadly its supposed to be a simulator which turned out to be an insult against humanity.

a reason to buy this game could be showing your younger kids the basics about how farming machines work, for that its fair enough, ... or when youre bored with friends and just need a good laugh.

but if youre a mature person, looking for a good farming simulator: SKIP THIS ONE!
