About This Game

The gods have trapped your sister. Prove yourself worthy to bring her back.

Maui is a free action-adventure game based on Hawaiian mythology. The game features an innovative "stat grid" that lets you switch stats on the fly to face any situation.

Features:
  • Play the game completely for free--no microtransactions, no DLC, just a free game for you to play
  • Explore a beautiful ancient Hawaiian island
  • Innovative "stat grid" lets you switch character stats instantly at any time
  • Collect offerings and face trials from the gods

  • Narrative and mechanics drawn directly from Native Hawaiian culture and mythology

System Requirements

    Minimum:
    • OS: Windows 7, 8, 8.1, 10
    • Processor: Intel i5 or equivalent
    • Memory: 4 GB RAM
    • Graphics: Not recommended for Intel onboard graphics
    • Storage: 1 GB available space

PROS: Cute graphics, music that fits the theme, lore is available but does not force itself on you. Nice use of color.

CONS: Consistently got 14 FPS in this game. There is no weight to the character or his actions. Speed up ability will glitch the character. "Boss" levels are also moderately glitchy and nonsensical. No sense of weight in the map itself, as you can walk into buildings but not be able to trigger their use unless clicking a hotkey at the door. Cutscenes have the same theatrics as tutorials. Items glitch. Enemies glitch.

WORTH: Maui lacks a feeling of reward, but to experience it is free. I finished the game in a little under an hour, but am not sure if I would consider the time well spent. It's an easy 100%, so it boils down to how much you want that extra perfect game in your percentage.

OVERALL: Plays like a demo. Is sparing in the way of substance. You may pick up a little bit of information about Hawaii, but most of it would be stuff you've already known from basic US history.
